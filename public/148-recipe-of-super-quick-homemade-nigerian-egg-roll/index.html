The ingredients needed to make Nigerian Egg Roll:
  1. Take Dough
  2. Get 2 Cups Flour
  3. Get 3 tbsp Sugar
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp butter
  5. Make ready Pinch salt
  6. Take Pinch Nutmeg
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking powder
  8. Prepare 1 egg (for the dough)
  9. Make ready 1/2 cup evaporated milk
  10. Make ready Filling
  11. Prepare 5 hard boiled eggs
Steps to make Nigerian Egg Roll:
  1. Add all the dry ingredients (flour, sugar, salt, baking powder, nutmeg) and butter mix together.
  2. Add egg(beaten) and milk to form the dough. Allow to rest for 5 minutes.
  3. Cut the dough into five pieces, roll out, place the boiled egg and fold to cover the egg with the dough.
  4. Dip and fry in a low heat oil.
