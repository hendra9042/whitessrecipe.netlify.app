How to Make Award-winning Korean Egg Roll

To begin with this particular recipe, we must first prepare a few ingredients. You can have korean egg roll using 6 ingredients and 8 steps. Here is how you cook that.

The ingredients needed to make Korean Egg Roll:
  1. Make ready 7 eggs
  2. Get 1 cucumber
  3. Make ready 1 carrot
  4. Make ready 1 bunch green onion
  5. Make ready 1 pinch salt
  6. Get black pepper
Steps to make Korean Egg Roll:
  1. Chop all the ingredients(carrot, cucumber and green onion) into dice.
  2. In a large bowl, crack eggs and stir nicely.
  3. Mix the chopped ingredients into the bowl. Add salt and black pepper to taste.
  4. A pan is heated with strong flame. Then, switch to low flame.
  5. Pour the mixture nicely and spread the egg first. Then, take a spoon and scatter the ingredients on top.(just like rinse something and take the liquid out first)
  6. Swith to medium flame. Roll eggs till half catefully by using two spatulas.
  7. Pour the remaining mixture into the pan. Repeat the rolling step.
  8. Cool the egg role for a while before cut. Ready to serve. Eat best with soup.
