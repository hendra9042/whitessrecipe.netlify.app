Steps to make Healthy Banana Bread:
  1. Mash 3 bananas till it smooth
  2. Add all the ingredients and mix all together
  3. Pour the mixture into the tray. You can add chocolate chips or some slides of bananas on top. (It would be incredible)
  4. Bake for 25-30 mins at 350F (170C)
  5. Your bread is finished. Now, let cool it down for 15 mins
  6. Add some Nutella or Hazelnut spread for more sweet if you want to.
