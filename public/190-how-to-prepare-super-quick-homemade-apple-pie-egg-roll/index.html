The ingredients needed to make Apple pie egg roll:
  1. Prepare 2- 3 pieces White/brown bread-
  2. Get 2 Eggs
  3. Make ready 1 Apple
  4. Prepare 1/4 tsp Cinnamon
  5. Get 1/2 tsp Sugar
  6. Make ready as required Chocolate syrup
  7. Get as required Olives
  8. Get 2 tsp Milk

Instructions to make Apple pie egg roll:
  1. Cut the apple into small pieces..cook it on a pan with sugar a cinnamon powder.
  2. Cut the edges of the bread..roll it into thin.
  3. In another bowl add the egg,2tsp of milk and whisk it.
  4. Now put the apple mixture on bread, roll it.Seal the edges with water.
  5. Dip the roll into the egg mixture and pan fry it..
  6. Garnish with chocolate syrup and slieces of olives..enjoy
