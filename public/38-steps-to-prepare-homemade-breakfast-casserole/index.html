The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Get Ground Sausage
  2. Make ready Eggs
  3. Make ready Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  4. Take Milk
Instructions to make Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Brown ground sausage
  2. While sausage is browning, scramble the eggs with the milk and cheese in a large bowl
  3. Drain grease from sausage and pour into a baking dish and pour egg mixture over it and blend well
  4. Bake at 350 for 40 minutes
