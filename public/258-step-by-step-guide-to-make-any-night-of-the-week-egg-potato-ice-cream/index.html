The ingredients needed to make Egg Potato ice cream:
  1. Get 5-6 Potato
  2. Prepare 1 tsp Salt
  3. Get 1 tsp red chilli powder
  4. Get 1 tsp Dhania
  5. Prepare 1 tsp Black pepper
  6. Take Podina chutney as a required
  7. Prepare Ajvain as a required
  8. Prepare 3 Egg full boil
Steps to make Egg Potato ice cream:
  1. Aloo ko boil kar ke mash kar ly
  2. Then us ma sb spices add kar ke achy sy mix kar ly
  3. Then full boil egg ly r drmaian sy cut kar ly
  4. Then hand py oil lgae aloo wala material rakhy then us podina chutney rakhy then egg piece then stick rakhy then again podina chutney & again aloo wala material rakhy r usy cover kar ly set kar ly
  5. Then us ko kuch time ke lie fridge ma set hony ke lie rakh dy
  6. Then egg & crums ki double coating kar ke deep fry kry
