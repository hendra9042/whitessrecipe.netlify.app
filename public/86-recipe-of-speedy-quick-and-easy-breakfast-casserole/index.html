Instructions to make quick and easy Breakfast casserole:
  1. pre heat oven@ 350
  2. fry sausage
  3. mix eggs and milk,add bisquick and season all
  4. drain sausage and put as first later in cassarole dish
  5. pour egg mixture over sausage
  6. sprinkle cheese until covered
  7. sprinkle bacon pieces over cheese
  8. bake for 40 mins
  9. can be prepared the night before and put in fridge overnight for the morning
