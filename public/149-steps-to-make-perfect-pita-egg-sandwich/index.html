The ingredients needed to make Pita Egg Sandwich:
  1. Prepare Pepper jack cheese
  2. Prepare Egg
  3. Prepare Trader joes guacamole
  4. Prepare Trader joes pita bread whole wheat pocket
  5. Prepare Olive oil spray
  6. Get Salt
Instructions to make Pita Egg Sandwich:
  1. Spray pan with olive spray
  2. Cook egg on pan over easy
  3. Cut open pita pocket and insert slice of cheese
  4. Once egg is cooked (with egg yoke still runny), insert egg inside pita pocket
  5. Spray pan with olive oil spray again, throw dash of salt on pan.
  6. Cook each side of sandwich until each side is golden brown and the cheese is melted
  7. Take sandwich off pan and spread guacamole on top of sandwich
  8. All done!
