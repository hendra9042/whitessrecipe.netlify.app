The ingredients needed to make Hashbrown Casserole:
  1. Prepare 3 tbsp butter
  2. Prepare 1 small chopped onion
  3. Take 4 cup frozen shredded hashbrowns
  4. Make ready 1 lb sausage
  5. Prepare 2 1/4 cup milk
  6. Make ready 8 large eggs
  7. Take 1 tsp salt
  8. Prepare 1/4 tsp black pepper
  9. Get 1/4 tsp freshly grated nutmeg
  10. Prepare 2 tbsp dijon mustard
  11. Prepare 8 cup cubed french or italian bread, crusts removed
  12. Make ready 2 cup grated cheddar cheese
  13. Get 2 cup grated parmesan cheese
Instructions to make Hashbrown Casserole:
  1. preheat oven to 350
  2. spray 9x13 pan with cooking spray
  3. melt butter in a large pan
  4. saute onion over medium-low heat for 5 mins
  5. add hashbrowns and break apart
  6. saute until soft (about 5 mins)
  7. in a 2nd frying can saute sausage until cooked, drain
  8. in large bowl combine milk, eggs, salt, pepper, nutmeg and mustard
  9. spread highbrown mixture evenly on bottom of pan
  10. evenly add bread cubes
  11. distribute sausage over bread
  12. pour on egg mixture
  13. add parmesan cheese
  14. top with cheddar cheese
  15. bake uncovered for 45-50 mins until puffed and golden brown
