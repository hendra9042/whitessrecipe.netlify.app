Steps to make Savory pork and vegetable egg roll:
  1. Prepare the ingridient and chop the vegetable into smaller pieces
  2. I used food processor to mince them if you dont have any just use grater
  3. On a large bowl combine the vegestables and the ground pork together with minced pickles
  4. Season with salt, sugar and black pepper
  5. Wrap the filling with egg roll or spring roll wrapper
  6. Then fry it on medium to low heat until golden brown
  7. For the deeping sauce start with a tbsp of sugar on a small bowl, 2 tbsp of white vinegar, 1 tbsp of soy sauce, a touch of black pepper then add the minced or chop onion and chillies then give it a little mix
  8. Drain the excess oil, serve together with the sauce and enjoy you food ☺
