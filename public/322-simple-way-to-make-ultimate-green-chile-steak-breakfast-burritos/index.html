The ingredients needed to make Green Chile Steak Breakfast Burritos:
  1. Make ready large eggs
  2. Take chopped ham
  3. Make ready handfulls hashbrowns
  4. Make ready onion chopped
  5. Prepare green chile sauce (Hot)
  6. Take (chopped) new york strip steak
  7. Get Oil
Instructions to make Green Chile Steak Breakfast Burritos:
  1. Heat oil & Start with hash browns. About 5 minutes stir and add ham
  2. Add steak and move too the side. Cook eggs to firm texture
  3. Add green chlie to the pan mix together or stack in layers upon a flame heated tortilla. Enjoy
