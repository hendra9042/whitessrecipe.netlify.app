Instructions to make Eggs, the ones with Mustard Powder:
  1. Heat the pan with oil. Crack the eggs and add to a bowl. Add the spices. Beat the eggs till just frothy.
  2. Pour the egg mixture into the heated pan. Roll the egg as you raise a side of the pan. Letting the raw egg pour past the cooked bottom layer you just rolled up.
