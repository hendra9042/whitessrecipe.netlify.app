The ingredients needed to make Doggie Birthday Cake:
  1. Make ready cake
  2. Take 1 cup whole wheat flour (can substitute white flour)
  3. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
  4. Take 1 egg
  5. Take 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  6. Get 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
  7. Make ready 1/3 cup honey
  8. Get 1 cup shredded carrots
  9. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
  10. Prepare frosting
  11. Take 4 oz cream cheese
  12. Make ready 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
  13. Prepare 2 splash milk
Steps to make Doggie Birthday Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 ° F
  2. Combine all cake ingredients in large bowl, mix well.
  3. Place in a greased 8 inch round cake pan. I doubled the recipe and used a 9 inch cake pan
  4. Bake for 30 minutes, or until toothpick comes out clean. mine was done in 32 minutes
  5. Move to wire rack to cool.
  6. Wait until cake is cool to make frosting! Beat the frosting ingredients until well blended and smooth.
  7. Frost cake and garnish with dog kibble if you'd like! Keep refrigerated.
