Instructions to make Pancakes:
  1. Add in bowl 1 all the powders together (sugar, baking powder, flour and salt) and mix them well
  2. In bowel 2 add the egg, oil, milk and vanilla and mix them
  3. Add contents of bowel 2 to bowel 1 and mix them
  4. Put a pan with 2 drops of oil on the stove for 1 minute to heat up then add 1 scoop from the mixture to the pan and cook for 3-4 minutes until the edges are dry and there are bubbles at the top
  5. Flip the pancake and leave them for 1 minute then remove it and do the same for the rest of the mixture
