Instructions to make Egg roll:
  1. Mix water and salt with semolina and flour till it becomes a medium hard dough and cover the dough for rest till 15 Minutes
  2. Break the eggs and mix it well with black pepper & Salt.
  3. Take some finely chopped onions and cumbers. Now take some part of the dough and roll it with a rolling pin and put it in the frying pan for frying
  4. Now make an omlete then join the omlete with the previous fried chappati and fry them once more
  5. Now put tomato sauce, chopped onions and cucumbers. Now roll it and close it with a tissue paper and our egg roll is ready to eat
