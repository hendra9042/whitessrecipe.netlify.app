The ingredients needed to make One Pot Smoked Sausage and Noodles:
  1. Get smoked sausage (14 ounce)
  2. Take Olive oil
  3. Get onion, sliced
  4. Make ready green pepper, sliced
  5. Make ready garlic, minced
  6. Prepare black pepper
  7. Get oregano
  8. Make ready parsley flakes
  9. Make ready Sazon (sin achiote)
  10. Make ready crushed red pepper
  11. Get low sodium beef broth
  12. Make ready wide egg noodles
  13. Make ready cream of mushroom soup
  14. Get Rotel diced tomatoes & green Chilies
Instructions to make One Pot Smoked Sausage and Noodles:
  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot……
  2. Sauté your sliced onion and green pepper in olive oil until tender, add in your minced garlic…….
  3. Slice your smoked sausage into coins, sauté with your veggies until sausages are slightly browned……
  4. Add in your black pepper, oregano, parsley flakes, Sazon and crushed red pepper, sauté for several minutes……
  5. Add into pot your beef broth and egg noodles, cover and boil for about 5 minutes or until noodles are slightly tender……stir gently……
  6. Next add in your cream of mushroom soup and Rotel tomatoes, cover and cook on low heat until noodles are tender, about 10 minutes or less, stirring occasionally…..
  7. Serve and enjoy 😉!
