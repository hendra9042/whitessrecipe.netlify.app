The ingredients needed to make Hacked Corn Bread (Gluten Free/Low Fat Carb) (1.0 Release):
  1. Take 1 scoop protein whey isolate (100 cals)
  2. Make ready 2 can (15 ounce) Baby Corn (Chinese Takeout variety) (150 cals)
  3. Get 1 egg whites (25 cals)
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup sweetener
  5. Get 2 teaspoons baking powder
  6. Take 5 TBSP Resistant Wheat Starch (or your favorite fiber) (25 Calories?)
  7. Get Total calories (~300 for pan)
Steps to make Hacked Corn Bread (Gluten Free/Low Fat Carb) (1.0 Release):
  1. Pre heat stove 425
  2. Blend / Puree baby corn and egg whites in food processor until smooth
  3. Add dry ingredients
  4. Spray a eight inch square glass container and pour in the batter.
  5. Bake for 40 minutes or until toothpick test comes out clean.
