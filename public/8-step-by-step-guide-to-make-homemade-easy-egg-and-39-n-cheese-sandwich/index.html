The ingredients needed to make Easy Egg 'n Cheese Sandwich:
  1. Prepare 2 Slice bread
  2. Make ready 1-2 eggs…. depends on your cholesterol intake ✌ 😀
  3. Take 2 slice cheese… depends how you like cheese 😀.
  4. Make ready Salt to taste
  5. Get Oil… little only
Instructions to make Easy Egg 'n Cheese Sandwich:
  1. Pre-heat pan add oil. Beat egg with little salt.. if you want can add pepper
  2. Dip the bread on egg mixture, one at a time.
  3. Fry bread on pre-heated oil, flip over then add slice cheese.
  4. Fold the slice bread with cheese together.
  5. Add remaining egg on the side of the bread. Keep turning bread until cook.
