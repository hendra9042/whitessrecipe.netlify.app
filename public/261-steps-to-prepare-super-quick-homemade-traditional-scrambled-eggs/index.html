Steps to make traditional scrambled eggs:
  1. Heat a griddle or pan to medium heat.
  2. Whisk together eggs and milk in a small container (I use a mug)
  3. Melt a tiny bit of butter on the pan
  4. Before the butter burns pour egg and milk mixture on heat
  5. Use a spatula to push the outsides in
  6. Go until it is nota liquid then flip (it is not quite of a solid)
  7. Wait until fully cooked then remove from heat and enjoy!♥
