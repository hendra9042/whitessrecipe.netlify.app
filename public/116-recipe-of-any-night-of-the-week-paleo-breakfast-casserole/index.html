The ingredients needed to make Paleo Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Take sausage
  2. Get spinach (other veggies welcome)
  3. Get large potatoes
  4. Get Avocado oil (or substitute oil)
  5. Take eggs
  6. Make ready coconut milk
  7. Get nutritional yeast
Instructions to make Paleo Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Set oven to 375 F
  2. Saute sausage, place in large bowl
  3. In same pan saute spinach and veggies, place in bowl
  4. In same pan saute diced/chopped potatoes, place in bowl
  5. Mix up bowl and place in cooking pan/tray
  6. In new bowl, whisk eggs, coconut milk, and nutritional yeast
  7. Pour egg mixture over item in cooking pan/tray and bake on 375 til cooked through middle (~1.5 hours)
