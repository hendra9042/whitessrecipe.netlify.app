The ingredients needed to make Egg roll:
  1. Make ready As needed Maida (all-purpose flour)1-1/2cup
  2. Take 4 Eggs
  3. Take 1 Onion chopped
  4. Take 1 Cucumber sliced
  5. Prepare 1 Carrot grated

  9. Take 1 tsp Black pepper powder
  10. Take 1 tsp Chat masala
  11. Prepare Salt as per taste
  12. Prepare 2 tbsp Oil

Instructions to make Egg roll:
  1. Take Maida in a mixing bowl. Add salt, little oil. Mix well. (OR you can take half each of Maida and atta.)
  2. Add water little by little and knead a soft dough. - Cover for 10mins.
  3. Divide the dough into equal parts. - Roll medium thickness chapati.
  4. Cook the chapati with little oil. - Make all the chapatis and keep aside.
  5. Beat one egg with a pinch of salt.
  6. Heat oil in a tawa, pour the egg mixture. - Cook for 1/2min undisturbed.
  7. Place one chapati over it. - After a min flip it. - Cook few secs and take it out.
  8. Let it cool a bit.
  9. Over the egg side Spread 1/2tsp each of sauce and mayonnaise.
  10. Place carrot, onion, green chilli and cucumber.
  11. Sprinkle salt, chat masala and black pepper powder. - Roll carefully.
  12. If you want roll butter paper over it.
  13. Repeat the same for all chapatis. - Serve immediately.
