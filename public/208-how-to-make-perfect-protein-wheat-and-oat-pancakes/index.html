Steps to make protein wheat and oat pancakes:
  1. add oatmeal and milk in bowl, let oatmeal soak for a few minutes
  2. add everything else
  3. stir :-)
  4. let mixture sit for a few minutes. if mixture is a little too thin more protein powder or wheat flower can be added. don't add too much though, should be somewhat thin
  5. on preheated pan ( medium heat) use non stick spray. pour 1/4 to 1/3 of mixture into pan
  6. let sit for a few minutes….
  7. flip
  8. let sit for a minutes
  9. done
  10. eat and enjoy.
