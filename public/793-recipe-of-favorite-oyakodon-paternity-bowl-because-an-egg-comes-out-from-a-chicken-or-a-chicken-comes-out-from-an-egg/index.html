The ingredients needed to make Oyakodon! (Paternity Bowl because an egg comes out from a chicken, or a chicken comes out from an egg):
  1. Prepare water
  2. Prepare soy sauce
  3. Make ready sugar
  4. Take Some salt if you want
  5. Make ready of an onion
  6. Make ready chicken (I used it, but you can use any chicken)
  7. Make ready eggs
  8. Prepare rice
Steps to make Oyakodon! (Paternity Bowl because an egg comes out from a chicken, or a chicken comes out from an egg):
  1. Cook rice first! It takes approximately 30 minutes.
  2. Boil water and soy sauce with a pan and put sugar in the pan.
  3. Put sliced onion and chicken in the pan and boil them with middle fire.
  4. After the onion and chicken are boiled well, put egg into the pan.
  5. Before egg becomes hard boiled, put the content of the pan on the top of rice in a bowl!
