The ingredients needed to make Curried Sweet Potato with Eggs:
  1. Prepare eggs
  2. Make ready peeled, cubed sweet potato
  3. Make ready onion, sliced thinly
  4. Get curry powder (I used a Japanese brand)
  5. Get Crushed black pepper
  6. Get salt
  7. Prepare oil
Steps to make Curried Sweet Potato with Eggs:
  1. Heat the oil in the pan. I used medium heat.
  2. Add the sweet potato. Stir from time to time until they are cooked. (you should be able to crush it easily with a spatula)
  3. Add the onion. Lower the heat.
  4. Add curry powder and salt. Stir until the onion becomes translucent.
  5. Make a well in the center. Break the eggs there. You can also use one egg, but I think two eggs work better in this recipe.
  6. Top with crushed black pepper. Cook to your preference. I like my yolks soft, so it probably took about 10 minutes on low heat. You might want to wait for five more minutes if you like firmer eggs.
