The ingredients needed to make Fluffy Nagaimo Yam and Tofu Gratin:
  1. Take Nagaimo yam
  2. Make ready Silken tofu
  3. Get Egg
  4. Make ready Cheese that melts
  5. Make ready Mentsuyu
Steps to make Fluffy Nagaimo Yam and Tofu Gratin:
  1. Grate the yam.
  2. Wrap the tofu with a paper towel and microwave for 1 minute to remove the moisture.
  3. Combine the grated yam with the tofu from Step 2 and mash. Mix in the beaten egg. Flavor with mentsuyu.
  4. Top with cheese and keep your eye on it as it bakes for 20 minutes in a 200°C oven.
