The ingredients needed to make Fancy Pancake:
  1. Prepare plain flour
  2. Get baking powder
  3. Make ready baking soda
  4. Make ready sugar
  5. Prepare salt
  6. Take butter milk (or 125 ml milk+1 tbsp lemon juice)
  7. Take egg
  8. Prepare melted butter
Steps to make Fancy Pancake:
  1. Combine all dry ingredients
  2. Beat egg, add to butter milk and melted butter (wet ingredients). So, you have dry and wet ingredients
  3. Pour wet ingredient to dry ingredient, mix well. Dont over mix.
  4. Heat a pan, pour a scoop butter to hot pan. Just turn the pancake if bubbles appear. Serve and enjoy it. Decorate your pancake with whatever you want so it looks fancy.
