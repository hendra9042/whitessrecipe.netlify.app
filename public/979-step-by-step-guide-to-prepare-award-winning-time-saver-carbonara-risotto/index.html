The ingredients needed to make Time-Saver Carbonara Risotto:
  1. Make ready rice bowl's worth Hot cooked white rice
  2. Get Bacon (ham or seafood is fine)
  3. Prepare Milk (or heavy cream)
  4. Make ready Egg
  5. Make ready Consommé cube
  6. Prepare Grated cheese
  7. Take Black pepper
Instructions to make Time-Saver Carbonara Risotto:
  1. Cook the bacon on medium heat until aromatic and golden brown.
  2. Add the milk and consommé and cook on low. Mix as the consommé dissolves.
  3. Once it starts bubbling, add the grated cheese and rice.
  4. Simmer on low heat for 3 minutes, and check the flavor. Add salt if you don't think it's salty enough. (the salt from the bacon and consommé is enough, so I omit it).
  5. Pour in the egg, and cook for 30 seconds on low heat while mixing, then stop the heat.
  6. Transfer to a serving plate, top with black pepper if you like, and it's complete.
