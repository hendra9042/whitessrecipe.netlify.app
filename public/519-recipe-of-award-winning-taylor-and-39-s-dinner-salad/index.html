The ingredients needed to make Taylor's Dinner Salad:
  1. Get salad
  2. Make ready 1 cup of bibb lettuce
  3. Make ready 1 cup of red lettuce
  4. Make ready 1 cup of baby greens
  5. Get 1 large baked potato, russet. You may substitute yukon gold or red.
  6. Prepare 1 can sliced pickled beets, drained. 14 .5 ounce size
  7. Prepare 1/2 medium red onion, cut in half then thinly sliced
  8. Take 1 1/2 cup cooked chicken breast, cubed
  9. Take 2 hard boiled eggs, peeled and sliced
  10. Take 1 salt and pepper to taste
  11. Make ready 3 tbsp vinaigrette, your choosing to toss with the greens
  12. Prepare 1/4 cup croutons, your favorite
Steps to make Taylor's Dinner Salad:
  1. Place cleaned and dried greens in large mixing bowl. Greens should be torn into bite sized pieces.
  2. Add vinaigrette and toss.
  3. On a large platter arrange greens first covering platter.
  4. Take eggs and spread neatly in a row at top of platter.
  5. On opposite side, place sliced beets across in same manner.
  6. Next to eggs spread onions across platter.
  7. Next to beets, spread cooked chicken across platter.
  8. Top middle row with sliced potatoes. Sprinkle a touch of salt and pepper over the entire platter.
  9. Top each serving with croutons.
