The ingredients needed to make Fluffy Scrambled Eggs:
  1. Take grade A large chicken eggs
  2. Get unsalted butter
  3. Get whole milk
Instructions to make Fluffy Scrambled Eggs:
  1. Put all of the ingredients into a bowl and mix for 40-50 seconds.
  2. Put a medium saucepan on the stove and allow it to heat up a little
  3. Pour the mixture into the saucepan and stir. NEVER STOP STIRRING!! You will need to take the saucepan off the fire and put it back on 2-3 times. When the eggs are almost done, take the saucepan off of the fire and still mix until the eggs are completely cooked.
