The ingredients needed to make Cake For 2 | Homemade Chocolate Chip Cookie Ice Cream Cake | Egg Less| No Oven:
  1. Prepare 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream (C0ld)
  2. Get 1/2 cup mascarpone cheese (at room temperature)
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp sugar
  4. Take 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  5. Get 1 tsp vanilla essence
  6. Get As required Chocolate chip cookies
  7. Make ready As required Chocolate shavings
  8. Make ready As needed Sprinkles
Instructions to make Cake For 2 | Homemade Chocolate Chip Cookie Ice Cream Cake | Egg Less| No Oven:
  1. In a large bowl add whipping cream, sugar, cocoa powder and vanilla.
  2. Beat for 2 minutes or until soft peaks form.
  3. Add mascarpone cheese, continue to whip until stiff peaks forms.
  4. Cover and refrigerate meanwhile place the cookies into the spring-form pan (bottom layer).
  5. Top cookie with cream and spread into an even layer (2nd layer).
  6. Again place the cookies (3rd layer)
  7. Top cookie with cream and spread into an even layer (4th layer).
  8. Finally, sprinkle chocolate shavings and sprinkles.
  9. Refrigerate for 2-3 hours or overnight. - Note: Refrigerate overnight for better taste and add sugar according to your liking.
