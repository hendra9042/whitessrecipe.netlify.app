The ingredients needed to make Quick Delicious Soba Noodles with Soft-Poached Eggs:
  1. Prepare 2 servings Soba noodles (dried or boiled)
  2. Take 2 Soft poached eggs
  3. Make ready 1 Japanese leek
  4. Make ready 2 servings Mentsuyu
  5. Get 1 Shichimi spice
Instructions to make Quick Delicious Soba Noodles with Soft-Poached Eggs:
  1. Use storebought hot spring eggs (onsen eggs), or make them using other COOKPAD recipes. It's quick and convenient to make soft set poached eggs in the microwave. Heat up some mentsuyu (noodle dipping sauce) seasoned as you like. If you're using dried noodles, boil them first （don't over-boil.）
  2. Put the cooked soba noodles into a noodle bowl with hot Japanese noodle dipping sauce. Place the hot spring egg on top, add finely chopped leek and it's done! Season with shichimi spice to taste.
