The ingredients needed to make Southwest Egg Rolls:
  1. Prepare egg rolls
  2. Prepare chopped Roasted Red Bell Peppers (in a jar)
  3. Prepare chopped green onions
  4. Get corn
  5. Get black beans (rinsed and drained)
  6. Prepare frozen spinach (thawed and squeeze well to drain)
  7. Get diced pickled jalapeño pepper slices
  8. Take chopped fresh flat leaf parsley
  9. Prepare chopped fresh cilantro
  10. Prepare ground cumin
  11. Take chili powder
  12. Make ready garlic salt
  13. Get cayenne pepper
  14. Make ready pepper jack cheese
  15. Prepare egg roll wrappers
  16. Make ready vegetable oil for frying
  17. Get Avocado Dip Sauce
  18. Prepare avocados
  19. Prepare sour cream
  20. Make ready Real Mayonnaise
  21. Take apple cider vinegar
  22. Make ready kosher salt
  23. Make ready finely chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley
  24. Get onion powder
  25. Get chopped fresh dill
  26. Make ready garlic powder
  27. Get buttermilk
Steps to make Southwest Egg Rolls:
  1. To a bowl add all ingredients except for wrappers and vegetable oil. Mix well
  2. Take 2 heaping tablespoons of mixture and place on an egg roll wrapper.
  3. Wrap up "burrito" style. Before closing burrito dab water on the inside of the end flap to seal.
  4. Heat oil in pan (approximately 1/2" in bottom) to 350°
  5. Fry egg rolls on one side until brown then turn over and cook other side until brown. I prefer to do in batches of two so the oil stays hot and doesn't cool down.
  6. Drain on paper towel and serve with Avocado Dip.
  7. For Avocado Dip Sauce: In a small bowl smash avocados with a fork then mix in the rest of ingredients.
  8. Serve as an appetizer or with a meal Enjoy!
