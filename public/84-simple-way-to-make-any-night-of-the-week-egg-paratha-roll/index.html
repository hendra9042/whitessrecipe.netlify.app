The ingredients needed to make Egg paratha roll:
  1. Get For parhata
  2. Prepare 2 cups Flour
  3. Get Salt to taste
  4. Take Water as required
  5. Take 2 tbsp Ghee
  6. Prepare For egg
  7. Get 2 Eggs
  8. Make ready Salt to taste
  9. Make ready 2 tsp Red chilli flakes
  10. Get 2 Green chillies chopped
  11. Make ready Oil

Steps to make Egg paratha roll:
  1. Mix the salt, ghee,water in flour and knead the flour. And make a soft dough. Cover the dough for 5 minutes.
  2. Heat the fry pan.
  3. Now take a roller pin give a shape now add oil and roll out again and fry it until golden brown.
  4. For egg: in eggs add all the spices beat well and fry the egg.
  5. In paratha add ketchup and fry egg make a roll and serve.
