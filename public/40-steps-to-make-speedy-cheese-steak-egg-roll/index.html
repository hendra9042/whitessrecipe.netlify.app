The ingredients needed to make Cheese steak egg roll:
  1. Get 1 small onion cut into slivers
  2. Make ready 2 lb beef steak of choice..sliced thin
  3. Prepare 2 cup provolone cheese
  4. Make ready 4 white american cheese slices
  5. Take 1 packages egg roll wrapper
  6. Prepare 2 cup mushroom
  7. Prepare 1 1/2 cup slivered peppers
  8. Prepare 1 salt pepper garlic to taste

Steps to make Cheese steak egg roll:
  1. Heat onions, peppers,mushroom salt, pepper and garlic in a bit of oil over medium/high heat .stir frequently…cook until tender
  2. Add steak and brown
  3. Once cooked lower heat and add cheeses cover ..let cheese melt stir occasionally…turn off heat and let stand for a while to cool
  4. Once cool..follow the directions on package of egg rolls to stuff fold and roll egg rolls.
  5. Fry in oil until desired color of shell is achieved.(3 to 4 minutes)
