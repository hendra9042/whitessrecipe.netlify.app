The ingredients needed to make My Mom's Fried Egg Sandwich:
  1. Prepare 4 large eggs
  2. Get 8 slices bread
  3. Prepare 8 tablespoon mayonnaise
  4. Take 1/3 pound Bacon
  5. Get To taste salt
Instructions to make My Mom's Fried Egg Sandwich:
  1. Fry the bacon crisp
  2. Add the mayonnaise to the bread and add the bacon crumbled upto cover all the bread
  3. Fry the egg crispy.
  4. Put together the sandwich and serve
  5. I hope you enjoy!
