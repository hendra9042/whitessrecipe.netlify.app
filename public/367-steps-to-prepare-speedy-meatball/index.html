The ingredients needed to make Meatball:
  1. Prepare 11/2 lbs extra lean ground beef
  2. Take 3/4 cup Italian seasoned breadcrumbs
  3. Take 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  4. Get 1 tablespoon Italian spices
  5. Prepare 1/2 tablespoon garlic powder
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup egg substitute

Instructions to make Meatball:
  1. Mix all ingredients together and form 1½ inch ball. Put in top of steamer basket.
  2. Put ¼ cup water in bottom of steamer, cover and cook 5 to 8 mins on highe or until cook through.
  3. Rinse under hot water for 5 minutes
  4. Use in normal way you would use meatball
  5. To freeze, flash freeze meatball and the place in a freezer bag use as many as or few as needed.
  6. Makes about 3 meatball per serving
