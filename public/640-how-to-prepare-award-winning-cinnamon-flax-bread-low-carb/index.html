The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon Flax Bread (Low Carb):
  1. Get 3 eggs
  2. Make ready 1/4 cup plus two tablespoons oil (I used canola)
  3. Take 2 tbsp cinnamon
  4. Take 1/4 cup sugar free caramel syrup (Torani)
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp water
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp vanilla flavoring
  7. Make ready 1 cup ground flax meal
  8. Take 1/2 tsp baking soda
  9. Get 1/2 tsp baking powder
Instructions to make Cinnamon Flax Bread (Low Carb):
  1. Preheat oven to 350°
  2. Mix all ingredients together
  3. Pour mixture into a greased 8x8.dish
  4. Bake fifteen to twenty minutes or until desired texture
