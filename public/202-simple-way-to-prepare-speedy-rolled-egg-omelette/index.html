Steps to make Rolled-egg omelette:
  1. Here are the ingredients. Chop green pepper, red pepper and Onion. Break eggs in a bowl.
  2. Add chopped green pepper,red pepper, onions and seasonings then whisk and set aside.
  3. Get a pan,place on fire and add little oil,pour egg mixture (not all).lift up your egg(with a spatula) and fold it in,keep folding until it's almost at the tip.
  4. Shift folded egg to the side of the pan and pour another mixture of the egg,fold in egg platter till it's almost at the tip.keep pouring and folding till the last fold. Drop in a plate and serve.
  5. Enjoy!
