The ingredients needed to make Rhubarb custard pie:
  1. Get 6 cup rhubarb cut into 1" pieces
  2. Get 2 eggs
  3. Get 1 3/4 cup White sugar
  4. Take 4 tbsp flour
  5. Take 1 pinch salt
  6. Get 2 tsp vanilla

Instructions to make Rhubarb custard pie:
  1. Pour boiling water over cut rhubarb and let sit for 2 minutes.
  2. Drain rhubarb.
  3. Beat eggs.
  4. Combine flour, sugar and salt.
  5. Add to beaten eggs.
  6. Add vanillla.
  7. Fold into rhubarb.
  8. Make double pie crust.
  9. Roll out and place bottom crust.
  10. Add filling.
  11. Roll out and place top crust.
  12. Bake at 400° for 45 minutes.
