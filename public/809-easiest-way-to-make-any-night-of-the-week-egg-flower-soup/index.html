The ingredients needed to make Egg Flower Soup:
  1. Prepare Chicken stock (I prefer fresh stock but had none. So I used 6 cups water and 3 tsp chicken bouillon)
  2. Get finely diced carrot
  3. Make ready thin sliced green onions
  4. Prepare to 3 beaten eggs (preference)
  5. Take diced tofu (optional)
  6. Make ready cornstarch mixed with 2 tsp cold water (to thicken - Optional)
  7. Take sesame oil (optional)
  8. Take ground ginger or fresh (optional)
  9. Make ready soy sauce (optional condiment)
  10. Prepare hot chili pepper oil (optional condiment)
Instructions to make Egg Flower Soup:
  1. Bring stock to a boil.
  2. Add carrot, green onions, and other desired optional items at this time. Let boil for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Pour in eggs and stir to break the egg up. (If you want more string-like eggs, pour the eggs in slowly while stirring in a circle).
  4. Stir for 1 minute and remove from heat.
  5. Enjoy!
