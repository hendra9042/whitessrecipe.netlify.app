The ingredients needed to make Simple Egg Custard:
  1. Get 8 large eggs
  2. Prepare 1 can evaporated milk (I use pet milk brand)
  3. Take 1 cup sugar
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp vanilla extract (you can use less if you don't like flavor)
  5. Make ready 2 large frozen pie crust
  6. Make ready 1 nutmeg (optional)
Instructions to make Simple Egg Custard:
  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°
  2. Combine all ingredients in bowl with whisk.
  3. Pour mix into pie crust.
  4. Sprinkle top with nutneg. (optional)
  5. Cook in oven 30 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.
