The ingredients needed to make Crepe Egg Roll:
  1. Get For the crepe
  2. Prepare 1 cup flour
  3. Make ready 1 cup milk
  4. Prepare 1/8 cup water
  5. Prepare 1 egg
  6. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon salt
  7. Take 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  8. Take For the fillings
  9. Get 2 eggs
  10. Get Chopped tomatoes
  11. Prepare Chopped scotch bonnet
  12. Get Chopped onions
  13. Prepare to taste Salt
  14. Get Pinch seasoning cubes
  15. Prepare Thyme
  16. Get tumeric
  17. Take Veg oil for frying
Instructions to make Crepe Egg Roll:
  1. Stir fry ur chopped onions, pepper, tomatoes and ur seasonings in 2 tablespoons oil and set aside. This is totally optional, I just wanted it to be a little soft before adding to the eggs.
  2. In a bowl, add 1 egg, salt, milk and mix.I dissolved 1 peak milk sachet(about 3 tablespoons) into 1 cup of water.
  3. Then add the flour, baking powder and water. Combine together until it is lump free and set aside
  4. Combine 2 eggs and the pepper mix in a bowl and set aside as well. U don't need to add anymore seasoning if ur pepper mix is well seasoned.
  5. Heat up a well greased frying pan on a low flame. Using ‚Öì cup of measuring cup or a spoon, add the batter into the pan and tilt to circulate evenly. When it is almost cooked, spread the egg on it and cover to cook for 1-2 mins. U can also spread some cheese if u have.This should be on a low flame so it won't burn before it's done.
  6. When the egg is done, flip and cook for a minute. U can flip 2 times to ensure it is well cooked. Take off pan and roll while it is still hot.
  7. Continue with the process until the batter is finished. I got 4 crepe egg rolls which is perfect for 2 people. When done, I arrange all into the hot frying pan and covered it until i was done with the coffee. This is off heat and to enable it stay hot. Ur Crepe Egg Rolls are ready.
