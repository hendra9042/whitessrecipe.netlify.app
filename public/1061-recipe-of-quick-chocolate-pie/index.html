The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Pie:
  1. Get sugar
  2. Prepare unsweetened cocoa powder
  3. Prepare all-purpose flour
  4. Make ready can evaporated milk
  5. Prepare vanilla
  6. Make ready egg yolks
  7. Make ready butter
  8. Take single pie crust
  9. Prepare Meringue
  10. Take egg whites
  11. Take sugar
  12. Make ready Optional 1/4 tsp cream of tartar
Steps to make Chocolate Pie:
  1. Preheat oven 350 degrees. Place all ingredients for pie in small bowls and set aside. Whisk egg yolks in small bowl and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan whisk together 2 cup sugar cocoa and flour. Blend in milk, vanilla and egg yolks. Turn stove to medium heat add butter and stirring constantly just until butter is melted. Pour into unbaked pie shell.
  3. Bake for 35 to 40 minutes or until pie is not wobbly when shaken.
  4. Beat egg whites until soft peaks form. Gradually add sugar beating constantly, until stiff peaks form. Spread meringue on pie and return to oven until meringue is golden. Enjoy!
