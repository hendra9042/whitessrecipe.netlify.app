The ingredients needed to make Died and Went 2 Heaven Chocolate Cake:
  1. Take 3 cup flour
  2. Get 1/2 cup splenda no calorie sweetner, granulated
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup splenda brown sugar blend
  4. Prepare 3/4 cup unsweeted cocoa powder
  5. Get 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  6. Get 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  7. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  8. Prepare 1 1/4 cup milk
  9. Get 1/4 cup oil
  10. Prepare 2 large eggs, lightly beaten
  11. Prepare 2 tsp vanilla extract
  12. Get 1 cup hot strong coffee
Instructions to make Died and Went 2 Heaven Chocolate Cake:
  1. preheat oven to 350Â°F. grease cookie sheet.
  2. blend flour, splenda granulated sweetner, splenda brown sugar blend, baking powder, baking soda, cocoa powder, and. salt in a large mixing bowl.
  3. combine milk, oil, eggs, vanilla, and coffee in a small bowl.
  4. add flour mixture, using and electric mixer on medium speed, mix until smooth (about 2 minutes).
  5. place tablespoon amounts on cookie sheet (about a dozen).
  6. bake 10-15 minutes, until an inserted toothpick in center of cakes come out clean. let cool in pan for 5 minutes.
  7. filling: sugar free frosting (any flavor you want) and sugar free cool whip. mix frosting and cool whip 2gether and fill cakes.
  8. top with cool whip and berries.
