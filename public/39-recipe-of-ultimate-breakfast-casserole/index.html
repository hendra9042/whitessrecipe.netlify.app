Steps to make Breakfast casserole:
  1. Cook sausage in a large pan
  2. Season with flat iron prime rib rub
  3. Add chopped onion when sausage is almost done
  4. Add mushrooms
  5. Season with garlic sprinkle.
  6. Remove sausage mix. leave the grease in the pan.
  7. Add chopped potatoes to the pan grease.
  8. Add Italian dressing blend
  9. Add pasta sprinkle
  10. Add minced garlic when potatoes are halfway done.
  11. Cook for a few more minutes
  12. Place potato mixture to a 8x8 inch baking dish.
  13. Mix sausage mixture in with the potatoes.
  14. Can store in the fridge until morning
  15. Preheat oven to 350
  16. Beat 9-12 eggs and add to the potatoes mix
  17. You can mix it or let the egg run into the empty spaces
  18. Place in the oven for 40 minutes. no need to cover the dish.
  19. Top with shredded cheese. bake 10 more minutes
