The ingredients needed to make Elisa's Simple Tomato, Cheese and Ham Omelette:
  1. Prepare 2 Eggs
  2. Take 1 slice Ham, diced (Can be substituted with the meat of your preference)
  3. Prepare 1 tsp Butter
  4. Take 1 Cheese (Your preference)
  5. Take 1 bunch Cherry Tomatoes, cut in half
  6. Take 1 Salt & Pepper
Instructions to make Elisa's Simple Tomato, Cheese and Ham Omelette:
  1. On a pan at medium heat, melt butter
  2. Beat eggs on a bowl and add salt and pepper
  3. Mix the ham with the eggs and put the on the pan with the butter
  4. Let them cook and turn.
  5. Add the tomatoes and the cheese on the top of the eggs and close (I used two slices of American Cheese)
  6. Let the cheese melt
  7. Serve and enjoy!
