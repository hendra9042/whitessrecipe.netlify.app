The ingredients needed to make Coconut Box Cake:
  1. Get 7 oz shredded coconut bags (2)
  2. Make ready 6 cup milk
  3. Get 3 box Jello Coconut Instant Pudding (Sugar free)
  4. Get 1/2 cup egg nog
  5. Make ready 1 1/2 box honey gramham crackers
Instructions to make Coconut Box Cake:
  1. Pudding, milk, egg nog and 2 handfulls of coconut in bowl. Beat with mixer on low speed until smooth.
  2. In plastic or tin pan, line bottom with gramham crackers, then pudding and then coconut. Repeat and then top with coconut and crushed gramhams. Fridge overnight.
