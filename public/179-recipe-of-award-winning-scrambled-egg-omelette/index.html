The ingredients needed to make Scrambled Egg Omelette:
  1. Prepare potato
  2. Get eggs
  3. Take turkey bacon
  4. Make ready Kraft cheese
Steps to make Scrambled Egg Omelette:
  1. Dice cut potato and fry in pan with a little oil or butter.
  2. Cook the 4 slices of turkey bacon &then rip into bite sized pieces
  3. Crack 4 eggs into a bowl with some salt&pepper&whip eggs until mixture is all yellow.
  4. Cut cheese slice into a bunch of small pieces & add them in with the whipped eggs.
  5. Put potatoes &bacon in same pan &pour the egg mixture in the pan &cook all together until eggs are scrambled
  6. Serve
