The ingredients needed to make Crepe egg roll:
  1. Make ready For batter
  2. Get 1 egg
  3. Take 1/4 tsp salt
  4. Prepare 1 cup milk
  5. Prepare 3/4 cup flour
  6. Make ready 1/4 tsp baking powder
  7. Take For egg mixture
  8. Take 2 eggs
  9. Take 1/4 tsp salt
  10. Make ready 1/4 tsp red chili powder
  11. Get 1/4 cup oninon(chopped)
  12. Get 1/2 cup tomatoes (chopped)
  13. Make ready 2 green chili (chopped)
  14. Make ready 1 tbsp fresh Coriander (chopped)
  15. Prepare 1 tbsp ghee/butter
  16. Get 2 tbsp mozzarella cheese(optional)
Instructions to make Crepe egg roll:
  1. Sb se pehle batter bnane ke lye 1 bowl me 1 egg, salt & milk daal kar achi trhan mix krlen then us me maida or baking powder add karein or achi trhan beat krlen itna ke koi lumps na rahein
  2. Ab egg mixture bnane ke lye 1 bowl me 2 eggs, salt,red chili powder,onion,tomato,green chili, Coriander add karein or sb ko achi trhan mix krlen
  3. Ab ek pan me butter/ghee garam kren or us me egg or maide ka batter daal kar low flame p cook karein
  4. Jab batter cook hone lage to uske upar egg mixture b daal dein or low flame p cook hone dein
  5. Jab achi trhan 1 side cook hojae to dusri side turn karein and cook krlen
  6. Dusri side cook hone ke bad usko dobara turn karein then is stage p ap upar se mozzarella cheese daal saktein hen (mene ni daali ye optional hai) or cheese dalne ke bad pan ko cover kar ke low flame p 2 min cook krlen
  7. Jab cheese achi trhan melt hojae to 1 plate me usko fold kar ke roll bana lein or cut kar ke kisi b man pasand sauce ke sath serve karein
  8. Yummy crepe roll is ready
