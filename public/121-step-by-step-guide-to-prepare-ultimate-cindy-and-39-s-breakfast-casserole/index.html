The ingredients needed to make Cindy's Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Prepare ground sausage
  2. Get eggs
  3. Make ready Milk
  4. Prepare salt
  5. Make ready Ground Mustard
  6. Make ready White Bread (cut crust - 1/2" cubes)
  7. Take shredded cheddar cheese
Steps to make Cindy's Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Beat eggs; add milk, salt, mustard. Stir in bread cubes, cheese and sausage.
  2. Pour into greased 11x7x2 pan. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
  3. Bake uncovered @ 350 for 40 minutes
