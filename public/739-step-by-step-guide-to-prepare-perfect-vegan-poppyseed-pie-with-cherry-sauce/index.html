The ingredients needed to make Vegan Poppyseed Pie with Cherry Sauce:
  1. Prepare For the Pie:
  2. Get 3 flax eggs (3 tbsp flax seeds + 7.5 tbsp water)
  3. Prepare 200 g coconut sugar
  4. Make ready 200 ml plant-based milk
  5. Prepare 50 g plant-based butter
  6. Take 200 g flour
  7. Take 200 g poppyseeds
  8. Get 12 g baking powder
  9. Prepare 1 lemon zest
  10. Make ready For the Sauce:
  11. Make ready 300 g frozen cherries
  12. Prepare 100 g coconut sugar
  13. Take 1-2 tbsp cognac
  14. Prepare 1 lemon juice
  15. Make ready 1 tbsp corn flour

Steps to make Vegan Poppyseed Pie with Cherry Sauce:
  1. Mix the “eggs” with the sugar, the butter, the lemon zest and the milk till they are a smooth mixture.
  2. Add the flour, the baking powder and poppyseeds also (the whole or grinded poppyseeds is optional)
  3. Bake in a 25x35 cm baking sheet lined with parchment paper, preheated to 160 degrees, in 45 minutes. Let's do the needle test!
  4. Meanwhile, we make the cherry sauce. Boil the frozen cherries in a pan with the sugar, lemon juice and cognac.
  5. The starch is dissolved in a little cold water, added to the boiling sour cherries, thickened with constant stirring and removed from the heat.
  6. Serve the sliced ​​poppy seed pie with the cherry sauce.
