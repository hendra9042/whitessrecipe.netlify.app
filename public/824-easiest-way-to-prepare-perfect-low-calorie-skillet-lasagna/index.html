The ingredients needed to make low calorie skillet lasagna:
  1. Make ready 1 lb lean ground beef
  2. Prepare 1 onion chopped
  3. Take 2 clove garlic minced
  4. Take 1 tomatoe chopped
  5. Take 8 oz tomatoe sauce
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp parsley
  7. Take 1 tsp Sweet basil
  8. Get 1 tsp oregano
  9. Take 1 tsp salt
  10. Take 2 1/2 cup broken lasagna noodles
  11. Get 1 1/4 cup water
  12. Prepare 1 cup Cottage cheese
  13. Get 1/4 cup seasoned grated parmasean - I use the garlic and basil one
  14. Take 1 egg
  15. Prepare 1 mozzarella cheese for garnish

Steps to make low calorie skillet lasagna:
  1. Brown meat with the onion and garlic in a large skillet
  2. Add tomatoe, tomatoe sauce, water, parsley, oregano, basil , salt
  3. Stir in your pasta and bring to a boil. Keep stirring
  4. Reduce Ur heat to low and cover. Cook for 20 minutes or until your pasta is tender
  5. In small bowl combine your egg, parm, and cottage cheese
  6. Pour over skillet and cover and cook about 5 more minutes
  7. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese when serving
