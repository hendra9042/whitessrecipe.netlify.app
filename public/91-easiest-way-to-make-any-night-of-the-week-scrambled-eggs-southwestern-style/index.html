The ingredients needed to make Scrambled Eggs Southwestern Style:
  1. Make ready left over baked potato
  2. Make ready left over grilled chicken breast
  3. Prepare large tomatoe
  4. Prepare sliced jalapeños
  5. Get eggs
  6. Get Salt
  7. Prepare Pepper
  8. Get cooked bacon
  9. Take Bacon grease or cooking oil
  10. Get heavy cream or milk
  11. Prepare Cheese shredded
  12. Prepare Sour cream
  13. Make ready Favorite Salsa
Steps to make Scrambled Eggs Southwestern Style:
  2. Cook bacon. While bacon is cooking peel baked potato, dice tomato & grilled chicken breast.
  3. In a large skillet on medium high put in some bacon grease or cooking oil and cook the diced baked potato. Once done add the chicken, tomato,bacon & jalapeños and simmer 3 to 5 minutes
  4. In a separate bowl scramble the 7 eggs with the heavy cream or milk.
  5. Pour into simmering pan of meat, potatoes, tomato & jalapeños and add cheese.
  6. Cook till eggs are done then place some on a plate with a spoon of sour cream on top and your favorite salsa. Enjoy!
