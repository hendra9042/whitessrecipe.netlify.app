The ingredients needed to make Cashew cream chocolate Easter Eggs:
  1. Get 150 gm cooking Chocolate
  2. Get 200 gm cashews soaked in water for 2 hours
  3. Make ready 6 tbsp maple syrup
  4. Prepare 1/3 cupCoconut oil
  5. Make ready 4 tbsp water just enough for blending
  6. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla essence
  7. Take 1 pinch turmeric
  8. Make ready 1 pinch salt
Steps to make Cashew cream chocolate Easter Eggs:
  1. Chop the chocolate up.
  2. Temper the chocolate in double boiler.
  3. Pour 65%of the chocolate into chocolate mould and then tilt as it dries.Try to make a even coating. Chill it. Keep the remaining chocolate warm to stay liquid.
  4. Blend cashews with maple syrup,coconut oil,salt vanilla extract. It makes a smooth paste.
  5. Add a few tsp of water if it is too thick.
  6. Take out 1 tbsp of this cashew cream and add a pinch if turmeric to make a yolk.
  7. Now spoon the white cashew cream and then blob of turmeric yolk.
  8. Place back in in the freezer for 5 min.
  9. Take out the mould and pour the remaining melted chocolate and tilt to cover.
  10. Store in the fridge.Gift it to friends on Easter
  11. Tip.. Don't over blend cashews.
