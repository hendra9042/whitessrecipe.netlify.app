Instructions to make Mushrooms & Ham with scrambled eggs:
  1. Chop the mushrooms, the onion and the ham.
  2. Fry the onion and the mushrooms till it loses most of the water.
  3. Add the ham and keep frying for 10 minutes (all the ingredients should get similar colors)
  4. Add the eggs, fry for another 5 minutes.
  5. Serve & Enjoy!!
