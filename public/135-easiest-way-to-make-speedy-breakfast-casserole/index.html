The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Prepare Browned pork sausage
  2. Prepare Hard-boiled eggs
  3. Take Chopped green onions
  4. Make ready Sliced fresh mushrooms
  5. Get Sour cream
  6. Get Grated cheese
Instructions to make Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Combine all ingredients, reserving 4 ounces of the cheese with enough cream to moisten well.
  2. Place in a 9 x 13-inch pan.
  3. Cover with the remaining cheese and cream and bake at 350°F until heated thoroughly or for 30 minutes.
