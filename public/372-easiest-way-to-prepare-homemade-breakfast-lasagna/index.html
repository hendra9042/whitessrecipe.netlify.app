The ingredients needed to make Breakfast lasagna:
  1. Prepare Bacon
  2. Make ready Left over mashed potatoes
  3. Make ready Sprinkle double cheddar cheese (2 light layers)
  4. Make ready Baby spinach (2 layers between potatoes & fried veggies)
  5. Get Mixture of crimini mushrooms sliced
  6. Prepare Red onion sliced
  7. Prepare Cherry tomatoes quartered
  8. Make ready eggs scrambled
  9. Get Salt & pepper
  10. Take Homemade bread pan fried to crisp

Instructions to make Breakfast lasagna:
  1. Cook bacon until crispy
  2. Pan fry left over potatoes in bacon grease
  3. Chop up mushrooms, red onion & cherry tomatoes and saute slightly
  4. Layer potatoes, cheese x2, spinach x2, veggie mixture, bacon then pour eggs on top with more cheese
  5. Cook at 400' for 40 mins or until eggs are fluffy & set
