The ingredients needed to make Dark Chocolate Chunk Cookies:
  1. Prepare 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  2. Get 1/2 cup sugar
  3. Take 1 egg (substitute meringue powder if you don’t have eggs)
  4. Prepare 6 bite size Dove dark chocolate (cut into quarters)
  5. Get Pinch salt
  6. Take 1/2 cup flour
  7. Take Dash baking soda
  8. Get Tbsp vanilla extract (water if you use meringue)

Instructions to make Dark Chocolate Chunk Cookies:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. In a mixer, cream together sugar and room temperature butter
  3. Add egg or meringue substitute, then add pinch of salt, dash of baking soda, and flour until combined (add 1 tbsp of water or vanilla)
  4. Add chocolate chunks and lightly mix
  5. Bake for 12 minutes! Enjoy warm.
