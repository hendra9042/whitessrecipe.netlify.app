The ingredients needed to make Carbonara without Heavy Cream:
  1. Get Hot spring egg - soft poached egg
  2. Make ready Grated garlic
  3. Get or more Parmesan cheese
  4. Prepare Butter
  5. Make ready Salt and pepper
  6. Take Toppings:
  7. Make ready Bacon (or Sausage)
  8. Prepare as much (to taste) Mushroom or Vegetables
Steps to make Carbonara without Heavy Cream:
  1. Prepare the soft poached egg. You can refer to this. - - https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/146127-quick-and-easy-onsen-tamago-hot-spring-soft-boiled-eggs
  2. Put all the ingredients as well as the poached egg into a deep dish for pasta. You don't have to melt the butter. For the salt, go light to adjust later to taste.
  3. Cook the pasta in salted boiling water as the instructions on the package. Remove from heat to drain.
  4. While the pasta is cooking at Step 3, sauté the bacon. Add mushroom or vegetables if you wish.
  5. Toss the piping pasta in the the reserved sauce and sprinkle salt to taste. Serve immediately garnished with the bacon and it's done.
