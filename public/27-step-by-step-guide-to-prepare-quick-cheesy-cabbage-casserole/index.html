The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Cabbage Casserole:
  1. Take finely sliced cabbage
  2. Get salt
  3. Take olive oil
  4. Prepare sausages sliced
  5. Prepare small onion chopped
  6. Get ginger garlic sauce
  7. Make ready mixed vegetables (red, green, and yellow bell peppers with broccoli florets)
  8. Make ready eggs
  9. Make ready cream
  10. Make ready English mustard
  11. Make ready Worcestershire sauce
  12. Prepare all-purpose flour
  13. Get cornflour
  14. Take baking powder
  15. Make ready herb
  16. Make ready grated mozzarella cheese
Instructions to make Cheesy Cabbage Casserole:
  1. To begin, take the sliced cabbage in a large bowl. Add the salt and scrunch the cabbage so that it softens. Keep aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan. Add the sausages and cook until they colorize.
  3. Add the chopped onions. Once they are translucent, add the ginger garlic paste. Cook for a minute.
  4. Add the vegetables and saute. Do not overcook the vegetables. Finally add the cabbage and mix well. Turn off the heat.
  5. In another bowl, whisk three eggs. Add the cream, English mustard, and Worcestershire sauce. Mix well.
  6. In a third bowl, mix the flour, cornflour, baking powder, and herb of your choice.
  7. Take a baking dish and generously grease it. In the meantime, preheat the oven to 180 degrees Celsius.
  8. Add the flour mix to the eggs and cream. Mix well until it is smooth in texture..
  9. To assemble the casserole, add the vegetables to the baking dish and flatten. Pour the egg mix on top. Finally top it with the grated mozzarella cheese.
  10. Bake at 180 degrees Celsius for 20-30 minutes or until cooked. The creamy cabbage casserole is ready to serve.
