The ingredients needed to make Simple Meatballs Soup:
  1. Take 500 gr ground beef
  2. Prepare 3-4 tbsp katakuriko (you can substitute it with corn starch or sago flour)
  3. Get 1 tbsp onion paste (or grated onion)
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp garlic paste (or grated garlic)
  5. Prepare Crushed black pepper to your preference
  6. Take 1 egg
  7. Take 1/2 tsp celery salt
  8. Make ready 1/4 cup frozen peas
  9. Prepare 2 cups beef broth
Steps to make Simple Meatballs Soup:
  1. Boil beef broth in pot.
  2. Mix everything except beef broth and frozen peas. Knead with your hand until the mixture is pliable.
  3. After the broth boils, form balls and put inside the pot. After the meatballs are cooked (they'll float a bit), add frozen peas.
  4. Stir now and then and give it a taste. I usually do not add salt at this stage, but if it feels too plain, add more salt (or celery salt) and crushed black pepper.
