Instructions to make Sausage and egg biscuit casserole:
  1. Preheat oven to 425°F Start by browning 1 pound of sausage and chop. I also added some onion powder for more taste
  2. Butter a 9x13 baking dish. Cut biscuits in quarters. I use a pizza cutter. Put biscuits in bottom of baking dish.
  3. When sausage is done. Scramble up a dozen of eggs in a bowl, add 1/2 cup of milk, salt and pepper. Then add 1 cup of shredded cheese , then add the sausage and stir.
  4. Pour the egg mixture over the biscuits. Then add 1 cup of shredded cheese.
  5. Bake at 425 for 20-30 minutes. Check after 20 minutes. Cook time varies.
