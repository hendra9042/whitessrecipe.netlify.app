The ingredients needed to make Sausage Potato Casserole:
  1. Make ready frozen potatoes obrien
  2. Get ground Italian sausage
  3. Make ready onion, chopped
  4. Take red bell pepper, chopped
  5. Make ready garlic, minced
  6. Prepare milk
  7. Get eggs
  8. Get black pepper
  9. Make ready shredded cheddar cheese
  10. Get green onions, sliced thin
Steps to make Sausage Potato Casserole:
  1. Preheat oven to 375. Brown sausage in a Dutch oven.
  2. Add onion and pepper and saute until softened.
  3. Add garlic and saute an additional 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in potatoes and 1.5 cups cheese.
  5. Whisk together eggs, milk, and black pepper. Pour egg mixture over sausage mixture and stir to combine.
  6. Sprinkle remaining cheese over top. Bake for 30 minutes.
  7. Sprinkle green onions on top for garnish. Serve.
