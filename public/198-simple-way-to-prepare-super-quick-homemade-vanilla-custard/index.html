The ingredients needed to make Vanilla Custard:
  1. Get milk
  2. Take sugar
  3. Prepare egg yolks
  4. Make ready corn starch
  5. Take vanilla extract
  6. Prepare butter
Instructions to make Vanilla Custard:
  1. Whisk together egg yolks and sugar until they turn pale
  2. Add corn starch and mix until well incorporated
  3. Meanwhile, heat the milk on the pan until it soft boils.
  4. When the egg yolks mixture are ready and the milk are warm, pour half the milk into the mixture and mix it well
  5. Pour the mixture from step 4 into the milk in the pan and put it with a medium heat while keep mixing it until it turns thick.
  6. Add the butter to make it looked more glossy
  7. Take the pan off the heat and transfer it to another bowl to cool down.
  8. Cover the surface with plastic wrap and put it on the fridge at least 2 hour before used
