Easiest Way to Make Quick ramen noodle egg soup

Sally Ray

To get started with this recipe, we have to prepare a few components. You can have ramen noodle egg soup using 2 ingredients and 7 steps. Here is how you can achieve that.

The ingredients needed to make ramen noodle egg soup:
  1. Make ready 3 eggs
  2. Take 1 ramen noodle pouch
Steps to make ramen noodle egg soup:
  1. bring water to a boil.
  2. add in eggs.
  3. add noodles
  4. continue boiling until noodles and eggs are to ur liking
  5. dump out excess water leaving the amount of water you prefer
  6. put in bowl and mix in the flavor pouch.
  7. I prefer using creamy chicken flavored ramen noodles. while eggs n noodles are still boiling you may add chicken bullion to water for a better flavored soup but not too much because you still have to add in the flavor pouch.
