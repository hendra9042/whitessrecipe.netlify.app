Steps to make Egg rollðŸ¥š:
  1. In a bowl add flour, baking powder, sugar a pinch of salt and butter then mix altogether with your hand
  2. Add one raw egg(beaten), mix and add a little water, work the dough till soft and sticky
  3. Cover it and let it rest for some minutes,
  4. After resting, place the dough on a work place, and divide it into four equal parts, then bring out your boiled eggs
  5. Place dough on your palm and platten it, place the boiled egg in the center of the dough, and wrap the dough around the egg
  6. Add oil in a pan, and start frying when hot, till golden brown, flip to the other side and do the same,
  7. Serve and enjoye
