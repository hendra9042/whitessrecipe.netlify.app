The ingredients needed to make Chilli Egg chapathi scramble:
  1. Get chapathi, <indian grocery store frozen section>
  2. Take eggs
  3. Get onions, chopped
  4. Get tomatoes, chopped
  5. Take ginger garlic paste
  6. Prepare green chillies, chopped or slit
  7. Prepare turmeric powder
  8. Get chilli powder, depending on ur spice level
  9. Prepare Salt
  10. Prepare Oil
  11. Prepare Coriander leaves, chopped
Instructions to make Chilli Egg chapathi scramble:
  1. Cook the frozen chapathi according to the package instructions. Once cooled down, roll them up and chop into pieces
  2. In a heavy bottomed pan.,heat oil, add onions, and green chillies and fry till golden brown.
  3. Add ginger garlic paste and dry for 3 minutes until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, chilli powder, salt and cook till tomatoes are mushy.
  5. Add the eggs to the pan and mix well until they are cooked
  6. Add the chopped up chapathi to the pan and mix well
  7. Season with salt if required. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
