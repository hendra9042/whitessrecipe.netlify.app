The ingredients needed to make Noodle With Egg Broth / Wat Tan Hor:
  1. Take soy noodle
  2. Make ready eggs
  3. Make ready chicken stock
  4. Prepare choi sum or bak choy
  5. Prepare dice garlic
  6. Get dice shallot
  7. Get vegetable oil
  8. Get MEAT OPTION
  9. Prepare ham
  10. Take GARNISH
  11. Get scallion
Instructions to make Noodle With Egg Broth / Wat Tan Hor:
  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil then add dash of salt and soy noodle then simmer for 3 minutes till soy noodle are cook then drain and set aside
  2. In pan saute ham (optional you can use chicken ,pork or any meat you prefer ) with dice garlic and shallot till fragrant then reserved half and set aside as garnish
  3. Add chicken stock and bring it to a boil then vegetable
  4. Add cooked soy noodle with seasoning then cornstarch mixture and bring it to a rapid boil
  5. Off heat and quickly Add eggs and gently circle 2 round to break the yolk and cover lid immediately till egg white are no longer translucent,
  6. Serve immediately soy noodle in egg gravy top ham and scallion
