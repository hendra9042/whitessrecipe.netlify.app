The ingredients needed to make Mediterranean Poached Egg Breakfast:
  1. Take 2 slice Garlic Naan Bread
  2. Make ready 4 slice Bacon
  3. Get 1/3 cup Pesto
  4. Prepare 2 slice Cheddar Cheese
  5. Prepare 4 large Eggs
  6. Take 4 slice Tomatoes
  7. Take 5 small Yukon Gold Potatoes
  8. Make ready 1/2 cup Fresh Tyme
  9. Make ready 1 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  10. Prepare 3 tbsp White Wine Vinegar
  11. Prepare 3 tbsp Chopped Garlic
  12. Prepare 1 Chopped Yellow Onion
  13. Take 1/4 cup Fresh Parsley
Instructions to make Mediterranean Poached Egg Breakfast:
  1. Preheat Oven to 250°F. Spread pesto on the Garlic Naan and place cheese slices on top. Warm in the oven for 10 - 15 minutes.
  2. Cook, Drain, and Set aside Bacon
  3. Chop potatoes into small cubes and add to hot frying pan with extra virgin olive oil, garlic, and onions. Sauté on medium heat until potatoes are golden brown.
  4. Bring large pot o water to a rolling boil and add white wine vinegar. Crack eggs one at time into a bowl and slowly let the egg slide into the boiling water. Tip: Spin the water with a spoon before pouring the egg in to the egg white stay close to the egg yolk! Repeat for each egg and set eggs aside.
  5. Take the Toasted Naan out of the oven, place the sliced tomatoes on top, then the bacon, and then add the poached eggs on top. Salt and Pepper the poached eggs to you liking. Garnish with fresh chopped parsley. Add the garlic potatoes on the side and enjoy!
