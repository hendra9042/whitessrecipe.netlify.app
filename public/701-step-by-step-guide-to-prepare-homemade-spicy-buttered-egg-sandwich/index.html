The ingredients needed to make Spicy Buttered Egg Sandwich:
  1. Take 1 slice toasted olive oil classic loaf with cranberry or any bread
  2. Prepare 1 medium lettuce salad
  3. Make ready 1 egg
  4. Make ready GARNISH
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp garlic salad dressing
  6. Prepare 1 tsp fresh dill
  7. Take SPICY BUTTER
  8. Get 1 tsp fine chili powder
  9. Get 2 tbsp butter
Steps to make Spicy Buttered Egg Sandwich:
  1. TOAST BREAD
  2. toast a slice of olive oil classic loaf with cranberry then set aside
  3. SPICY BUTTER
  4. in pan with butter once its lighly brown off heat and add fine chili powder and mix well
  5. SPICY BUTTER FRIED EGG
  6. add fried egg in spicy butter and scoop spicy butter onto egg untill egg is set but still runny
  7. TO SERVE
  8. on toasted bread layer lettuce then spicy egg
  9. top garlic salad dressing and spicy butter and dill and serve immediately
