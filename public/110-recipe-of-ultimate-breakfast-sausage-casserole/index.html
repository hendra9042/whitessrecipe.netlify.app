The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Sausage Casserole:
  1. Make ready Jimmy Dean Sausage
  2. Get eggs
  3. Take light cream
  4. Make ready salt
  5. Take green onions chopped
  6. Make ready dry mustard
  7. Get day old bread, cubed
  8. Get cheddar, shredded
  9. Make ready swiss, shredded
Instructions to make Breakfast Sausage Casserole:
  1. grease 9x13 pan
  2. place bread in pan
  3. sprinkle with cheese and sausage
  4. combine rest of ingredients and mix well
  5. pour over bread
  6. refrig overnight
  7. 350 for 1 hour or until golden brown
