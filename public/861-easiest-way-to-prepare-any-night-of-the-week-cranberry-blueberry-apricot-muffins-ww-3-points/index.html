The ingredients needed to make Cranberry blueberry apricot muffins w.w. 3 points:
  1. Take 2 1/2 cup whole wheat flour
  2. Take 2/3 cup sugar
  3. Make ready 2 tsp double acting baking powder
  4. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
  5. Get 1/4 tsp salt
  6. Make ready 1/3 cup reduced calorie margarine diced
  7. Prepare 3/4 cup plain nonfat yogurt
  8. Take 1/3 cup frozen orange juice concentrate
  9. Get 2 eggs
  10. Get 1 1/2 cup fresh or frozen blueberries
  11. Make ready 1 cup fresh or frozen cranberries
  12. Prepare 24 dried apricot halves, chopped

Steps to make Cranberry blueberry apricot muffins w.w. 3 points:
  1. oven to 400
  2. spray 18 non stick muffin cups either spray or line with paper liners
  3. in large bowl combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt
  4. using pastry blender or two knives to cut in the margarine until mixture resembles coarse crumbs
  5. in medium bowl, combine yogurt, orange juice concentrate, and eggs.
  6. stir into flour , mixture until just combined
  7. gently stir in blueberries, cranberries, and apricots
  8. spoon batter into cups filling to thirds full
  9. bake until golden and toothpick comes out clean….15 to 20 minutes
  10. cool on rack 10 minutes….remove from pan and continue to cool on rack
