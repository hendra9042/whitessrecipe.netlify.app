The ingredients needed to make Egg Crep Roll:
  1. Get 3 Egg
  2. Make ready as needed oil
  3. Get 1 Cup Milk
  4. Take 1/2 Tsp Salt
  5. Make ready 3/4 Cup Wheat flour
  6. Take 1/4 Tsp Baking powder
  7. Make ready 1/4 Tsp Chat Masala
  8. Prepare 1 Onion (Chopped)
  9. Prepare 1 Tomato (Chopped)
  10. Take 1 Green Chilli (Chopped)
Steps to make Egg Crep Roll:
  1. In a mixing bowl, add 1 egg, 1 cup milk, ¼ Ts salt and mix well.
  2. Add ¾ cup wheat flour, ¼ Tsp baking powder and beat well till it is combined well. Beat well till combined. Mix well to make it lump free.
  3. In another bowl, add 2 eggs, ¼ Tsp salt, ¼ Tsp chat masala, 1 onion chopped, 1 tomato chopped, 1 green chilli chopped and mix well.
  4. Brush some ghee over the pan. - Pour batter and tilt the pan slightly to spread the batter. Cook for 2 minutes over medium flame.
  5. Now pour and spread the egg mix over that. - Cook for 3 minutes over medium flame. - Flip and cook for 2 minutes.
  6. Again, flip and spread the shredded cheese. - Cover and cook for 1 minute. - Transfer to a plate and roll it when it is hot.
  7. Serve hot with chilli mayo
