The ingredients needed to make Skillet Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Take 1 packages sausage gravy
  2. Prepare 4 cup tater tots
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup cooked sausage, crumbled
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 cup water
  6. Get 2 large eggs
  7. Get 1 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  8. Take 1 tbsp chives, minced
Steps to make Skillet Breakfast Casserole:
  1. preheat oven to 350
  2. heat oil in 10-12" oven proof skillet
  3. brown tater tots 3-4 mins, remove from heat
  4. evenly sprinkle on sausage
  5. whisk gravy mix into water
  6. beat eggs into gravy mix
  7. pour evenly over tater tots
  8. bake 25-30 mins or until filling is set into center
  9. sprinkle cheese and bake another 3 mins or until melted
  10. cut into wedges and garnish with chives
