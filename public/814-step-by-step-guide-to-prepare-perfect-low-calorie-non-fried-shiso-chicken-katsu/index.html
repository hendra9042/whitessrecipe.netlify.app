The ingredients needed to make Low-Calorie Non-Fried Shiso Chicken Katsu:
  1. Take 4 Chicken tenders
  2. Get 8 leaves Shiso leaves
  3. Prepare 1 dash Salt and pepper
  4. Prepare 1 dash Plain flour
  5. Get 1 Egg
  6. Make ready 3 tbsp Olive oil
  7. Take Cheese panko
  8. Prepare 6 tbsp ☆ Panko
  9. Make ready 2 tbsp ☆ Parmesan cheese

Steps to make Low-Calorie Non-Fried Shiso Chicken Katsu:
  1. Remove the sinews from the chicken and butterfly them. Use a rolling pin to pound them to 2~3 mm thickness. 1. Once flattened, season with salt and pepper and top with the shiso leaves, then fold the meat to sandwich the shiso.
  2. Combine the ☆ ingredients to make the cheese panko.
  3. Lightly coat the chicken in flour, then beaten egg, then the cheese panko.
  4. Heat olive oil in a frying pan and cook the chicken over medium heat.
  5. Once browned and crispy, flip them over and cook the other side in the same manner. Enjoy!
