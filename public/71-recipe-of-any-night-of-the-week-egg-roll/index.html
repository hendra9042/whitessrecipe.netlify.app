The ingredients needed to make Egg Roll:
  1. Get 3 chapati
  2. Make ready 3 egg
  3. Take 2 tomatoes
  4. Make ready 2 cucumbers
  5. Make ready 1 onion
  6. Take 3 tsp mustard sauce
  7. Take 3 tsp tomato sauce
  8. Prepare 1 tsp black pepper powder
  9. Make ready to taste Salt
  10. Make ready as required Oil for fry
Instructions to make Egg Roll:
  1. Cut vegetables. Add black pepper on it. Whisk the egg separately.
  2. Put very small amount of oil in pan. Spread egg. Place chapati on it. And turn the chapati
  3. Place vegetables in centre. Put mustard sauce and tomato sauce. And roll it
