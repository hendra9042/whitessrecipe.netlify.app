The ingredients needed to make Banana Caramel Cream Muffins:
  1. Prepare 85 grams ★Cake flour
  2. Get 30 grams ★Almond flour
  3. Get 1 tsp ★Baking powder
  4. Take 60 grams White sugar
  5. Get 30 grams Margarine
  6. Take 30 grams Vegetable oil
  7. Make ready 1 large egg
  8. Take 50 grams Heavy cream (recommended, but can be substituted with milk)
  9. Make ready 1 Caramelised bananas (same method as https://cookpad.com/en/recipes/146241-caramelized-apple?ref=search)
  10. Make ready 1 Chocolate chips

Instructions to make Banana Caramel Cream Muffins:
  1. (Preparations) Sift the ingredients marked with ★. Use glassine paper to make muffin cups in the muffin tins and set aside (if using a teflon or silicon muffin tin, grease it with butter and set aside).
  2. Usingas a reference, make the caramelised bananas and chill them. Use 1 banana (about 90 g), and half the amounts of each of the listed ingredients. - - https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/146241-caramelized-apple
  3. Warm the margarine in the microwave for 40 seconds, then mix it well with the sugar in a bowl. (It's alright if it's a little crumbly at this point)
  4. Add the vegetable oil and use a whisk to mix them together until the mixture looks like mayonnaise.
  5. Add the beaten egg in 3 batches, mixing well after each addition.
  6. Add the heavy cream (or milk) all at once and stir it in until the mixture becomes smooth.
  7. Add the dry ingredients from Step 1, mixing them in by stirring up from the bottom of the bowl with a rubber spatula. Add the caramelised bananas from Step 2 just before the flouriness of the batter is gone.
  8. Pour the batter into the prepared muffin tins and sprinkle them with chocolate chips. Bake the muffins in a preheated oven at 180°C for 30 minutes. If they seem like they're going to burn, cover the muffins with aluminium foil.
  9. Remove the muffins from the tin once they've cooled.
  10. You can add caramelised apples as well. Here, I used raisins instead of chocolate chips.
