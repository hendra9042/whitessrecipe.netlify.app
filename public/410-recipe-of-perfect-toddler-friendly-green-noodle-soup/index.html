The ingredients needed to make Toddler Friendly Green Noodle Soup:
  1. Get 1/2 zucchini, finely shredded
  2. Make ready 3 cups water
  3. Get 1.5 tbsp chicken bouillon powder (or 1 cube)
  4. Take 2 eggs scrambled
  5. Take 1 pkg instant ramen noodles(toss the pkg of powder in the trash)
Steps to make Toddler Friendly Green Noodle Soup:
  1. Bring the water and chicken soup seasoning to a boil.
  2. Once its boiling, drop in the rest of the ingredients. Cook for 5 min or until noodles are cooked to your liking then serve♡
  3. My 2 year old scrambled the eggs in a bowl and helped shred the zucchini. If you let them help in the kitchen they're more likely to eat the meal. He LOVED this soup!
