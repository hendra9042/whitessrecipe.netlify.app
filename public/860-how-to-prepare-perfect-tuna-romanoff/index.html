The ingredients needed to make Tuna Romanoff:
  1. Make ready 1 Vegetable cooking spray
  2. Make ready 1 cup mushrooms, fresh, sliced
  3. Prepare 1 cup onion, chopped
  4. Make ready 1/3 cup celery, sliced thin
  5. Make ready 1 clove garlic, minced
  6. Make ready 3/4 cup milk, 1% low fat
  7. Prepare 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  8. Take 1/4 cup Mayonnaise, reduced calorie
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp dillweed, dried (optional)
  10. Get 1/4 tsp salt
  11. Take 1/4 tsp pepper
  12. Take 10 3/4 oz Cream of celery soup, undiluted (1 can) can use reduced- fat and sodium condensed
  13. Get 3 1/2 cup cooked medium egg noodles (2 1/4 cups uncooked)
  14. Make ready 1 cup frozen green peas
  15. Prepare 9 1/4 oz tuna in water, drained (1 can)
  16. Prepare 2 oz diced pimiento, drained (1 jar)
  17. Take 1/4 cup bread crumbs, dry
  18. Make ready 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  19. Get 1 tbsp margarine, melted
Instructions to make Tuna Romanoff:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Coat large nonstick skillet with cooking spray
  3. Place over medium heat until hot.
  4. Add mushrooms, onion, celery, and garlic and saute for 6 minutes or until tender.
  5. Combine milk and next 6 ingredients in a bowl, stir well.
  6. Add mushroom mixture, noodles, and next 3 ingredients. Stir gently
  7. Spoon mixture into shallow 2-quart casserole coated with cooking spray.
  8. Cover and bake for 40 minutes
  9. Combine breadcrumbs, rest of Parmesan cheese and margarine in a small bowl; stir well, and sprinkle over casserole
