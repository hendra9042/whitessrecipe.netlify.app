Instructions to make Noodles pizza:
  1. Take 2packs of maggi noodles and put it in water along with noodles masala. Add corn flour to it. Corn flour act as binding agent. We can make base with noodles.
  2. Take a pan add 2tsp oil then add boiled noodles evenly on a pan. Then add one egg to it.
  3. Keep it for 10mins then it will form a crunchy base then change the side of base in pan for 10 min after it gets brown add grated cheese pizza sauce,carrot,onion tomato and again add cheese.
  4. Then keep it in oven at 250degrees for 15mins.pizza is ready to serve.
