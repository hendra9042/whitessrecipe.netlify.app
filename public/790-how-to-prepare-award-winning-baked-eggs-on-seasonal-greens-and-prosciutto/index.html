Steps to make Baked eggs on seasonal greens and prosciutto:
  1. Preheat oven to 175 Celsius
  2. Brush pan with olive oil
  3. Layer prosciutto on the bottom of the pan
  4. Layer seasonal greens on top of prosciutto
  5. Season with salt and pepper
  6. Lay 4 eggs on top of the greens
  7. Bake for 15-20min
