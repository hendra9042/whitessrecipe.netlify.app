The ingredients needed to make Chicken Mushroom Noodles:
  1. Prepare 300 grams Chicken breast or pork
  2. Get 1 cup Button mushrooms
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp Oyster sauce
  4. Get 1 tbsp Soy sauce
  5. Take 2 tbsp Olive oil
  6. Get 1 tbsp Sesame oil
  7. Get 300 grams Egg noodles
  8. Prepare 2 bunches Caisim, cut in bite size
  9. Get 6 pcs Meatballs (Optional)
  10. Get 2 pcs Shallots, thin sliced
  11. Prepare 1/2 cup Celery leaf, chopped
  12. Prepare Fried shallots onion (Optional)
  13. Get Water
  14. Get Noodle sauce (per serve):
  15. Prepare 1 tsp Sesame oil
  16. Take 1 tsp Olive/vegetable oil
  17. Prepare 1 tsp Soy sauce
  18. Make ready 1 pinch Salt
  19. Get 1 pinch Pepper
Steps to make Chicken Mushroom Noodles:
  1. Heat 2 tbsp olive oil, add garlic and stir until fragrance. Add chicken, stir for a minute. Add mushrooms, oyster sauce, soy sauce and sesame oil and stir until the chicken cooked properly. Remove from the heat.
  2. Add water to a pan and boil it. Add cut caisim into boiling water for a minute, then remove it from the water. Use the same boiling water, add 150 grams noodles into boiling water and cook for 2 or 3 minutes.
  3. While boiling noodles, prepare a bowl, add all of noodles sauce ingredients and mix.
  4. Remove noodles from the water and drain it. Put the drained noodles into a bowl (step 3) and mix well with the mixed sauce.
  5. Put chicken, cooked caisim and meetball on the top. Garnish it with sliced shallots and celery leaf and also fried shallots onion.
