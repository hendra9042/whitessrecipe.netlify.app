The ingredients needed to make Quinoa and cannellini bean burgers:
  1. Make ready 1 can drained rinsed cannellini beans
  2. Make ready 2 cups cooked quinoa
  3. Prepare 1 tsp lemon zest plus juice of half lemon
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup corn (canned or thawed frozen)
  5. Get 1/4 cup finely diced red bell pepper
  6. Prepare 1 finely minced shallot
  7. Take 2 tablespoons minced flat leaf parsley
  8. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon dried herb (thyme, marjoram, dill etc)
  9. Take 1/4 teaspoon smoked paprika
  10. Take 2 tablespoons Just Egg (egg substitute) or 1 small beaten egg
  11. Prepare 2-3 tablespoons panko bread crumbs
  12. Make ready Salt and pepper to taste *start with 1/2 teaspoon salt

Steps to make Quinoa and cannellini bean burgers:
  1. In a large bowl mash the beans (I used potato masher) till all smashed then add all the ingredients and mix thoroughly. It should be able to form a patty now, if not add a little more panko.
  2. Cover mixture and put in fridge for at least 30 minutes
  3. Using a half cup measuring cup or ring mold,press mixture in to form patties, you should get 6 to 8 from this batch.
  4. You can put patties on wax paper and refrigerate up to 3 days at this point.
  5. Preheat a nonstick skillet to medium/medium high heat and add a tablespoon of high smoke point oil like coconut or avocado.
  6. Gently place the desired number of burgers in the pan. Cook for two minutes
  7. Carefully flip burger and press with back of spatula with even pressure until burger is about 1/2 thick. Cook 4 to 5 minutes
  8. Carefully turn burger again and continue cooking for another 4 minutes until browned on both sides and cooked thru.
