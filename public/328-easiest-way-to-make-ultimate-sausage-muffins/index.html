The ingredients needed to make Sausage Muffins:
  1. Prepare cooked, drained and crumbled breakfast sausage
  2. Prepare Bisquick
  3. Get eggs
  4. Make ready Cheese
Steps to make Sausage Muffins:
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Mix all ingredients and spoon into muffin tin that has been sprayed with non stick spray.
  3. Bake 20 minutes or until lightly brown. I got 9 muffins out of this mixture but it will depend on the size of your tin.
