Instructions to make Quick jalapeno cheese egg roll:
  1. Take a bowl crack eggs, and beat it, add as required salt and black pepper.
  2. Take a pan add butter, spread egg batter and after 3 min spread veggies, jalapeno sauce, tomato sauce and mayonnaise.
  3. After quick add chopped veggies And cheese.
  4. Roll it, garnish with some coriander leaves and,black pepper.
  5. Serve it with jalapeno sauce.
