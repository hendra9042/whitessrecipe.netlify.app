The ingredients needed to make Grandmother Pete's Buttermilk Cornbread:
  1. Take butter
  2. Get sugar
  3. Make ready eggs
  4. Get buttermilk
  5. Prepare teaspoon baking soda
  6. Make ready cornmeal
  7. Make ready a.p. flour
  8. Get teaspoon salt
  9. Prepare chedder
  10. Get pickled jalepenos
Steps to make Grandmother Pete's Buttermilk Cornbread:
  1. preheat oven to 375°F
  2. melt butter in a large skillet
  3. when butter is melted, dissolve sugar
  4. add eggs and beat until well blended
  5. combine buttermilk, and baking soda. Stir into mixture
  6. stir in cornmeal, flour, and salt until only a few lumps remain.
  7. in a well greased pan, bake 30-40 minutes, or until a toothpick come out the center clean
