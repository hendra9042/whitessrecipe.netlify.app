The ingredients needed to make Microwave Egg Nog:
  1. Get 1 T sugar or to taste
  2. Take 1 t vanilla
  3. Make ready 1 egg
  4. Prepare 3/4 milk
  5. Prepare 1 oz Rum or other liquor (optional)
Instructions to make Microwave Egg Nog:
  1. Mix ingredients well in a microwave safe mug
  2. Microwave on 1 minute, then stir.
  3. Microwave for 30 seconds, then stir.
  4. Continue to microwave, stirring whenever it thickens and rises up, about every 10-15 sec
