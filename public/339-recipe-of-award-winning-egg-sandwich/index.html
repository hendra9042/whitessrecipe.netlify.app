The ingredients needed to make Egg Sandwich:
  1. Make ready Boiled Egg
  2. Prepare Bread Slices
  3. Get chopped Cucumber
  4. Prepare Pickled Jalapeno
  5. Get Mustard Sauce
  6. Prepare Pepper Powder
  7. Take Salt
Steps to make Egg Sandwich:
  1. Peel and chop the boiled egg.
  2. Add to a bowl, mix salt, pepper, mustard and jalapeno. The filling is ready.
  3. Place the filling over the bread slices. Make sandwiches. Serve with ketchup on the side.
