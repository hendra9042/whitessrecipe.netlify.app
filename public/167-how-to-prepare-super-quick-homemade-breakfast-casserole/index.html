The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Get eggs
  2. Get salt
  3. Make ready milk
  4. Prepare grated american cheese
  5. Get pepper
  6. Get bread (cubed and buttered)
  7. Make ready sausage (cooked, may also use ham or bacon)
Steps to make Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Arrange bread cubes in bottom of greased pan.
  2. Pour the eggs, which have been beaten, along with the milk, salt and pepper over the bread crumbs.
  3. Put meat over this and sprinkle with cheese.
  4. Prepare and refrigerate overnight if desired.
  5. Bake at 350°F for 45 minutes.
