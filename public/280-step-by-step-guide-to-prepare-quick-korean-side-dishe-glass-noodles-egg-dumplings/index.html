The ingredients needed to make Korean Side Dishe| Glass Noodles Egg Dumplings:
  1. Get appropriate Chinese chives
  2. Get ea Glass Noodles
  3. Prepare ea egg
  4. Make ready Seasoning
  5. Get salt
  6. Take soy sauce
  7. Prepare sesame oil
  8. Take bit pepper
Steps to make Korean Side Dishe| Glass Noodles Egg Dumplings:
  1. Prepare all ingredients
  2. Cut Chinese chives into small pieces - Beat the eggs
  3. Boiled water to cook glass noodles - After the glass noodles are cooked, remove them from water and put them in a bowl, cut them with scissors
  4. Seasoning: 1 teaspoon salt, 1 teaspoon soy sauce, 0.5 teaspoon sesame oil, a bit pepper - After adding seasoning, add Chinese chives and egg liquid - Then mix all the ingredients evenly
  5. Warm the pot, put oil and then ready to cook yummy “glass noodles egg dumplings” - When the bottom of the egg is done, fold in half - Both sides are fried to a beautiful reddish yellow color and they will become delicious
