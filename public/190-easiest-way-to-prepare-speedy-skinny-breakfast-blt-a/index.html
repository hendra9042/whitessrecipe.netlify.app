The ingredients needed to make Skinny Breakfast B.L.T. + A.:
  1. Prepare 2 slices of healthy bread of choice (I used WW wheat,only 2 pts for 2 slices)
  2. Take 4 tbsp egg whites or egg beaters
  3. Get 1 slice of low fat cheese(I used Sargento ultra thin)
  4. Make ready 1 baby spinach leaves(or lettuce of choice)
  5. Make ready 1 thick slice of tomato
  6. Make ready 1/4 of an avocado, thinly sliced
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp light mayonaisse
  8. Make ready 1 slice of turkey bacon, torn in half
  9. Prepare 1 freshly cracked pepper
Instructions to make Skinny Breakfast B.L.T. + A.:
  1. Toss bread in the toaster. While the bread is toasting spray a pan and cook the egg whites. When cooked,add cheese slice on top to melt slightly.
  2. On 1 side of toast spread 1/2 tbsp of light mayo then layer spinach, tomato, cooked egg white with cheese, turkey bacon and finally avocado slices. Pepper generously! Spread remaining 1/2 tbsp of light mayo on other toast slice. Place it on top and dive in!!
