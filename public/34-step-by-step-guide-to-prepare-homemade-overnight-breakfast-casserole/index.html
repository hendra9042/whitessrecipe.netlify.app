The ingredients needed to make Overnight Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Take bag of frozen shredded hashbrowns
  2. Prepare bags 2 cups each taco cheese with spices
  3. Make ready can sweetened condensed milk
  4. Take Little splash of milk
  5. Get pure maple syrup
  6. Make ready salt
  7. Take black pepper
  8. Make ready eggs
  9. Prepare mild ground sausage
  10. Prepare bag diced or cubed ham about 1 cup
  11. Take red bell pepper diced
  12. Take green bell pepper diced
Instructions to make Overnight Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Cook sausage and set a side and place half of the hashbrowns in a lightly greased 9×13 inch dish then sprinkle on 2 cups cheese
  2. Then add the rest of the hashbrowns and add cooked sausage, ham and peppers
  3. Mix eggs, salt, pepper, maple syrup, condensed milk with a little splash of milk just to lightly thin it out and mix it all together and then pour evenly over top and sprinkle on rest of cheese. Cover and refrigerate overnight then bake covered at 350 degrees for 1 hour then remove cover and bake for 15 more minutes. Let rest for a few minutes then serve. Hope you enjoy !
