The ingredients needed to make Custard pie with shredded phyllo - Galaktoboureko üçÆ:
  1. Get 600 grams brown sugar
  2. Prepare Peel of 1 big lemon
  3. Make ready 1 cinnamon stick
  4. Prepare 60 grams honey
  5. Take 600 grams water
  6. Prepare 2 eggs
  7. Make ready 80 grams sugar
  8. Make ready 50 grams cold butter
  9. Prepare 300 ml milk full fat
  10. Make ready 80 grams fine semolina
  11. Make ready 2 TS vanilla extract
  12. Get 200 grams heavy cream
  13. Take Pinch salt
  14. Get 200 grams butter melted
  15. Get 500 grams shredded phyllo pastry (you can also use fine vermicelli noodles if you find but they have to be very thin)

Instructions to make Custard pie with shredded phyllo - Galaktoboureko üçÆ:
  1. Syrup: in a pot on medium high heat add 600 grams water, 600 grams sugar, cinnamon stick, lemon peel and honey and let simmer until sugar is dissolved- keep aside
  2. Custard filling: in a pot on low heat, add milk, 50 grams of the 100 grams of sugar, semolina, vanilla extract, and the heavy cream. Whisk for 5 minutes. Once ready you will see that the whisk leaves lines in the cream. Now remove from heat and add the cold butter. This will help it stay fluffy and fresh. Add salt and whisk. Now add a bit more of vanilla extract and whisk. - now turn oven on 160 on fan.
  3. In a mixer add the eggs and rest of the sugar - the 50 grams that are left. Mix for 5 min until fluffy.
  4. Now add a big spoon of the cream you have created and mix well with the spatula. Then continue with the rest of the cream until all have been incorporated well.

  6. Now in a cake tin, add butter and in all around, add half of the phyllo pastry and slowly without pushing it down throw some of the melted butter. Then add the custard filling, and at the end continue with the rest of the phyllo pastry. Make sure you add on the sides and press it a bit down.
  7. It will look like this. Add the rest of butter and place in the oven for 1 hour.
