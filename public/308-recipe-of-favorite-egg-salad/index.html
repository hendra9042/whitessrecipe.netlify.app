Steps to make Egg salad:
  1. Cut your carrot,green beans and cabbage and set aside
  2. Cut the chicken frankfurters into circular shapes and set aside
  3. Heat your pan and add the oil,when the oil is hot enough fry the chicken frankfurters
  4. Flip it up and down till it gets to the texture that you want
  5. Bring it down and fry the carrot,green beans and cabbage
  6. Once it's ready,fry the eggs,make sure you add the salt in the eggs
  7. When it's done,serve in a plate and enjoy
