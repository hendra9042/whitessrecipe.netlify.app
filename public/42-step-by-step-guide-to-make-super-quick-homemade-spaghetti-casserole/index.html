The ingredients needed to make Spaghetti Casserole:
  1. Take thin spaghetti
  2. Get meat (ground, sausage or meatballs)
  3. Take sliced mushrooms
  4. Prepare melting cheese, like mozzarella
  5. Make ready eggs
  6. Take tomato sauce
  7. Prepare milk
  8. Make ready garlic powder
  9. Prepare salt
  10. Make ready black pepper
Steps to make Spaghetti Casserole:
  1. Preheat oven to 450F
  2. Brush or spray a 9X13 baking dish
  3. Cook noodles per package instructions to el dente, if the noodles can be done the same time add the meat, that works the best, or you may have to oil them do they don't stick together if the finish first
  4. Slice your mushrooms
  5. Cook your meat I a skillet, if using ground or sausage, you can leave it a little chunky if you want
  6. When your meat is about 3/4 of the way cooked, add your sliced mushrooms
  7. Stir and cook meat/mushrooms until done.
  8. Drain noodles and adds to baking pan
  9. Add meat/mushrooms to noodles, stir together.
  10. If you are using block cheese, cut it in 1/2 inch cubed, pressed the cubes into the noodle mixture for a cheesy surprise add you eat. If using grated/shredded cheese, sprinkle on and mix it in add best you can.
  11. The cubed cheese is the way to go, I just had grated this time.
  12. I like to give a few grinds of black pepper at this point.
  13. Mix the rest of the ingredients together.
  14. Even the noodle mixture out in the baking dish add good add you can.
  15. Pour the tomato mixture evenly over the noodles
  16. Cover the dish, I use foil
  17. Bake for 30 minutes
  18. Uncover and bake for 10 minutes
  19. Let set out for 5 or so minutes to solidify a little. Serve
