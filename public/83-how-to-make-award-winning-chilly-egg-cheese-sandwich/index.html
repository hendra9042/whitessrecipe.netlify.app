The ingredients needed to make Chilly, Egg cheese sandwich:
  1. Get 1 clove chopped onion
  2. Get 2 envelope sliced cheese
  3. Prepare 2 stick of green and red chills each
  4. Prepare 1 Boiled egg
  5. Prepare 4 slice of Bread
  6. Make ready small shredded chicken or beef
Steps to make Chilly, Egg cheese sandwich:
  1. Toast bread slices
  2. Boil egg for 8mins. Once finished peel boiled egg and slice with an egg slicer
  3. Lay your cheddar, or American cheese on slices of toasted bread
  4. Next add your sliced eggs, distributing on the bread evenly
  5. Now chop both green and red chilies unto the bread, then cover
  6. Now cover bread neatly with another bread slice, and slice bread into a neat triangle
  7. Ready to serve. You can sprinkle a bit of shredded beef or chicken in the sandwich.
