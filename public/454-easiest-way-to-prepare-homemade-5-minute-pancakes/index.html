The ingredients needed to make 5 Minute Pancakes:
  1. Prepare milk
  2. Prepare butter
  3. Prepare self raising flour
  4. Prepare egg
Steps to make 5 Minute Pancakes:
  1. Put all the ingredients in a large bowl
  2. Gently mix until combined
  3. Then use a ladle to pour into a hot, buttered pan
  4. Cook on a medium to high heat until you start to see small bubbles form - about a minute
  5. If you’re feeling adventurous, toss the pancake to cook the other side for a further 1 minute
  6. Serve up immediately with maple syrup, or sugar and lemon, or just butter.
