The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. Make ready canola oil
  2. Make ready brown sugar
  3. Get egg whites
  4. Take sea salt
  5. Take baking soda
  6. Make ready vinegar
  7. Take flour
  8. Make ready mini chocolate chips
Instructions to make Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. In a large bowl mix together oil, sugar, and egg whites.
  2. Add remaining ingredients.
  3. Roll dough into 1-inch balls, then place on cookie sheet.
  4. Bake 350 degrees for 8-10 minutes, or until cookies are just barely browned on the edges.
