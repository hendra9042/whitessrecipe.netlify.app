The ingredients needed to make Mandys breakfast sandwich:
  1. Make ready 2 slice bread toasted
  2. Make ready butter
  3. Get 3 black pepper sausages
  4. Make ready 1 large flat mushroom fried
  5. Prepare 1 large egg fried
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp oil for frying
  7. Make ready salt and pepper

Instructions to make Mandys breakfast sandwich:
  1. Toast bread until golden, grill sausages until cooked about 5-6 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan fry egg and mushroom. Butter toast.
  3. Slice sausages down the middle and spread open on toast top with mushroom and egg, finish with top piece of toast. Cut in half and serve with Ur favourite dip e.g. ketchup/ mayo etc.
