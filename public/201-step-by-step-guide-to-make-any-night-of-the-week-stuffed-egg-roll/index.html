The ingredients needed to make Stuffed egg Roll:
  1. Prepare 1/2 cup wheat flour
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp oil
  3. Get 2 eggs
  4. Take 1 tsp green chilli paste
  5. Make ready 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  6. Prepare Pinch turmeric
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup mix vegetable thin slic (onions,carrot,cabbage,capsicum)
  8. Make ready 1 tsp chat masala
  9. Get 1 tbsp mayonnaise
  10. Prepare As required Salt

Instructions to make Stuffed egg Roll:
  1. Make dough for paratha put aside.
  2. Break eggs in a bowl add green chilli paste, garlic ginger paste, salt and turmeric. Whisk it well.
  3. Roll paratha and put on tawa after a while turn it and spread half of mixture on one side after 1 minute turn paratha and spread rest of egg mixture on another side.
  4. Spread mayonnaise on one side and put vegetables on middle sprinkle chat masala and roll it.
  5. Ready to eat.
