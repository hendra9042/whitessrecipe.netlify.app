The ingredients needed to make Egg Roll:
  1. Take 1 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 tablespoons sugar
  3. Get 1 1/2 tablespoons butter
  4. Make ready 1 teaspoon milk flavour (optional)
  5. Get 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  6. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg (optional)
  7. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon salt
  8. Get 1/3 cup water
  9. Get 4 small boiled eggs
  10. Take Vegetable oil for deep frying
Instructions to make Egg Roll:
  1. Mix the dry ingredients
  3. Add wet ingredient and combine
  4. Cut into 4 places
  5. Flatten, add egg
  6. Seal and roll properly
  7. Deep Fry on low heat
