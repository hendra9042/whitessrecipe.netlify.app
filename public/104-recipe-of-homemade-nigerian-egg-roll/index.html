The ingredients needed to make Nigerian Egg roll:
  1. Get 1 cup Flour
  2. Get 3 tablespoons Butter
  3. Make ready 1 teaspoon Yeast
  4. Prepare 3 tablespoons Sugar
  5. Prepare Pinch salt
  6. Prepare Hard boiled egg
  7. Get Milk (optional)
  8. Get Spice (white pepper, garlic pepper, paprika) (optional)
Steps to make Nigerian Egg roll:
  1. Prove yeast and sugar
  2. Add flour and butter
  3. You can add those optional ingredients too but I don’t add them myself. I just like my dough simple, sweet and soft.
  4. Knead, roll and cut dough into rounds. Wrap around eggs. And leave to rise for about 30 minutes. N.B: wrap a flat dough. Let it not be too much as the inside won’t fry.
  5. Fry in hot oil while continuously rolling it around the oil until golden brown
