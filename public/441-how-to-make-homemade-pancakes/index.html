Steps to make Pancakes:
  1. Get a bowl, pour your cup of flour into the bowl
  2. Crack an egg and mix
  3. Add a bit of milk and carry on mixing
  4. Every minute or so add a little more milk until you have a smooth mixture
  5. Get a frying pan and put it on a medium to hot heat
  6. Add oil and wait for for it to heat up
  7. Then pour a bit of your mixture into the frying pan and quickly spread it evenly
  8. Flip every 30 seconds or so, until it has a light golden surface
  9. Serve with lemon and sugar
