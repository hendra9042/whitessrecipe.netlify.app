The ingredients needed to make In a flash, shortcut coconut custard pie:
  1. Prepare pie mixture
  2. Make ready 1 cup whole milk
  3. Take 1 cup heavy cream
  4. Get 1 1/4 cup shredded sweetened coconut
  5. Get 1 1/2 cup flour
  6. Make ready 4 large eggs
  7. Make ready 2 tsp pure vanilla
  8. Take 5 tbsp butter, cut into small chunks
  9. Take 2 oz full fat cream cheese
  10. Make ready 3/4 cup sugar
  11. Get 4 dash of allspice
Instructions to make In a flash, shortcut coconut custard pie:
  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Butter and flour a 9" pie pan.
  3. In a blender add all ingredients except for allspice.
  4. Pulse 10 times, then blend 10 seconds.
  5. Pour into pie plate. Add on dashes of allspice. Put plate on cookie sheet and cook for 55 minutes.
  6. Cool 1 hour than refrigerate 1 more hour. Then serve and enjoy.
