The ingredients needed to make Egg roll:
  1. Take 2 cups flour
  2. Take 1 tsp baking powder
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp sugar
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Get 2 tbsp butter
  6. Prepare 6 boiled eggs
  7. Take 1 raw egg
  8. Take 1/2 tsp nutmeg(optional)
  9. Make ready 1/2 cup warm milk

Steps to make Egg roll:
  1. Mix flour,salt,sugar and baking powder. Add butter and mix well
  2. Whisked egg and add water to it. Add water little by little and keep mixing until sticky dough is formed.
  3. Kneed the dough well till it is no longer sticky to hand
  4. Divide the dough into six fletted each dough and put your egg in the middle and mold it well,repeat the process untill your done with all your eggs
  5. Set deep pot on fire and pour oil,a lot it to be hot on a medium flame
  6. Drop your egg roll in hot oil and a lot it to fry,till it is golding brown
