Steps to make Deviled eggs:
  1. In large pot fill with cold water and set eggs in making sure water covers eggs
  2. Turn burner on high and set timer for 20 minutes
  3. The water should be at a full boil at around 10 minutes
  4. When time is up pour off most of the hot water while running it under cold water
  5. Once water is cold put ice on top as fast as you can
  6. Let rest at least 10 minutes
  7. Peal eggs and cut in half
  8. Gently pop out yolks into a bowl
  9. Mix remaining ingredients except paprika
  10. Fill mix into whites
  11. Dusk with paprika
  12. Remember older eggs hard boil the best new eggs poach the best.
