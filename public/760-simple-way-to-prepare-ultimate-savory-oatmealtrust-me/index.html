The ingredients needed to make Savory Oatmeal…trust me:
  1. Take egg
  2. Prepare oats old fashioned
  3. Make ready spinach frozen
  4. Prepare almond milk
  5. Prepare chia seeds
  6. Make ready flax seeds tbl ground
  7. Get lemon juice tbl
  8. Prepare olive oil egg tbl and more for frying
  9. Make ready salt pepper and
  10. Make ready basil julienned
Instructions to make Savory Oatmeal…trust me:
  1. Heat a pan on Medium high heat with a little olive oil. Crack the egg in the pan and cook sunnyside up.
  2. In the meantime, place all the ingredients except for the egg in a bowl. Microwave on 50% power for 2 minutes. Stir and test for appropriate temperature. Microwave again for another minute at 50% power if needed.
  3. Slide the egg on top of the bowl of oatmeal.
  4. Top with basil, ground pepper and a drizzle of olive oil.
  5. Grab a spoon and enjoy
