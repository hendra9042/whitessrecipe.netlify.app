Steps to make Egg roll:
  1. Knead the flour with salt and water. Roll into a ball and flatten it in a circular shape
  2. Heat a pan and cook the rolled dough with oil or ghee. Once cooked on both sides remove and keep aside
  3. Beat the eggs with salt, onions and chilli. Put some oil in the pan and fry the eggs. Once cooked on one side turn it to other side and put the cooked paratha onto it.
  4. Turn it over. Once cooked remove and add some more onions and sauce and roll it. Serve it hot.
