The ingredients needed to make Egg roll:
  1. Take 200 g flour
  2. Take 50 g margarine
  3. Prepare 1 tbspn sugar
  4. Prepare 1/2 tspn baking powder
  5. Prepare 1/4 tspn salt
  6. Get 2 tbspn milk
  7. Take 1/4 nutmeg
  8. Make ready 1/2 cup water
  9. Take 6 pieces egg

Steps to make Egg roll:
  1. Boil your egg, remove the shell and allow to cool
  2. Into a dry bowl, sieve flour and baking powder.Add sugar, nutmeg,and salt to flour and mix together.rub in margarine into the mixture until it is well mixed and resembles fine bread crumbs
  3. Into another bowl,mix water and milk and set aside
  4. Make a well in the center of the flour mixture, pour the mixture of water and milk and mix together with your hands and set aside to rest
  5. Rub your palms with some flour,take certain amount of pastry set aside enough to cover an egg.take an egg place at the center of the pastry,try to cover up the egg with the pastry until is completely covered.
  6. Deep fry in moderately hot vegetable oil until golden brown.
