The ingredients needed to make Marinated Pork Loin Sandwiches:
  1. Make ready Sandwich—————
  2. Prepare 2 hamburger buns
  3. Prepare 1/2 pound pork loin marinated
  4. Make ready 2 teaspoon mayonnaise
  5. Prepare Tomato—————
  6. Take To taste salt
  7. Make ready To taste ground black pepper
  8. Get 2 large Roma tomatoes washed
  9. Prepare As needed vegetable oil to fry in
  10. Take Both————–
  11. Get 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  12. Take 1-2 large eggs
  13. Make ready 2 tablespoons bacon onion sauce see my recipe
Steps to make Marinated Pork Loin Sandwiches:
  1. Heat the oil. Beat the eggs
  2. Slice and season the tomatoes
  3. Slice the pork loin thinly
  4. Dip the tomatoes into the eggs
  5. Then from the egg into the flour
  6. Fry till done
  7. Move to a paper towel to absorb excessive oils.
  8. Now do the same for the pork loin.
  9. Add mayonnaise to the top part of buns.
  10. Add bacon onion sauce to the bottom part of the buns.
  11. Add the loins and fried tomato.
  12. Top with the top of buns and serve. I hope you enjoy!
