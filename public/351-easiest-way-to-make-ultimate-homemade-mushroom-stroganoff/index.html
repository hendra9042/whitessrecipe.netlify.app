The ingredients needed to make Homemade Mushroom Stroganoff:
  1. Prepare wide egg noodles
  2. Prepare small white onion
  3. Prepare baby bella mushrooms
  4. Take beef broth
  5. Prepare Minced garlic
  6. Take sour cream
  7. Prepare flour
  8. Make ready Worcestershire sauce
  9. Take Seasonings to taste
Steps to make Homemade Mushroom Stroganoff:
  1. Cook wide egg noodles Al dente
  2. Melt 1 tbsp. butter in saute pan then add onion and saute for 3 minutes
  3. Add the remaining butter pats, mushrooms, and minced garlic and cook for 7 minutes
  4. In a small bowl whisk together beef broth, flour, and Worcestershire sauce until smooth. Pour mixture into the saute pan and simmer for 5 minutes until slightly thickened.
  5. Stir in the sour cream evenly into the mixture until well combined, take off heat.
  6. Serve mixture over bed of pasta with freshly grated parmesan and enjoy!
