The ingredients needed to make Holiday Egg Nog Pie:
  1. Make ready 2 (4 serving size) vanilla pudding (not instant)
  2. Make ready 2 cup egg nog
  3. Make ready 1 1/4 cup milk
  4. Take 1 9 inch pie shell
  5. Get 1/8 tsp ground nutmeg
  6. Get 1 container whipped topping
  7. Take 1 tbsp light rum (optional)
Steps to make Holiday Egg Nog Pie:
  1. Combine pudding mix, egg nog, milk, and nutmeg in a saucepan.
  2. Cook and stir over medium heat until mixture comes to a FULL BUBBLING boil.
  3. Remove from heat; add rum and cool 5 minutes, stirring twice.
  4. Pour into pie shell. Cover surface of filling with plastic wrap.
  5. Chill at least 3 hours. Garnish with whipped topping and ground nutmeg.
