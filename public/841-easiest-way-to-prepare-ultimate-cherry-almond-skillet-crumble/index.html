The ingredients needed to make Cherry Almond Skillet crumble:
  1. Take 1/2 cup Kodiak power cake mix
  2. Take 1/2 cup halo top vanilla bean ice cream
  3. Make ready 1/2 ounce sliced almonds
  4. Take 1 1/2 tsp Swerve no calorie sweetner
  5. Take 2 tsp almond extract
  6. Make ready 1 egg white
  7. Take 1/2 cup frozen cherries
  8. Get 1 Quest beyond cinnamon bun cereal bar
Steps to make Cherry Almond Skillet crumble:
  1. Melt halo top then add to Kodiak mix
  2. 1 tsp almond extract
  3. Combine well to form a thick dough
  4. Spread out into a 6 inch skillet sprayed well
  5. Combine cherries with no calorie sweetner
  6. Add in almonds and a crumbled up cereal bar
  7. Egg white and tsp of almond extract
  8. Combine well you don't want any cooked whites, the egg white will keep top layer together
  9. Place on top of biscuit layer then bake in a 350 degree oven for 30 minutes
