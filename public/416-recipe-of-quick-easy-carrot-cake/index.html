The ingredients needed to make Easy Carrot Cake:
  1. Make ready 1 1/4 cup miracle whip
  2. Make ready 1 box gluten-free yellow cake mix (reg. works too)
  3. Get 4 eggs or equivalent of egg substitute
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup cold water
  5. Prepare 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  6. Prepare 2 cups finely shredded carrots
  7. Make ready 8 oz cream cheese
  8. Get 1 tbsp vanilla
  9. Get 3 1/2 cups powdered sugar
Instructions to make Easy Carrot Cake:
  1. For cake: heat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Beat miracle whip, cake mix, eggs, water, & cinnamon until well blended. Stir in carrots. Pour into greased 13x9 baking pan.
  3. Bake 30-35 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool completely.
  4. For frosting: beat cream cheese & vanilla at medium speed with electric mixer until well blended. Gradually add sugar, beating until light & fluffy. Frost cake.
