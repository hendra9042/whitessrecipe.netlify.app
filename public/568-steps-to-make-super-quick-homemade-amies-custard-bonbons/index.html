The ingredients needed to make AMIEs Custard Bonbons:
  1. Prepare sweetened codensed milk
  2. Prepare egg yolks
  3. Prepare vanilla or lemon extract
  4. Make ready brown sugar
  5. Take desiccated (flaked) coconut (optional)
Steps to make AMIEs Custard Bonbons:
  1. Combine milk and egg yolks in a double boiler. Cook, add the vanilla or lemon extract, stirring continously , until mixture thickens and hold together. Let it cool. Shape into bite-size balls and set aside.
  2. Place and heat the sugar in a sauce pan and stir well over low-medium heat until the sugar is melted and becomes golden brown.
  3. Dip the yema balls (use a toothpick) in the hot caramel syrup, sprinkle with coconut, and put in a greased pan. (Reheat the syrup to ensure it remains in melting point.) Leave to set. Chill, if desired.
  4. NOTE: You can leave step no. 3. You can directly sprinkle the coconut without dipping the balls into the caramel syrup, if you want.
