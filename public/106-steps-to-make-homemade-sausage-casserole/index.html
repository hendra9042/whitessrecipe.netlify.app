The ingredients needed to make Sausage Casserole:
  1. Take Grands biscuit can
  2. Make ready sausage
  3. Get chopped onion
  4. Get eggs
  5. Get half and half
  6. Make ready to taste salt
  7. Take to taste pepper
  8. Prepare to taste poultry seasoning
  9. Get shredded cheddar cheese
Instructions to make Sausage Casserole:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Grease 9x13 casserole dish.
  3. Pull apart biscuits and layer biscuits in dish.
  4. Brown sausage and onion.
  5. Layer on top of biscuits.
  6. Sprinkle cheese on top of sausage.
  7. Mix eggs, half and half, and seasonings.
  8. Pour egg mixture of sausage and cheese.
  9. Bake and check at 45 minutes.
