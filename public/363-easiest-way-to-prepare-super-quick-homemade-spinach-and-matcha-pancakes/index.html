The ingredients needed to make Spinach and Matcha Pancakes:
  1. Get Spinach
  2. Get matcha powder
  3. Prepare milk
  4. Get eggs
  5. Take vanilla essence
  6. Make ready tblsp maple syrup
  7. Get self raising flour
  8. Prepare baking powder
Instructions to make Spinach and Matcha Pancakes:
  1. In a blender, blend the spinach milk and matcha till smooth.
  2. Add the remaining ingredients. And let the mixture stand for 30 minutes.
  3. On medium heat, using a laddle with a little oil,spoon the mixture onto the pan. Once bubbles have popped, flip the pancakes and cook for 1 minute. Repeat till the end.
