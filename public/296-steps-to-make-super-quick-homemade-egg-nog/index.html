The ingredients needed to make Egg Nog:
  1. Prepare 16 oz Egg Beaters
  2. Take 3/4 cup sugar
  3. Get 2 tbsp rum extract
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  5. Make ready 2 quart half-and-half
  6. Prepare 2 cup Reddi-wip
Instructions to make Egg Nog:
  1. Beat first 4 ingredients in large bowl until sugar is dissolved and mixture is well blended.
  2. Add half-and-half gradually, stirring constantly.
  3. Gently stir in whipped topping until well blended.
  4. Cover and refrigerate until chilled.
  5. Stir with a whisk just before serving.
