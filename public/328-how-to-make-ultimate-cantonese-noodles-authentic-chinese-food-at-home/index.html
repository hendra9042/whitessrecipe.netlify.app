The ingredients needed to make Cantonese Noodles- Authentic Chinese Food at Home:
  1. Prepare Topping:
  2. Prepare Napa cabbage
  3. Get Carrot
  4. Make ready Green pepper
  5. Make ready Quail eggs
  6. Get Onion
  7. Take Bean sprouts
  8. Prepare to 4 grams Wood ear mushrooms
  9. Take Thinly sliced pork rib meat
  10. Prepare Soup:
  11. Make ready and 1/2 liters Water
  12. Take to 3 tablespoons Chinese soup stock
  13. Prepare Soy sauce
  14. Take to 6 tablespoons Oyster sauce
  15. Make ready Pepper
  16. Make ready Other:
  17. Make ready Katakuriko slurry
  18. Prepare servings Ramen noodles
Instructions to make Cantonese Noodles- Authentic Chinese Food at Home:
  1. Chop the topping ingredients into bite-sized pieces. Soak the wood ear mushrooms in hot water to reconstitute.
  2. Put the pork in a frying pan and sauté until cooked through.
  3. Add the rest of the topping ingredients and lightly sauté until evenly coated in oil.
  4. Put the soup ingredients in a pot. Adjust the seasoning with soy sauce and oyster sauce to taste. You can also use salt.
  5. When the vegetables are cooked, add the dissolved katakuriko to thicken to your desired consistency. I like mine very thick.
  6. Boil the noodles, transfer to a bowl, add the soup, then serve.
