Instructions to make Sofie's veg fusion noodle:
  1. Cook the noodles in the hot masala water till tender. At same time fry the thinly sliced zucchini and eggplant and carrot with some salt on a little oil. Stir often but make it grilled
  2. When pasta and veggies are both ready then tranfer the pasta to the veggies and fry a little. Then add the cooking water of the noodles and add the raisins. I also added the onion oil from the little pockets that came with the noodles
  3. Mix well and serve with omelett stripes
