The ingredients needed to make Use Up Egg Whites Making Mini Cakes:
  1. Prepare <<Cocoa Flavor>>
  2. Get Egg whites
  3. Get Granulated sugar
  4. Get Cake flour
  5. Prepare Cocoa powder
  6. Take Almond flour
  7. Take Powdered sugar
  8. Prepare Butter (salted or unsalted, either is ok)
Instructions to make Use Up Egg Whites Making Mini Cakes:
  1. Sift the cake flour and cocoa powder. Put the almond powder and the powdered sugar into a plastic bag and mix.
  2. Whisk the egg whites and granulated sugar until the sugar dissolves.
  3. Add the dry ingredients from Step 1 to the mixture from Step 2. Mix with a whisk. Add cake flour and mix again.
  4. Make browned butter and add it to the mixture from Step 3. Mix with a whisk.
  5. Pour into a mold and bake for 20 minutes in a 190ºC preheated oven.
  6. To make plain flavored cakes, make the batter using 50 g of cake flour.
  7. To make matcha cakes, make the batter using 50 g of cake flour and 5 g of matcha.
  8. To brown the butter, put the butter into a pot and stir continuously over low heat. When the perfect color is reached, place the bottom of the pot over ice water.
