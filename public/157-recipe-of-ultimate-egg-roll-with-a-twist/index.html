The ingredients needed to make Egg roll with a twist:
  1. Prepare 11/2 cup flour
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp sugar
  3. Get to taste Salt
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp butter
  5. Take Water as required
  6. Take 4 eggs
  7. Get Spring onion

Steps to make Egg roll with a twist:
  1. Mix the dry ingredients in a bowl. Add butter mix until its crumby.
  2. Add water to make a dough. Set aside. Boil eggs, peel off the shell & set aside
  3. Divide dough into four parts. Take each part dough, flatten with your hands, place the egg and fold. Leave the excess dough @ the top. Do same for all
  4. Get your spring onion (washed & drained) tie at the end of each egg roll as shown in the pic. Fry until golden brown
  5. Iftar special. Enjoy
