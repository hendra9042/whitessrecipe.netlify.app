The ingredients needed to make Meat Sauce & Scrambled Egg Sandwich:
  1. Take Meat Sauce
  2. Prepare Egg
  3. Prepare Ketchup
  4. Prepare Sliced bread
Instructions to make Meat Sauce & Scrambled Egg Sandwich:
  1. Break the egg into the frying pan and cook together with the meat sauce to make scrambled eggs.
  2. Add ketchup and mix together. Once it has cooled, sandwich it between the bread, and it's done.
