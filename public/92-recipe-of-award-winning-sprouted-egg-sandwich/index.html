The ingredients needed to make Sprouted Egg Sandwich:
  1. Take 1 bunch sprouts
  2. Prepare 2 slice bread
  3. Make ready 1 mayonnaise
  4. Get 1 mustard
  5. Get 1 each egg
  6. Get 1 dash garlic salt
  7. Take 1 dash ground black pepper
  8. Prepare 1 dash salt
Instructions to make Sprouted Egg Sandwich:
  1. Grease pan
  2. Preheat pan to medium
  3. Crack egg in pan
  4. Add mayonnaise to taste on bread
  5. Add spices to egg
  6. Flip egg and cook til yolk doesn't run
  7. Add mustard to taste to bread
  8. Add egg to bread
  9. Put a handful of sprouts on egg
  10. Put other bread slice on top sprouts
  11. Eat
