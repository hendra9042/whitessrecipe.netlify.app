Steps to make sausage breakfast casserole:
  1. preheat oven to 350
  2. spread butter on bread
  3. place in 13 x 9 x 2 inch greased pan
  4. cook sausage until brown, stir to crumble, drain well
  5. spoon over bread sprinkle with cheese
  6. combine eggs, milk,& salt. mix well and pour over cheese
  7. cover and chill overnight
  8. remove from refrigerator 15 minutes before baking
