Instructions to make Pancakes:
  1. In a small microwaveable bowl, melt butter
  2. In a large mixing bowl, put in eggs, butter and milk. Then whisk until fully incorporated
  3. Add in flour, sugar and salt and whisk together
  4. Set frying pan on a low medium heat, (when hot put a tablespoon of oil or butter or half and half)
  5. With a laddle scoop the mix, and place on the pan, when you see bubbles forming on to flip
  6. Cook until golden brown on both sides
  7. When done add your toppings and enjoy!!
