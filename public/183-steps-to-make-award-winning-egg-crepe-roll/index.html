The ingredients needed to make Egg Crepe Roll:
  1. Make ready 7 eggs
  2. Take 1 cup milk
  3. Take 1 cup Maida
  4. Make ready as per taste salt
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  6. Get 1/2 cup chop and boiled carrot
  7. Take 1/2 cup chopped and boiled beans
  8. Get 1/2 cup boiled peas
  9. Get 1/2 cup boiled sweetcorn
  10. Take 1 onion chop
  11. Take 1 green chilli chopped
  12. Get 2 tsp chopped green coriander
  13. Make ready 1 tomato chopped
  14. Prepare As per taste chat masala
  15. Prepare pinch baking powder
  16. Get as required butter
Instructions to make Egg Crepe Roll:
  1. Take a mixing bowl 2 eggs break then add salt and half quantity of pepper mix then add maida mix then add milk and make a semi-liquid batter for lumps free then mix baking powder and 1 tsp chop green coriander keep aside.
  2. Take another mixing bowl then 5 eggs break add salt, pepper, onion, green chilli, coriander and all boiled vegetables mix and whisk.
  3. Take a nonstick pan pour butter and one ladle full of maida batter spread evenly cook for 2 minutes then flip second side then pour one ladle of vegetable batter cover with lid cook for 2 minutes then after 2 minutes flip on another side.
  4. After 2 minutes flip and carefully roll and enjoy.
