The ingredients needed to make Scrumptious Egg Salad Sandwich:
  1. Prepare 2 boiled eggs
  2. Take 1/2 cup chopped spring onion greens
  3. Get 1 tsp mustard powder
  4. Get 1 tsp garlic powder
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp pepper
  6. Take to taste Salt
  7. Make ready 4 slices sandwich bread
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp mayonnaise
Steps to make Scrumptious Egg Salad Sandwich:
  1. Mash the boiled eggs with a fork. Add mayonaisse to this. 1. Add the chopped spring onions, pepper, salt, mustard powder, salt. Mix well.
  2. Place lots of egg salad filling inbetween the 2 slices of bread and serve with tea or coffee.
