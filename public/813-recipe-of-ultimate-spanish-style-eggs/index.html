Instructions to make Spanish style eggs:
  1. Brown the chorizo and ground meat. Add cumin, salt, ketchup, and garlic.
  2. Beat the eggs add 2 tablespoonfuls of water as you beat them
  3. In another pan add your beaten eggs fry, and roll the pan a bit. add water and steam with lid on this is to toughen the egg a bit and to get eggs done.
  4. Remove from heat add meat to it and roll it up shred the cheese on top.
  5. Serve hope you enjoy!
