Instructions to make Toad in a hole:
  1. Add flour to a large bowl.
  2. Add 1 Egg.
  3. Make well in the middle and add some milk and stir with a wooden spoon, add Cajun seasoning and a little black pepper and a little salt, mix all well to make batter
  4. Place sausages to a large deep casserole type dish.
  5. Place in oven for 10 mins at gas, 5 then remove and rest for 10 mins
