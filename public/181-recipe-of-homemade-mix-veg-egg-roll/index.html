The ingredients needed to make Mix Veg Egg Roll:
  1. Get 1 cup Sooji
  2. Take 1/2 cup besan
  3. Get 2 tomatoes chopped
  4. Make ready 1 Capsicum choppt
  5. Make ready 1 onion chopped
  6. Make ready To taste Salt and pepper
  7. Take 1 tsp oregano
  8. Make ready 1 cup water
  9. Prepare 3-4 tbsp oil
  10. Prepare 4 eggs
Steps to make Mix Veg Egg Roll:
  1. Make the batter by mixing Sooji, besan and water. Add salt and water. Let it rest for 10 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile saute all the veggies, add salt, pepper and oregano and keep aside.
  3. Make pancakes with the batter. On a separate tawa, make half fry eggs, sunny side up.
  4. Layer egg on pancakes, then keep veggies and roll. Serve hot.
