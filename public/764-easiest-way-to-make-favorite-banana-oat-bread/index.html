Steps to make Banana oat bread:
  1. Put your oven on at 170°. And then prepare your pan.
  2. Mash the ripe bananas to your liking. Add the eggs one at a time and mix. Add the essence then sugar and oil and mix
  3. Onto your dry ingredients. Put the oats then yoghurt mix well. And finally your flour and b.powder. if the consistency is to hard add more yoghurt. But it should not be watery.
  4. Finalize by adding the raisings. Then transfer your batter to the pan. Level it then sprinkle the flaxseeds.
  5. Put in your oven and bake for 45 to 1hour. Use a toothpick to check if its ready.
  6. When its ready. Remove from the oven and let it cools down. Then you can enjoy
