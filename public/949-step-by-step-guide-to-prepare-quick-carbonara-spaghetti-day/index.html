The ingredients needed to make Carbonara Spaghetti Day:
  1. Make ready Spaghetti
  2. Make ready egg
  3. Get parmesan cheese
  4. Prepare olive oil
  5. Get champignons
  6. Get prawns
  7. Make ready garlic
  8. Get any herbs
Instructions to make Carbonara Spaghetti Day:
  1. Cook the pasta.
  2. While waiting for the pasta, cook the sauce.
  3. Heat garlic & olive oil in a pan. Wait until goldening. Then put herbs, for a while. Then put the prawns.
  4. Break an egg into a bowl. Mix with parmesan cheese for about 5 minutes.
  5. Put the pasta to the pan (also pour 1 cup of pasta water). Turn off the heat. Put the egg sauce & dried oregano. Stir it for a while.
  6. Ready to serve.
