The ingredients needed to make Beef morcon:
  1. Take marinade
  2. Take beef flank
  3. Get calamansi or 1 big lemon
  4. Make ready soy sauce
  5. Get ground black pepper
  6. Get filling
  7. Take cheddar cheese
  8. Prepare pickled cucumber
  9. Prepare carrots
  10. Prepare sausage or chorizo
  11. Get hardboiled egg (sliced into 4 pcs each)
  12. Get morcon sauce
  13. Take red bell pepper
  14. Prepare tomato sauce
  15. Get grated cheese
  16. Make ready pineapple juice
  17. Get sugar
  18. Get water
Steps to make Beef morcon:
  1. Marinade the beef into the mixture of calamansi/lemon,soysauce and pepper for an hour.
  2. After marinating,keep the marinade sauce because you will add it on the morcon sauce mixture later.
  3. Fill the beef with the each slice of the cheese,carrots,pickles,egg and sausage/chorizo.arrange them nicely and roll the beef.
  4. Get a cooking string and tie it around the beef tightly.
  5. Pan seared the beef in olive oil or butter to seal it.
  6. Start mixing the ingredients for the sauce. Put the water and all the sauce ingredients in a caserole including the marinade sauce.
  7. Put the pan seared beef and let it simmer for an hour or more on a low to medium heat until the beef is tender.
  8. Once the beef is cooked put it out and cut the strings. Slice the beef and pour some of the sauce before serving.
