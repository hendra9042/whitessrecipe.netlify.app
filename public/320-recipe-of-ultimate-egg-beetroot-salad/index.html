The ingredients needed to make Egg Beetroot Salad:
  1. Make ready Egg (boiled)
  2. Get Beetroot (boiled)
  3. Prepare Potato (boiled)
  4. Prepare Carrot (boiled)
  5. Prepare Onion (diced)
  6. Take Olive Oil
  7. Take Vinaiger
  8. Get Salt
Steps to make Egg Beetroot Salad:
  1. Grate all the items,except the egg yolk.
  2. Crumble it with olive and vinaigre,beat it till it become sauce like and pour in the salad.
  3. Delicious with baguette (long bread) or Chapati.
