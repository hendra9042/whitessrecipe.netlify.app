Steps to make Shortcake as needed:

  2. Cut butter into flour and sugar and baking powder.
  3. Whisk eggs, sour cream and vanilla together. Then fold into flour mixture just until dry ingredients are moistened.
  4. Drop 1/3 cup dough (greased measuring cup) onto a greased baking sheet.

  6. Serve with ice cream or whipped cream, berries etc
