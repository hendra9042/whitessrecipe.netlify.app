Instructions to make Jalapeno Pickled Eggs:
  1. Put peeled eggs in 2 1/2 quart jar
  2. Boil all ingredients for 15 mnutes
  3. Pour hot liquid over eggs to cover
  4. Add hot water if needed to cover eggs
  5. Marinate in refridgerator for two weeks or longer
  6. for a stronger flavor, poke holes in eggs with a toothpick
