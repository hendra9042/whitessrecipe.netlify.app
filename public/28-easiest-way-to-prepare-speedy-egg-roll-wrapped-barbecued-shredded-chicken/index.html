The ingredients needed to make Egg Roll wrapped barbecued shredded Chicken:
  1. Get 1-1/2 cup leftover chicken shredded in batch88 barbecue sauce see my recipes with the same name
  2. Take 4 large egg roll wraps
  3. Make ready as needed oil to fry
Steps to make Egg Roll wrapped barbecued shredded Chicken:
  1. Cook the shredded chicken and let cool
  2. Heat oil
  3. Lay the wrapper pointing straight up and down, add shredded chicken to the center.
  4. Fold the sides inward. Fold the bottom upwards then roll it up
  5. Lay the flap side down into oil fry till crispy.
  6. Move to a paper towel to absorb excessive oils
  7. Serve I hope you enjoy!
