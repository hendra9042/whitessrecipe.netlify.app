The ingredients needed to make Harusame (Japanese Vermicelli) Salad:
  1. Prepare Harusame Noodles
  2. Make ready Cucumber
  3. Prepare Carrot
  4. Make ready Ham
  5. Prepare Eggs *Make Thin Egg Omelette
  6. Take <Dressing Ingredients>
  7. Prepare Sugar
  8. Take Soy Sauce
  9. Get Rice Vinegar
  10. Get Sesame Oil
  11. Make ready Roasted Sesame Seeds
  12. Make ready Mustard *Optional
Instructions to make Harusame (Japanese Vermicelli) Salad:
  1. Cook Harusame Noodles as instructed. Basically cook in boiling water until turns transparent. Rinse in cold water and drain well.
  2. Cut Cucumber, Carrot and Ham into thin strips.
  3. Make thin Egg Omelette and cut them into thin strips.
  4. Combine all the Dressing Ingredients in a bowl and mix well.
  5. Place everything in a large mixing bowl, add the Dressing and mix well.
