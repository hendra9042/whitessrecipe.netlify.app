The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Scrambler:
  1. Get red potatoes, peeled and sliced
  2. Get met link, sliced (or meat of your choice)
  3. Make ready onions, diced
  4. Prepare green bell peppers, diced
  5. Prepare red bell peppers, diced
  6. Take large eggs
  7. Prepare milk
  8. Take Unsalted butter
  9. Prepare olive oil
  10. Take garlic and onion powder
  11. Take ground pepper and salt
Instructions to make Breakfast Scrambler:
  1. Heat stove top to medium-high heat
  2. In a cast iron skillet, Add two to three tablespoons of unsalted butter and melted.
  3. Peel and slice red potatoes and add to the skillet; fry for 20 to 25 minutes, or to desired doneness.
  4. While cooking potatoes; dice all vegetables and meat.
  5. Cooked the meat in olive oil, garlic powder, and onion powder for 3 minutes; set aside.
  6. In a medium bowl, beat together eggs and milk; now, add all ingredients to the egg mixture and blend together.
  7. In the cast iron skillet with the potatoes in it; (might need to add more butter)
  8. Chop up potatoes into bit size pieces, then add the egg mixture to the potatoes and scramble until desired doneness.
  9. Remove and serve with toast and fruit.
