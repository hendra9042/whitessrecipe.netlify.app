The ingredients needed to make Crock Pot Pizza Casserole:
  1. Take spaghetti sauce
  2. Prepare ground beef or Italian sausage
  3. Take chopped onion
  4. Get mushrooms
  5. Take salt
  6. Make ready garlic powder
  7. Make ready oregano
  8. Take pepper
  9. Take wide egg noodlles cooked, drained
  10. Make ready sliced pepperoni
  11. Take shredded cheddar cheese
  12. Make ready shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese
Instructions to make Crock Pot Pizza Casserole:
  1. Brown meat and onion:drain.
  2. Add spaghetti sauce, mushrooms, salt,garlic powder,oregano, and pepper; heat through.
  3. Spoon approx. 2 1/2 cups mixture into 5 qt. crock pot.
  4. Top with half noodles, pepperoni and cheeses. Repeat layers so that sauce is the top layer.
  5. Cover and cook on high for 1-2 hours or until until cheese melts.
