The ingredients needed to make Scrambled Duck Eggs with Spinach #KidsFriendly:
  1. Make ready 5 duck eggs (or you can use big chicken eggs)
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 cups spinach, washed and patted dry
  3. Prepare 1 garlic, finely minced
  4. Take 1/2 onion, sliced
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp freshly cracked blackpepper
  7. Get 1 tbsp oil
Instructions to make Scrambled Duck Eggs with Spinach #KidsFriendly:
  1. Heat the oil in a pan. Add garlic and onion and stir occasionally until fragrant.
  2. Add the spinach and stir until the leaves wilt.
  3. Crack the eggs and put them inside the pan. Stir until the eggs are set.
