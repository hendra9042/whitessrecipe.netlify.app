The ingredients needed to make Hearty Vegetarian Scramble:
  1. Make ready 2 large eggs
  2. Get 1 Handful of spinach
  3. Get 1/4 cup Diced mushrooms
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup Cooked quinoa
  5. Prepare 1 pinch Salt
  6. Make ready 1 pinch Pepper
  7. Get 1 as needed Shredded cheese
  8. Take 1 Splash of milk or milk substitute

Instructions to make Hearty Vegetarian Scramble:
  1. Beat eggs together. Add a splash of milk.
  2. Spray pan with cooking spray and heat over medium high heat.
  3. Pour egg mixture in pan and add spinach and mushrooms. Let it cook 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add remainder of ingredients except for cheese. Mix continuously.
  5. When eggs are cooked to desired doneness sprinkle with cheese and serve.
