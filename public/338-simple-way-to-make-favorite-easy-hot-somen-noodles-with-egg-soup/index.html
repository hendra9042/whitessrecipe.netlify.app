The ingredients needed to make Easy, Hot Somen Noodles with Egg Soup:
  1. Take bundle Somen noodles
  2. Take to 2 Egg
  3. Make ready For soup
  4. Take ・Japanese soup stock
  5. Get ・ Usukuchi soy sauce
  6. Prepare ・ Salt
  7. Prepare ・Cooking sake
  8. Make ready ・ Sugar
Instructions to make Easy, Hot Somen Noodles with Egg Soup:
  1. Cook the somen noodles with plenty of hot water following the directions on the package, and rinse under running water and drain.
  2. Put all the soup ingredients into a pot and bring to the boil.
  3. Add the cooked somen noodles in the pot and bring the soup to the boil again. Add the beaten egg while the soup is boiling, and turn the heat off immediately.
  4. Serve in a noodle bowl and sprinkle with chopped scallion if you have any.
