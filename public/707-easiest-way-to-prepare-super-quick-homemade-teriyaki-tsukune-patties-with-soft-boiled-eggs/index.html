Instructions to make Teriyaki Tsukune Patties With Soft Boiled Eggs:
  1. In a bowl, knead the ingredients marked with ※, and shape into 12 patties. Mix the ingredients marked with ● in a dish.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, and brown the patties from step 1 on both sides over medium heat while covered with a lid.
  3. Once they have browned nicely, add in the mixed ingredients from ●, and cook the patties while rotating them 1-2 times until they are evenly coated with the teriyaki sauce.
  4. Transfer to a plate and arrange them with white sesame, green onions, and a soft-boiled egg. Eat it while dipping the tsukune patties into an egg yolk.
