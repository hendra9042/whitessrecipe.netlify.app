The ingredients needed to make Banana Cake cookies:
  1. Make ready maida/all-purpose flour
  2. Take baking powder
  3. Prepare baking soda
  4. Prepare flax egg(one tbsp flax seed powder mix with three tbsp hot water)
  5. Prepare sugar
  6. Prepare buttermilk
  7. Get medium ripe banana
  8. Take vanilla extract
  9. Get nutmeg powder
  10. Get oil
  11. Make ready White chocolate
  12. Take Milkmaid
Instructions to make Banana Cake cookies:
  1. Mash the banana nicely
  2. In a bowl combine banana mash vanilla essence sugar oil and whisk it well till combined.Lastly add the flax egg and whisk well
  3. Sift together maida baking powder baking soda and nutmeg powder in a separate bowl
  4. Now add dry flour mix to banana mix alternating with the buttermilk
  5. Mix by folding method till you use the entire dry flour
  6. Prepare a baking tray with little oil and line a baking sheet or paper
  7. At this point preheat the oven too at 180degree for ten min
  8. Now pour the cake batter in a baking tray that is prepared already
  9. Tap it well and bake the cake for 25 to 30 min
  10. Once the cake is done take it out on a rack till it cools down
  11. For the amount of maida used a dense cake is expected so no need to panic
  12. Now break the cake grind it in mixer
  13. This crumbled cake powder is mixed with milkmaid shape it as you like it and bake once more for 5 min to seven min both sides
  14. Top it with white chocolate and coloured sprinkles
