The ingredients needed to make Pizza Casserole:
  1. Prepare 14 oz jars
  2. Get 16 oz egg noodles
  3. Prepare 2 can pizza sauce
  4. Get 1 lb ground beef or sausage
  5. Get 1 medium onion, chopped
  6. Make ready 1 can cheddar cheese soup
  7. Get 2 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  8. Prepare 4 oz pepperoni
  9. Make ready 4 oz canadian bacon
Instructions to make Pizza Casserole:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. Cook the egg noodles.
  3. Brown the ground beef and onion.
  4. Combine all the ingredients except mozzaella cheese, pepperoni, and Canadian bacon.
  5. Place in a 4-quart casserole.
  6. Add the cheese.
  7. Chop up your pepperoni and Canadian bacon and add to the casserole.
  8. Bake at 350° for 45 min.
  9. You may add some more mozzarella cheese the last 15 minutes if you would like.
