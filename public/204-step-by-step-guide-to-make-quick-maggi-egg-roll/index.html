The ingredients needed to make Maggi Egg Roll:
  1. Prepare 3 eggs
  2. Get 1 bowl cooked maggi noodles
  3. Prepare 4 tbsp onion chopped
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp tomato chopped
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp coriander leaves chopped
  6. Get 1 tsp green chillies chopped
  7. Take 1/4 teaspoon red chilli powder
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp grated carrots
  9. Prepare 1 tsp maggi masala
  10. Take as per taste salt
  11. Prepare 2 tbsp oil
Instructions to make Maggi Egg Roll:
  1. Take bowl. Add eggs and beat it well. Now add chopped onion, tomato, green coriander, green chilies,salt and maggi masala and red chilli powder, mix all together.
  2. Heat oil in pan. Pour egg batter on the pan and spread it.Cook on a medium heat till golden brown and spot appear on both side.
  3. Now spread cooked maggi noodles and grated carrots. Spread it all over omelette. Now gently roll the omelette slowly. Cut it.
  4. Maggi Omelette Roll is ready.
