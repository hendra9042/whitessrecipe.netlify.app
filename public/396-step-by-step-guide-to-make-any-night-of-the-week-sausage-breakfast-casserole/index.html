The ingredients needed to make Sausage breakfast casserole:
  1. Get eggs beaten
  2. Get milk
  3. Take red pepper chopped (optional)
  4. Take onion chopped finely
  5. Get medium or small potatoes diced finely
  6. Get low fat cheddar cheese
  7. Get loose sausage browned and drained
  8. Prepare Salt and pepper
Steps to make Sausage breakfast casserole:
  1. Brown sausage, onion, potatoes, and peppers (and drain if needed)
  2. Wisk eggs, milk, and seasoning and half the bag of cheese
  3. Place cooked sausage and veggies in greased casserole dish
  4. Pour egg mixture over and then top with remaining cheese
  5. Bake at 350 for 55 min. Or cover and bake next morning for fresh breakfast
