The ingredients needed to make Scrambled eggs:
  1. Take 3 Eggs
  2. Prepare Sausages
  3. Make ready Butter
  4. Take Mushroom
  5. Make ready Sundried tomatoes
  6. Prepare Parsely
  7. Get Dill
  8. Prepare Roumi cheese
  9. Make ready Cheddar cheese
Steps to make Scrambled eggs:
  1. Cut all the ingredients (except eggs)
  2. Butter the pan and break the eggs and Add the rest of the ingredients.
  3. Cook to desired consistency and serve with multigrain toast
