The ingredients needed to make Medifast scones:
  1. Prepare 4 tsp low calorie butter spread
  2. Take 4 tsp Splenda or stevia
  3. Take 1/4 cup sugar free maple syrup
  4. Take 1 egg
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking powder
  6. Get 2 packages Medifast oatmeal
  7. Take 1/4 tsp cinnamon
