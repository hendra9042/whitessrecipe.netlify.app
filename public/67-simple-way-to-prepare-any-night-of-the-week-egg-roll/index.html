The ingredients needed to make Egg roll:
  1. Prepare 2 cups flour
  2. Make ready 2 tablespoon vegetable oil
  3. Get Pinch salt
  4. Take 1.5 tablespoon sugar
  5. Make ready 1/4 cup milk
  6. Prepare 1 teaspoon baking powder
  7. Take 3 Cooked egg
  8. Take Oil for frying

Steps to make Egg roll:
  1. Put in your flour, baking powder, sugar and salt….mix thoroughly
  2. Add in your oil and egg - Mix thoroughly
  3. Put in the milk bit by bit until you make a dough, allow dough to rest for about 10 minutes
  4. Divide dough into 3 or 6…am using only 3 eggs so I divided mine into 3 - Take a dough open and place the egg then enclose it…ensure the egg is properly closed
  5. Heat your oil on medium - low heat then deep fry your egg roll
