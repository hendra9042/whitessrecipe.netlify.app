The ingredients needed to make Scrambled Eggs with Spinach and Cherry Tomatoes:
  1. Make ready 6 eggs
  2. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  3. Prepare 1/2 tsp pepper
  4. Take 2 cup chopped spinach
  5. Take 6 cherry tomatoes, halved
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp oil (they used fermented garlic oil)
  7. Take 1 onion, diced
Instructions to make Scrambled Eggs with Spinach and Cherry Tomatoes:
  1. Combine all ingredients except the oil. Stir well.
  2. Heat oil in a pan (medium)
  3. Pour the eggs to the pan and use a wooden spatula to scramble the eggs.
