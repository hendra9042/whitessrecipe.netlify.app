The ingredients needed to make Turkey Meatloaf:
  1. Make ready 1 1/4 lb Turkey Meat
  2. Get 2 tbsp butter
  3. Make ready 3 Green Onions
  4. Make ready 1/4 cup Bell Peppers
  5. Make ready 3 clove garlic
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup onion, chopped
  7. Get 1/4 cup Planco Bread Crumbs
  8. Make ready 1/4 cup Oatmeal
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup ketchup
  10. Take 1 Egg
  11. Make ready 2 tbsp worcestershire sauce
  12. Take 2 tbsp brown sugar
  13. Make ready 2 tsp dry mustard or Dijon Mustard
  14. Take 1/3 cup chicken broth
  15. Take 5 Baby Blond Potatos
  16. Prepare Salt and Pepper
  17. Take 2 tsp paprika
  18. Get 2 tsp garlic powder
  19. Prepare 2 tsp onion powder
  20. Make ready 2 tsp basil
  21. Get 1 tsp thyme
  22. Take 1 packages meatloaf seasoning g mix (above spice substitute)

Instructions to make Turkey Meatloaf:
  1. Saute all Veggies in butter little Evoo
  2. Boil potatoes then cut into quarters add to meat.
  3. Add chicken broth dry mustard to meat enough broth to keep meat moist.
  4. Add All ingredients mix to meat.
  5. Cover with foil cook for 55 minutes at 375°F.
