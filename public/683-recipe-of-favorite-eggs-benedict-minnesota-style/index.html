The ingredients needed to make Eggs Benedict (Minnesota style)!!!!:
  1. Get 2 English muffins (split, toasted)
  2. Prepare 1 lb hamburger
  3. Take 5 strips bacon cut in half
  4. Prepare 8 eggs
  5. Get 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  6. Make ready 5 tablespoons clarified butter
  7. Make ready 1 teaspoon liquid smoke
  8. Make ready 3/4 teaspoon black pepper
  9. Take 1/4 cup white vinegar

Steps to make Eggs Benedict (Minnesota style)!!!!:
  1. Boil 4 cups water with 1/4 cup white vinegar
  2. Fry bacon, drain, set aside
  3. Mix liquid smoke, black pepper and hamburger, make into pattys and fry
  4. Seperate 4 egg yolks put in small kettle on low heat add lemon juice and 1 teaspoon clarified butter. If to high of heat the yolks will cook (you dont want that) I added a little pepper and parsley to this
  5. With boiling water add 2 eggs carefully not to break yolks, poach
  6. Turn pattys so the dont burn
  7. Take split muffins add patty
  8. Add poached eggs
  9. Bacon and drizzle on your hollandaise
  10. Enjoy!
