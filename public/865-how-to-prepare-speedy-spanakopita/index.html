The ingredients needed to make Spanakopita:
  1. Prepare Half pound feta cheese
  2. Make ready 1 pound ricotta cheese
  3. Prepare 10 ounces baby spinach cooked and chopped
  4. Get 1 bunch scallions finally sliced
  5. Make ready 1 bunch green chili finally chopped
  6. Prepare 1/3 cup freshly grated Parmesan
  7. Prepare 1 egg
  8. Make ready 1 good grating nutmeg
  9. Prepare Half cup of fresh white breadcrumbs
