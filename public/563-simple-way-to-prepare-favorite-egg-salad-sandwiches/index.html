The ingredients needed to make Egg Salad Sandwiches:
  1. Prepare 6 Hard Boiled Eggs
  2. Take 1/4 cup green onion
  3. Get 1/4 cup celery
  4. Get 1/4 cup Mayonaise
  5. Take 1/4 tsp Ground Black Pepper
  6. Make ready 1 tsp Prepared Yellow Mustard
  7. Get 1/4 tsp Lemon Juice

Instructions to make Egg Salad Sandwiches:
  1. Hard boil 6 large eggs & chill in cold water bath, changing water out frequently.
  2. Dice celery & green onion and set aside.
  3. Combine mayonaise, mustard, black pepper, lemon juice in large bowl.
  4. Peel eggs & dice into mayonaise mixture.
  5. Fold in eggs, mayonaise mixture, green onions & celery.
