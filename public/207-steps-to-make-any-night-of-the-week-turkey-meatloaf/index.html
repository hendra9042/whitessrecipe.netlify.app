The ingredients needed to make Turkey Meatloaf:
  1. Make ready Ground Turkey Breast
  2. Make ready Egg Beaters
  3. Prepare Mrs Dash Chipotle Seasoning
  4. Get Lite Salt
  5. Get Black Pepper
Steps to make Turkey Meatloaf:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 or warm grill.
  2. Mix all ingredients, without over mixing.
  3. Shape into burgers or meatloaf.
  4. Cook in oven for 20 min or until 160.
  5. Let rest, slice and serve.
  6. For burgers, grill 2 minutes each side.
  7. 1 protein, 4 servings.
