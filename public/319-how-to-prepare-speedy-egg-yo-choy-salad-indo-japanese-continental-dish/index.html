The ingredients needed to make Egg Yo Çhoy salad : Indo Japanese continental dish:
  1. Take Amaranth green leaf
  2. Take Coconut grated
  3. Make ready Finely chopped onions
  4. Prepare Groundnuts
  5. Get Ginger garlic paste
  6. Get eggs
  7. Get Mustard, Curry leaf, Bengal gram,cummin & Salt
  8. Take Refined cooking Oil
  9. Take Red chilli powder
Instructions to make Egg Yo Çhoy salad : Indo Japanese continental dish:
  1. Wash green leaf with turmeric for 2-3 times in plan water.
  2. Grind coconut in to fine powder
  3. Add 3 table spoons of oil with mustard,cumin and ginger garlic paste. Now add onion and fry till golden brown
  4. Now add finely chopped green leaf and mix it well on mideum flame.
  5. Once the raw smell goes off from green leaves add salt to taste along with fried groundnuts.
  6. Now add two raw eggs with chilli powder and mix it on low flames till egg losses it's raw smell.
  7. Finally add coconut powder with curry leaves before serving.
