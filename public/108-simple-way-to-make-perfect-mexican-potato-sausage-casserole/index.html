The ingredients needed to make Mexican Potato Sausage Casserole:
  1. Prepare browned sausage
  2. Get green pepper
  3. Make ready southwest style hash browns
  4. Prepare shredded cheddar
  5. Prepare eggs
  6. Prepare milk
Steps to make Mexican Potato Sausage Casserole:
  1. preheat oven to 375
  2. brown sausage and saute green pepper
  3. place two layers potatoes, sausage, cheese
  4. pour eggs and milk over layers
  5. cover and bake at 375 45 minutes. add more cheese and cook 15 minutes longer.
