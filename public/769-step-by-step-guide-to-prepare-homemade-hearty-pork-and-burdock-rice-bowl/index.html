The ingredients needed to make Hearty Pork and Burdock Rice Bowl:
  1. Take 200 grams Pork (offcuts)
  2. Prepare 2 Burdock root
  3. Make ready 1 small piece Ginger
  4. Get 3 rice bowls worth Cooked rice (warm)
  5. Get Seasoning Ingredients:
  6. Make ready 4 tbsp Soy sauce
  7. Take 3 tbsp Sake
  8. Prepare 3 tbsp Mirin
  9. Get 1 tbsp Sugar
  10. Take 1 tsp Japanese soup stock (granules)
  11. Take Toppings:
  12. Take 3 Eggs
  13. Make ready 2 stalks Green onions (finely sliced)
  14. Prepare 1 Ichimi chilli pepper powder, white sesame seeds
Instructions to make Hearty Pork and Burdock Rice Bowl:
  1. Cut the burdock into long thin shavings and soak in water. You can easily do this with a vegetable peeler. You can also thinly slice it diagonally.
  2. Cut the pork into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Finely chop the ginger.
  4. Heat a small amount of oil (not listed), and sauté Step 3 until fragrant. Sauté over low heat, being careful not to let it burn.
  5. Turn the heat to medium and stir-fry the pork until the color changes. Once the color changes, add the burdock and stir-fry.
  6. When everything is covered with the fat from the pork evenly, add all the seasoning ingredients.
  7. Simmer while stirring occasionally until most of the liquid is gone. This way, the flavor will be strong.
  8. Transfer on top of some rice in a rice bowl and it's done. Top with a poached or raw egg or with an egg yolk, whichever you prefer.
