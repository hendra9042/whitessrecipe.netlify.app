The ingredients needed to make Keto Vanilla Chocolate Custard Pie:
  1. Take 2 1/2 cups full cream
  2. Get 1 1/2 cups powdered artificial sweetener
  3. Take 6 egg yolks
  4. Prepare Vanilla
  5. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
  6. Take 4 oz cream cheese
  7. Prepare A few unsweetened chocolate pieces
  8. Get crust:
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup coconut flour
  10. Make ready 1/2 cup powdered artificial sweetener
  11. Take 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  12. Make ready 1/2 cup melted butter
  13. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt
Instructions to make Keto Vanilla Chocolate Custard Pie:
  1. Preheat oven to 350. Mix all crust ingredients and press in pie dish. Bake for a few minutes until set.
  2. Mix first five of the custard ingredients in a saucepan and bring to a boil on medium heat. Add cream cheese and a few chocolate pieces and allow to melt. She said she swirled in a few chocolate pieces. Allow custard to cool just a bit. Pour custard into pie crust and allow to refrigerate til completely cooled.
