The ingredients needed to make Boston cream pie:
  1. Take Custard Filling
  2. Get 2 cups milk
  3. Take vanilla bean split or 1.5 tsp vanilla extact
  4. Prepare 2/3 cup sugar
  5. Take 1/3 cup all purpose flour
  6. Make ready 1/8 tsp salt
  7. Take 2 large eggs
  8. Get 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp rum
  10. Take cake batter
  11. Get 2 cups sifted flour
  12. Get 1 cup sugar
  13. Make ready 2 tsp baking powder
  14. Prepare 1/2 salt
  15. Prepare 1/3 cup unsalted butter (room temp)
  16. Make ready 3/4 cup milk
  17. Make ready 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  18. Get 1 large egg
  19. Take chocolate ganache
  20. Get 2 cups bitter sweet chocolate
  21. Make ready 1 tbsp brute cocoa powder
  22. Take 1 tbsp butter
  23. Prepare 1/4 cup heavy cream
  24. Get 1 tbsp bourbon (optional)
Instructions to make Boston cream pie:
  1. FOR CAKE BATTER: mix all your wet ingredients and then add to your dry, mix until smoothe. Divide the mix into two 8 inch spring pans. Bake in a 350° pre heated oven for 20 mins. Let sit until cool
  2. FOR CUSTARD: Add all ingredients in a heavy bottom sauce pan, on low heat continue stirring until it starts to thicken. Let cool
  3. FOR GANACHE: Add all ingredients in a heavy bottom skillet on low heat and stir until melted
  4. TO BUILD: 1. On serving platter lay one half of the cake 2. Spread your custard 3. Top with the other cake 4. Pour your ganache over the top and spread evenly. Slice and enjoy!
  5. Note* You can also bake these in muffin pans and turn these into Boston cream pie mini's just build them the same way and maybe double the ganache recipe.
