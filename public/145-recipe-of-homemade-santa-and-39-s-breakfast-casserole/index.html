The ingredients needed to make Santa's Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Get pork sausage
  2. Make ready eggs
  3. Make ready half and half…. or milk
  4. Prepare dry mustard
  5. Make ready salt
  6. Take black pepper
  7. Prepare hashbrown (shredded or southern style)
  8. Take nutmeg
  9. Get paprika
  10. Take french or italian bread…. stale bread is fine… cubed
  11. Prepare onion, minced
  12. Take shredded cheddar cheese
  13. Get cayenne pepper… if you use spicy pork sausage dont use cayenne….. CAYENNE IS OPTIONAL
Steps to make Santa's Breakfast Casserole:
  1. fry sausage with onion and bell pepper. drain sausage mixture.
  2. *** I put sausage in colandar and let it drain while I put the eggs, milk, and all the seasoning in a bowl and beat.
  3. stir in drained sausage, hashbrowns, bread, & cheese.
  4. pour mixture in lightly buttered 8x8 baking pan. cover and refrigerate overnight.
  5. remove from refrigerator 1 hr before baking. put in a 325° oven and bake for 75 minutes, until eggs are lightly brown.
  6. knife inserted in the center should come out clean. cool for 10 minutes and serve.
  7. **** I serve with a bundt cake pan with monkey bread….
  8. **** can be served with toast and Sasha's Berry Butter on profile… let the butter get room tempature… yum
