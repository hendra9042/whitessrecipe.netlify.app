The ingredients needed to make Egg Salad:
  1. Prepare 2 boiled eggs
  2. Get 1 tomato
  3. Get 1 cucumber
  4. Get 1/2 cup cabbage
  5. Prepare Salt to taste
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp lemon juice
Steps to make Egg Salad:
  1. Boiled eggs and cut them in pieces.
  2. Now in a bowl add tomatoes, cucumbers, cabbage, Salt, pepper, lemon juice, eggs and mix well.
  3. And serve immediately. This is good when you don't want to eat oily foods.
