The ingredients needed to make Egg Custard Puff Tarts:
  1. Take hot water
  2. Get Confectioners sugar to taste
  3. Prepare milk
  4. Take salt
  5. Get eggs, beaten + sifted
  6. Prepare vanilla extract
  7. Take Puff pastry
Steps to make Egg Custard Puff Tarts:
  1. Preheat oven to 400F.
  2. Thaw puff pastry. Cut pastry sheet into twelve 3x3 squares. Line pastry squares into muffin cups to create a shell.
  3. In a mixing bowl combine hot water, sugar, salt, milk, eggs, and vanilla extract. Pour mixture into pastry shell leaving approximately 1 cm at the top. Bake for approximately 25 mins until custard sets and pastry is crisp.
