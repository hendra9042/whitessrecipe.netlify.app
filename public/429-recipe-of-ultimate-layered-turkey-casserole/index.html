The ingredients needed to make Layered Turkey Casserole:
  1. Prepare 8 slices white bread
  2. Get 4 cups shredded cooked turkey
  3. Get 5 tbsp butter, divided
  4. Get 8 oz canned mushrooms, drained
  5. Take 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  6. Make ready 4 oz sliced cheddar cheese
  7. Make ready 6 oz sliced monterrey jack/colby
  8. Prepare 1 1/2 cups soy milk (or milk of your choice)
  9. Take 1 can (10.75 oz) cream of chicken
  10. Make ready 1 can (10.75 oz) cream of mushroom
  11. Prepare 3 eggs, beaten (or equivalent of egg substitute)
  12. Prepare 2/3 cup bread crumbs
Steps to make Layered Turkey Casserole:
  1. Spray a 13x9 baking dish. Arrange bread slices in prepared baking dish. Sprinkle turkey over bread.
  2. Melt 2 tbsp butter in medium skillet. Cook mushrooms in butter for 5 minutes. Spoon over turkey.
  3. Spread mayonnaise over top of mushrooms. Arrange cheese on top of mayonnaise.
  4. Combine milk, cream of chicken, cream of mushroom, & eggs in a large bowl. Mix well. Pour on top of cheese. Chill civered for 3 hrs.
  5. Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Bake casserole for 1 1/4 hrs.
  6. Melt 3 tbsp of butter. Add bread crumbs & mix until well combined. Sprinkle over casserole. Bake for 15 minutes longer.
