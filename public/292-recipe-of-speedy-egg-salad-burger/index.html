Instructions to make Egg salad burger:
  1. To make the burgers we will first make egg salad. Chop three boiled eggs, one onion and some green coriander.
  2. In a mixing bowl collect boiled eggs, onion, green coriander, mayonnaise, salt and pepper. Mix well. Egg salad is ready.
  3. Now to make the patties, cover a tray with cling wrap. Take a round cookie cutter or a ring. Place the ring on the cling wrap. Put 2-3 tbsp of salad in the ring and press lightly with a spoon to flatten it out. Make all the patties like this.
  4. Now cover the patties lightly with the cling wrap and freeze for 3-4 hours minimum. I did it overnight. This is how they came out in the morning nice and firm.
  5. Now grind the bread slices and cornflakes in a grinder to get crumbs. And make a slurry of water and maida.
  6. Heat oil in a kadai. Dip the patty in maida slurry and then roll in bread crumbs. Repeat the process. Deep fry till golden and crisp.
  7. Remove on an absorbent paper. Immediately keep the cheese slice on the hot patty.
  8. Cut the buns horizontally into halves. Heat a tawa, melt some butter and roast the buns with the cut side down. Roast till golden brown and crisp.
  9. Take the bottom portion of the bun, spread some tomato sauce and mayonnaise.
  10. Now add some lettuce and place the patty on the Lettuce.
  11. Add some onion and tomato slices. Add some more lettuce if you like. Cover with the top half of the bun and secure with a bamboo skewer. Finish all the burgers like this. Enjoy yummy and juicy egg salad burger.
