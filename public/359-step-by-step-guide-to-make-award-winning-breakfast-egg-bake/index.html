The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Egg Bake:
  1. Make ready eggs
  2. Take evaporated milk
  3. Get dry mustard
  4. Take black pepper
  5. Get salt
  6. Get onion, chopped
  7. Prepare green pepper, chopped (use a small pepper)
  8. Make ready shredded cheddar cheese
  9. Take shredded Mexican cheese
  10. Make ready Butter cooking spray
Instructions to make Breakfast Egg Bake:
  1. In a blender combine : eggs, evaporated milk, dry mustard, black pepper and salt, blend well…..
  2. Pour egg mixture into a 9 X 13 glass baking dish that has been sprayed with butter cooking spray…..
  3. Sprinkle over the top of egg mixture: chopped onion, green pepper and both cheeses…..
  4. Bake in a preheated 325 degree oven for 40 minutes, or until eggs are set…..
  5. Let eggs rest for 10 minutes….
