Steps to make Potato & egg salad:
  1. Peel the potato skin and cut them into 1/4pieces. Boil the potatoes in a pot till it's soft enough to mash it. (10-15 minutes)
  2. Make boiled eggs in another pot and remove the shell.
  3. Mash both potatoes and eggs in a bowl, with a fork. Leave some lumps for texture.
  4. Mix sliced onions and other optional vegetables into the bowl.
  5. Add mayonnaise and vinegar. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Enjoy!!
