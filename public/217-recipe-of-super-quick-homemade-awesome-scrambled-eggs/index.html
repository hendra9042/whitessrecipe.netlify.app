The ingredients needed to make Awesome scrambled eggs:
  1. Prepare 10 grams Salted Butter
  2. Make ready 2 large Eggs
  3. Prepare 175 ml Low Fat Milk
  4. Get 1 pinch Chili Flakes
  5. Take 1 pinch Coarse Black Pepper
  6. Take 1 pinch Salt
Steps to make Awesome scrambled eggs:
  1. Heat pan. Low heat. Insert butter. Wait till melt.
  2. Put in the eggs. Immediately put in the Low Fat Milk
  3. Put in the chili flakes, coarse black pepper and salt.
  4. Stir softly (dont overly scramble)
  5. Serve in plate with medium cooked look.
