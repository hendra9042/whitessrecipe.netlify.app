The ingredients needed to make Chicken Egg Roll:
  1. Take FOR covering
  2. Prepare 3/4 cup wheat flour
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  4. Get 2 tbsp oil
  5. Take As per taste Salt
  6. Take For filling (chicken tikka)
  7. Make ready 200 grams chicken boneless
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp hung curd
  9. Get 1 tbsp tandoori masala
  10. Prepare 1 tsp kashmiri chilli powder
  11. Take 1 tsp cumin powder
  12. Make ready 1 tsp lemon juice
  13. Make ready 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  14. Make ready 1 tbsp kasuri methi
  15. Get as per taste Salt
  16. Prepare 1 tbsp oil for tikka
  17. Make ready 2 medium sized onion(thin slices)
  18. Get 2 chopped green chillies
  19. Make ready 2 tbsp coriander leaves
  20. Make ready 2 tbsp lemon juice
  21. Take 2 tbsp tomato ketchup
  22. Make ready 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  23. Take 1/4 cup oil for frying roll covering
  24. Take 4 eggs
  25. Get as per taste Salt
Instructions to make Chicken Egg Roll:
  1. In a bowl take ingredients for a roll covering. Mix and make a semi-soft dough by adding some water. Cover and keep aside.
  2. In another bowl take chicken pieces, add curd, lemon juice, and all the spices mentioned. Add salt also and mix well. Marinate for 30 minutes.
  3. After 30 minutes, arrange chicken pieces into skewers. Heat a non -stick pan. Add oil and grill skewers on low -medium flame till done. Keep aside.
  4. Now prepare to cover of egg roll. Divide dough into 4 equal portions. Take 1 portion. Roll out into a roti. Make spirals and roll like a lachcha paratha.
  5. Heat a tawa. Put the paratha on tawa and cook 1 side for 1 minute. Flip and pour 1 egg (blended)on the paratha. Spread on the whole paratha. Flip after 1 minute and fry on both sides after adding some oil. Take out and keep aside. Prepare all parathas in the same way.
  6. Making chicken egg roll: Put 1 tsp tomato ketchup on 1 paratha. Add 2 tbsp sliced onion, some chopped green chilies, and coriander leaves. Put chicken tikka of 1 skewer on onion slices. Add lemon juice and 1 tbsp mayonnaise.
  7. Roll and cover the one end with a kitchen napkin or aluminum foil. Serve.
