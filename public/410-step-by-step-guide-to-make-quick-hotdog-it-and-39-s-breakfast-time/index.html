The ingredients needed to make Hotdog! It's Breakfast Time:
  1. Get Hotdogs—————-
  2. Make ready hotdogs 4 of them
  3. Prepare bread
  4. Get Bacon 4 slices
  5. Prepare large eggs
  6. Prepare kosher salt
  7. Prepare ground black pepper
  8. Make ready oil to fry in
  9. Take Grits—————————-
  10. Make ready grits
  11. Prepare water
  12. Get kosher salt
  13. Prepare butter
  14. Get Potatoes——————–
  15. Take kosher salt
  16. Prepare new potatoes, white
  17. Take Extra needed———————
  18. Take butter
Instructions to make Hotdog! It's Breakfast Time:
  1. Bring the water with salt to boil and stir in the grits. Cook stirring often to eliminate lumps. After 5-7 minutes they should be ready to remove from heat and add butter.
  2. Beat the eggs and add the salt and pepper. Add the hotdog to a slice of bread. Heat the oil.
  3. Add some toothpicks.
  4. Dip the bread into the egg and then wrap with bacon removing the toothpicks as you wrap it.
  5. Fry the hotdogs open side of bread down. 3 minutes.
  6. Turn and fry the opposite side. 3 minutes.
  7. Turn on side 3 minutes till all is fried move to a paper towel to absorb excessive amounts of oil.
  8. Add the 2 tablespoons of butter to a pan melt and scramble the remaining eggs. Set aside.
  9. Quarter the potatoes and fry in the oil you fried the hotdogs in.
  10. Serve the plate here is for my oldest granddaughter, I hope you enjoy!!
