The ingredients needed to make Mixed colorful salad:
  1. Take 1 head Romaine lettuce
  2. Prepare 1 pcs carrots
  3. Prepare 100 gr cucumber
  4. Get 50 gr Corn (can)
  5. Take 3 pcs medium tomato
  6. Take 3-4 pcs hard boiled eggs
  7. Take 1 can tuna fish (100gr)
  8. Prepare 3 tbsp olive oil
  9. Take 1-2 tsp Salt
  10. Make ready 1/2 tsp Pepper
  11. Take 1/2 tsp Lemon juice
  12. Prepare Pinch sugar

Steps to make Mixed colorful salad:
  1. Wash and dry all vegetables
  2. Sliced all vegetables into bite size.
  3. Mix all vegetables in a bowl except tuna fish and egg
  4. Add olive oil, salt, pepper, lemon juice and sugar in a bowl. Pour into salad bowl, mix it well and adjust the taste.
  5. Top up with tuna fish and boiled eggs
