The ingredients needed to make Grandma Price's Egg Casserole:
  1. Take Wheat or white bread (include crust)
  2. Take Shredded cheddar
  3. Take Bacon or ground sausage
  4. Prepare Eggs
  5. Prepare Milk
  6. Take Salt
  7. Take Pepper
  8. Make ready Onion flakes
  9. Make ready Dry mustard
  10. Make ready Diced green chilies
Steps to make Grandma Price's Egg Casserole:
  1. Mix the following in a bowl (May use mixer): - Eggs - Milk - Salt - Pepper - Onion flakes - Mustard
  2. Grease 9x13 casserole. Preheat oven to 350*.
  3. Tear bread into small pieces. Layer bread, cheese, bacon/sausage into casserole. Pour egg mixture over.
  4. Bake 45 minutes.
