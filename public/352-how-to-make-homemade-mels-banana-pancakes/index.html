The ingredients needed to make Mel’s Banana Pancakes:
  1. Make ready ripe bananas
  2. Prepare medium eggs
  3. Make ready flour
  4. Take Olive Oil
  5. Get baking powder
Steps to make Mel’s Banana Pancakes:
  1. Mash the bananas, add the oil, flour and the baking powder. Mix thoroughly.
  2. Preheat non stick frying pan, the add tbsps of pancake mixture. Leave spaces in between.
  3. Cover pan while cooking and be careful while flipping it over.
  4. Enjoy with any fruits and syrup or just with a dollop of peanut butter. Xx
