The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Okara Steamed Bread for Low Sugar Dieters:
  1. Take 10 grams Okara powder
  2. Get 50 grams Hot water (if you use fresh okara, it is not necessary)
  3. Make ready 20 grams Unsweetened chocolate chips (or unsweetened cocoa powder)
  4. Make ready 1 Whole egg
  5. Take 2 grams Baking powder
  6. Prepare 10 grams Calorie-free sweetener
  7. Get 1 pinch Salt (optional)

Instructions to make Chocolate Okara Steamed Bread for Low Sugar Dieters:
  1. Combine the hot water and cocoa powder in a microwave-proof tupperware with a spoon. (If you use salt, add at this stage).
  2. Add the okara powder, baking powder and sweetener. Stir and mix.
  3. At this point the batter should be warm to the touch. Add an egg and mix well.
  4. Cover roughly with cling film (or the tupperware lid). Microwave at 600 W for 2.5 - 3 minutes.
  5. This is the bread with sugar-free cocoa powder. It doesn't look moist, but it is fluffier.
