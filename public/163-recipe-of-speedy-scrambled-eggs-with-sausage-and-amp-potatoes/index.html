The ingredients needed to make Scrambled Eggs with Sausage & Potatoes:
  1. Prepare eggs
  2. Prepare of Owens Country Sausage
  3. Make ready grated cheese
  4. Prepare milk
  5. Make ready leftover baked potato
  6. Prepare small sweet tomatoes
Steps to make Scrambled Eggs with Sausage & Potatoes:
  1. Peel leftover baked potato and cut in small cubes. In a small skillet add oil to lightly cover whole skillet and turn heat to medium high. Place potatoes and cook till lightly browned.
  2. In a large skillet on medium high brown sausage. While sausage is cooking Mix 8 eggs and milk together in mi zing bowl and set aside till Sausage is done. Pour eggs and cheese on top of sausage and mix as eggs start cooking. Cook till eggs or done to your taste. Enjoy!!
