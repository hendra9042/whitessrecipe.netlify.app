Instructions to make Sausage and cheese grit casserole:

  2. Cook sausage in a skillet till done then drain
  3. To cooked grits add butter, eggs and shredded cheddar.
  4. Add in sausage and mix well.
  5. Place mixture into an oven safe casserole dish
  6. Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 1 hour
