The ingredients needed to make Vegan Gluten-Free Tofu Omlette:
  1. Make ready Egg Mix
  2. Take Soft Mori-Nu Tofu
  3. Get Golden Flax Seeds
  4. Take Gluten Free Garbanzo Flour
  5. Take Black Kavala Salt
  6. Prepare Nutritional Yeast Flakes
  7. Make ready Onion Powder
  8. Get Garlic Powder
  9. Make ready Paprika
  10. Prepare Turmeric
  11. Take Chili powder
  12. Take Chicken Seasoning (vegetarian)
  13. Make ready Soy Kaas Mozzarella Cheese
  14. Get extra virgin olive oil
  15. Take Omelette Fillings
  16. Get Small White Onions
  17. Take Red Pepper
  18. Take White Mushrooms
  19. Get Green Olives
  20. Get Chive Onions
  21. Prepare garlic
  22. Take Large Tomato
Steps to make Vegan Gluten-Free Tofu Omlette:
  1. Lightly oil a pan with Extra Virgin Olive Oil and heat pan on medium high. Be sure to have your own sufficiently hot and ready
  2. Combine all egg mix ingredients in a blender
  3. Blend till smooth
  4. Set egg mix aside and prepare your omelette fillings
  5. Chop omelette fillings however you like, so they fit in the omelette
  6. Pour your egg mix into your pre-heated pan, about half an inch thick
  7. In another pan, combine your omelette fillings in another pan set to high. Leave out tomatoes from pan for now
  8. Your egg mix will be ready to flip when the edges are golden brown. Flip carefully with a large spatula
  9. Add fillings to your omelette, and allow the other side to cook a bit
  10. Once cooked, remove omelette carefully and fold over once you place it on the plate
