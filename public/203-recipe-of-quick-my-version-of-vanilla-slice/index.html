The ingredients needed to make My Version of Vanilla Slice:
  1. Get frozen puff pastry sheets
  2. Get Vanilla Custard:
  3. Prepare milk
  4. Prepare thickened cream
  5. Prepare unsalted butter
  6. Take vanilla bean paste
  7. Prepare caster sugar
  8. Get corn starch
  9. Get egg yolks
  10. Get Icing sugar for dusting
Instructions to make My Version of Vanilla Slice:
  1. Bake 3 pastry sheets 200C for 10 minutes of until cooked. Set aside to cool.
  2. Bring milk, cream, butter and vanilla paste to the simmer.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk sugar, corn starch and egg yolk together.
  4. When the milk mixture reach to 60C, put 1/3 cup milk into the egg yolk mixture. Whisk until combine and put the whole mixture into the milk mixture.
  5. Return to a pan and cook for 4 - 5 in medium heat.
  6. Put into a big bowl and cover with cling wrap attach to the custard.
  7. Let it rest until completely cool.
  8. Assemble the Vanilla slice dusting with icing sugar.
  9. Put in the fridge at least 3-4 hours or overnight before you cut and serve with fresh berries or ice cream.
  10. Enjoy
