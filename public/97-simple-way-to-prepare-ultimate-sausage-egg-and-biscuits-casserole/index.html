The ingredients needed to make Sausage, Egg, and Biscuits Casserole:
  1. Prepare Buttermilk Biscuits
  2. Prepare Pork Sausage
  3. Take Shredded Mozzarella
  4. Take Shredded Cheddar
  5. Prepare Eggs
  6. Get Milk
  7. Make ready as needed Salt & Pepper
Steps to make Sausage, Egg, and Biscuits Casserole:
  1. Preheat oven to 425°
  2. Cut biscuits into six to eight pieces
  3. Place in a grease 8x8 pan
  4. Brown sausage on the stove top
  5. Drain sausage
  6. Spread sausage over the cut up biscuits
  7. Beat eggs
  8. Add milk, salt, and pepper to eggs
  9. Pour eggs over sausage and biscuits
  10. Sprinkle both shredded cheeses over the top
  11. Bake for 30-35 minutes
  12. Let sit 5 minutes
