The ingredients needed to make In the microwave Custard Cream with Rice Flour and Soy Milk:
  1. Prepare Egg yolk
  2. Make ready Soy milk
  3. Get Sugar
  4. Make ready Rice flour
Instructions to make In the microwave Custard Cream with Rice Flour and Soy Milk:
  1. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine the egg yolk, soy milk, and sugar, and whisk very well.
  2. Next, add the rice flour and mix thoroughly.
  3. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap, and microwave for 2 minutes. Take out of the microwave and mix quickly.
  4. Again, cover the bowl with plastic wrap, microwave for another minute, take it out, and mix quickly. If it has reached the desired consistency, it's done if not, microwave for a bit longer in 30 second increments.
