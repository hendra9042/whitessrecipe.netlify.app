The ingredients needed to make Easy Swedish Meatballs and Noodles:
  1. Take butter
  2. Make ready small onion, chopped
  3. Make ready sliced Baby Bella mushrooms
  4. Make ready can of cambell's cream of mushroom
  5. Get can of Campbell's cream of celery
  6. Make ready beef broth
  7. Take allspice
  8. Make ready nutmeg
  9. Get bag of frozen "Swedish Style Meatballs"
  10. Make ready Wide Egg Noodles
  11. Take sour cream
Instructions to make Easy Swedish Meatballs and Noodles:
  1. Assemble all of your ingredients, and chop onion in med dice.
  2. Melt the butter over med high heat, in a large Dutch oven or soup pot. Add the onion and mushrooms, and sautee, stirring often until the mushrooms shrink and the onion has a tinge of brown. 7 minutes.
  3. Add the cans of creamed soup and the broth stirring well to combine. Also add the spices.
  4. Turn to high heat and continue to stir, bring to a boil before adding the frozen meatballs.
  5. Add the bag of frozen meatballs, let simmer for 15 minutes.
  6. Pour in the half a bag of egg noodles, and stir well. Cover and cook 20 minutes, stirring the noodles through the simmering sauce, often.
  7. Lower to a gentle simmer, and make sure the pasta doesn't stick.
  8. Add the sour cream just before service and mix in entirely.
  9. The final product.
