The ingredients needed to make Pressure Cooker Swedish Meatballs with Noodles:
  1. Prepare Cream of Mushroom
  2. Get Cream of Chicken
  3. Take water
  4. Get Worcestershire Sauce
  5. Take Beef Broth
  6. Take Better than Beef Bouillon
  7. Take Pork Roast Gravy Mix
  8. Get Garlic Powder
  9. Make ready dried parsley
  10. Take Frozen Italian Meatballs
  11. Take egg noodles
  12. Prepare sour cream
Instructions to make Pressure Cooker Swedish Meatballs with Noodles:
  1. Place inner pot in pressure cooker. Whisk together everything but the noodles, meatballs, and sourcream
  2. Put meatballs in
  3. Layer egg noodles over the meatballs and gently spread out and push down until mostly covered with water. Add a little more water if needed. Don't mix!
  4. Make sure inner seal is in the lid. Lock the pressure cooker lid and change vent to sealed.
  5. Cook on manual high pressure for 8 minutes. It took my 8 quart instant pot around 13 minutes to come to pressure.
  6. Once timer goes off, carefully do a quick release.
  7. Remove the lid and mix everything up. Stir in the sour cream.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy!
