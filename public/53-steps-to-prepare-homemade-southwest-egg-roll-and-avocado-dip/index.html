The ingredients needed to make Southwest Egg Roll And Avocado Dip:
  1. Take 2 1/2 cup Shredded cooked chicken
  2. Get 1 1/2 cup Shredded Mexican cheese blend
  3. Prepare 2/3 cup Frozen corn thawed
  4. Get 2/3 cup Canned black beans drained and rinsed
  5. Get 5 Green onions chopped
  6. Get 1/4 cup Fresh minced cilantro
  7. Prepare 1 tsp Salt
  8. Prepare 1 tsp Ground cumin
  9. Get 1 tsp Grated lime peel
  10. Take 1/4 tsp Cayenne pepper
  11. Make ready 20 Egg roll wrappers
  12. Take 1 Oil for frying wrappers
  13. Take 1 cup Ranch salad dressing
  14. Prepare 1 medium Ripe avocado peeled and mashed
  15. Get 1 tbsp Cilantro
  16. Prepare 1 tsp Grated lime peel
Instructions to make Southwest Egg Roll And Avocado Dip:
  1. In a large bowl, combine the first ten ingredients. Place 1/4 cup of chicken mixture in the center of 1 egg roll wrapper. ( keep remaining wrappers covered with a damp paper towel until ready to use). Fold bottom corner over filling. Fold side towards center over filling. Moisten remaining corner with water; roll up tightly to seal. Repeat
  2. In an skillet or deep fryer, heat oil to 375. Fry egg rolls, a few at a time, for two min on each side or until golden brown. Drain on paper towels.
  3. Meanwhile combine the last 4 ingredients and serve as a dip for the egg rolls.
