The ingredients needed to make Ham And Cheese Egg Salad Sandwich:
  1. Prepare egg salad
  2. Make ready 6 hard boiled eggs
  3. Take 1/3 cup mayonnaise
  4. Make ready dash celery salt
  5. Make ready dash dried dill
  6. Prepare 3 grinds fresh pepper
  7. Take 1/4 tsp onion powder
  8. Prepare 1/3 tsp paprika
  9. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt
  10. Get sandwich
  11. Get 4 slice english muffin bread. Recipe attached
  12. Prepare 4 slice thin cut deli ham
  13. Prepare 2 slice cheddar cheese
Instructions to make Ham And Cheese Egg Salad Sandwich:
  1. Hard boil eggs and peel.
  2. In large bowl add eggs and rest of ingredients for egg salad.
  3. Mix together and break up eggs with a fork.
  4. Toast the english muffin bread slices. You can make it plain english muffin bread by omitting the cinnamon and raisins. - - https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/349613-cinnamon-raisin-english-muffin-bread-easy
  5. Place two slices of ham, then half of the egg salad, then slice of cheese.
  6. Top with piece of toast and you have a satisfying tasty sandwich. Cut in half. Serve with a pickle if you like.
