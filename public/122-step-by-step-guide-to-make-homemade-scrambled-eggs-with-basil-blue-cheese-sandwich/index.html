The ingredients needed to make Scrambled Eggs With Basil Blue Cheese Sandwich:
  1. Make ready Butter
  2. Take 5 eggs
  3. Prepare Dash salt
  4. Get 15 basil leaf
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp Of blue cheese
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp Of mix cashew, walnut And Macadamia nuts
  7. Take 2 slice bread
  8. Prepare Chili sauce
Instructions to make Scrambled Eggs With Basil Blue Cheese Sandwich:
  1. Toast bread then set aside
  2. Melt butter on pan then add in the beaten eggs that’s season with a dash of salt
  3. Add in the basil and blue cheese crumble and mix well by folding over and let it set for another 1 minutes then off heat
  4. Add in the mix nuts and Serve on toast bread and generously work some Chili sauce
