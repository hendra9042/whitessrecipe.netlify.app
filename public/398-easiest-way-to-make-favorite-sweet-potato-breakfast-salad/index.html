The ingredients needed to make Sweet potato breakfast salad:
  1. Make ready medium sweet potato
  2. Take egg
  3. Get Baby Spinach
  4. Take Broccoli (optional)
  5. Take Garlic
  6. Make ready Olive oil
  7. Get Pepper
  8. Take Garlic & Herb seasoning
Steps to make Sweet potato breakfast salad:
  1. Cube and wash sweet potato. Drizzle skillet with olive oil. Place in hot skillet over medium high heat. Put potatoes, garlic and seasoning in pan. Saute potatoes 10-15 minutes. Add broccoli at this point.
  2. Cover and turn to medium low heat for 10 more minutes.
  3. Once potatoes and broccoli are almost done remove from heat. Let cool 5 minutes.
  4. Fry egg in same pan. (Or egg whites for extra healthy)
  5. Serve over bed of spinach or kale. I like to have some fruit with mine also :)
