The ingredients needed to make Grilled Cheese and Egg Sandwich:
  1. Prepare 2 slice of Bread
  2. Get 2 Eggs
  3. Get 2 slice of American or Smoked Provolane Cheese
  4. Get 1 A bit of butter or margerine to cook the eggs with
Instructions to make Grilled Cheese and Egg Sandwich:
  1. Fry eggs to your liking using a bit of butter.
  2. Remove from heat and set aside.
  3. While on medium add a bit of butter to the frying pan. Once melted, place 1 slice of bread in the pan with a slice of cheese on top.
  4. Place the cooked egg on top of the bread and cheese add the remaining slice of cheese then bread on top. Allow it to cook for about 3 minutes. Flip the sandwich over and cook another 3 minutes. You are done!
