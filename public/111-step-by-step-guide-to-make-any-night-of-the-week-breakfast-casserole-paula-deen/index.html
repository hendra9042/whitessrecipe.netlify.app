The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Casserole (Paula Deen):
  1. Take slices- white bread
  2. Take Cheddar Cheese- however much you like to use
  3. Get pound- crumbled sausage
  4. Make ready - large eggs
  5. Get cups- milk
  6. Take salt- to taste
  7. Get pepper- to taste
  8. Prepare garlic powder- to taste
Instructions to make Breakfast Casserole (Paula Deen):
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spray a 13x9 inch glass baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.
  2. In a large skillet, cook sausage over medium heat; crumble it up and when it's fully cooked drain the fat. (I just take a plate and put paper towel on it and spoon the sausage onto it to let the fat drain off.)
  3. Cut or pull off the crusts from the bread and discard. Tear slices in half and arrange in baking dish; cutting pieces to fit as necessary to cover bottom of dish.
  4. Sprinkle with sausage and cheese.
  5. In a large bowl whisk together the eggs, milk and seasonings; carefully pour the mixture over the cheese and sausage.
  6. Bake casserole until set and golden, about 40 minutes. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
