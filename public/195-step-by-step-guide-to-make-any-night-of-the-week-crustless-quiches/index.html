The ingredients needed to make Crustless Quiches:
  1. Take Bell peppers and onions
  2. Prepare Frozen broccoli
  3. Get Fresh spinach
  4. Make ready Garlic, chopped
  5. Get Celery salt
  6. Take White pepper
  7. Make ready Oregano
  8. Take Extra virgin olive oil
  9. Prepare Morningstar veggie sausage patties
  10. Get Grape tomatoes, halved
  11. Make ready Freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  12. Prepare Medifast garden veggie crackers
  13. Prepare Shredded low fat Mexican cheese
  14. Get Southwestern egg beaters
Instructions to make Crustless Quiches:
  1. Saute peppers, onions, and garlic in olive oil for 2 minutes. Add broccoli and spinach and heat until spinach wilts and broccoli is warm. Meanwhile, microwave sausage patties, cut into small pieces and add to veggie mixture. Add celery salt, oregano, and white pepper. Heat through.
  2. Spray a muffin tin with cooking spray. Put equal amount veggie mixture in each cup.
  3. Crumble crackers and mix with Parmesan cheese. Sprinkle evenly over veggies.
  4. Sprinkle Mexican cheese evenly on top of cracker mixture.
  5. Top with cherry tomatoes.
  6. Pour egg beaters evenly into each cup.
  7. Bake at 350 for 18 - 25 minutes or until bubbly and golden on top.
