The ingredients needed to make Keto Granola:
  1. Take Dry ingredients
  2. Get walnuts (Chopped)
  3. Make ready pecans (Chopped)
  4. Get un-sweetened coconut (shredded)
  5. Get sunflower seeds
  6. Prepare flax seeds
  7. Make ready ground flax meal
  8. Prepare chia seeds
  9. Get Granulated monk fruit Sweetner
  10. Take cinnamon
  11. Prepare Wet ingredients
  12. Make ready butter (melted)
  13. Make ready egg white
  14. Get vanilla
Steps to make Keto Granola:
  1. Pre heat oven to 350 degrees
  2. In a large bowl, mix together dry ingredients (Nuts, Seeds, flax meal, sweetener, cinnamon.
  3. Combine wet ingredients in separate bowl (melted butter slightly cooled, egg white, vanilla.)
  4. Pour wet ingredients into dry and combine.
  5. Pour mixture onto parchment lined baking sheet and press down.
  6. Optional* Sprinkle 1/4-1/2 tsp pink Himalayan salt on top prior to baking
  7. Bake for 20-25 mins until slightly browned.
  8. It will come out soft like a cookie, allow to fully cool (it will harden) break into desired size chunks.
