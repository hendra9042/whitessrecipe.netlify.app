The ingredients needed to make Mom's Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Prepare Pork Sausage
  2. Make ready Parmesan Cheese
  3. Take Eggs
  4. Take Bread
  5. Get Milk
  6. Prepare Salt
Steps to make Mom's Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Preheat oven at 350
  2. Crumble bread and line bottom of greased 9x13 pan
  3. Cook and drain sausage and spread on top of bread
  4. Add parmesan cheese on top of sausage
  5. Mix eggs, milk and salt together and pour over
  6. Bake uncovered for 45 minutes
  7. *You can also prepare dish the night before and keep in refrigerator for quick start the next morning
