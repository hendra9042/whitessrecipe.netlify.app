The ingredients needed to make Our Favorite Pumpkin Pie Recipe:
  1. Take pumpkin filling, 15 oz.
  2. Make ready pie shells
  3. Make ready eggs, slightly beaten/egg beaters
  4. Take splenda/sugar
  5. Make ready salt
  6. Take ground cinnamon
  7. Make ready ground cloves
  8. Take ground nutmeg
  9. Make ready ground ginger
  10. Get evaporated milk or vanilla almond milk
Steps to make Our Favorite Pumpkin Pie Recipe:
  1. Preheat oven to 450. Combine ingredients in large bowl
  2. Put pie tins/pans onto flat cookie sheet to prevent bubbling over. Pour into 2 pie pans evenly.
  3. Bake in hot 450 oven for 10 minutes. Reduce heat and bake at 350 for 40-45 minutes. Pie is done when,knife inserted in middle, comes out clean.
