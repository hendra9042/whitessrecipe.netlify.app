The ingredients needed to make Stir fry noodles with spinach:
  1. Prepare Noodles
  2. Prepare Spinach
  3. Make ready Seasonings
  4. Get Tomato pepper sauce
  5. Make ready Boiled eggs for garnishng
Instructions to make Stir fry noodles with spinach:
  1. Parboil your noodles and drain the water
  2. In a pan put little oil and Onion's, add small amount of tomato pepper sauce and stir, add your seasonings, and spinach and keep stiring
  3. Add in your parboiled noodles and keep stirring until all the mixture is absorbed by the noodles.
  4. Your stir fry noodles is done. Garnish with boiled eggs
