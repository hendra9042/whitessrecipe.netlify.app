The ingredients needed to make Egg-Chicken Chowmein:
  1. Get Chowmein noodles
  2. Make ready eggs
  3. Prepare chicken cubes merinated with 1tsp ginger garlic paste
  4. Take carrot sliced
  5. Prepare onion sliced
  6. Prepare capsicum sliced
  7. Get beans diagonally chopped
  8. Make ready cabbage thinly chopped
  9. Prepare green chillies chopped
  10. Get garlic chopped
  11. Make ready salt
  12. Prepare soya sauce
  13. Make ready chilli sauce
  14. Get tomato sauce
  15. Take vinegar
  16. Make ready oil
  17. Take black pepper powder
Instructions to make Egg-Chicken Chowmein:
  1. Boil the noodles in water with salt and 1 tsp oil.
  2. Drain the water and wash the noodles with cold water. Keep aside.
  3. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan. Add the chicken cubes with little salt and fry for 5-6 minutes. Remove and keep it aside.
  4. In the same pan add 1tbsp oil to heat. Beat the eggs into it with a pinch of salt. Fry for 2-3 minutes and keep aside.
  5. Now in another pan heat remaining oil. Add chopped garlic, green chilli and saute for a minute.
  6. Next add sliced onion, capsicum, carrot, beans, cabbage and salt. Fry it for 4 minutes in high flame.
  7. Now add the fried chicken and scrambled egg. Cook for a while.
  8. Next add boiled noodles. Add all the sauces with vinegar and mix with the noodles. Add black pepper powder and mix it well in high flame.
  9. Turn off the flame and serve Egg- Chicken Chowmein hot with some ketchup/red chilli sauce.
