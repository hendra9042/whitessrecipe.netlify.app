The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Prepare browned sausage
  2. Prepare hashbrowns
  3. Prepare shredded cheese
  4. Prepare eggs
  5. Get milk
  6. Get salt
Steps to make Breakfast Casserole:
  1. brown sausage & drain
  2. place hashbrowns in casserole dish
  3. place sausage over hashbrowns, cover with cheese
  4. combine eggs, milk & salt. pour over mixture.
  5. bake @350 for 50 minutes
