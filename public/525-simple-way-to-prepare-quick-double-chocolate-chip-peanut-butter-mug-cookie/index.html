The ingredients needed to make Double Chocolate Chip Peanut Butter Mug Cookie:
  1. Take 1 tbsp butter (can substitute with butter spread)
  2. Take 1 tbsp brown sugar
  3. Take 1 tsp granulated sugar
  4. Prepare 3 drops vanilla extract
  5. Take 1 tsp 1/8 baking powder
  6. Make ready 1 egg yolk
  7. Prepare 2 tsp cocoa powder
  8. Get 1 tbsp flour
  9. Make ready 1 tbsp mini chocolate chips
  10. Get 1 tbsp peanut butter

Instructions to make Double Chocolate Chip Peanut Butter Mug Cookie:
  1. Place butter in an 8 ounce coffee mug.
  2. Melt butter in the microwave in 30 second increments.
  3. Stir in sugars, vanilla, and baking powder.
  4. Stir in egg yolk, cocoa, and flour.
  5. Stir in chocolate chips.
  6. Place peanut butter in the center and press it down slightly into the batter.
  7. Heat mugs in the microwave for 1 minute.
  8. Let the mugs cool slightly before eating.
