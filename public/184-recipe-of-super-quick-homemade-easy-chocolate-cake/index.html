The ingredients needed to make Easy Chocolate Cake:
  1. Take devil's food cake mix
  2. Prepare water
  3. Prepare apple sauce
  4. Get egg beaters or 3 eggs
  5. Take Chocolate frosting
Instructions to make Easy Chocolate Cake:
  1. Mix cake mix, water, applesauce, & egg beaters until mixed together.
  2. Pour into greased 9 x 13 pan. Unless it's a nonstick pan.
  3. Bake in a preheated 350 degree oven for 25 minutes or until done.
  4. Cool completely and frost.
