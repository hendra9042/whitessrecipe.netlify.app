The ingredients needed to make Flourless Brownies:
  1. Make ready 1 (14 oz.) canned low-sodium black beans, rinsed and drained
  2. Take 2 large eggs
  3. Get 1/2 t cocoa powder
  4. Prepare 3/4 cup sugar
  5. Make ready 1/2 t Oil
  6. Get 1 T unsweetened almond milk (or dairy, skim)
  7. Get 1 t balsamic vinegar
  8. Take 1/2 t baking powder
  9. Prepare 1/2 t Baking soda
  10. Make ready 1/2 t freshly ground coffee or instant coffee
  11. Get 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips, divided

Steps to make Flourless Brownies:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease a nonstick 9x9 inch square baking pan with baking spray and line with parchment paper, leaving a 2 inch overhang on all sides.
  2. Blend the blakbeans, eggs, cocoa powder, sugar, oil, almond milk, balsamic, baking soda, baking powder andcoffee in the blender until smooth and pour into a bowl. Fold in 1/2 cup chocolate chips until combined. Pour the brownie batter into the prepared pan. Sprinkle the remaining chocolate chips evenly over the top of the brownies.
  3. Bake the brownies until a toothpick comes out clean, about 30-32 minutes. Allow the brownies to cool completely before slicing them into squares.
