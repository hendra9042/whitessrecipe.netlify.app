The ingredients needed to make Ultimate Scrambled Eggs:
  1. Take Regina's Ultimate Scrambled Eggs
  2. Prepare eggs
  3. Get cooked boneless chicken breast chopped
  4. Prepare garlic salt
  5. Prepare thyme
  6. Take parsley
  7. Take cheese (your choice of cheese) shredded.
  8. Make ready pepper
  9. Make ready sour cream
Steps to make Ultimate Scrambled Eggs:
  1. Scramble your eggs, add the chopped chicken, garlic salt, parsley, thyme.
  2. When cooked add the shredded cheese (your choice of cheese)
  3. Put on plate, add your sour cream at the side.
  4. Add the dash of pepper
  5. Enjoy!
