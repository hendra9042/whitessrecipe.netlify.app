The ingredients needed to make Egg and chives sandwich:
  1. Make ready 3 large eggs
  2. Get 2 tbsp chopped chives
  3. Get 1 1/2 tbsp margarine
  4. Take 3 slice white bread
  5. Make ready 3 tsp margarine
  6. Make ready 1/4 tsp ground black pepper, fresh
  7. Make ready salt
  8. Take 1 cup mild cheddar cheese
  9. Get 3 tsp mayonnaise
Steps to make Egg and chives sandwich:
  1. Crack your egg, tip crack into a bowl by itself I found a bloody on it was the last I cracked I would have had to throw three away
  2. Toast your bread
  3. To the eggs add chive salt and pepper beat all together shread the cheese
  4. Add to wok or pan the beaten eggs and cheese
  5. Stirfry the eggs and cheese till done
  6. Put mayonnaise on all three slices of bread
  7. Stack the eggs on two pieces of bread stack the them otop of each other top with third
  8. Cut in half if you like enjoy
