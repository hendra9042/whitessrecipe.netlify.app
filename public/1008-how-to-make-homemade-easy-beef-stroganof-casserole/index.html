The ingredients needed to make easy beef stroganof casserole:
  1. Get 1 lb ground beef
  2. Make ready 1 can cream of chicken or cream of onion soup
  3. Get 1 cup milk
  4. Prepare 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  5. Take 8 oz sour cream
  6. Get 1 packages frozen egg noodles
  7. Make ready 1 cup crushed potato chips
  8. Get 1 season to taste
  9. Get 1 small onion chopped
