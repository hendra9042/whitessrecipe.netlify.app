Steps to make Egg custard:
  1. In a bowl. Add half cup of milk and cornflour mix and keep aside
  2. In a saucepan add half liter milk, condensed milk and egg and beta well
  3. Put the saucepan on a stove and boil the mixture keep stirring so the egg will not becomes srumble
  4. Add cornflour mix and stir until the custard texture appears
  5. Remive from stove and cool down at room temperature
  6. The add fresh cream and mix well
  7. Add fruits of ur choice or cocktail fruit tin ar jelly nuts
