The ingredients needed to make Pan-poached egg:
  1. Make ready 4 eggs
  2. Make ready 1/2-1 c water (based on the sizeof your pan)
  3. Prepare 1-2 tsp vinegar
Steps to make Pan-poached egg:
  1. Heat a pan at medium high, add water and vinegar, heat until the water start to simmer.
  2. Crack the egg and put carefully into the pan, keeping the eggs separate from each other.
  3. Cover with the lid and heat until cook to your style
  4. Done and enjoy:)
