The ingredients needed to make RHUBARB CUSTARD PIE:
  1. Get 1 1/2 cup rhubarb, finely cut
  2. Make ready 1 cup white sugar
  3. Take 2 tbsp margarine
  4. Get 3 tbsp flour
  5. Make ready 2 egg yolks

Steps to make RHUBARB CUSTARD PIE:
  1. Mix all ingredients together, add to uncooked pie shell, cook at 350° until cooked
  2. When cooked, beat egg white with 1/4 cup white sugar to stiff peaks, spread on pie, put back in oven until golden
