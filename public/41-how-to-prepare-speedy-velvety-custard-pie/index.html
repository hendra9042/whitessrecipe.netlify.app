The ingredients needed to make Velvety Custard Pie:
  1. Get 4 eggs
  2. Get 1/2 cup sugar
  3. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
  4. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla extract (I add a bit extra)
  5. Prepare 2 1/2 cup scalded milk
  6. Take 1 nutmeg to taste
Steps to make Velvety Custard Pie:
  1. Beat eggs slightly.
  2. Blend in sugar, salt, & vanilla.
  3. Stir hot milk slowly into egg mixture.
  4. Sprinkle with nutmeg.
  5. Bake at 475°F for 5 minutes.
  6. Reduce heat to 425°F and bake 15 minutes longer, or until knife inserted in the center comes out clean.
