The ingredients needed to make Merritt Quiche:
  1. Prepare 1 ready made Pet Ritz pie crust (9 inch)
  2. Prepare 8 oz package of grated cheddar cheese
  3. Get 1/2 pound crumbled and Cooked Sausage
  4. Get 1/2 cup Hellman's mayonnaise
  5. Take 1/2 cup milk
  6. Take 2 eggs
  7. Prepare 1 Tbsp. cornstarch
  8. Prepare 1/3 cup sliced green onions(I use the long green onions) or you can cut a red onion (the round one) as a substitute
Steps to make Merritt Quiche:
  1. Mix all ingredients in Large Bowl then pour into 9" pie crust.
  2. Bake in oven at 350° for 35-40 minutes.
