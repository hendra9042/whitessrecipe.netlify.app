The ingredients needed to make 5-Ingredient Skillet Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Get hashbrowns
  2. Get turkey breakfast sausage
  3. Prepare eggs
  4. Get oil (coconut or canola)
  5. Take shedded cheese
  6. Make ready Salt, pepper, Mrs Braggs herbs
Steps to make 5-Ingredient Skillet Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Cook hashbrowns according to package directions sprinkle with salt & pepper
  2. In a separate skillet crumble & cook breakfast sausage
  3. Add sausage on top of hashbrowns and pour whisked eggs on top. Cook for a few minutes on medium-low heat.
  4. Sprinkle shredded cheese, salt, pepper & herbs on top
  5. Turn oven to Broil (High) and place skillet in center of oven for 3-5 min or until eggs are set and cheese is golden
