The ingredients needed to make Soft boiled egg Thai spicy salad (Yum Kai Tom):
  1. Make ready eggs
  2. Make ready Yum sauce (spicy salad sauce)
  3. Get shallot
  4. Get bird eye chilli (up to your taste)
  5. Make ready fish sauce
  6. Get large lime
  7. Take sugar
  8. Get salt
Steps to make Soft boiled egg Thai spicy salad (Yum Kai Tom):
  1. Boil some water. When it’s boiling, reduce the heat to gentle simmer.
  2. Add the eggs and set the timer for 5 minutes. After 5 minutes, Take the eggs out of the water and put into a bowl of cold water.
  3. Prepare Yum sauce ingredients
  4. Add fish sauce, lime juice and mix them until the sugar dissolved. Then add shallot and chillis
  5. Cut eggs to half. Drizzling yum sauce over the eggs. Garnish with coriander before serving.
