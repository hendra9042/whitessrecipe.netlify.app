The ingredients needed to make Best Ramen Soup from Scratch:
  1. Take 1 bell pepper
  2. Get 5 eggs
  3. Make ready 3 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  4. Take 2 dash szechuan peppercorn
  5. Prepare 1 red onion
  6. Make ready 1 liter vegetable stock
  7. Take 1 clove ginger
  8. Make ready 1 pinch crushed red pepper
  9. Take soy sauce
  10. Make ready tsp salt
  11. Prepare 1 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions to make Best Ramen Soup from Scratch:
  1. Pour flour into bowl. Make a well and add eggs to middle. Beat eggs and slowly incorporate to form dough, adding water if too dry.
  2. Let dough rest for 15-20 min
  3. Add broth to a large pot on stove. Grate ginger and add. Add soy sauce and salt to taste. Pour in 1 tablespoon vegetable oil.
  4. Roll out dough and cut into strips. Bring broth to a boil and sprinkle in cut noodles. Cook for 4-5 minutes.
  5. Take remaining egg and poach it in the broth for 3-4 minutes (optional)
  6. Chop onions and pepper and add. Add crushed red pepper. Serve immediately and enjoy!
