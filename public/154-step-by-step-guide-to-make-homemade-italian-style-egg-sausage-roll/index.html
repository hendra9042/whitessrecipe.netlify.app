Steps to make Italian style egg sausage roll:
  1. First let's make the dough. Take the wheat flour and madia, add salt, black pepper powder, parsley, oregano and red Chile powder oil mix them all. Add water and make the dough. It should be soft dough. Keep it for 30 min. Then roll it into roti
  2. Now fry the sausage. Keep it aside. Now add finely chopped garlic, then add the chopped onion, capsicum and tomato, just saute it. Add the pizza sauce. Add salt, black pepper powder, parsley and oregano and red Chile flakes.
  3. Now take the sausage and cut them into small pcs and mix them with the vegetables. Add some gratted cheese now take the egg add salt black pepper powder, parsley, oregano and red Chile powder and wishk it.
  4. Now in a pan put the roti cook it in both side the add the egg put the roti. Add oil and fry it.
  5. Then take the egg paratha, put the stuffing add gratted cheese. Now roll it with paper.
