The ingredients needed to make SKhan's Cheesy Potato Sandwich:
  1. Take 2 potatoes mashed
  2. Get 2 tbsp coriander leaves
  3. Get 1 tbsp chili sause
  4. Make ready 1 tsp ground cumin
  5. Make ready 1 tsp ground black pepper
  6. Prepare salt
  7. Take 1 tsp ground coriander
  8. Take 2 tbsp chili hot sauce
  9. Get green chilies
  10. Get 5 slice cheese - cut into pieces (according to your taste, i used burger slice cheese)
  11. Make ready 12 slice Sandwich bread
  12. Get 2 large beaten eggs
  13. Take 1 cup vegetable oil
Steps to make SKhan's Cheesy Potato Sandwich:
  1. In mashed Potatoes, add all the ingredients from steps 2-9. Mix it well.
  2. Take a slice of bread and divide it into half with the help of your palm and fill the mashed potatoes and then with cheese. Press the bread.
  3. Dip it in beaten egg and fry it in oil until light golden brown. (I brushed few slices with brush on both the sides)
  4. Turn it so that both sides are coated with egg and light brown.
