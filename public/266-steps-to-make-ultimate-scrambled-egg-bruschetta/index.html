The ingredients needed to make Scrambled Egg Bruschetta:
  1. Take eggs
  2. Make ready salt and pepper to taste
  3. Make ready butter
  4. Prepare French bread
  5. Make ready about 1/2 cup chopped vegetables, such as grape tomatoes, green onions, & mini sweet peppers
Instructions to make Scrambled Egg Bruschetta:
  1. Beat together eggs, salt and pepper.
  2. Heat butter in a large nonstick pan on medium high.
  3. Add egg mixture and vegetables.
  4. Cook, stirring frequently, until eggs are set and vegetables are warm.
  5. Meanwhile heat broiler.
  6. Arrange bread slices on a baking sheet.
  7. Broil 1 to 2 minutes per side until light golden brown.
  8. Top bread with egg mixture.
  9. Garnish with fresh herbs, if desired.
  10. Serve warm.
