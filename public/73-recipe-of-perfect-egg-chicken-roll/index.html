The ingredients needed to make Egg Chicken Roll:
  1. Prepare 1 cup wheat flour
  2. Get 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  3. Take 1/2 cup cabbage, carrot. Chopped
  4. Prepare 1 large onion chopped

  11. Prepare as needed chopped Coriander leaves

  13. Take as required Oil
  14. Get as per taste Salt
  15. Take As needed Water

Instructions to make Egg Chicken Roll:
  1. Take the wheat flour and all-purpose flour, add salt 2 tsp oil and knead with warm water and cover it. Let it rest for 20 mins.
  2. Chop all the vegetables. In a pan add oil, grated ginger, chopped veggies. Add salt and saute for few minutes.
  3. Make balls of the dough and Roll little thin. In a pan add little oil and cook both sides of the roti.
  4. In a pan, add oil and pour the beated egg with little salt. Then above it, add a roti. Add oil and cook the back side of the roti.
  5. Flip over. On the egg side, add the sauted veggies, raw chopped cabbage, onion, coriander leaves, chicken, mayonnaise, tomato ketchup, grated cheese.
  6. Then fold the roll and pin a toothpick. You can roll with aluminium foil or butter paper.
  7. Serve hot.
