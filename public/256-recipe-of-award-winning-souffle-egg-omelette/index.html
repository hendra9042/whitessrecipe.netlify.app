Instructions to make Soufflé egg omelette:
  1. First separate the eggs and put the whites into a medium sized bowl and the yolks in a tiny bowl
  2. Now put your sugar into the bowl with the eggs whites. (You can add more it just won’t taste very good)
  3. Now after that is done, grab a mixer and mix the egg white sugar mixture until it becomes meringue. You can use a whisk but it will take a long time and your arms will be sore
  4. After you have mixed your egg white mixture, put the salt with the yolks and mix
  5. Now add the yolks to the egg whites and mix just a little bit. Over mixing will cause it to deflate.
  6. Turn your stove to a low heat, I would go super low for best results, and coat the pan in butter/veggy oil to cover the pan. I would use a big pan
  7. After the pan heats up add your mixture and spread it out. Put a cover on it then wait about 5-10 minutes depending on how low your stove is. I usually go with about 9 minutes so the inside cooks.
  8. After the 5-10 minutes is up, flip the soufflé in half. Mine turned out as a total fail but whatever, just keep cooking until it’s fully cooked
  9. After it looks cooked enough serve it to a plate, pour syrup, ketchup, whatever you would like and enjoy! Link to original video: https://m.youtube.com/watch?v=hQH5o-IzwJI
