The ingredients needed to make Egg Casserole:
  1. Make ready White Sandwich Bread
  2. Prepare eggs
  3. Make ready dry mustard
  4. Get milk
  5. Prepare grated cheese
  6. Make ready Small evaporated milk
  7. Take mushroom soup
  8. Prepare link sausage
Instructions to make Egg Casserole:
  1. Butter casserole dish.
  2. Trim crust and cut four squares to slice.
  3. Line casserole
  4. Brown and drain sausage
  5. Beat eggs and add mustard, cheese, milk and sausage(cut into bite size pieces)
  6. Pour over bread and refrigerate overnight.
  7. Next day mix evaporated milk and soup and put on top.
  8. Bake at 300 covered until last 10 minutes.
