The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Mint Dessert:
  1. Prepare 1 cup gluten-free flour (or reg flour)
  2. Take 1 cup sugar substitute, such as splenda (or granulated sugar)
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup butter, softened
  4. Make ready 4 eggs (or equivalent of egg substitute)
  5. Get 1 1/2 cups chocolate syrup
  6. Prepare 2 cups powdered sugar
  7. Take 1/2 cup butter, softened
  8. Get 2 tbsp green creme de menthe (or 1 tbsp water, 3/4 tsp mint extract, & 3 drops green food coloring)
  9. Take 6 tbsp butter
  10. Prepare 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
Instructions to make Chocolate Mint Dessert:
  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 13x9 baking dishes.
  2. Combine flour, sugar, 1/2 cup butter, eggs, syrup in a large bowl. Beat unril smooth. Pour batter in prepared pan.
  3. Bake 25-30 minutes or when top springa back when lightly touched in center. Cool completerly.
  4. Combine powdered sugar, 1/2 cup butter, & creme de menthe in medium bowl. Beat until smooth. Spread over cake. Cover. Refrigerate for at least an hour.
  5. Melt 6 tbsp butter & chocolate chips in a saucepan over very low heat. Remive from heat. Stir until smooth. Cool slightly. Spread over chilled dessert. Cover. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.
