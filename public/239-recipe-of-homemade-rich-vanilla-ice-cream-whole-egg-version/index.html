The ingredients needed to make Rich Vanilla Ice Cream - Whole Egg Version:
  1. Prepare 3 Egg
  2. Make ready 200 ml Heavy cream or whipping cream
  3. Prepare 6 tbsp Sugar
Steps to make Rich Vanilla Ice Cream - Whole Egg Version:
  1. Put the eggs and 3 tablespoons of sugar in a bowl and whip with an electric mixer until ribbons form on the surface.
  2. In another bowl, add heavy cream and 3 tablespoons of sugar. Using the same electric hand mixer, whip until soft peaks form. You don't need to rinse the mixer.
  3. Gently fold the egg mixture into the whipped cream. The mixture should be thoroughly combined.
  4. Pour the mixture into containers and freeze. You don't need to mix in the middle. If you like it sweeter, increase the amount of sugar.
