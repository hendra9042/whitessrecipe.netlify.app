The ingredients needed to make tripple berry custard pie:
  1. Take 3 1/2 cup mix of blue,black & raspberries
  2. Get 1 prepared pie crust, thawed according to directions
  3. Take dry custard ingredients
  4. Prepare 1 cup sugar
  5. Prepare 1/3 cup flour
  6. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
  7. Get wet custard ingredients
  8. Prepare 2 eggs beaten
  9. Make ready 1/2 cup plain greek yogert or sour cream
  10. Get crumb topping
  11. Get 1 stick of unsalted butter
  12. Prepare 1/2 cup four, white or whole wheat
  13. Make ready 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  14. Prepare 1/4 cup raw sugar or another 1/4 cup of granulated sugar
  15. Make ready 1 tsp cinnamon or sprinkle to preference
  16. Get 1 tbsp raw sugar (optional)

Steps to make tripple berry custard pie:
  1. preheat oven to 350
  2. place berries into prepared pie crust
  3. mix wet custard ingredients
  4. mix dry custard ingredients & place into wet custard ingredients
  5. spoon custard over berries
  6. use pastry knife to cut butter into flour & sugar to make crumbs
  7. place crumbs on berries & custard
  8. sprinkle with remaining raw sugar & cinnamon
  9. bake 50 to 55 minutes or until crust & topping are golden brown
