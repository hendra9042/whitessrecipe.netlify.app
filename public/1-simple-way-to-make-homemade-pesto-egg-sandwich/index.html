The ingredients needed to make Pesto Egg Sandwich:
  1. Take 1 slice bread you like
  2. Make ready 2 slices beefsteak tomato
  3. Get 1 tbsp pesto sauce
  4. Take 1 egg
  5. Make ready Salt and pepper to taste
Steps to make Pesto Egg Sandwich:
  1. Heat up a skillet on medium heat. Add 1 tbsp pesto sauce to the skillet and spread out evenly.
  2. Crack an egg on top of pesto sauce, and cook 1-1.5 minutes on medium heat. - (Optional) Sprinkle some cheddar cheese on top of the egg a little before the egg is ready.
  3. Toast a slice of bread. Then add 2 slices of beefsteak tomato on top of toast. Place cooked pesto egg on tomato slices.
  4. Delicious Pesto Egg Sandwich ready to serve!
