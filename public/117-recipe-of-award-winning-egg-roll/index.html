The ingredients needed to make Egg roll:
  1. Prepare 2 cups flour
  2. Get 6 Eggs
  3. Take 1 tspn baking powder
  4. Make ready 4 tablespoon sugar
  5. Take 1/4 teaspoon salt
  6. Make ready Oil for frying
  7. Take 2 tablespoon butter

Instructions to make Egg roll:
  1. In a bowl,sieve flour,add baking powder,salt,sugar,butter and mix well
  2. Add water little by little to form a soft dough..Allow it to sit for 10 minutes..
  3. Boil eggs and remove the shell,divide the dough into six and take 1 flatten it and put the egg on it..
  4. Mould the dough make sure it covers the egg and keep it aside..repeat thesame process with the remaining ones…keep them for 8-10 minutes..
  5. Heat oil in a pan and fried it on medium to low heat..
