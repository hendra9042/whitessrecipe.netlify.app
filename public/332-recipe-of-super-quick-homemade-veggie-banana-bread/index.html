The ingredients needed to make Veggie banana bread:
  1. Prepare 4 bananas
  2. Make ready 1/3 cup coconut sugar
  3. Take 1/3 cup oil
  4. Prepare 2 cups flour
  5. Prepare 1/2 bag baking powder
  6. Make ready 2 spoons crushed linseed
  7. Get 1 spoon cinnamon
  8. Get Pinch salt
  9. Prepare Your favorite nuts and seeds
  10. Take Egg substitute (1 egg)
  11. Get 1 spoon chia seeds
  12. Get 3 spoons water
