The ingredients needed to make Fluffy Scrambled Eggs with Shiitake Mushrooms:
  1. Prepare Shiitake mushrooms - thick ones are best
  2. Make ready Eggs
  3. Make ready Katakuriko (potato starch)
  4. Prepare Salt
  5. Get Dashi stock granules (Japanese instant stock)
  6. Prepare Water
  7. Take Sesame oil for stir-frying
Steps to make Fluffy Scrambled Eggs with Shiitake Mushrooms:
  1. Roughly chop the shiitake mushrooms.
  2. Dissolve the katakuriko in water.
  3. Beat the eggs.
  4. Combine the dissolved katakuriko from Step 2 and the eggs from Step 3. Add the dashi stock granules and salt.
  5. Heat the sesame oil in a pan and cook the shiitake mushrooms.
  6. When the mushrooms are cooked, add the egg mixture from Step 4 and plate when the eggs are still soft (just enough to cook through with the residual heat).
  7. Your fluffy eggs are done!
