The ingredients needed to make Exquisite Pound Cake to Use Up Egg Whites:
  1. Get Flour (cake flour)
  2. Take Baking powder
  3. Make ready Egg white
  4. Make ready Sugar
  5. Prepare Vegetable oil
  6. Make ready For decoration: chocolate, etc (optional)
Instructions to make Exquisite Pound Cake to Use Up Egg Whites:
  1. Whip the egg whites until stiff peaks form. It's quicker when you do it over a bowl with ice-cold water.
  2. Add the sugar and vegetable oil to the Step 1 bowl, and mix. When mixed, add the dry ingredients, and fold them in.
  3. Pour into the cake pan, sprinkle with chocolate chips or chopped walnuts on top as a decoration. It's optional.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 - 30 minutes at 180℃. Insert a bamboo skewer and if it comes out clean, it's done.
