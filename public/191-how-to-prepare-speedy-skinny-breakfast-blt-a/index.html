The ingredients needed to make Skinny Breakfast B.L.T. + A.:
  1. Make ready slices of healthy bread of choice (I used WW wheat,only 2 pts for 2 slices)
  2. Prepare egg whites or egg beaters
  3. Take slice of low fat cheese(I used Sargento ultra thin)
  4. Make ready baby spinach leaves(or lettuce of choice)
  5. Take thick slice of tomato
  6. Make ready of an avocado, thinly sliced
  7. Get light mayonaisse
  8. Make ready slice of turkey bacon, torn in half
  9. Make ready freshly cracked pepper
Steps to make Skinny Breakfast B.L.T. + A.:
  1. Toss bread in the toaster. While the bread is toasting spray a pan and cook the egg whites. When cooked,add cheese slice on top to melt slightly.
  2. On 1 side of toast spread 1/2 tbsp of light mayo then layer spinach, tomato, cooked egg white with cheese, turkey bacon and finally avocado slices. Pepper generously! Spread remaining 1/2 tbsp of light mayo on other toast slice. Place it on top and dive in!!
