The ingredients needed to make 66 calorie coffee vanilla cake:
  1. Get 4 tbsp oats(39)
  2. Make ready 7 tbsp double toned milk(84)
  3. Prepare 1 pinch baking soda(0 calories)
  4. Get 1 egg(60 calories)
  5. Prepare 6 tbsp sugar lite(it has half the calories of normal sugar-144)
  6. Prepare 2 1/3 rd tsp coffee powder(it has zero calories)
  7. Prepare As required Vanilla essence
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp maida(57 calories)
  9. Take 5 almonds crushed (15 calories)

Instructions to make 66 calorie coffee vanilla cake:
  1. Crush the oats into coarsed powder and take it in a bowl.
  2. Now add in a pinch of baking soda.
  3. To this, add milk and mix well.
  4. Now add coffee powder into it and whisk properly.
  5. Now add the vanilla essence to this mixture.
  6. Now add sugar lite into it.
  7. Now add the egg and mix well.
  8. Now grease a microwave safe bowl with olive oil.
  9. Now add the mixture and tap well.
  10. Now microwave it for 5 minutes and it's done. Let it cool down and then flip.
  11. To make the coffee ganacche, add 2tbsp of sugar, 2 and a half tbsp of water, 1 tsp of sugar and 1 tbsp of maida mix well and stir over gas to come to a ganacche consistency.
  12. Now spread the ganacche over the cake and spread evenly.
  13. Top it off with chopped almond and serve.❤️
