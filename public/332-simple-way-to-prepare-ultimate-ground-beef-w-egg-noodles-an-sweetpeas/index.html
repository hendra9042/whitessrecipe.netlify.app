The ingredients needed to make Ground Beef w/ egg noodles an sweetpeas:
  1. Prepare ground beef
  2. Prepare wide egg noodles
  3. Prepare 14oz sweetpeas
  4. Prepare onion power
  5. Make ready garlic power
Steps to make Ground Beef w/ egg noodles an sweetpeas:
  1. prepair egg noodles an drain at the same time brown your ground beef
  2. add onion an garlic power to the ground beef as it cooks
  3. when all is brown add the sweetpeas in the ground beef stir
  4. in a bowl put 1/2 egg noodles then your ground beef
  5. add the remaining egg noodles mix well an serve salt to taste
