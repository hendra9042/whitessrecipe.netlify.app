The ingredients needed to make My Egg & Sweetcorn Sandwich ðŸ’›:
  1. Make ready 8 Eggs hard boiled and peeled
  2. Take 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp Sour Cream
  4. Get 1/2 cup loose corn
  5. Get 1 pinch Salt
  6. Get 1/4 tsp Black cracked pepper
  7. Prepare 1 tsp lemon juice
Instructions to make My Egg & Sweetcorn Sandwich ðŸ’›:
  1. Break the hard boiled eggs in a large bowl, pressing down with a fork
  2. Add the mayonnaise and sour cream and stir in gently
  3. Add the salt and pepper and mix in.
  4. Lastly add the corn and the lemon juice. Mix in until its all creamy but not to break the corn.
  5. Add to a dish and make some tasty sandwiches with it. Sprinkle wth parsley dried or fresh.
  6. You can make open sandwiches and sandwiched together. Whats left you can cover leave in the fridge and have it next day on toast for breakfast.
