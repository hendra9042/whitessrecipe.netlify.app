The ingredients needed to make Rhubarb strawberry custard pie:
  1. Make ready 3 cups rhubarb sliced
  2. Get 1 c strawberries sliced
  3. Take 1/4 c all-purpose flour
  4. Prepare 1 c sugar
  5. Prepare 1/4 t salt
  6. Get 3 eggs beaten until frothy
  7. Get 1/2 t vanilla
  8. Prepare Crust for 2 layer pie:
  9. Take 2 c flour, 1 t salt, 3/4 c Crisco, 5-8 T cold water
  10. Take Refrigerate for 1/2 hr. Roll out

Instructions to make Rhubarb strawberry custard pie:
  1. In bowl combine sugar, flour & salt. Add rhubarb & strawberries, stir to coat. Spread in tin. Beat eggs & vanilla, pour over filling. Top with pastry
  2. Bake at 425° - 15 min, 350° - 30 min until knife is clear 1 in from center.
  3. Let cool on rack.
