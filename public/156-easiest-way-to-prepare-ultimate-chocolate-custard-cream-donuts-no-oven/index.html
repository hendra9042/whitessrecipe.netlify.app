The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Custard Cream Donuts [ No Oven ]:
  1. Make ready (for 8 pcs of donuts):
  2. Take Bread flour (high protein flour)
  3. Get Cake flour (low protein flour)
  4. Take Sugar
  5. Prepare Salt
  6. Make ready Yeast
  7. Make ready Eggs + Milk = 200 gr
  8. Prepare Unsalted butter
  9. Prepare // Chocolate custard:
  10. Take Egg yolks
  11. Get Vanilla essence
  12. Take Sugar
  13. Get Cake flour (low protein flour)
  14. Get Milk
  15. Prepare Chocolate
Steps to make Chocolate Custard Cream Donuts [ No Oven ]:
  1. First put all the flours (high-gluten flour + low-gluten flour + sugar + salt + yeast) into a large bowl and mix well. - - Prepare eggs and milk. - Eggs of any size can be used, as long as the combined weight of the eggs and milk is 200 grams. - - Beat the eggs, pour them into the flour and mix. - - Please watch the video for details:) - https://youtu.be/EmC-NguIoh0
  2. When the dough starts to stick together, you can start to transfer it to the table and knead the dough! Knead until the dough becomes smooth, about 3~4 minutes. - - Add the butter and knead until the dough sticks together again. - - Roll the dough into a circle, cover it with plastic wrap, and place it in a warm place. - Ferment for 1-1.5 hours or double the size.
  3. Prepare the chocolate custard: - Put the egg yolk + sugar + vanilla extract + low-gluten flour into a large bowl and mix well. - - Put the milk in the pot and heat it up. You can also use a microwave. - The temperature about 50°C~60°C. - Pour the milk into the egg mixture, then add the chocolate.
  4. Stir a little and then we transfer to the pot again. - Stir over medium-low heat until the chocolate thickens. - Put it in the refrigerator to cool down.
  5. The fermented dough slightly vents the air. Divide the dough into 8 portions. And then knead it into a small ball. - Cover with plastic wrap and ferment for 20 minutes.
  6. After 20 minutes, let the air out again, and then knead it into a small ball. This time put the dough on top of baking paper. - Cover with plastic wrap and wait for 1 hour to ferment.
  7. Preparation Oil temperature 160°C~170°C - Use low to medium heat fry for 1.5 to 2.5 minutes on each side. - - The frying time depends on the temperature, and the best way is to check whether the color of the donut has turned golden.
  8. Rub a small hole with chopsticks. - Squeeze the chocolate sauce into the doughnut and add a little sugar. - - Enjoy!!
