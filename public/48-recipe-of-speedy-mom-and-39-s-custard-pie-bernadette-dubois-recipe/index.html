The ingredients needed to make Mom's Custard Pie. Bernadette Dubois recipe:
  1. Make ready 4 slightly beaten eggs
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup sugar
  3. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt
  4. Prepare 2 1/2 cup scalded milk
  5. Get 1 tsp vanilla
  6. Make ready 1 9 inch unbaked pie cuust
Instructions to make Mom's Custard Pie. Bernadette Dubois recipe:
  1. mix all ingredients together and pour into the uncooked pie shell.
  2. sprinkle top with a little bit of nutmeg
  3. Preheat oven to 450°F. Bake for 10 minutes. Then turn oven down to 300°F and bake another 45 to 50 minutes
