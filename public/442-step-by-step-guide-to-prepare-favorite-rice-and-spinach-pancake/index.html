The ingredients needed to make Rice and Spinach Pancake:
  1. Get or cooked rice
  2. Make ready eggs
  3. Take handfull of spinach
  4. Make ready Grated cheese as desired
  5. Prepare Salt
  6. Prepare Butter
Steps to make Rice and Spinach Pancake:
  1. Put everything in a bowl.
  2. Mix well.
  3. Then heat the butter in a pan.
  4. Take 1 tbsp of the mixture then put them on the pan.
  5. Cook one side until golden brown then cook another side until well done.
  6. Serve.
