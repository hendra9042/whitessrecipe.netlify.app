The ingredients needed to make Farmer's casserole:
  1. Prepare frozen hashbrowns
  2. Make ready shredded cheddar cheese
  3. Make ready ground sausage
  4. Get green onions, chopped
  5. Take eggs
  6. Prepare salt
  7. Get ground black pepper

Steps to make Farmer's casserole:
  1. Preheat oven to 400.
  2. Cook and crumble sausage in a skillet.
  3. In a small bowl, beat the eggs, add milk, salt, and pepper.
  4. Grease a 9x13" casserole dish. Layer hashbrowns, sausage, cheese, egg mixture. Top with green onions.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes to 1 hour, until eggs are cooked through.
  6. **timing can really vary depending on your oven temp!
