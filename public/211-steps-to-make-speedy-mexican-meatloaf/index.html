The ingredients needed to make Mexican Meatloaf:
  1. Prepare chili powder
  2. Make ready light sour cream
  3. Take egg beaters
  4. Get extra lean ground beef
  5. Prepare reduced fat colby and monterey jack shredded cheese
  6. Make ready ground cumin
  7. Make ready salt
  8. Take minced garlic
  9. Get diced green chili peppers
Instructions to make Mexican Meatloaf:
  1. preheat oven 400°F. In a large bowl, mix the ground beef, egg beaters and spices together.
  2. cut a large square of waxed paper and roll out the ground beef mixture into a flat square
  3. sprinkle with half of the cheese and of the green chile peppers.
  4. roll the waxed paper with the meatloaf in the ,middle, until shapes into a loaf.
  5. carefully unfold the wax paper from the meatloaf and remove.
  6. fold the ends of the meatloaf to the cheese and chiles will not spill out during cooking
  7. place in a loaf pan, top with cheese, green chiles, and sour cream
  8. bake in oven at 400°F for 45 to 60 min.
