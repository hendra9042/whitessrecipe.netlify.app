The ingredients needed to make The better for you breakfast sandwich:
  1. Prepare 1 English Muffin ( preferably whole wheat)
  2. Get 1 slice Any type of cheese, preferably low fat
  3. Take 1 tsp olive oil
  4. Prepare 1/2 Tomato
  5. Get 1 oz Smoked Turkey
  6. Get 1 tbsp Vinegar
  7. Prepare 1 tsp Salt
  8. Make ready 1 Egg
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp Oregano

Steps to make The better for you breakfast sandwich:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Fork split your English muffin and toast it
  3. Heat water just until a simmer and put a tablespoon of vinegar and a tsp of salt
  4. Crack the egg in a separate bowl then carefully put it into the water. Gather the whites by using a slotted spoon
  5. Leave for about 3 minutes or more if you want the yolk to be firmer
  6. To assemble your sandwich: put one slice of the cheese over the bottom side of the muffin. Let it melt in the over for about 3 minutes on a baking dish. Remove from oven and put the tomato slice and smoked turkey.
  7. Put it in the over for another 2 minutes and then remove. Finally put the poached egg on top of the sandwich. Season with salt and pepper
  8. Enjoy!
