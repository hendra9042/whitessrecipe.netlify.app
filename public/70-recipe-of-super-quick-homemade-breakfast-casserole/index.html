The ingredients needed to make Breakfast casserole:
  1. Make ready 1/2 loaf Italian bread
  2. Get 3 cup Milk
  3. Get 1/2 lb Mild breakfast sausage
  4. Get 1 cup Cubed ham
  5. Prepare 2 cup cheddar cheese
  6. Make ready 6 scrambled eggs
  7. Make ready 1 dash salt
  8. Make ready 1 dash pepper
  9. Take 1 dash garlic powder
  10. Make ready 1 Real bacon bits

Instructions to make Breakfast casserole:
  1. cook sausage
  2. Break apart bread in large bowl
  3. Add rest of ingredients except bacon and mix it all together
  4. Butter 9 x 12 pyrex or foil pan
  5. Pour mixture in pyrex
  6. Sprinkle bacon bits on top
  7. Bake @ 350 for 20 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean
