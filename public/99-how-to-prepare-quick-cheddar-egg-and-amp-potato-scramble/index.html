Steps to make Cheddar Egg & Potato Scramble ðŸ¥”ðŸ§€ðŸ¥š:
  1. In a large skillet heat up oil on medium heat. Once oil is heated add in chopped onions and potato's. Add paprika, onion powder, garlic powder, salt, green onion, and pepper to potato's and onions. Cover with lid and let cook on medium heat.   3. Break up eggs and mix together with potato's. Add chedder cheese to top and cover. Let sit 1-2 minutes or until cheese has melted.
  4. Serve with more chopped green onion (optional) and a side of toast! Enjoy!!
