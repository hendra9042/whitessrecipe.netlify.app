Steps to make Potato salad:
  1. Boil potatoes in skins and peel.
  2. Make vinaigrette and pour over potatoes while still hot.
  3. Leave to cool overnight.
  4. Add mustard, mustard pickle, and mayo. Break up potatoes and mix.
  5. Chop and add dill peppers and celery.
  6. Shop and add 2 boiled eggs
  7. Add season and chives.
