Instructions to make Pastry cheese straws:
  1. On lightly floured surface, roll one sheet of pastry out to about 14" x 12".
  2. Brush surface with beaten egg.
  3. Roll out 2nd pastry to same size.
  4. Sprinkle cheese on top of pastry with egg mixture leaving 1/8" of edges clear.
  5. Sprinkle garlic powder lightly over cheese. Top with second pastry sheet.
  6. Roll lightly to press sheets together. Then cut pastry with the back of a knife or pizza wheel ,cross length into 24 1/2 " strips.
  7. Twist each strip as you then place on a parchment lined baking tray.
  8. Bake in a preheated 400°F oven for 15-25 minutes. Remove when golden brown.
