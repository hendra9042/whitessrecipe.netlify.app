The ingredients needed to make Rolled Egg Omelette:
  1. Take 7 eggs
  2. Get 1/8 Cup finely diced carrot
  3. Get 1/8 Cup finely diced green bell pepper
  4. Get 1/8 Cup finely diced red bell pepper
  5. Prepare 1/8 finely diced spring onion
  6. Get 1/3 Cup finely diced sausage
  7. Take 1 Teaspoon garlic powder
  8. Take 1/3 Teaspoon black pepper
  9. Make ready 1/8 Teaspoon salt
  10. Get 1/2 Teaspoon seasoning
  11. Prepare 1 Tablespoon vegetable oil
Instructions to make Rolled Egg Omelette:
  1. Crack the eggs in a mixing bowl and whisk. Add the finely diced vegetables (bell peppers, spring onion, carrot and sausage).
  2. Add salt, seasoning powder, garlic powder and black pepper. Whisk thoroughly. Heat up a non stick pan on medium low flame. Grease with oil and pour in the egg mix ( enough to completely coat the pan.
  3. Allow for about 2 minutes to set then use a spatula or chopstick to gradually fold the omelette from one end of the pan to the other. Once you fold to the end of the pan, bring the omelette back to the starting point and add another egg mix to repeat the process again.
  4. Continue this process until all the egg mix is exhausted. Allow the rolled omelette to cook on both side for about 2 minutes so it can set completely. Take off heat and allow to cool. Slice and serve with ketchup, pancakes or bread. EnjoyðŸ˜‰ðŸ˜Š
