The ingredients needed to make Omelette Egg Roll:
  1. Take 3 eggs
  2. Make ready Small carrot chopped
  3. Make ready Onion chopped
  4. Make ready Scott bonnet chopped
  5. Take Green pepper chopped
  6. Make ready to taste Salt
  7. Make ready Half knorr cube
  8. Make ready 1 pinch tumeric
Instructions to make Omelette Egg Roll:
  1. Break egg and mix
  2. Add veggies, seasonings and mix
  3. Heat up pan pour in egg and cook until the bottom is set on a low heat
  4. Fold and pour in another. Continue the process until done
