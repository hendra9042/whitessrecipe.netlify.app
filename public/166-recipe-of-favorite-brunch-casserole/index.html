Instructions to make brunch casserole:
  1. line bottom of buttered 9 x 13 baking dish with cresent roll dough
  2. pinch perferated lines to seal
  3. cook sausage until done then drain on paper towel
  4. sprinkle sausage over dough evenly
  5. sprinkle cheeses evenly over sausage
  6. beat eggs, milk,salt and pepper slightly
  7. pour mixture into pan over cheeses and sausage
  8. bake 15 min @ 425°F oven…do not cover
  9. let stand 10 min before serving
