The ingredients needed to make Monster Cookies:
  1. Take 12 eggs
  2. Get 5 cup brown sugar
  3. Make ready 4 cup white sugar
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp vanilla
  5. Get 1 tbsp corn syrup
  6. Make ready 3 tsp baking soda
  7. Prepare 2 cup butter (no substitutes)
  8. Get 6 cup peanut butter
  9. Get 18 cup oatmeal (i use quick oats)
  10. Take 2 cup semi sweet chocolate chips
  11. Make ready 2 cup m & m's (no substitutes)
Instructions to make Monster Cookies:
  1. Mix in order given in LARGE bowl. (My kitchen aid can only handle this up to the peanut butter, then I MIX by hand)
  2. Spoon onto ungreased cookie sheet. Make these cookies big!
  3. Bake at 350°F for 8-10 min
