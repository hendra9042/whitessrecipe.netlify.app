Steps to make Banana walnut and raisin bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 180°c
  2. in an electric mixer with beater attachment cream butter, sugar, maple and vanilla
  3. add eggs one at a time beating well after each addition
  4. with mixer on slow add mashed banana till incorporated
  5. while still slowly mixing add remainder of ingrediants till just combined
  6. pour into greased and lined large loaf tin
  7. cook for approx 40 min or till starting to brown on top
