The ingredients needed to make Egg Salad Sandwiches:
  1. Prepare Base Ingredients
  2. Make ready 12 Large Eggs
  3. Make ready Loaf Sandwich Bread
  4. Take Flavor
  5. Make ready Fresh Oregano
  6. Get Green Onions
  7. Get Dijon Mustard
  8. Make ready Mayonnaise
  9. Take Salt
  10. Take Pepper
Instructions to make Egg Salad Sandwiches:
  1. Prep: Hard Boil Eggs, and then Dice them.
  2. Combine diced eggs, green onions, oregano, mayo and mustard. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Toast bread, spread mayo on the bread and then egg salad.
