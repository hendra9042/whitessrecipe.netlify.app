The ingredients needed to make ＊Basic Custard Cream＊:
  1. Prepare Milk
  2. Make ready Egg yolk
  3. Prepare Granulated sugar
  4. Take Cake flour
  5. Make ready Cornstarch
  6. Make ready Butter
  7. Take Vanilla essence
Steps to make ＊Basic Custard Cream＊:
  1. Mix the flour and cornstarch together. Put the egg yolks and sugar into a bowl and whisk until it turns pale.
  2. Sieve Step 1 into a bowl and mix well.
  3. Put the milk in a saucepan and put on a medium heat. Take it off the heat just before it starts to boil, and add to Step 2 little by little.
  4. Strain through a fine mesh strainer back into the saucepan, and heat whilst stirring with a wooden spoon.
  5. Turn off the heat once it turns into the consistency of cream. Add the butter and vanilla essence, and mix well.
  6. Pour the custard cream into a shallow tray and let it cool. Wrap with cling film and chill in the fridge.
