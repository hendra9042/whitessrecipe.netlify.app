The ingredients needed to make Oatmeal Breakfast Bars:
  1. Prepare vanilla
  2. Get ground cinnamon
  3. Take sugar
  4. Take applesauce
  5. Get eggs
  6. Prepare flour
  7. Take lemon juice
  8. Prepare salt
  9. Prepare quick oats
  10. Get bananas
  11. Prepare dried fruit (I did blueberries and craisens)
Instructions to make Oatmeal Breakfast Bars:
  1. Preheat the oven to 350*f
  2. Mix everything well
  3. Grease a big pan or a couple small ones
  4. Smooth mixture in pans
  5. Bake 30 min or until golden brown edges
