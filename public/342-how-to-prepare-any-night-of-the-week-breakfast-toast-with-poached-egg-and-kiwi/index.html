The ingredients needed to make Breakfast toast with poached egg and kiwi:
  1. Prepare bread
  2. Make ready egg
  3. Get salted butter
  4. Make ready Salt
  5. Get Topping
  6. Make ready eggs
  7. Make ready kiwi (or other fruit)
  8. Get water
Steps to make Breakfast toast with poached egg and kiwi:
  1. Prepare the bread and kiwi first. Cut kiwi in small cubes.
  2. Beat 1 egg, add some salt and soak the bread into it.
  3. Warm the pan, add butter. Wait until melted, then fry the soaked bread in low heat until turns golden brown. Set aside.
  4. On the same pan, fry the kiwi for 1 mins. Pour kiwi on top of bread.
  5. Boil the water in a saucepan. Crack 2 eggs in different small bowls. After the water start boiling, carefully put the eggs into the water. Turn to low heat.
  6. Wait until 1-2 mins. If you like firm egg, you can add 1 mins more. Then turn off the heat.
  7. Slowly take the egg with spoon and put on top of breads.
