The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Potato Pancakes:
  1. Take 3 large or 4 medium potatoes
  2. Take 3 eggs or equivalent amount of egg substitute
  3. Get 1/4 cup Coconut flour
  4. Prepare 1 tsp dried parsley
  5. Prepare 1 tsp onion salt
  6. Prepare 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  7. Get 1/3 cup olive oil
Instructions to make Cheesy Potato Pancakes:
  1. Peel & grate potatoes to yield about 4 cups. Press excess moisture from the potatoes & pat dry with paper towels. Place in a large bowl.
  2. Whisk eggs in small bowl. Add to potatoes & mix well. Stir in flour, parsley, onion salt, & cheese.
  3. Heat oil in skillet. Shape potato mixture into patties. Add patties to hot oil in batches. Cook turning once, until browned, about 3 minutes per side.
  4. Place on paper towel lined plate to cool. Serve warm.
  5. Can serve with sour cream or any other desired garnish or condiment.
