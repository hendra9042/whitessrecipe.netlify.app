The ingredients needed to make Aunt's Fried Noodle Recipe:
  1. Get egg noodles
  2. Take shallots
  3. Take garlic
  4. Prepare cayenne peppers
  5. Take red chili
  6. Prepare sweet soy sauce
  7. Get sugar
  8. Make ready pepper
  9. Prepare Water
  10. Make ready Cooking oil
  11. Make ready eggs
  12. Make ready chicken powder
Instructions to make Aunt's Fried Noodle Recipe:
  1. Put noodles into boiled water about 2 to 3 minutes, and dried it then set a side
  2. Heat cooking oil and make scrambled eggs, then set a side
  3. Blend shallots,garlic cloves and cayenne pepper, and for red chili just remove the seeds and chopped
  4. Heat cooking oil and fry all ingredients until fragrant
  5. Add sweet soy sauce,chicken powder, sugar,pepper and water,stir well
  6. Put in scrambled eggs and noodle then mix well
  7. Last step taste it, and add more seasoning if needed. Then fried noodles is ready to serve
  8. Place it in the serving dish and have a nice lunch happy people..❤️
