The ingredients needed to make Easy Whole Egg Custard Cream:
  1. Make ready 260 ml Milk
  2. Take 2 tbsp Sugar
  3. Take 1 Egg
  4. Get 1 1/2 tbsp Cake Flour
  5. Take 1 1/2 tbsp Corn starch
  6. Get 10 grams Butter
  7. Prepare 30 grams Heavy cream
  8. Make ready 3 drops Vanilla essence
Instructions to make Easy Whole Egg Custard Cream:
  1. Heat the milk until it is warm to the touch, then add all of the sugar and dissolve thoroughly.
  2. Mix the egg, the flour, and the corn starch in a bowl.
  3. Slowly add the milk mixture to the bowl from Step 2 and mix slowly.
  4. Strain Step 3 into a sauce pan.
  5. Cook on medium heat while constantly mixing with a wooden spatula, for about 10 minutes or until the custard starts thicken.
  6. Add the butter and mix well. Finally, add the cream and the vanilla and mix, and you are done.
