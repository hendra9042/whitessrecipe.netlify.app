The ingredients needed to make Korean Egg Roll:
  1. Get 10 Eggs
  2. Make ready Salt to taste
  3. Make ready 1/2 Onion
  4. Take 1 Green pepper
  5. Prepare 1/2 Red pepper
  6. Get 1 Spring onion
  7. Prepare Curry qnd thyme to taste
  8. Make ready 1 Maggi
Instructions to make Korean Egg Roll:
  1. Separate the egg, the york from the white, add a little maggi, and curry on both eggs
  2. Add veggies to the white, heat up pan pour the yellow egg and cook until the bottom is set, fold and pour in another
  3. Pour the white egg continue the process until done
  4. Cut in circle shape and serve 1. Done
  5. Enjoy
