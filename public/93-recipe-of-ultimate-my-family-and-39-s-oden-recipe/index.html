The ingredients needed to make My Family's Oden Recipe:
  1. Take Dashi stock:
  2. Prepare Water

  6. Prepare Bonito-based dashi stock granules
  7. Make ready Salt
  8. Prepare Main ingredients:
  9. Get Daikon radish
  10. Take Konnyaku
  11. Get Tied kombu
  12. Get Eggs
  13. Make ready as much (to taste) (Fish or meat sausages) and other ingredients of your choice
Instructions to make My Family's Oden Recipe:
  1. Cut the daikon radish into 2 cm wide pieces. Also cut the konnyaku into triangles and put small cuts in the surface so that the flavor penetrates well. Parboil the daikon radish and the konnyaku. Start boiling the eggs.
  2. Soak musubi-konbu (tied konbu seaweed) or just regular konbu seaweed in hot water for about 5 minutes to rehydrate. Also pour hot water over the fish cakes to drain excess oil. Cut hanpen and other ingredients into your preferred sizes.
  3. Put all the dashi stock ingredients except for salt in a pot and bring to the boil. Add 2 to 3 pinches of salt to adjust to your taste.
  4. Put the parboiled daikon radish and konnyaku, musubi-konbu, boiled eggs, and other ingredients of your choice in the pot and simmer. All done!
