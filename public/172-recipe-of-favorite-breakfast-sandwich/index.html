The ingredients needed to make Breakfast sandwich:
  1. Get 1 each egg whites
  2. Take 2 slice turkey
  3. Take 2 slice tomatos
  4. Get 1/3 packages spinach
  5. Take 2 slice whole wheat bread
  6. Get 1 dash butter
Instructions to make Breakfast sandwich:
  1. Place butter in frying pan
  2. Scramble egg whites in a bowl
  3. While butter is heating slice up your tomatoes
  4. Now lets start scrambling thoes eggs
  5. After eggs are done lets put everything together
  6. Add your raw baby spinach on top of tomatos and enjoy your breakfast sandwich
