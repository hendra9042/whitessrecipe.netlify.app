The ingredients needed to make Grilled Breakfast Burritos:
  1. Make ready Main
  2. Prepare Eggs *Usually i use egg beaters, but did not have those on hand this time /:
  3. Take potatoes
  4. Prepare onions
  5. Get shredded cheese of your choice
  6. Get milk
  7. Make ready olive oil, extra virgin
  8. Make ready Chopped red bell pepper
  9. Take Seasonings
  10. Prepare sea salt
  11. Get black pepper
  12. Prepare Red Robbin Seasoning, or a season salt of your choice
  13. Prepare onion powder
  14. Get garlic powder (I use A LOT!)
  15. Prepare Southwest Chipotle Dressing
  16. Prepare Hot sauces of preference
Instructions to make Grilled Breakfast Burritos:
  1. First, clean your potatoes (I leave the skin on and just wash them extra well), and then chop into small squares. *Do the potatoes first because they usually take the longest.
  2. Heat some olive oil in a wok on low-med. *You can use a frying pan - I chose a wok so that I could mix my potatoes around to get an even fry.
  3. While the oil is heating up, chop the onions how you like (I chop mine into little squares).
  4. Once the oil is heated, add the potatoes & the onions and turn heat up to medium. Here is where i add some seasonings also - the garlic, season salt, etc. *Make sure to keep a close eye on everything and stir frequently to prevent burning. You may need to add a little extra olive oil too during this process.
  5. While the potatoes & onions are cooking, crack your eggs into a bowl, add milk, & whisk until well blended.
  6. Spray a non-stick frying pan with PAM, turn your burner heat to medium, and add the egg mixture, and cover with a lid.
  7. I let my eggs cook without stirring them until they resemble a sloppy omelette in process. At this point, I turn the heat down a little bit and slowly move the eggs around so they don't start to burn. I then cover them again to let them finish cooking. *The reason I use this process is because I like my eggs fluffy and in large, bouncy chunks. Everyone seems to love my eggs & think I do something "special" to them, but really I just exercise a little patience lol ðŸ˜‰
  8. Once the eggs are done cooking, add your cheeses (I go overboard lol), turn off the heat and cover them to let the cheese melt.
  9. Once both the eggs & potatoes are ready, lay a tortilla shell on a plate, drizzle it with dressing, then load it up with 'tatoes & eggs! (I do potatoes first, then eggs on top).
  10. Wrap up the tortilla up burrito style, spray the outside of it with some PAM, and place it in another PAM sprayed skillet with heat on low.
  11. Brown to your liking, and be careful when turning it over to brown the other side so as not to bust the ends open.
