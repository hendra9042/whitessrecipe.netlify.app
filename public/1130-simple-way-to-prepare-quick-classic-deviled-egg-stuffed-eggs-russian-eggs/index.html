The ingredients needed to make Classic Deviled Egg/ Stuffed eggs/Russian eggs:
  1. Make ready hard boiled and peeled eggs
  2. Get Mayonnaise
  3. Take Mustard paste
  4. Prepare Salt
  5. Prepare Black pepper powder
  6. Prepare Chopped coriander, tomato for garnishing
  7. Prepare Piping bag with nozzle
Instructions to make Classic Deviled Egg/ Stuffed eggs/Russian eggs:
  1. Cut the boiled and peeled eggs into half.
  2. Take out the yolk.
  3. Mix with mayonnaise, mustard paste, salt and pepper powder.
  4. Take a piping bag with star nozzle.
  5. Pipe this mixture into each cavity of egg.
  6. Top with chopped coriander leaves and chopped tomato.
  7. Serve cold as a side dish.
