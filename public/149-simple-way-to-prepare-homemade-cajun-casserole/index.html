The ingredients needed to make Cajun Casserole:
  1. Take smoked Andouille sausage
  2. Make ready onion
  3. Get green bell pepper
  4. Take red bell pepper
  5. Get jalapeno pepper
  6. Take tomato
  7. Make ready eggs
  8. Prepare milk
  9. Take Cajun seasoning
  10. Make ready black pepper
  11. Get hot sauce
  12. Make ready of bread
  13. Make ready cheddar cheese
  14. Make ready shrimp
  15. Take garlic
  16. Take butter
  17. Prepare olive oil
Steps to make Cajun Casserole:
  1. preheat oven to 400°F Fahrenheit
  2. in a large skillet mix shrimp butter olive oil garlic and 2 teaspoons of Cajun seasoning
  3. cook until garlic is translucent
  4. turn heat to low stir occasionally
  5. in another large skillet cook sliced sausage with onions red pepper and bell peppers until vegetables are translucent
  6. add tomatoes and let them cook for a minute or 2
  7. with eggs with milk in a bowl with 1 teaspoon Cajun seasoning jalapeno pepper and a few dashes of hot sauce
  8. care bread into little pieces
  9. arrange torn piece of bread over the bottom of a buttered baking dish
  10. coat with the sausage and vegetables
  11. top sausage and vegetables with cheddar cheese
  12. add shrimp and seasoning on top of cheese
  13. pour mixture evenly on top
  14. bake for 40 minutes at 400°F Fahrenheit
