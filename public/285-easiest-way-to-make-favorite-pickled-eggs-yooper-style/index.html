The ingredients needed to make Pickled Eggs - Yooper Style:
  1. Make ready Eggs
  2. Prepare Vinegar
  3. Get Tabasco Sauce
  4. Prepare water
  5. Prepare Salt
  6. Get Black Pepper, ground
  7. Make ready Red Pepper, crushed, dry
  8. Make ready onion, chopped
  9. Prepare jalapeños, sliced (16oz)
Steps to make Pickled Eggs - Yooper Style:
  1. Boil eggs for 12 Minutes
  2. Allow eggs to cool
  3. Peel eggs and rinse
  4. Chop onion
  5. Combine vinegar, water, jalapeños with sauce, onion, Tabasco, salt, black pepper, and red pepper in large sauce pot. This will be called the 'juice'
  6. Cover sauce pan
  7. Bring the juice to a boil (warning: boiling vinegar is very stinky)
  8. In a 1 gallon glass jar, place 1 layer of eggs at the bottom
  9. Cover the eggs with Jalapeños slices and 'juice'
  10. repeat last 2 steps until all eggs are in the jar. There may be some 'juice' left over.
  11. Store in the refrigerator.
  12. Soak for at least 5 days.
  13. Serve 1 egg with 3 or 4 jalapeño slices and enjoy with your favorite beverage.
