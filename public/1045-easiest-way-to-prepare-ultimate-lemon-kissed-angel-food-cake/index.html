The ingredients needed to make Lemon Kissed Angel Food Cake:
  1. Get cane sugar
  2. Get salt
  3. Make ready cake flour, sifted
  4. Make ready egg whites, room temp
  5. Prepare warm water
  6. Make ready lemon extract
  7. Prepare cream of tartar
Steps to make Lemon Kissed Angel Food Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 350º
  2. Food process the sugar for 2 minutes until superfine
  3. Sift half the sugar with the salt and the cake flour
  4. In a large mixing bowl, use a balloon whisk to combine the egg whites, water, lemon extract, and cream of tartar. After 2 minutes, switch to a stand mixer. Slowly sift the remaining sugar on medium speed until you have medium peaks.
  5. Stir 1/4 of the egg whites into the flour to moisten it.
  6. Gently fold the rest of the egg whites into the flour mix
  7. Spoon the mix into an ungreased tube pan
  8. Bake for 35 minutes or until golden brown. The visual cue here is more reliable than time, as your time may vary wildly due to cake pan, oven temp variation, etc. Test doneness with a wooden skewer
  9. Cool upside down on a cooling rack for 2 hours
  10. Use a butter knife to run around the edge of the pan to make sure it is loose all the way around
  11. Serve with a berry compote
