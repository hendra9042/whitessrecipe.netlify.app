The ingredients needed to make Egg Salad Super Sandwich:
  1. Get 6 eggs
  2. Prepare 1 dash dill
  3. Get 1 pinch paprika
  4. Take 1 pinch Tony Chacheres creole seasoning
  5. Take 1 dash garlic powder
  6. Get 1/2 cup Red onion
  7. Get 1/2 cup pickles or relish. I prefer cutting my own sour dill pickles.
  8. Take 1 mayonnaise/mayonnaise substitute
  9. Make ready 1 tbsp spicy yellow mustard, grey pupon
  10. Get 1 grated parmesan cheese, to taste
  11. Take 4 slice sharp cheddar cheese
  12. Get 1 bunch sprouts, clover, broccoli or bean whichever you prefer
  13. Prepare 1 bread

  3. Fold all ingredients together until desired chunkiness.

  5. Put slices of cheese on bread
