The ingredients needed to make Pasta Carbonara:
  1. Prepare spaghetti (or any other pasta)
  2. Prepare bacon
  3. Prepare eggs
  4. Get table spoons of shredded parmesan cheese (or pasta cheese)
  5. Make ready tooth of garlic
  6. Prepare Olive oil
  7. Make ready Salt
  8. Take Pepper
