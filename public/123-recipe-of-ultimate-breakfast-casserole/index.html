The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Prepare Hash browns
  2. Take Sausage
  3. Take Eggs
  4. Take Milk
  5. Prepare Grated cheddar cheese
Instructions to make Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Cover bottom of 9x13 dish with hash browns. Brown 1 lbs sausage.
  2. Brown sausage and drain grease.
  3. Spread sausage over hash browns. Cover with shredded cheese.
  4. Mix 12 eggs and milk. Season to taste with pepper and salt. Pour over cheese
  5. Can be refrigerated over night. Bake at 350 for 50 minutes.
