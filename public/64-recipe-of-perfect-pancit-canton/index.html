The ingredients needed to make Pancit Canton:
  1. Get 250 g Egg Noodle
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup carrots cut into strips
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup sayote cut into strips
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup Baguio beans cut diagonally in small pieces
  5. Make ready 1 cup cooked chicken breast shredded
  6. Prepare 2 cups chicken broth
  7. Get 1 cup cabbage chopped
  8. Take 1 big onion cut into small slices
  9. Get 6 cloves garlic minced
  10. Make ready to taste Soy suace and pepper
  11. Make ready Slice calamansi to serve when eating
Steps to make Pancit Canton:
  1. Sauté onions and garlic and shredded chicken.
  2. Add carrots and sayote. Then add baguio beans.
  3. Add chicken broth and bring to boil then add cabbage.
  4. Add egg noodles and cook until broth dries out. Add soy sauce and pepper. Squeeze calamansi to bring out all the flavors when eating.
