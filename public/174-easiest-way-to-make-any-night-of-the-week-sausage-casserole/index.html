Steps to make sausage casserole:
  1. pre heat oven to 350 degress
  2. start boiling water, at a rolling boil put noodles in. cook until nearly done.
  3. cook sausage and onion until done. don't drain
  4. in a bowl mix all ingredients together except stuffing.
  5. make stuffing according to directions.
  6. spoon stuffing on top.
  7. bake in greased 11x7 baking dish uncovered for 30 minutes
