The ingredients needed to make Easy Low-Calorie Tofu and Egg Dish!:
  1. Take 1 block Tofu
  2. Prepare 1 Egg
  3. Make ready 1 dash Salt
  4. Take 1 Dashi soy sauce (soy sauce premixed with dashi)
  5. Take 250 ml Water
  6. Make ready 1 dash Green onion
  7. Take 1 tbsp Katakuriko
Instructions to make Easy Low-Calorie Tofu and Egg Dish!:
  1. Mash up the tofu in a bowl and make it smooth. Add the egg and salt, then mix some more.
  2. Transfer everything from the bowl into a container that's about the size of a rice bowl. Cover it with aluminum foil. (Poke a few holes in the foil with a fork.)
  3. Microwave for 5 minutes! (Please adjust the heating time to your microwave.)
  4. Put the water, dashi soy sauce, and green onions in a small pan and bring to a boil. Add the katakuriko dissolved in water. When the mixture becomes thick, pour it over the egg-tofu and enjoy!
