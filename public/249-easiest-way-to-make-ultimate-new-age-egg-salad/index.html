The ingredients needed to make New Age Egg Salad:
  1. Take Avacado
  2. Make ready large eggs
  3. Prepare spinach
  4. Take couscous
  5. Prepare lemon juice
  6. Get Coconut oil
  7. Make ready Cajun seasoning
  8. Prepare parts salt
  9. Take parts garlic powder
  10. Make ready parts Teaspoon paprika
  11. Prepare part black pepper
  12. Make ready part onion powder
  13. Prepare part cayenne
  14. Get parts oregano
  15. Prepare parts thyme
  16. Get parts chilli flakes
Instructions to make New Age Egg Salad:
  1. First start with your Cajun seasoning, simply mix all the ingredients listed above together.
  2. De-stone the avocado then mash and add a splash of lemon juice
  3. Add your couscous and a tablespoon of your Cajun seasoning to a bowl with 90ml of boiling water and a half tablespoon of your coconut oil, cover and leave for 10 mins.
  4. Wash your spinach thoroughly
  5. Add 1to2 teaspoon(s) of coconut oil to a pan and heat, once up to heat crack in your eggs and fry (I prefer this with a runny yoke)
  6. Once all is cooked serve in layers, fluff your couscous with a fork and create the bottom layer with this.
  7. Then spread the spinach to create you second layer.
  8. Use your avocado to create the third layer
  9. And place your eggs on top
