The ingredients needed to make Breakfast casserole Lunch Bell:
  1. Take pork sausage
  2. Take bread
  3. Make ready eggs
  4. Prepare milk
  5. Prepare cream of mushroom soup
  6. Take salt
  7. Make ready dry mustard
  8. Make ready cheddar cheese shredded
Steps to make Breakfast casserole Lunch Bell:
  1. brown sausage in frying pan until thoroughly cooked.
  2. drain fat and place sausage on paper towels and set aside
  3. cut shapes out of bread using beverage glass
  4. take remaining bread and tear into pieces
  5. in medium bowl beat the eggs
  6. add milk, mushroom soup, salt and dry mustard to eggs and mix well
  7. in 9 x 13 inch glass baking dish, layer pieces of bread, then sausage, then cheese, then place bread shapes on top
  8. slowly pour egg mixture over everything, being sure to saturate each piece of bread
  9. cover with foil and refrigerate overnight or at least 8 hours before baking
  10. when ready to bake, tent foil over casserole bad bake 350 for 1 hour.
  11. remove foil last 15 minutes to brown
