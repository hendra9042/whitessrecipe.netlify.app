Instructions to make Sausage gravy casserole:
  1. Roll out biscuits and line 9x13 baking dish. (Use 1 roll, save the other for later)
  2. Scramble the eggs, spread evenly on top of biscuits.
  3. Fry hash browns and season with onion powder/seasoning salt. Add on top of eggs. You can also add any veggies or other meats as well.
  4. Brown sausage, bouillon and flour mixture. Add milk and stir continually until gravy consistency.
  5. Pour gravy on to mixture then cover with other can of rolled biscuits. Sprinkle cheese on top of casserole.
  6. Bake 400° for 15 minutes.
