The ingredients needed to make Peanut Butter Freaks:
  1. Make ready peanut butter
  2. Make ready sugar
  3. Prepare brown sugar
  4. Make ready custard powder (sub with flour if you don't have this)
  5. Make ready egg
  6. Take semi sweet chocolate chips
  7. Make ready quick oats
  8. Prepare baking powder
Instructions to make Peanut Butter Freaks:
  1. Mix everything up
  2. Roll mixture into 1 inch balls
  3. Place in cookie sheet and flatten with fork
  4. Bake 12 minutes at 350
  5. Seriously, if you don't have custard powder, go out and get some. The one I use is made by Horne's. I add that stuff in many desserts.
