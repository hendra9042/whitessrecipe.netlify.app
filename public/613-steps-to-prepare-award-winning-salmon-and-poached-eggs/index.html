Instructions to make Salmon and poached eggs:
  1. Add water in the pan and bring it to boil. Add a few dashes of vinegar
  2. Once water has boiled turn the heat to low and let it simmer. Start swirling the water and quickly break egg into the simmered water. Should poach the eggs for 3 mins
  3. While the eggs are poaching prep your salad to your liking and add your smoked salmon. Drizzle a bit of olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
  4. Once poaching is done take it out and serve. Season with salt and pepper
