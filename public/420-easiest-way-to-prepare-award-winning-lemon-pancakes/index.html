The ingredients needed to make Lemon pancakes:
  1. Take 300 g flour
  2. Take 2 eggs
  3. Make ready Grated rind of one lemon
  4. Prepare 250 ml milk
  5. Get 3 tablespoons oil
  6. Prepare 3 tablespoons sugar
  7. Take Oil for frying

Steps to make Lemon pancakes:
  1. Mix everything except oil for frying, whisk to blend completely and ensure it is smooth.
  2. Heat the pan, brush lightly with oil
  3. Pour the batter thinly in the pan and spread evenly
  4. Once slightly browned, turn to the other side and brown lightly.
  5. Repeat until all the batter is used.
  6. Serve hot
