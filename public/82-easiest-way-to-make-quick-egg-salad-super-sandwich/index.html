The ingredients needed to make Egg Salad Super Sandwich:
  1. Get 6 eggs
  2. Make ready 1 dash dill
  3. Make ready 1 pinch paprika
  4. Make ready 1 pinch Tony Chacheres creole seasoning
  5. Take 1 dash garlic powder
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup Red onion
  7. Take 1/2 cup pickles or relish. I prefer cutting my own sour dill pickles.
  8. Make ready 1 mayonnaise/mayonnaise substitute
  9. Get 1 tbsp spicy yellow mustard, grey pupon
  10. Take 1 grated parmesan cheese, to taste
  11. Get 4 slice sharp cheddar cheese
  12. Make ready 1 bunch sprouts, clover, broccoli or bean whichever you prefer
  13. Prepare 1 bread
Steps to make Egg Salad Super Sandwich:
  1. Boil eggs then cold wash them and peel.
  2. Mash up peeled whole eggs and add
  3. Fold all ingredients together until desired chunkiness.

  5. Put slices of cheese on bread
  6. Put a dollop or two of egg salad mixture on top of the cheese, then add bunch of sprouts
