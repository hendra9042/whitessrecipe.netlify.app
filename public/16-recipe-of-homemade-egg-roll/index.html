The ingredients needed to make Egg roll:
  1. Make ready 1 chapati
  2. Prepare 1 capsicum
  3. Take 1 onion
  4. Prepare 1 tomato
  5. Get as per taste salt and pepper
  6. Get 2 eggs
  7. Prepare as needed oil

Steps to make Egg roll:
  1. Break eggs mix all half quantity veggies and salt and pepper,give a nice mix
  2. On a hot tawa put oil add half egg mixture cook it now place chapati and spread remaining mixture on it..now cook well
  3. In other bowl mix capsicum,onion,tomato and salt and pepper,place this mix in roll,fold it and serve.
