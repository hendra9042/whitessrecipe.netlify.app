The ingredients needed to make Flax Nutmeg Pancakes:
  1. Get flour
  2. Prepare salt
  3. Make ready baking powder
  4. Get milk
  5. Take oil
  6. Prepare spoonful of honey
  7. Make ready eggs
  8. Make ready vanilla extract
  9. Get ground flax seed (flax seed meal), usually sold near the oats
  10. Make ready to 2 teaspoons ground nutmeg
Steps to make Flax Nutmeg Pancakes:
  1. In a medium bowl, combine the flax seed meal, flour, salt, baking powder and nutmeg.
  2. Stir and set aside.
  3. In a blender, or using a hand mixer on high, combine the milk, oil, honey, eggs and vanilla.
  4. Blend until well mixed.
  5. Add the dry ingredients and mix until well incorporated.
  6. Heat a cast iron frying pan or griddle over medium high heat and swirl a tablespoon of oil to cover.
  7. Pour the pancake batter on to the hot griddle.
  8. When bubbles form, flip the pancakes and cook until done.
  9. It took me about 3 minutes on the first side, and 1 to 2 minutes on the second.
  10. Watch your temperature because the pan will tend to get hotter and cook the pancakes faster as you go.
  11. This recipe came from Penzey's Spices.
