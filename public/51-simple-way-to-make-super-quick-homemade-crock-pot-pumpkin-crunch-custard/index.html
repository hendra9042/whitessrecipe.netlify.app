The ingredients needed to make Crock-Pot Pumpkin Crunch Custard:
  1. Make ready Custard
  2. Prepare 2 can 15 oz Pumpkin (not pumpkin pie filling)
  3. Get 3 Eggs
  4. Get 2 tsp Pumpkin Pie Spice
  5. Take 1 tsp Cinnamon
  6. Get 1 can 14 oz Sweetened Condensed Milk
  7. Take 2 tsp Pure Vanilla Extract
  8. Get Crunch Topping
  9. Make ready 2 cup Quick Cooking Oatmeal
  10. Make ready 1 cup Brown Sugar
  11. Get 1 cup All Purpose Flour
  12. Prepare 1 tsp Cinnamon
  13. Prepare 1 cup Crushed Walnuts or Pecans
  14. Prepare 1 cup Melted Butter
Instructions to make Crock-Pot Pumpkin Crunch Custard:
  1. In a medium sized bowl mix together all six of the custard ingredients until everything is well combined.
  2. Pour custard mixture in to a 6-7 quart oval crock-pot.
  3. In another bowl combine the next five crunch topping ingredients and combine well. Pour melted butter over the top and mix well.
  4. Sprinkle crunch topping over the custard evenly and completely.
  5. Cover crock-pot and cook on low for four hours.
