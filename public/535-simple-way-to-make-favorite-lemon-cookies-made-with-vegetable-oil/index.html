The ingredients needed to make Lemon Cookies Made with Vegetable Oil:
  1. Get 1 Egg yolk (at room temperature)
  2. Make ready 1 tiny pinch Natural Salt
  3. Get 1 tbsp Lemon juice
  4. Prepare 50 ml Vegetable oil
  5. Get 40 grams Powdered sugar
  6. Get 90 grams ○Cake flour
  7. Make ready 30 grams ○Corn starch

Steps to make Lemon Cookies Made with Vegetable Oil:
  1. Sift the ○ ingredients together and set aside.
  2. In a bowl containing egg yolk, add lemon juice and salt. Beat well with an electric mixer.
  3. When it is well mixed, add vegetable oil a little bit at a time, beating after each addition. The mixture will start to thicken.
  4. Add the powdered sugar and beat again. It's even better if you can heat the bowl over warm water at 60℃ (140℉).
  5. Sift the dry ingredients once more, adding them into the bowl and lightly fold in.
  6. Once the dough comes together, wrap in plastic wrap and leave in the refrigerator for more than 30 minutes.
  7. Preheat the oven to 170℃ (338℉). Roll out the dough and cut into shapes with any cookie cutter.
  8. I made them look like shortbread cookies this time. I used a chopstick for designing.
  9. Bake for 13 to 15 minutes at 170℃ (338℉). Baking time make differ a bit depending on the oven.
