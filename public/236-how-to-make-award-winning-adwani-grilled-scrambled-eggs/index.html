The ingredients needed to make Adwani Grilled scrambled eggs:
  1. Take Eggs
  2. Take Milk
  3. Take Salt
  4. Prepare Pepper
  5. Prepare Sumac
  6. Prepare American Cheese
  7. Make ready Shreaded carrots
  8. Make ready Cheddar
  9. Get Sumac
  10. Take Butter
  11. Make ready Grilling pan
  12. Take Beef sausages
Steps to make Adwani Grilled scrambled eggs:
  1. Mix the eggs and milk verywell and start adding sumac, salt, black pepper and the fine shreded carrots untill its homogeneous mixture
  2. Heat grilling pan add butter, grill sliced susages untill desired degree, and start cooking the egg, start adding the shreded cheddar snd american cheese, until its all creamy
  3. Once done add slices of cheese on top to create melting looks.
