The ingredients needed to make Egg custard pudding #Ramzan special:
  1. Prepare 3 Eggs
  2. Take 1 tablespoon Custard powder
  3. Take 1/2 teaspoon Vanilla essence
  4. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon Pineapple or any fruit or rose essence
  5. Make ready 1 cup Warm Milk
  6. Get 1/2 teaspoon Cardamom powder
  7. Make ready Saffron strands & pistachio for garnishing
  8. Take Sugar for caramel layer
Instructions to make Egg custard pudding #Ramzan special:
  1. Take a blender jar add in the eggs, cardamom powder, vanilla essence, custard powder, milk, condensed milk or sugar give it a nice blend until it’s very fluffy and done well
  3. Besides u caramelise sugar in baking tin or in the one dish which u want to steam
  4. Then steam it for about 30-35 minutes on low flame then check if it’s giggle soft & hard steamed well then remove it and let it cool down to normal temperature
  5. Then refrigerate for 1-2 hours then serve it cool.
  6. Tip : make sure while u steam it water must not drop in the tin in which pudding is being steamed so cover it well with foil or lid properly & the caramel must not be burned it will not taste good and steam well it must not be watery it must giggle soft to hard while u touch & check then enjoy it cool !!!
