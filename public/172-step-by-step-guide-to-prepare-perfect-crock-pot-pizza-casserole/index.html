The ingredients needed to make Crock Pot Pizza Casserole:
  1. Take Casserole
  2. Make ready Hamburger
  3. Make ready Italian Sausage
  4. Take Canadian Bacon
  5. Prepare Pepperoni
  6. Get Mushrooms
  7. Take Spaghetti Sauce
  8. Take Black Olives
  9. Prepare Cheese
  10. Take of egg noodles
Steps to make Crock Pot Pizza Casserole:
  1. Cook your Hamburger and Italian Sausage then drain access grease. Add your jar of Spaghetti Sauce and mix together
  2. Cook your noodles then drain. Add a little butter so your noodles don't stick together
  3. In your Crock Pot layer noodles, your hamburger Italian sausage mix, add your Canadian bacon, Pepperoni, Black Olives, Mushrooms then top it off with Shredded Cheese
  4. Repeat 1 - 3 and top off with lots of Cheese!!
  5. Turn Crock Pot on High till Cheese is melted :-)
  6. You can also add whatever it is you like on your pizza's.
