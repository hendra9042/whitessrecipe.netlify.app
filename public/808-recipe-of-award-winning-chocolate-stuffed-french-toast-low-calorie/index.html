The ingredients needed to make Chocolate-stuffed french toast low calorie!:
  1. Make ready 4 ounces semisweet chocolate finely chopped
  2. Take 3 ounces Neufchatel cheese at room temperature
  3. Prepare 2 cups sliced strawberries
  4. Get 1 tablespoon sugar
  5. Get 1 teaspoon grated orange zest
  6. Prepare 6 ounces italian bread cut into 8 slices 1/2" thick
  7. Get 2 large eggs
  8. Get 2 large egg whites
  9. Take 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  10. Get 1 tablespoon trans-free margarine

Steps to make Chocolate-stuffed french toast low calorie!:
  1. Combine the chocolate and cheese in a small bowl and mix
  2. Combine strawberries, sugar, and orange zest in a seperate bowl
  3. Spread 1/4th of the chocolate mixture on 4 slices of the bread. Top each with a second slice and press lightly to form a sandwhich.
  4. Combine the whole eggs, egg whites, and vanilla extract in a medium bowl
  5. Melt the margarine in aarge nonstick pan or on a griddle over medium heat. Wait for foam to subside.
  6. Working one at a time dip both sides of each sandwhich in the egg mixture. Place sandwhiches in pan or on griddle and cook until golden and cooked through. About 4 minutes per side.
  7. Serve hot topped with strawberry mixture.
