The ingredients needed to make @KetoBlondie's Egg Roll In a Bowl:
  1. Make ready 1 pb ground pork, chicken, or turkey
  2. Make ready 1 (14 oz) bag Cole Slaw Mix
  3. Take 1/2 cup chopped green onion
  4. Get 1/4 Cup low sodium soy sauce or Bragg's aminos
  5. Get 1 TBSP ground ginger
  6. Make ready 1 TBSP garlic powder
  7. Get 1 tsp sesame oil
  8. Prepare 1/4 Cup chopped cilantro
  9. Make ready Pinch red pepper flakes
  10. Make ready Sriracha
Steps to make @KetoBlondie's Egg Roll In a Bowl:
  1. Cook sausage, stirring and crumbling until cooked through.
  2. Stir in Coleslaw mix, garlic, ginger, soy sauce, cilantro, green onion, sesame oil, and red pepper flakes and stir. Cook for 3-5 minutes, or until slaw mix has softened to your liking.
  3. Top with green onions, cilantro, and drizzle with Sriracha (optional).
  4. To take it to the next level, top with fried egg! üòç
