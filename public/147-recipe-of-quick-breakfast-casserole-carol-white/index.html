The ingredients needed to make breakfast casserole carol white:
  1. Take seasoned croutons
  2. Prepare jimmy dean sausage
  3. Take jimmy dean hot sausage
  4. Prepare eggs
  5. Take milk any percent
  6. Prepare cream of mushroom soup
  7. Prepare combined sauteed onions, peppers , mushrooms, zucchini
  8. Prepare Monterey jack cheese shredded
  9. Take sharp cheddar cheese shredded
  10. Take dry mustard

Steps to make breakfast casserole carol white:
  1. spread croutons in bottom of 13 x 9 Pam sprayed baking dish
  2. crumble cooked sausage over croutons
  3. whisk eggs, milk, soup, veggies, cheeses, dry mustard
  4. pour over croutons and sausage
  5. cover and refrigerate overnight
  6. bake 325 for 50 minutes or til golden
