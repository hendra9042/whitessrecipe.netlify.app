The ingredients needed to make Banana Pancakes:
  1. Take oats
  2. Prepare ripe bananas
  3. Take eggs
  4. Get cinnamon
  5. Prepare vanilla extract
  6. Get brown sugar
  7. Take Milk
  8. Take Butter for frying pan
Steps to make Banana Pancakes:
  1. In a jug, combine the eggs, vanilla extract, brown sugar, oats and cinnamon
  2. Smash the bananas with a fork and add to the pancake batter
  3. Add milk until desired consistency is achieved
  4. Once all ingredients are combined, heat pan with a little butter then fry the batter for a few minutes each side to create the pancakes
  5. Serve as they are or with your favourite pancake topping
