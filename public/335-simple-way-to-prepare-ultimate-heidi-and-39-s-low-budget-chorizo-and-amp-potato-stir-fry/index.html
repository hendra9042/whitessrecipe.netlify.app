The ingredients needed to make Heidi's Low budget Chorizo & Potato Stir fry:
  1. Take 1/2 tube of Chorizo
  2. Prepare 2 1/2 cups frozen seasoned steak fries (or 1/3 of bag) refrigerate
  3. Make ready 1/2 bag your favorite stir fry vegetables
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp sweet chili sauce (or substitute with your favorite)
  5. Make ready 1/2 tbsp garlic salt (optional)
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp ground ginger (optional)
  7. Take 3/4 cup water
  8. Get 1 1/4 tbsp vegetable oil (peanut oil optional?)
  9. Prepare 4 eggs
Steps to make Heidi's Low budget Chorizo & Potato Stir fry:
  1. In a large 12" round skillet, fry up chorizo until lightly browned (5-7 minutes)
  2. Meanwhile chop refrigerated (or defrosted) steak fries into bite sized cuts toss with chorizo, & fry until nearly done. set aside
  3. In same pan add oil on medium / med-high until hot. Add in your favorite stir fry vegetables and cook/fry until cooked part down, add seasonings toss around until coated.
  4. Add in your favorite sauce and simmer on medium for 2 minutes occasionally tossing around from burning. Add water and simmer until sauce is thickened up (about 2-3 minutes)
  5. Toss in Chorizo and fries with the vegetables and mix around for another minute or so. Crack eggs over top. Turn heat down to medium /med-low, add lid and cook until eggs are done (About 5-7 minutes). - Serve warm with or without extra sauce.
