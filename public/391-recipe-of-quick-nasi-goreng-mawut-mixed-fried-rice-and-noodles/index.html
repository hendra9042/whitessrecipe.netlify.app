The ingredients needed to make Nasi Goreng Mawut (Mixed Fried Rice and Noodles):
  1. Take plates cooked white rice (leftover rice, put in the refrigerator for a night, is recommended)
  2. Take yellow noodles, soaked in warm water for 5-10 minutes, drained
  3. Get mustard greens, cut 3 cm
  4. Make ready boneless chicken breast, boiled, shredded, set aside
  5. Get sweet soy sauce
  6. Make ready salt and mushrooms bouillon
  7. Make ready vegetable oil
  8. Take Ground spices:
  9. Take garlic
  10. Get candle nuts 4 chilli peppers
  11. Take bird eye chilli peppers (optional)
  12. Take white peppercorn
  13. Make ready Complements
  14. Take sunny side up eggs
  15. Get some rice crackers
