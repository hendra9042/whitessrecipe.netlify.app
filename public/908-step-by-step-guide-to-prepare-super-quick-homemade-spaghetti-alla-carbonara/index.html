Instructions to make Spaghetti Alla Carbonara:
  1. In a skillet on high heat, add bacon and 1 cup water. Bring to a rolling boil.
  2. When the water has nearly boiled away, about 10 minutes, lower the heat to medium and fry the bacon in its own rendered fat, about 5 minutes. Remove and set aside. Save rendered fat for future use if desired.
  3. Cook spaghetti according to package directions.
  4. Combine egg and 2 tbsp grated Pecorino-Romano cheese. Mix well. Add egg yolks and ¼ cup pasta water.
  5. As soon as the spaghetti is done, mix it into the egg mixture and toss well. Add bacon and season with black pepper. Serve topped with more Pecorino-Romano cheese.
