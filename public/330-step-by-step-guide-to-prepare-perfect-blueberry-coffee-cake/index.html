The ingredients needed to make Blueberry Coffee Cake:
  1. Take 1 1/2 cup flour (I used 100% whole wheat)
  2. Get 1/2 cup quick oats
  3. Prepare 2 small bananas, mashed
  4. Prepare 4 tsp baking powder
  5. Take 1/2 cup milk
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup honey
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup sugar substitute (I used Monk fruit sweetener)
  8. Prepare 2 eggs, beaten
  9. Take 1/2 cup butter (1 stick softened)
  10. Take 1/2 cup blueberries (wild Maine blueberries work best.)
  11. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract
Instructions to make Blueberry Coffee Cake:
  1. Cream together butter and sweeteners
  2. Add eggs, vanilla, mashed banana and milk. Mix well
  3. Sift together flour and baking powder add it to liquid ingredients with the oats.
  4. Once mixed together, fold in the blueberries.
  5. Pour batter into greased round cake pan.
  6. Sprinkle about 1/2 cup oats across the top of the batter. Sprinkle about 1/4 cup melted butter and drizzle about 1/2 cup of honey or maple syrup all over the top of the oats.
  7. Place in 350 degree oven for about 1 hour. Check the middle for doneness by pricking the center with a toothpick or knife until it comes out clean and no batter attached.
