The ingredients needed to make Just Like Donuts Chaffles:
  1. Prepare Chaffles
  2. Make ready 4 eggs
  3. Get 4 oz cream cheese
  4. Prepare 2 Tbs almond flour
  5. Take 3 Tbs rice flour
  6. Take 1/2 tsp baking powder
  7. Take 3 Tbs granulated sugar substitute
  8. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla
  9. Take 1 tsp cinnamon
  10. Make ready Glaze
  11. Make ready 1 cup granulated sugar substitute
  12. Get 1 tsp cornstarch
  13. Get 8 Tbs half and half
Steps to make Just Like Donuts Chaffles:
  1. Mix chaffle ingrwdients in blender
  2. Spray preheated dash mini waffle maker with olive oil spray
  3. Add batter and cook until desired crispness.
  4. Place on rack to cool
  5. Mix glaze ingredients together and glaze chaffles when they have cooled. Enjoy!
