The ingredients needed to make Spicy Garlic Buttered Shrimp And Egg Sandwich:
  1. Make ready TOASTED BREAD
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp butter
  3. Make ready 2 slice bread
  4. Take SPICY GARLIC BUTTERED SAUCE
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp sweet papprika
  6. Get 6 1/2 tsp fine chili powder
  7. Get 1 tbsp dice garlic
  8. Take 2 tbsp butter
  9. Get 4 large drunken spicy shrimp or raw shrimp
  10. Prepare WASABI MAYONAISE
  11. Take 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  12. Take 1 tsp wasabi paste
  13. Get 1 large egg
