The ingredients needed to make A simple morning breakfast with scrambled eggs & fresh scallions:
  1. Take 5 Eggs
  2. Make ready 25 gr. Butter
  3. Get 25 gr. Heavy Cream
  4. Prepare 30 gr. Milk
  5. Get 1 tablespoon Fresh Scallions or Chives
  6. Get 1 teaspoon Sweet Paprika
  7. Get 1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese
  8. Take Salt

Instructions to make A simple morning breakfast with scrambled eggs & fresh scallions:
  1. In a large skillet add in butter.
  2. Add 5 eggs into a bowl and whisk,
  3. Chop scallions or chives.
  4. Pour into egg mix milk, heavy cream, salt, sweet paprika, parmesan cheese.
