Instructions to make Carrot & Onion Pancakes:
  1. To make Batter, mix Flour, Salt, Egg and Water in a small bowl.
  2. Place thinly sliced Onion, Carrot and Prawn pieces in a mixing bowl. Sprinkle with Flour and combine. Add the Batter and mix.
  3. Heat Oil in a frying pan, pour the mixture to form pancake(s). You can make 1 large pancake, or several small ones. When the edge of the pancake has set, flip over, and cook until nicely browned and cooked though. You may turn over the pancake(s) a few times while cooking.
  4. Cut the pancake into small pieces if large, and serve with a dipping sauce. Today I served with ‘Tentsuyu’. - (see recipe)
  5. *Other Dipping Sauce Suggestions: - • Soy Sauce, Toban Djan (Chilli Bean Sauce) & Sesame Oil - • Oyster Sauce, Soy Sauce & Sesame Oil - • Okonomiyaki Sauce
