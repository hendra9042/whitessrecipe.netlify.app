The ingredients needed to make Kolkata Style Egg roll:
  1. Get 4 eggs
  2. Make ready 2 cups all purpose flour
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup oil
  4. Prepare 2-3 green chillies, splits (chopped)
  5. Make ready as required Water
  6. Take For stuffing
  7. Make ready 1 cup chopped cucumber
  8. Take 2-3 chopped green chillies
  9. Take 1/2 cup chopped onion
  10. Prepare 4 tbsp tomato sauce
  11. Get as per taste salt
Steps to make Kolkata Style Egg roll:
  1. Take all purpose flour, salt, and oil together into a bowl. make a soft dough. Rolled it as Chapati & cut it from the middle rolled it like the picture.
  2. Make Chapati fry it with oil.
  3. Now beat an egg with Lil bit of salt and chopped green chili. Fried this, then added the Chapati into the raw part. Cooked it for few minutes. Spread 1 tbsp sauce and salad.
  4. Now rolled them with paper & served it with tomato sauce.
