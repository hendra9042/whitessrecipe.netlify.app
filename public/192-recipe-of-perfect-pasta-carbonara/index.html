The ingredients needed to make Pasta Carbonara:
  1. Get spaghetti
  2. Prepare bacon
  3. Take pepper
  4. Prepare salt
  5. Take parmesan cheese
  6. Get egg beaters
  7. Take parsley
  8. Make ready garlic
Steps to make Pasta Carbonara:
  1. Follow package instructions to prepare spaghetti, adding salt to the water.
  2. before draining, scoop out 1 cup of pasta water & set aside. drain the pasta.
  3. cook bacon in saute pan until crispy. chop bacon into pieces. add garlic & black pepper.
  4. add the bacon & garlic to the spaghetti. coat the spaghetti.
  5. remove pan from the heat & add parmesan cheese, egg beaters & pasta water. quickly toss to coat.
  6. sprinkle chopped parsley over the pasta, toss & serve.
