The ingredients needed to make Crispy Chicken Bites:
  1. Take oat bran
  2. Get wheat germ
  3. Prepare coarsely ground flax seed
  4. Take coarsely ground almonds
  5. Get sea salt
  6. Take white pepper
  7. Get garlic powder
  8. Get water
  9. Prepare egg white, lightly beaten
Instructions to make Crispy Chicken Bites:
  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Prepare baking sheet by lining with parchment paper.
  2. Cut chicken breasts into nugget sized pieces: about 1 1/2 inches. Set aside.
  3. Next combine all dry in a large ziploc bag. Shake well. This is your coating mixture. Combine water and egg in a medium bowl. Dip each each chicken piece in the water/egg mixture and then place in the coating mixture. Make sure each piece is well coated and your baking sheet is full, place in oven and bake for 10-15 minutes or until golden.
