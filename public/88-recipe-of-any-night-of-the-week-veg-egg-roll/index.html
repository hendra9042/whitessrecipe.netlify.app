The ingredients needed to make Veg Egg roll:
  1. Make ready 3 eggs
  2. Get 1 medium carrot
  3. Make ready Onions
  4. Make ready Spring onions
  5. Get Sweet corn
  6. Make ready Salt
  7. Get Black pepper
  8. Make ready Veg oil
  9. Take 4 tbsp Milk
Instructions to make Veg Egg roll:
  1. Cut all your veggies. Separate egg white and egg yolk. Add salt, black pepper and 2tbsp milk to each and mix.
  2. In a pan, add little oil. Then add half of d egg white and spread in d pan.
  3. Then put your veggies on one part on the egg
  4. Then fold one part of d egg and shift it to the end of d pan then add d remaining egg white and fold.
  5. Add in egg yolk mixture and fold.
  6. Transfer to a tray and cut.
