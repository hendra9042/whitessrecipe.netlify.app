The ingredients needed to make Cookie dough no egg for ice cream:
  1. Make ready 64 grams plain flour
  2. Prepare 32 grams brown sugar
  3. Get 38 grams white sugar
  4. Prepare 28 grams soft butter
  5. Get 1/8 tsp vanilla extract
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp water

Steps to make Cookie dough no egg for ice cream:
  1. Mix the brown sugar, white sugar and butter until smooth
  2. Stir in the vanilla extract and water
  3. Mix in the flour until blended
  4. Shape into a long sausage if you can. I put mine flat onto greaseproof paper and wrapped it up.
  5. Place in freezer for upto 2 hours then cut into chunks and mix into ice cream.
