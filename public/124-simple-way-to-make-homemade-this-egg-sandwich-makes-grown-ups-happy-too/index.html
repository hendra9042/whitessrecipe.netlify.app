The ingredients needed to make This Egg Sandwich Makes Grown-ups Happy Too:
  1. Make ready 4 Eggs
  2. Prepare 4 tbsp Sugar
  3. Get 1 pinch Salt
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp Margarine (for spreading)
  5. Take 1 tbsp Butter (for cooking)
Instructions to make This Egg Sandwich Makes Grown-ups Happy Too:
  1. Whisk together the egg, sugar, and salt.
  2. Spread margarine on both sides of the bread slices.
  3. Warm thebutter in a frying pan and add all the egg mixture in one go. Set the heat to medium.
  4. Scramble the eggs until soft set.
  5. Divide the scrambled eggs into three portions. Put each portion on top of a slice of bread and cover with another slice.
  6. Once you have sandwiched the eggs, wrap the whole sandwich in a tightly wrung out moistened kitchen towel and press lightly. Leave it as is for at least 10 minutes until the bread and filling have settled.
  7. Warm up a knife. Hold down the sandwich gently on the left and right applying even pressure, and cut the sandwich into any shapes you like.
