The ingredients needed to make Chicken Schnitzel with salad & Rice:
  1. Get Chicken breast
  2. Make ready Breadcrums
  3. Get eggs
  4. Take Sunflower oil
  5. Prepare Paprika
  6. Take Curry powder
  7. Prepare Dried coriander
  8. Get Garlic powder
  9. Make ready Fresh salad mix
  10. Prepare Cherry tomatoes
  11. Take Olive oil
  12. Prepare Balsamic vinegar
  13. Prepare Salt
  14. Get Parmesano
  15. Get Peppers
  16. Take Lemon
  17. Take Chicken stock
  18. Get Bay leaves
  19. Take Rice
Instructions to make Chicken Schnitzel with salad & Rice:
  1. In 1 bowl Mix the breadcrumbs with paprika, garlic powder, dried coriander, Parmesano & curry powder
  2. Wisk 3 eggs in a separate bowl
  3. In another bowl pour some flower. Start by dipping yogur chicken into the egg, then transfer to the flower, then soak again in the egg and then roll it in the breadcrumbs
  4. Once prepared, heat up half a pan of sunflower oil on medium to high heat. Cook for 10 minutes
  5. For the rice, wash the rice 4 times then fill up half of the pot with water, add 2 chicken stock cubes, 4 crushed garlic cloves, 3 bay leaves and a tablespoon of salt. Heat to a hard boil then turn right off with the lid on and leave till the water is fully absorbed
  6. For the salad mix the salad with cherry tomatoes and quick fried peppers. Dress in salt, olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
