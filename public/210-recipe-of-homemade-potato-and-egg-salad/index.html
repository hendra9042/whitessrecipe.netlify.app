The ingredients needed to make Potato and egg salad:
  1. Make ready 1 kg potatoes
  2. Prepare 5-6 eggs
  3. Prepare 2 tomatoes
  4. Get 1 onion
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp finely chopped parsley
  6. Take 1 small cucumber
  7. Make ready 1 handful olives
  8. Get olive oil
  9. Prepare 2 tbsp vinegar
  10. Take salt, pepper

Instructions to make Potato and egg salad:
  1. Wash the potatoes and boil them (unpeeled).
  2. Boil the eggs, peel them when they cool, peel the potatoes and slice them both into a bowl.
  3. Add the cut tomatoes, the onion, finely chopped, the parsley, the sliced cucumber and the olives.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, whisk the olive oil and vinegar, add them to the mixture and toss to coat.
