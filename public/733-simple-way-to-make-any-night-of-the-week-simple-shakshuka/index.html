The ingredients needed to make Simple Shakshuka:
  1. Make ready 1 onion chopped
  2. Get 2 red bell peppers chopped
  3. Make ready 1 can diced tomatoes
  4. Get 4 cloves garlic chopped
  5. Take 1 tbsp cumin
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp paprika
  7. Take 1/2 tbsp ground oregano
  8. Get 2 eggs
  9. Make ready Salt
  10. Take Pepper
  11. Make ready Sugar (optional)
  12. Prepare Cilantro
  13. Take Chicken bouillion cube (optional)
  14. Make ready Oil and butter
Steps to make Simple Shakshuka:
  1. Saute onions and red bell pepper in oil and butter, add garlic after 2 mins.
  2. Add diced tomatoes. Simmer.
  3. Season with salt and pepper. Add cumin, paprika and oregano. Add sugar if it suits your taste. Add chicken cube if it suits your taste.
  4. Make two wells in the mixture, add egg to poach. Cover.
  5. Sprinkle with chopped cilantro before serving with toasted bread.
