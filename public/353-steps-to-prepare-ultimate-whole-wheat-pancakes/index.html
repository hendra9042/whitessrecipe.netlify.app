The ingredients needed to make Whole Wheat Pancakes:
  1. Make ready Dry Ingredients
  2. Make ready 1 cup whole wheat flour
  3. Take 2 tsp. baking powder
  4. Prepare 1 tsp. baking soda
  5. Prepare 1/4 tsp. salt
  6. Make ready Wet Ingredients
  7. Prepare 3 eggs
  8. Prepare 1 cup water
  9. Make ready 1/4 cup applesauce
  10. Make ready Optional
  11. Take Add extra 1/2 cup flour for thicker batter (fluffy pancakes!)
  12. Prepare Oil can be substituted for the applesauce (same amount)
Instructions to make Whole Wheat Pancakes:
  1. Beat eggs, then add applesauce, baking powder, soda, and salt. Mix thoroughly to smooth any clumps.
  2. Add flour and water to mixture, mixing until incorporated.
  3. Measure 1/4 cup (for consistent pancakes), and pour the batter on a heated nonstick or greased skillet.
  4. Enjoy your healthy and hearty pancakes!
