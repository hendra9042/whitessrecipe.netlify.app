The ingredients needed to make Wacky Apple Cake:
  1. Take 2 tbsp butter
  2. Get 1 cup sugar or equivalent of sugar substitute
  3. Make ready 1 egg, beaten (or equivalent of egg substitute)
  4. Get 2 cups flour (gluten-free or regular)
  5. Make ready 1 tsp xanthum gum, if using gluten-free flour
  6. Take 2 tsp baking powder
  7. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  8. Make ready 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  10. Make ready 3/4 cup milk of choice
  11. Prepare 2 apples, pared & thinly sliced
  12. Make ready 2 tbsp melted butter
  13. Make ready 2 tbsp sugar or equivalent of sugar substitute
  14. Get 1/2 cup heavy cream

Steps to make Wacky Apple Cake:
  1. Cream butter, 1 cup sugar, & eggs. Beat well.
  2. Sift flour, xanthum gum, baking powder, salt, nutmeg, & cinnamon together. Sift 2 more times. Add to creamed mixture alternately with milk.
  3. Pour into 8x8 pan.
  4. Place apple slices in rows until batter is completely covered.
  5. Pour melted butter over all. Sprinkle with 2 tbsp sugar.
  6. Place in 375 degrees oven. Bake for 30 minutes.
  7. Pour heavy cream over cake.
  8. Bake for another 30 minutes or until cake is done.
