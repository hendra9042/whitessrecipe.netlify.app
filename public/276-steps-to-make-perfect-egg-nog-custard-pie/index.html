The ingredients needed to make Egg Nog Custard Pie:
  1. Make ready 2 cup Egg Nog
  2. Make ready 3/4 cup Sugar
  3. Take 1 9" Pie Crust
  4. Get 1 tsp Vanilla
  5. Make ready 1 Egg White
  6. Take 4 Eggs
  7. Take 1/4 tsp Salt
  8. Prepare 1 cup Milk (2% or whole)
Steps to make Egg Nog Custard Pie:
  1. Preheat oven to 450°F
  2. Brush unbaked pie crust with egg white
  3. Mix egg nog and milk in pot and scald the mixture
  4. Add sugar, salt, eggs, and vanilla into scalded mixture and mix it together til its a nice yellow
  5. Pour into crust and bake for ten min on 450
  6. While pie is still in oven turn oven to 300°F and bake for 45-50min
