The ingredients needed to make Breakfast sandwich:
  1. Take Pancake layer
  2. Prepare 1 cup pancake mix
  3. Take 1 egg
  4. Get 1 cup milk
  5. Prepare Egg layer
  6. Make ready 3 eggs
  7. Make ready 1/4 cup
  8. Get Salt for taste
  9. Get Pepper for taste
  10. Prepare Any cheese (optional)
Steps to make Breakfast sandwich:
  1. Put pancake mix in the bowl
  2. Add eggs
  3. Put in the milk
  4. Wisk
  5. Put in a pan with parchment paper on the bottom
  6. Bake in oven For 450° F and 15 (min)
  7. Go to egg layer
  8. Put eggs in bowl
  9. Add milk
  10. Add salt an pepper
  11. Wisk
  12. Put in pan with parchment paper
  13. Bake in oven for 425°F for 10 (min)
  14. Take the pancake, flip it over and take parchment off
  15. Cut in half
  16. Put egg on middle and add the second half of pancake on the top of the egg and voila ready to eat
