The ingredients needed to make Chikuwa Roll Isobe Fries with Quail Eggs:
  1. Prepare 5 Chikuwa
  2. Prepare 10 Quail eggs
  3. Make ready 4 tbsp Flour
  4. Get 1 tbsp Aonori (optional)
  5. Get 150 ml Cold water
  6. Take 10 Toothpicks
  7. Take 1 for dipping Mentsuyu, grated daikon radish, shiso leaves
Instructions to make Chikuwa Roll Isobe Fries with Quail Eggs:
  1. Boil the quail eggs to your desired hardness, peel the shells, and set aside. Cut the chikuwa in half lengthwise to make 10 sticks.
  2. Wrap the chikuwa around the eggs, and secure with toothpicks.
  3. Mix the flour and cold water, and add aonori. Dip Step 2 in the batter, deep-fry in oil at 180℃, then you're done.
