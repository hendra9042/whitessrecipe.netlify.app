The ingredients needed to make Scramble Eggs:
  1. Take garbanzo bean flour
  2. Make ready sweet basil
  3. Take onion powder
  4. Prepare sea salt
  5. Prepare oregano
  6. Get Cayenne powder
  7. Get grapeseed oil
  8. Make ready nutritional yeast (cheesy flavor)
  9. Prepare spring water
  10. Get garlic powder
Steps to make Scramble Eggs:
  1. Mix together flour, water, and seasoning make sure it isn't to thick use more water if needed
  2. 1 tsp of oil to pan make sure it is hot (very important)
  3. Let it cook for 1 min then take spatula and start chopping it in skillet keep cooking until it has some brown edges
