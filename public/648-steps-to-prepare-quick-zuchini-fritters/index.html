The ingredients needed to make Zuchini Fritters:
  1. Make ready shredded zucchini
  2. Prepare flax eggs
  3. Make ready garlic cloves
  4. Make ready almond flour
  5. Prepare fresh chopped parlsley, thyme, chives, dill. I used thyme
  6. Prepare onion powder
  7. Take salt. Not big salt fan so could use 1.5 easily if needed
  8. Make ready Pepper for taste
  9. Make ready Oil for frying (I used olive)
Instructions to make Zuchini Fritters:
  1. Shredded zucchini and prepared the flax eggs first.
  2. Once the eggs are set then I add all ingredients together at once in a large mixing bowl. I chose to hand mix this recipe I am sure you can do it in the KitchenAid just is easy
  3. I did about four at a time and made them on the smaller side due to the fact I wanted snackable fritters. I use for multiple things, and at later dates. I will end up freezing most of these.
  4. They go great with a cashew sour cream, any type of yogurt that you like, salsa, melted cheese, fresh avacados or just plain :-) there's so many options you can throw on these. That imagination of yours is endless :)
