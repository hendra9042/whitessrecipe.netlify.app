The ingredients needed to make Egg-Free Butter Cream:
  1. Get 125 g Butter *softened
  2. Prepare 1 pinch Salt *if you use Unsalted Butter
  3. Make ready 1 & 1/2 cups Icing Sugar
  4. Make ready 1-2 tablespoons Cream OR Milk *You don’t need to use it all
Instructions to make Egg-Free Butter Cream:
  1. Beat softened Butter in a large bowl, add half of the Icing Sugar and beat until smooth. Add the remaining Icing Sugar and beat until smooth. Then you can add Cream OR Milk to achieve the creaminess that you want.
  2. *Note: You can add a few drops Food Colouring. You can add flavours such as Vanilla Extract, Cocoa Powder, Matcha Powder, melted White/Dark Chocolate, Citrus Zest & Juice, or etc. Use Cream OR Milk to loosen.
