The ingredients needed to make Egg Mayo Roll:
  1. Make ready 250 gm all purpose flour
  2. Get 200 gm paneer
  3. Take 4-5 eggs
  4. Get 1 bowl mix sliced vegetables
  5. Take 4 Tsp mayonnaise
  6. Make ready Salt
  7. Get 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
Steps to make Egg Mayo Roll:
  1. Make a dough all purpose flour.add salt
  2. Take a fry pan add little bit oil make a chapati,cook half done,break 1 egg over it add salt pepper.
  3. Take a voke add 1 tbs refind oil add saute onion,green chilli,ginger and garlic. Add slice vegetables, stir fry.
  4. On tave roll spread mayonnaise, add Paneer and saute vegetables add sprinkles origano and chilli flakes salt.Roll it and cut it and serve hot.
