The ingredients needed to make Egg Salad Sandwich:
  1. Make ready 4 Bread Slices
  2. Make ready 3 Hard Boiled Eggs
  3. Get 1 Tablespoon Mayonnaise + 1 Tablespoon for spread
  4. Make ready 1 Teaspoon Sugar
  5. Prepare Pinch Salt, Black Pepper and Seasoning
  6. Get 1 Teaspoon Lemon juice
  7. Get Lettuce
Instructions to make Egg Salad Sandwich:
  1. Separate the white from the yolk.
  2. Use a mesh strainer and the back of a spoon to mash the yolks and white. This gives the mashed egg a fine and smooth texture. Alternatively, you can blend or use a food processor/ potato masher.
  3. To the mashed egg, add mayonnaise, sugar, black pepper, salt and seasoning.
  4. Add lemon juice and stir to combine. Spread mayo on the first 2 bread slices.
  5. Spread the egg salad on each slice. Add lettuce to one of the slice or even both. Spread mayo on the second slices to enable it stick properly to the first one.
  6. Use a sharp knife to carefully remove the edges (crust) of the sandwich. Use a toothpick to secure the sandwiches and slice in triangles. Serve with tea/ coffee/juice. EnjoyüòçüíÉüçπ
