The ingredients needed to make Egg salad with cucumber pita sandwich:
  1. Prepare 1 tsp Light mayonnaise
  2. Get 6 large Eggs
  3. Take 2 tbsp Nonfat plain greek yogurt
  4. Make ready 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  5. Prepare 1/4 tsp Curry powder
  6. Take 1 pinch Fresh black pepper
  7. Get 2 tbsp Finely chopped sweet onion
  8. Take 1 tbsp Finely chopped fresh chives
  9. Make ready 4 each Whole wheat pitas
  10. Prepare 1/2 large English cucumber thinly slices whole
