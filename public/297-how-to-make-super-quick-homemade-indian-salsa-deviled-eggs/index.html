The ingredients needed to make Indian salsa deviled eggs:
  1. Prepare 6 hard boiled eggs(peeled)
  2. Get 1/2 cup onion chopped
  3. Take 1/2 cup tomato chopped (remove seeds)
  4. Get 1/2 cup orange capsicum
  5. Get 1 red pepper chopped (remove seeds)
  6. Get 1/2 cup spring onions (only green)
  7. Make ready 3 tbsp mayonnaise
  8. Take 1 tsp mustard paste
  9. Prepare as required Sweet paprika
  10. Prepare as required Some coriander/parsley leaves

Steps to make Indian salsa deviled eggs:
  1. First cut the eggs into half,now remove egg yolk in separate bowl and smash the egg yolks.now add in mayonnaise.
  2. Now add the mustard paste and mix well till smooth and creamy. now add veggies from above ingredients.
  3. Now mix well and remove the egg yolk batter into the pastry bag
  4. Now cut the tip of the pastry bag enough to make sure its easily ready to pipe.now fill some batter into each egg white.
  5. Now sprinkle some paprika powder and garnish with coriander leaves.
  6. Decorate the plate with salad leaves some veggies and serve immediately. Enjoy
