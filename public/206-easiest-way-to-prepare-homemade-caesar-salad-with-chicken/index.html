Instructions to make Caesar Salad with Chicken:
  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees (180 fan forced)
  2. Cut wholemeal bread into crouton-sized pieces and lay on an oven tray, place in pre heated oven, and bake for 10 mins or until croutons are crispy. Set aside in an air tight container once cooled.
  3. Dice bacon into small pieces and place in a frying pan, turning frequently until crispy, set aside to cool.
  4. Add a small amount of olive oil and, over a medium high heat cook the chicken breast for 4-5 minutes on each side or until cooked through. Set aside in the fridge to cool then slice thinly.
  5. Cook eggs until medium to hard boiled in a saucepan. Run cold water over the cooked eggs and set aside until cooled then peel and slice into quarters.
  6. When you are ready to serve, gather your pre-prepared ingredients and lay the lettuce leaves on a platter, arrange quartered egg, sprinkle bacon and croutons, arrange chicken slices, top with parmesan, Caesar dressing and enjoy!
