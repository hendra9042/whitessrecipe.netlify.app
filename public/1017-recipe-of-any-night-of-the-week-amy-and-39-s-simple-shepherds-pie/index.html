The ingredients needed to make Amy's Simple Shepherds Pie:
  1. Get ground beef
  2. Prepare 16oz. jar of beef gravy
  3. Get onion, diced
  4. Take sour cream & onion instant mashed potatoes
  5. Get cheese flavored instant mashed potatoes
  6. Take 12oz. package of shredded cheddar cheese
  7. Get season salt
  8. Make ready eggs, beatened
Instructions to make Amy's Simple Shepherds Pie:
  1. Brown ground beef & season with season salt or to taste. Add onion 3/4 way cooking. Drain grease, add gravy & set aside.
  2. Prepare potatoes as directed on package. Mixing all packages together. When potatoes almost done, add 1/2 package of shredded cheese.
  3. In 13x9 in. pan, put meat on bottom & spread. Then add potatoes on top & spread.
  4. Take eggs & pour over top of potatoes. Poke holes in potatoes to get egg to help combine dish.
  5. Sprinkle rest of cheese on top, cover with foil & bake for 30 minutes in a 375° preheated oven.
  6. Serve with a side of veggies. I use frozen mixed veggies, peas, corn, or carrots.
