The ingredients needed to make Quick Corn Pudding:
  1. Get 1/4 tsp onion powder
  2. Take 4 tbsp butter, melted
  3. Make ready 1 can (15.25 oz) whole kernel corn, drained
  4. Take 1 can (14.75 oz) cream style corn
  5. Prepare 1 cup sour cream
  6. Take 7 oz pkg corn muffin mix
  7. Take 2 eggs or equivalent egg substitute

Steps to make Quick Corn Pudding:
  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
  2. Spray 1 1/2 qt baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.
  3. In medium bowl, mix onion powder, butter, both cans of corn, sour cream, & muffin mix. Mix well.
  4. Whisk eggs in small bowl until well blended. Add eggs to corn mixture in bowl & mix just until ingredients are mixed.
  5. Spoon batter into prepared baking dish. Bake until set, about 45-55 minutes. Serve immediately.
