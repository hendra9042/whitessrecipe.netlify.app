Steps to make Bacon and egg sandwich:
  1. Toast your bread in the toaster or in the oven.
  2. Fry or bake the bacon till desired doneiness.
  3. Fry the eggs in a skillet over medium heat to your preference. I like over medium.
  4. Once everything is cooked. You assemble the sandwich.
  5. On a plate, spread mayonnaise or miracle whip on both slices of bread that's been toasted. On one side top the mayonnaise with cheese, egg, bacon,pickles, tomato and lettuce.
