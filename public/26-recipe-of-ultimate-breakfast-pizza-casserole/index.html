The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Pizza Casserole:
  1. Get biscuit dough
  2. Get breakfast sausage
  3. Get cream gravy
  4. Make ready eggs
  5. Prepare milk
  6. Make ready bacon
  7. Take Shredded cheddar cheese
  8. Make ready Mozzarella cheese
Instructions to make Breakfast Pizza Casserole:
  1. Form biscuits into pizza dough in the dish
  2. Cook sausage, then add 4 cups of gravy.
  3. Cook bacon
  4. Scramble eggs with milk and some cheddar cheese.
  5. Spread 1/2 the sausage & gravy onto the dough.
  6. Spread eggs onto dough
  7. Crumble bacon onto eggs
  8. Add the rest of the sausage & gravy
  9. Top with mozzarella cheese
  10. Bake at 350 for 45 min to an hour. Untill biscuits are browned and cooked.
