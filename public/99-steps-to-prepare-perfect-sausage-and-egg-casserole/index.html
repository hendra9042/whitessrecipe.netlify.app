Instructions to make Sausage and egg casserole:
  1. Cube bread & place evenly in greased 9x13 pan
  2. Sprinkle evenly with sausage and cheese.
  3. Mix eggs, half and half, salt and pepper.
  4. Pour over ingredients in pan. Cover and chill overnight.
  5. Remove from fridge 15 min before baking.
  6. Bake 45 min. at 325°F or until set.
  7. Cut into squares and serve.
