The ingredients needed to make Sour Cream Banana Bread (no eggs):
  1. Make ready bread
  2. Get 5 very ripe bananas (I let them get black and then freeze them until I'm ready to use)
  3. Prepare 1 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  4. Get 1 1/4 cup sugar
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup sour cream
  6. Take 1/4 cup canola oil
  7. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
  8. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla extract
  9. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  10. Get 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  11. Get glaze
  12. Make ready 1/4 cup sour cream
  13. Make ready 3/4 cup confectioners sugar
  14. Take 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
Instructions to make Sour Cream Banana Bread (no eggs):
  1. Mix all the dry ingredients together in a large bowl.
  2. Mash the bananas together with all the wet ingredients.
  3. Add the wet to the dry and mix until incorporated.
  4. Place in a lightly greased loaf pan
  5. Bake at 350° for 50 minutes to an hour.* update, an hour and 10 to 15 minutes if you like it a bit more dry. Just watch it so it doesnt get to dark. This is an extremly most bread!
  6. For the glaze~ put the confectioners sugar and sour cream in a small sauce pan on low heat. Stir until smooth. It doesn't need to be very warm. Add vanilla when removed from heat. Pour over cooled bread.
