The ingredients needed to make low calorie chocolate oatmeal breakfast muffin:
  1. Take 1 3/4 cup rolled oats
  2. Get 3 egg whites
  3. Get 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa
  4. Get 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
  5. Take 1 tsp caramel extract
  6. Get 1/2 cup plain low fat yogurt
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp vinegar
  8. Get 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  9. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  10. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt
  11. Make ready 1 cup Hot water
  12. Take 1/2 cup sweetener that measures like sugar
  13. Make ready 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Instructions to make low calorie chocolate oatmeal breakfast muffin:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Mix all Ingredients except the chocolate chips
  3. Once combined, stir in half of chocolate chips
  4. Spoon into muffin pan coated with nonstick cooking spray
  5. Bake for 10 minutes
  6. Remove from oven and sprinkle with remaining chocolate chips
  7. Bake for 10 more minutes or until toothpick inserted comes out clean
