Instructions to make Eclair:
  1. ~custard~ mix egg, sugar, milk, corn starch. Heat until bubble forms, add butter. Chill
  2. Heat butter, sugar, water, salt. Off heat, sift in flour. Mix
  3. Fry dough for 2 mins. Let cool
  4. Add egg in batches, until v tip forms. Use pastry bag to form eclair 10cm each
  5. 180 C for 25 mins. 160 C for 15 mins
  6. Let cool and pipe in custard and coated with chocolate
