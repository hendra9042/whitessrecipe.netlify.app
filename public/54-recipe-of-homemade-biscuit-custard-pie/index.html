The ingredients needed to make Biscuit custard pie:
  1. Prepare Ingredients for pudding
  2. Make ready 500 ml milk
  3. Get 1 egg
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp custard powder
  5. Take 4 tbsp sugar for custard
  6. Prepare Ingredients for syrup
  7. Prepare 4 tbsp sugar for chocolate syrup
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp butter
  9. Get 2-3 tbsp cocoa powder
  10. Make ready 1/2 cup water
  11. Prepare Ingredients for base
  12. Prepare 2 half roll biscuits

Instructions to make Biscuit custard pie:
  1. For making syrup: Add 4 tbsp sugar 1/2 cup water and cocoa powder and butter in a pan and cook till thick syrup is ready
  2. Let it cool down at room temperature
  3. Take a bowl and coursly crush biscuits into it
  4. Add cool chocolate syrup into it and mix with spoon till all biscuits coated nicely
  5. Add this mixture into a dish or mold and press gently to set evenly
  6. Keep it in refrigerator to cool down
  7. On the other side prepare pudding by adding milk, egg, sugar, custard powder in a saucepan and quickly stir till it become very thick
  8. Let mixture cool down when it become cool pour over biscuits base and refregerate again.
  9. Note: cover pudding mixture with cling foil to avoid crusting on top
  10. Serve chilled and enjoy with family
