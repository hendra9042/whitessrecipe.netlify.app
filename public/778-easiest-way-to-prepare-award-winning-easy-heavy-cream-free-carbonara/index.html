The ingredients needed to make Easy Heavy Cream-free Carbonara:
  1. Prepare 100 grams Spaghetti
  2. Make ready 100 ml ＊Milk
  3. Take 200 ml ＊Water
  4. Take 2 pinch ＊Salt
  5. Get 1 tbsp ＊Butter
  6. Prepare 1 Bacon
  7. Take 50 ml Milk
  8. Get 1 slice Easy melting cheese
  9. Make ready 1 Black pepper
  10. Make ready 1 Poached egg or egg yolk (for topping)
  11. Take 1 dash Salt and pepper
Steps to make Easy Heavy Cream-free Carbonara:
  1. Put all of the ＊ ingredients into a frying pan and turn the heat to medium.
  2. Once it begins to boil, add fold the spaghetti in half and add to the pan. Return the pan to a boil, reduce the heat to low, and cover with a lid.
  3. Boil the spaghetti for 1 minute less than instructed on the package. It's important to open the lid occasionally to stir the spaghetti.
  4. If there is too little moisture, add some milk (not listed).
  5. Once it is done boiling, open the lid, and add the bacon, milk, and shredded cheese and heat over medium.
  6. Once it becomes thick, season with salt and pepper and transfer to a plate. Top with a hot spring egg or egg yolk and sprinkle black pepper to taste.
