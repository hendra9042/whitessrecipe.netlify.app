The ingredients needed to make Oatmeal Cinnamon Bread:
  1. Take 1 cup Quick cooking oats
  2. Take 1 1/2 cup boiling water
  3. Prepare 1 3/4 cup Coconut flour
  4. Take 1/2 tsp xanthum gum
  5. Take 1 tsp baking soda
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  7. Get 1 tsp cinnamon
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup butter, softened
  9. Get 1 cup granulated sugar or sugar substitute such as splenda
  10. Take 1 cup firmly packed light brown sugar
  11. Prepare 2 eggs or equivalent of egg substitute
  12. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract
Steps to make Oatmeal Cinnamon Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease 9x5 loaf pan.
  2. Place oats in a small heatproof bowl. Add boiling water, stir, & set aside.
  3. Combine flour, xanthum gum, baking soda, salt, & cinnamon in a small bowl & mix well.
  4. Place butter in a large bowl. Beat with an electric mixer set at high speed until creamy. Add both sugars gradually, beating constantly until well blended. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Beat in vanilla.
  5. Gradually add flour mixture to creamed mixture & beat until smooth. Stir in oats mixture. Spoon batter into prepared pan.
  6. Bake until toothpick inserted into center comes out clean, about 1 hr.
  7. Cool in pan for 10 minutes. Transfer bread to wire rack to cool completely. Then slice & serve .
