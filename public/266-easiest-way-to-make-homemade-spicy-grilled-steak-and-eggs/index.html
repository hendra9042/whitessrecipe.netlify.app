The ingredients needed to make Spicy Grilled Steak and Eggs:
  1. Make ready leftover grilled steak, cooked as you love
  2. Make ready large eggs
  3. Get sharp cheddar cheese shredded
  4. Take sour cream
  5. Make ready diced pickled jalapeno peppers
  6. Get butter
Instructions to make Spicy Grilled Steak and Eggs:
  1. Heat a pan, melt 1 tablespoon of butter, add the eggs, and scramble. move to a plate and add cheese atop the eggs
  2. Slice the steak. Melt the rest of the butter and add the steak. Fry till a little crispy
  3. Add steak and jalapenos to the cheesy eggs.
  4. Add sour cream, serve I hope you enjoy!
