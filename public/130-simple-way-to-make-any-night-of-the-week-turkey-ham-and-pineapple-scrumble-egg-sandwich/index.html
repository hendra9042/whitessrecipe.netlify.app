The ingredients needed to make Turkey Ham And Pineapple Scrumble Egg Sandwich:
  1. Prepare 1 tbsp dice sweet ripe pineapple
  2. Prepare 1 egg
  3. Get 1 slice turkey ham
  4. Make ready 1/2 tbsp butter
  5. Get 2 slice sweet white bread
  6. Take dash salt and pepper
  7. Prepare garnish
  8. Get 1 dash Italian herbs
  9. Prepare 1 bunch thousand islands sauce
  10. Prepare 1 slice Cheddar cheese
Steps to make Turkey Ham And Pineapple Scrumble Egg Sandwich:
  1. Optional,Having cold or warm turkey ham
  2. With melted butter on pan scramble eggs then add seasoning and dice pineapple
  3. Serve on toasted bread add a slice of chedder cheese with warm turkey ham and pineaple scramble eggs top thousand island sauce and mix Italian herbs
