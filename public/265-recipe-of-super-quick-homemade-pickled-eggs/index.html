The ingredients needed to make Pickled Eggs:
  1. Get water
  2. Get white vinegar
  3. Make ready black pepper corns
  4. Prepare bay leafs
  5. Get small hot chile peppers
  6. Make ready pickling spice
  7. Get coarse salt
  8. Prepare mustard seeds
  9. Prepare dill
  10. Prepare large red onion
  11. Prepare garlic, peeled and roughly chopped
  12. Get splenda
  13. Get hard boiled eggs
Instructions to make Pickled Eggs:
  1. Mix all the ingredients except for the eggs in a large pan.
  2. Peel the hardboiled eggs and poke each one with a fork a few times.
  3. Bring to a boil and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Seal the jar and let it sit in your fridge for 2 weeks.
