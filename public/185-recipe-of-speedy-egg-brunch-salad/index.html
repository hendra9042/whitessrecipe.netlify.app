The ingredients needed to make Egg Brunch Salad:
  1. Prepare eggs
  2. Get button mushrooms
  3. Take spinach
  4. Make ready baby plum tomatoes
  5. Make ready olive oil
  6. Prepare worcestershire sauce
  7. Get salt
  8. Make ready Freshly ground Black Pepper
  9. Get Balsamic Vinegar
Steps to make Egg Brunch Salad:
  1. Boil 2 eggs for 4-5 mins
  2. Quarter the mushrooms
  3. Add a splash of olive oil, salt, pepper and some worcestershire sauce… toss the mushrooms until they are coated…
  4. Add a few baby plum tomatoes and a handful of baby spinach leaves with the stalks removed. Splash with balsamic vinegar.
  5. Peal, and slice the boiled eggs and season with more salt and pepper
