The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Get fresh cubed bread chunks
  2. Take eggs
  3. Prepare butter (melted)
  4. Prepare salt
  5. Make ready pepper
  6. Make ready gravy mix
  7. Get sharp cheddar
  8. Prepare sausage
  9. Make ready onion
  10. Make ready green pepper
Instructions to make Breakfast Casserole:
  1. preheat oven to 350°
  2. cut bread into 1" cubes, place in glass casserole dish.
  3. mix together eggs, salt, pepper, onions,green pepper, milk, gravy mix & 3/4 pkg of cheese. Pour over bread cubes.
  4. pour melted butter over bread, bake 30-35 min
  5. add remaining cheese to casserole, broil to brown. about 3 minutes.
  6. remove from oven, let stand 5-10 min. Cut & serve. Enjoy!
