The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Scrambled Eggs w/Potatoes,Smoked Ham and White Cheddar:
  1. Prepare large Russett Potato - diced
  2. Get cooked Smoked Ham - diced
  3. Get Eggs
  4. Prepare shredded White Cheddar
  5. Take Lipton Onion Soup mix
  6. Take Green Onion - Sliced (White part for frying - Green- garnish)
  7. Prepare Olive Oil
  8. Get Butter
  9. Get Sour Cream
  10. Prepare Crystal Hot Sauce -
  11. Take Minced Garlic
  12. Make ready Paprika
  13. Make ready Parsley for garnish
  14. Prepare Salt and Pepper -
Steps to make Breakfast Scrambled Eggs w/Potatoes,Smoked Ham and White Cheddar:
  1. Heat frying pan on medium heat
  2. Place potatoes in small mixing bowl
  3. Add Lipton Onion Soup Mix
  4. Add Olive Oil
  5. Add Salt & Pepper - to taste
  6. Mix and Coat potatoes well
  7. Add to frying pan - stir occasionally - cover
  8. Place diced Ham in small pudding size bowl - set aside
  9. In medium mixing bowl add Eggs, White Cheddar, and Sour Cream
  10. Beat Egg mixture 1 minute - set aside
  11. Once potatoes start to get tender, add white part of green onion. Mix well - cook 2 minutes
  12. Add Ham - mix well - cook 2 minutes
  13. Once potatoes are done, turn heat down to low.
  14. Add Egg mixture - stir continuously.
  15. Once eggs are cooked (about 2 minutes) remove from heat.
  16. Serve
  17. Garnish with Green part of Green Onion, and Parsley
  18. Enjoy!
