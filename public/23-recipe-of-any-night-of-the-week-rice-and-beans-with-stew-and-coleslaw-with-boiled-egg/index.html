The ingredients needed to make Rice and beans with stew and coleslaw with boiled egg:
  1. Get beans
  2. Get rice
  3. Get already made stew with chicken
  4. Get grated Carrot
  5. Prepare grated Cabbage
  6. Prepare mayonnaise
  7. Take boiled egg
  8. Prepare sausage(boiled and fried)
  9. Take salt
  10. Make ready Water

Both rice and eggs will be cooked in a whistle. I boil eggs in this way. To remove the egg shells place the eggs in a bowl of tap water for few minutes. Make sure that your eggs are thoroughly hard-boiled.

Instructions to make Rice and beans with stew and coleslaw with boiled egg:
  1. Pick, wash and put the beans on fire to parboil. When soft, wash and set aside.
  2. Wash and put the rice on fire to parboil. When soft,wash. Pour the rice in a pot, add the beans on top. Add salt and water. Put in fire to cook.
  3. Mix the carrot and cabbage together and add the mayonnaise. Cut the sausage on it. Coleslaw is ready for eating
  4. Check the rice, if the water is dried up. Drop and warm the stew.
  5. Serve the food and add the boiled egg on it.
