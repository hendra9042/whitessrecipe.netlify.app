The ingredients needed to make Hong Kong-Style Authentic Chinese Century Egg & Pork Rice Porridge:
  1. Take serving Uncooked white rice (or cooked white rice)
  2. Prepare Century egg
  3. Make ready Pork (offcuts)
  4. Get Plain flour
  5. Make ready Ginger (finely chopped)
  6. Take Chinese chicken stock powder
  7. Take Sesame oil
  8. Take Spring onion (finely chopped)
Instructions to make Hong Kong-Style Authentic Chinese Century Egg & Pork Rice Porridge:
  1. Chop up the century egg into small pieces as shown in the photo. Finely chop the ginger and spring onion.
  2. Chop the pork offcuts into small pieces and coat them with a little flour.
  3. Wash the rice and add it to a saucepan with 600 ml water. You can also use pre-cooked rice.
  4. Boil the water over high heat at first and then reduce the heat to low. If the water level gets too low, you can top it up when it needs it.
  5. After 10 minutes, add the pork and ginger.
  6. After another 10 minutes, add the century egg and chicken stock powder and simmer for a further 2 minutes. Lastly, gently stir in the sesame oil and stop the heat.
  7. Transfer the porridge to a dish and sprinkle some spring onion over the top to finish.
