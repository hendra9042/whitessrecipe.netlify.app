The ingredients needed to make Stuffed Egg Nog French Toast:
  1. Make ready 6 Multigrain whole wheat bread slices
  2. Get 1/2 cup Cream cheese –
  3. Prepare 4tsp Jam, your favorite
  4. Prepare 2 Eggs-
  5. Prepare 1 cup Egg nog-
  6. Prepare 1 tsp Vanilla extract-
  7. Get As needed Unsalted butter-
  8. Get As needed Fresh berries-
Steps to make Stuffed Egg Nog French Toast:
  1. In a bowl, mix together the cream cheese and jam. Spread this mixture onto the bread slices and pair them to make 3 sandwiches. In a large baking dish, whisk together the eggs, egg nog, & vanilla extract. Dip the sandwiches in the prepared mixture to soak up the egg nog. Melt butter in a non-stick pan over medium flame. Place the sandwiches in batches and cook for 2 mins on each side to brown. Transfer sandwiches to a plate and cut each in half and serve with fresh berries.
