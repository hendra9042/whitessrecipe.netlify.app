The ingredients needed to make Chicken & Mushrooms:
  1. Take 8 boneless chicken breasts
  2. Get 1 3/4 cups milk of your choice, divided
  3. Get 1 1/2 cups flour of your choice
  4. Prepare 3 eggs or equivalent of egg substitute
  5. Prepare 3/4 tsp salt
  6. Take 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  7. Make ready 1 tsp tarragon
  8. Make ready 4 oz canned sliced mushrooms, drained
  9. Take 1 tbsp butter
  10. Get 1 (10 3/4 oz) can cream of chicken

Steps to make Chicken & Mushrooms:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 13x9 baking pan.
  2. Arrange chicken in single layer in prepared baking pan.
  3. Combine 1 1/2 cups milk, the flour, eggs, & salt in medium bowl. Beat until blended. Add oil & tarragon & mix well. Pour over chicken in baking pan.
  4. Bake 65 minutes or until meat thermometer inserted in chicken registers 180 degrees.
  5. Heat butter in saucepan until melted. Stir in mushrooms. Stirring frequently, until heated through.
  6. Add remaining milk & soup. Mix well. Cook, stirring continually, until heated through & of sauce consistency. Serve with chicken.
