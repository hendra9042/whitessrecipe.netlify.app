The ingredients needed to make Vegetable Maggi with Poached Egg:
  1. Get 1 onion
  2. Take 1 capsicum
  3. Prepare 1 Tomato
  4. Take 1 Green chilli
Instructions to make Vegetable Maggi with Poached Egg:
  1. Roughly chopped onions, capsicum, tomato and green chilli
  2. Add the vegetables in a pan with 2tsp of mustard oil.
  3. Add 1/2 tsp of turmeric powder, 1 tsp of salt, 1/2 tsp red chilli powder, 1/2 tsp sugar and 1 packet maggi masala powder.
  4. The maggi is ready. Add the poached egg on the top and enjoy the food!!!
