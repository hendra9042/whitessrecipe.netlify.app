The ingredients needed to make Kimchi Tofu Gratin with Tomatoes:
  1. Make ready Tofu (silken or firm)
  2. Get Kimchi
  3. Prepare Tomato
  4. Get as necessary Leafy greens like mizuna
  5. Get Shredded cheese
  6. Make ready Eggs
  7. Make ready Almonds
  8. Get Flaxseed oil
Steps to make Kimchi Tofu Gratin with Tomatoes:
  1. Drain the tofu and cut into your desired size. Place into a dish.
  2. Cut the kimchi and tomato into bite-sized pieces. Mix these in a bowl and then put on top of the tofu.
  3. Cut the mizuna into 4 cm pieces and scatter on top.
  4. Top with the shredded cheese in a circle. Break an egg into the middle.
  5. Cook in a microwave, oven, or oven toaster until the cheese melts and the egg white starts to cook.
  6. Top with chopped almonds and drizzle flaxseed oil on top. Done☆
