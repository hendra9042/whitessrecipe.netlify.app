The ingredients needed to make Egg-Mayo for Sandwich Fillings:
  1. Make ready Eggs
  2. Make ready Mayonnaise
  3. Take Krazy Salt
  4. Get Margarine
  5. Get Coarsely ground black pepper
  6. Get Dried basil (optional)
Steps to make Egg-Mayo for Sandwich Fillings:
  1. Boil the eggs (I boiled for 13 minutes.)
  2. Put the boiled eggs and other ingredients in a bowl, and mash the eggs with a fork while mixing. It's done when everything is nicely mixed together. Taste and increase the amount of Krazy Salt if desired.
  3. Spread as much of it as you like on bread and enjoy. Of course, you can also use it as a sandwich filler.
