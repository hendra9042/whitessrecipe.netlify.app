The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon French Toast:
  1. Prepare 1 large egg
  2. Make ready 2 egg whites
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup skim milk
  4. Take 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Get 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  6. Take 1/9 tsp ground nutmeg
  7. Get 8 1-inch thick diagonally cut slices French or Italian bread
  8. Take 1 Cinnamon Sugar and reduced calorie maple syrup (optional)
Instructions to make Cinnamon French Toast:
  1. In a shallow bowl, using a wire whisk or a fork, beat the egg and egg whites until foamy. Add the milk, vanilla, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Beat well; set aside.
  2. Preheat the oven to 200°F. Lightly spray a large nonstick skillet with vegetable cooking spray; heat over medium heat. Dip 4 of the bread slices into the egg mixture, turning to coat and draining excess back into dish.
  3. Place bread slices into prepared skillet. Cook until golden brown, turning once, about 1 to 2 minutes per side.
  4. Transfer cooked slices to a plate; keep warm in oven. Dip remaining slices in egg mixture; cook as directed. Spray skillet with vegetable cooking spray as needed.
  5. Divide toast among serving plates. Sprinkle lightly with cinnamon sugar and top with maple syrup. Serve immediately.
  6. Variation: For puffy French toast, prepare egg mixture in a large, shallow glass dish. Add bread slices, turning to coat. Cover and refrigerate overnight. Cook and serve as directed.
