The ingredients needed to make Braised beef over egg noodles/pot roast:
  1. Prepare 3-4 lb. Bnls. beef chuck roast
  2. Take 1 lb. Egg noodles
  3. Get 4 cups beef stock or broth if using broth watch the salt
  4. Take 1 knorr beef bouillon
  5. Take 2 med onoin
  6. Take 1 bunch spring/green onion
  7. Prepare 1 (8 oz.) Package Baby bella mushrooms or your preference
  8. Prepare 3 ribs of celery with leaves
  9. Take 3-4 garlic cloves depending on size and your taste
  10. Get Handful fresh parsley
  11. Get 2 tbsp. Fresh thyme
  12. Prepare 2-3 bay leaves depending on size
  13. Take Salt
  14. Prepare Pepper
  15. Prepare 3 tbsp. Peanut oil or bacon grease

  2. Season roast on both sides with salt and pepper massage in and let sit for about 15-20 mins to absorb some of the salt.
  3. Heat pan that has a lid too med./high heat. Once heated add your oil and swirl along the bottom of pan allowing 1-2 mins to heat oil.
  4. Place roast in pan and brown 4-5 mins per side if your smoking and start to smell burning odor take off heat and allow to cool a bit and reduce heat and put pan back on burner
  5. While your Browning your beef cut up your celery and onions. Once nicely browned remove and place on plate or foil you seasoned on.
  6. Now reduce heat to med/low and add your vegetables. If you keep the heat where it was those beautiful brown bits will become dark and bitter burnt tasting ruining your gravy.
  7. Stir untill soft and translucent about 5-7 mins
  8. While sauteing smash and mince garlic and thyme.
  9. Add in the last minute and stir to incorporate.
  10. Now add a cup of stock and beef bouillon and stir untill the bouillon dissolved completely.
  11. Add the rest of stock. And bay leaves.
  12. Bring to a hard boil.
  13. Once boiling add your roast. Should not be completely covered where braising not boiling the meat.
