The ingredients needed to make Brad's grits for breakfast:
  1. Make ready 2 tbs butter
  2. Take 1/2 small onion, fine chopped
  3. Get 1 small jalapeño, seeded and minced
  4. Make ready 1 tsp minced garlic
  5. Take 1 1/2 cup water
  6. Get 1 tsp granulated chicken bouillon
  7. Get 1/2 cup dry grits
  8. Get 1/4 cup shredded white cheddar
  9. Prepare 2 eggs
  10. Make ready Sea salt
  11. Get Black pepper
  12. Take Tobasco sauce
  13. Make ready Salsa verde
Steps to make Brad's grits for breakfast:
  1. Melt butter in a medium saucepan. Saute onion, pepper, and garlic on med low heat until onion is translucent.
  2. Add water and bouillon to onion. Bring to a boil.
  3. Meanwhile, heat oil in a pan to fry egg. Fry egg to taste, I did Sunnyside up. Sprinkle with sea salt and pepper.
  4. When water boils, add dry grits. Stir well. Reduce heat to a simmer. Stir very often until grits become creamy and soft. Add cheese and remove from heat. Continue stirring until cheese is incorporated.
  5. Plate grits. Put eggs on top. Sprinkle tobasco sauce around the outside. And salsa verde over eggs. Serve immediately. Enjoy.
