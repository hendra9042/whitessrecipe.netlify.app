The ingredients needed to make Beck's Homemade Creamy Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. Get whole chicken, cut into 4 pieces.
  2. Prepare cups of water
  3. Take chicken bouillon cubes
  4. Get wide egg noodles or noodle of your chioce
  5. Take flat Italian parsley, chopped
  6. Prepare black pepper
  7. Take garlic cloves, minced
  8. Prepare carrots, sliced OR 2 cans to make easy
  9. Take small onions, chopped or bundle of green onions, chopped
  10. Prepare can of corn or frozen 12 ounces
  11. Prepare cornstarch
Steps to make Beck's Homemade Creamy Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. Add 15 cups of water to large pot. Bring to a boil.
  2. Add raw whole chicken or pieces. Put heat to medium med. heat and boil for 30 minutes or so…
  3. Add chicken bouillon cubes. Boil up..
  4. Take chicken pieces out and cool. As cooling, add noodles.
  5. Add pepper, garlic and parsley.
  6. Take chicken meat off the bones, and put back into soup. Discard the bones, etc. Keep boiling up for 10/15 minutes…
  7. Add onions. Cook 5 minutes
  8. Add carrots and corn. ( add carrots with noodles if they are raw and not canned! )
  9. Take out 2 cups of broth and add cornstarch. Boil up and turn off heat. Let sit for 15 minutes. Serve.
