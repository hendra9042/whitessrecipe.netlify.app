Steps to make Beef & Broccoli Stuffing Casserole:
  1. First off, choose whichever cream of soup and stuffing flavor you like best!
  2. Cook broccoli, brown meat with chopped onion, and grease a 9x13 glass casserole dish. I like Crisco.
  3. Mix soup, mayo, and beaten eggs in a bowl.
  4. Melt butter for easy pouring.
  5. Place broccoli in bottom of dish. Cover with beef. Cover with soup mix. Cover with stuffing. Drizzle butter over stuffing. Top with cheese.
  6. Cover with aluminum foil and bake at 350 for 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake 10 more minutes to melt cheese. Cool and serve.
