The ingredients needed to make Healthy Pancakes - Healthy Breakfast:
  1. Get oats
  2. Take eggs
  3. Take Bananas
  4. Make ready Milk
  5. Get Vegetable Oil
  6. Get Cinnamon Powder
  7. Take Sliced Bananas & Raspberry
  8. Prepare Maple Syrup
Instructions to make Healthy Pancakes - Healthy Breakfast:
  1. Take all the ingredients to a jar and Blend it to a fine paste
  2. Heat the pan, brush it with oil
  3. Take 1/4 cup of batter and pour it into pan then cook it at low heat
  4. For plating, Layer the pancake spreading 1 tsp of Nutella between each pancake. Top it with some banana and raspberry.
  5. Pour some maple syrup. And enjoy your quick healthy breakfast.
