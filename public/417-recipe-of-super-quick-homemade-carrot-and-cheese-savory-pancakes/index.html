The ingredients needed to make Carrot and Cheese Savory Pancakes:
  1. Prepare carrot, finely grated
  2. Get cheddar cheese, finely grated
  3. Prepare large eggs
  4. Get self raising flour
  5. Get salt and pepper to taste
  6. Make ready Butter or olive oil for frying
Steps to make Carrot and Cheese Savory Pancakes:
  1. Grate carrot and cheese then combine them in a mixing bowl.
  2. Lightly whisk the eggs then add to the carrot and cheese, stirring to coat.
  3. Add flour and mix to combine, season with salt and pepper.
  4. Heat a bit of butter or olive oil in a frying pan, medium heat. Add spoonfuls of batter to the pan using a large spoon. Space them evenly apart.
  5. Cook until golden brown underneath and the top is just starting to set. Flip pancakes with a spatula and allow other side to brown. Once both sides are golden brown, remove from pan.
  6. Allow to cool for a view minutes and serve.
