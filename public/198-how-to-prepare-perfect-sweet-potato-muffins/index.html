The ingredients needed to make Sweet Potato Muffins:
  1. Make ready - cups golden raisins
  2. Take - cups sweet potatoes, cooked and mashed
  3. Get - cup chopped nuts (pecans or other)
  4. Get - cups vegetable oil
  5. Make ready - cups sugar (or Splenda)
  6. Get - eggs (or egg beaters)
  7. Get - cups flour
  8. Get - Tablespoon Cinnamon
Steps to make Sweet Potato Muffins:
  1. Steam raisins 3 minutes and drain well.
  2. Combine eggs, sugar, oil and potatoes. Mix well.
  3. Add flour and cinnamon.
  4. Fold in raisins and nuts.
  5. Fill muffin cups 2/3 full.
  6. Bake 350 degrees 15 to 25 minutes until toasty brown.
