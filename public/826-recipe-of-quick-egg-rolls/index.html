The ingredients needed to make Egg rolls:
  1. Get cabbage (red and green)
  2. Prepare shredded carrot
  3. Get protein (your choice) i use chicken or pork
  4. Take egg
  5. Take salt
  6. Take all-purpose flour
  7. Get water. may need a little more or less.

Steps to make Egg rolls:
  1. Combine flour and salt. Make hole in middle then add egg.
  2. Mix well then slowly add water to make dough.
  3. Cover and let sit for 20 min.
  4. Make small balls with dough and roll out very thin.
  5. Add shredded cabbage, carrots and protein in middle and roll.
  6. Deep fry for a couple minutes till golden brown.
