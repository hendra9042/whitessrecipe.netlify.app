The ingredients needed to make Crispy Curried Fried Chicken:
  1. Prepare CHICKEN OPTION
  2. Prepare 2 chicken breast
  3. Take FLOUR COATING 1
  4. Prepare 2 cup flour
  5. Make ready EGG COATING 2
  6. Take 2 egg
  7. Prepare BREADCRUMBS AND CURRY MIXTURE 3
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp curry powder
  9. Take 2 cup breadcrumbs
  10. Make ready 1 tsp salt
Steps to make Crispy Curried Fried Chicken:
  1. Pat dry chicken with paper towel then Coat the chicken with flour
  2. Coat chicken with egg
  3. coat chicken with breadcrumbs and curry mixture
  4. Deep fry till its crispy and cooked about 20 minutes or longer depending on the thickness of the chicken
  5. Drain dry on paper towel
