The ingredients needed to make Spicy Egg Sandwich For Grown-ups:
  1. Get 1 Boiled egg
  2. Get 1 Bread of your choice
  3. Take 2 heaping teaspoons Caesar salad dressing or mayonnaise
  4. Take 1 to 2 shakes Chili pepper powder
  5. Get 1 Dried parsley, to taste
  6. Take 1 Salt and pepper (Coarsely ground recommended), to taste
Steps to make Spicy Egg Sandwich For Grown-ups:
  1. Mash up the egg finely with a fork. It's easier if you divide the white and yolk beforehand. Add the Caesar salad dressing and mix well.
  2. Add the Tabasco and chili pepper powder, and season with salt and pepper. Add some dried parsley for color. Sandwich between slices of bread of your choice.
  3. I spread it on sliced baguette and crackers to make small hors d'œuvres. If you add the chili pepper powder on top, it looks prettier.
  4. If using a baguette, you'll need about 1/3 to 1/4 of one. If using sliced bread (12-slice loaf), there is enough egg salad for 4 slices.
