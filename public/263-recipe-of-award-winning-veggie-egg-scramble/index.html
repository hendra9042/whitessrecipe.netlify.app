The ingredients needed to make Veggie Egg Scramble:
  1. Get fresh veggies
  2. Get eggs
  3. Make ready half & half
  4. Take salt & pepper
  5. Prepare cream cheese
  6. Make ready butter
  7. Make ready shredded cheese
Steps to make Veggie Egg Scramble:
  1. whisk together eggs, half & half, salt, and pepper
  2. break cream cheese into small chunks, and whisk into eggs
  3. melt butter over medium high heat. add veggies and saute 6-8 minutes. Reduce heat and let pan cool.
  4. pour eggs mixture into pan and sprinkle cheese over mixture. Allow to set 1-2 minutes.
  5. lightly stir eggs and allow to set another 1-2 minutes.
  6. repeat step five 2 more times.
