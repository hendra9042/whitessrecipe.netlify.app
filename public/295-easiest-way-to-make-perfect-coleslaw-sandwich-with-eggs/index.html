Instructions to make Coleslaw Sandwich with Eggs:
  1. Shread cabbage, carrot and purple cabbage.
  2. In a big mixing bowl take together mayonnaise and hung curd.
  3. Add in crushed black pepper, chilli flakes, sugar and salt.
  4. Mix together to make a thick paste.
  5. Add in shredded vegetables and mix well.
  6. Also add in sumac powder to give little tartness. You can substitute sumac powder with dry pomegranate seeds.
  7. Shread 3 boil eggs and add in to the bowlnof coleslaw.
  8. Mix together and your delicious coleslaw with egg is ready.
  9. Make sandwiches with this coleslaw, lettuce and bread of your choice. Or enjoy as healthy salad.
