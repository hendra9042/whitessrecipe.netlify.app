The ingredients needed to make Use Up Egg Whites! Baked White Doughnuts!:
  1. Prepare Egg white (medium)
  2. Take Butter
  3. Take Sugar
  4. Make ready Cake flour
  5. Prepare Oil
  6. Make ready Powdered sugar (for decoration)
Instructions to make Use Up Egg Whites! Baked White Doughnuts!:
  1. Bring the butter to room temperature. Whisk together with the sugar until creamy.
  2. Lightly mix the egg white with a fork. (It's okay if it becomes slightly frothy).
  3. Add the egg white to the butter and sugar in three portions. Mix with each addition.
  4. Once thoroughly mixed, sift the flour into the batter. Mix until no longer powdery.
  5. Lightly oil the doughnut mold.
  6. Put the batter into a pastry bag and squeeze into the doughnut mold.
  7. Bake in an oven preheated to 180°C for 15 minutes.
  8. When they have cooled, put the doughnuts into a plastic bag with some powdered sugar. Shake to coat with the sugar.
  9. A version without powdered sugar.
