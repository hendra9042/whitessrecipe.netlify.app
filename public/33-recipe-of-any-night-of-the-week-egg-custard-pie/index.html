The ingredients needed to make Egg Custard Pie:
  1. Get 2 cup sugar
  2. Take 6 eggs
  3. Get 2 large cans canned milk
  4. Take 2 tbsp corn starch
  5. Make ready 1 stick of margarine
  6. Get 2 tsp vanilla flavoring
Steps to make Egg Custard Pie:
  1. mix all of the above
  2. pour into 2 deep dish pie shells
  3. place in cold oven, then set to 350°F.
  4. for chocolate pies add 5 Tbsp cocoa to above mixture, save egg whites for meringue.
