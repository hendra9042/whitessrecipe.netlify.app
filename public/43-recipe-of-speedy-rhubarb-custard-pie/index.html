The ingredients needed to make Rhubarb (Custard) Pie:
  1. Prepare 3 cup raw rhubarb
  2. Take 1 cup flour
  3. Make ready 1 cup white sugar
  4. Get 2 tbsp butter
  5. Make ready 2 eggs (unbeaten)
  6. Take 1 TOPPING
  7. Prepare 1 cup flour
  8. Take 1/3 cup butter
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup brown sugar

Instructions to make Rhubarb (Custard) Pie:
  1. Mix all above ingredients except for topping and put into unbaked pie shell.
  2. Mix topping ingredients into crumbs and place on top of pie.
  3. Bake at 425°F for 10 min., then at 350°F for 30-40 min.
