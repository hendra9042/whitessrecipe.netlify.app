The ingredients needed to make Pasta, Tomato, Cheese Tart:
  1. Make ready angel hair pasta, cooked
  2. Make ready For Sauce
  3. Take box of Boursin garlic and herb cheese
  4. Get sour cream
  5. Make ready fresh grated Romano cheese
  6. Make ready shredded Italian cheese blend
  7. Get heavy cream
  8. Get green onions,sliced
  9. Get large egg, lightly beaten
  10. Take pepper and salt to taste
  11. Take hot pickled chili peppers, choppped
  12. Get To Finish
  13. Prepare large fresh red ripe tomato, sliced
  14. Take fresh grated Romano cheese
  15. Make ready green onions, sliced
  16. Make ready bacon, cooked crisp and crumbled
  17. Take Italian seasoning for sprinkling
  18. Get Salt and pepper
  19. Prepare provolone cheese
Instructions to make Pasta, Tomato, Cheese Tart:
  1. Preheat the oven to 400. Spray a 9 inch springform pan with non stick spray
  2. Combine all sauce ingredients in a nowl
  3. Add angel hair pasta and gent;y blend
  4. Spread in prepared pan
  5. Add tomato slices to cover pasta. Season tomato’s with salt. Pepper and Italian seasing
  6. Add Romano cheese
  7. Then cover with provalone slices
  8. Add green onions and bacon
  9. Bake about 20 minutes until hot and cheese is melted
  10. Remove sides from springform pan
  11. Slice and serve
