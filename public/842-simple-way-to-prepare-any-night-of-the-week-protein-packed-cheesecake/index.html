The ingredients needed to make Protein packed cheesecake:
  1. Make ready 1 container (24 oz) Siggis 0% fat Greek yogurt plain
  2. Make ready 1 cup Swerve no calorie sugar replacement
  3. Take 1 cup Lactaid low fat cottage cheese
  4. Get 2 scoops vanilla protein powder
  5. Prepare 6 tbsp light soft cream cheese
  6. Make ready The juice of 1 lemon
  7. Prepare 1 egg
  8. Get 1 Quest beyond cinnamon bun cereal bar for each ramekin
  9. Take 2 scoops vanilla protein powder

Instructions to make Protein packed cheesecake:
  1. In a blender combine all ingredients till liquid. This will break up the chunks in the cottage cheese
  2. Crumble up a cereal bar and put on the bottom of a ramekin
  3. Fill ramekins with mix depending on the size you will get 6-8 cheesecakes
  4. Preheat oven to 325 degrees
  5. Place ramekins in a shallow pan, pour water in pan around ramekins
  6. Cook cheesecakes for 40-45 minutes edges should be brown
