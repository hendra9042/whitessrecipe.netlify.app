The ingredients needed to make Scrambled Eggs with Spinach and Grilled Sausage:
  1. Take 12 bite sized beef sausages
  2. Take 8 eggs
  3. Get 12 oz fresh spinach
  4. Get 2 tbsp mill
  5. Make ready 1 tsp garlic powder
  6. Take 2 tsp minced onion
  7. Get 1 tsp crushed red pepper
  8. Take 1 tsp salt
  9. Take 1/4 tsp black pepper
  10. Take 2 tbsp ghee
Steps to make Scrambled Eggs with Spinach and Grilled Sausage:
