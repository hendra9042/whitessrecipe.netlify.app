The ingredients needed to make Breakfast casserole:
  1. Get 1 lb sausage- cooked, drained, and cooled
  2. Prepare 1 (12 oz) can biscuit dough (10 count)
  3. Take 2 cup shredded mozzarella
  4. Take 6 eggs well beaten
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup milk
  6. Get 1/4 tsp salt
  7. Get 1/4 tsp pepper
  8. Get 1/4 tsp oregano

Steps to make Breakfast casserole:
  1. Preheat oven to 425
  2. Butter or spray 9×13 inch baking dish
  3. Press biscuits into bottom of baking dish, sealing all areas to make solid crust
  4. Sprinkle cooked sausage and cheese over biscuit crust
  5. Combine well beaten eggs with milk, salt, pepper, and oregano
  6. Carefully pour egg mixture over sausage and cheese
  7. Bake for 25 min or until center is firmly set
