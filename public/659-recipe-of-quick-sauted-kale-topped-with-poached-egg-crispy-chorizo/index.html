The ingredients needed to make Sauted Kale Topped with Poached Egg + Crispy Chorizo:
  1. Prepare 1 Handful of Baby Kale (organic, pre-washed)
  2. Get 2 poached eggs
  3. Get 1/4 cup Spanish Chorizo Cold Cuts (lardons)
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup Honey Lime vinaigrette + Tb mayo
  5. Get 1 tbsp Chihuahua cheese (optional)
  6. Make ready 1 Cilantro garnish
Instructions to make Sauted Kale Topped with Poached Egg + Crispy Chorizo:
  1. Poach Eggs
  2. Pan fry chorizo lardons until lightly crisp
  3. Prepare dressing by adding the mayo to the vinaigrette + freshly cracked pepper
  4. Add handfuls of Baby Kale, pull off the heat before kale wilts considerably, and add dressing
  5. Plating: Kale base, poached eggs on top, sprinkle of chihuahua cheese (optional), cilantro garnish
