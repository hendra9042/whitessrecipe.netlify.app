The ingredients needed to make Egg Sandwich:
  1. Get 2 pieces honey wheat bread
  2. Get mayo
  3. Prepare 1/2 tomato
  4. Get 1 fried egg
  5. Get butter
Steps to make Egg Sandwich:
  1. Melt 1 TBS. Butter in shallow pan.
  2. Place bread in pan to soak butter and toast.
  3. Put aside. Fry egg to liking. I try to make sure the yolk is runny.
  4. Put good amount of mayo on both pieces of buttered toast.
  5. Place fried egg on piece of mayo'd toast.
  6. Follow with sliced tomato and last piece of mayo'd toast.
