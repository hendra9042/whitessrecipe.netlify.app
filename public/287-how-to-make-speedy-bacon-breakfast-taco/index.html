The ingredients needed to make Bacon Breakfast Taco:
  1. Prepare 2 flour tortillas
  2. Take 6 large eggs
  3. Get 6 slices bacon
  4. Get 1/4 cup 2% milk
  5. Take 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
  6. Make ready 1/3 cup fire roasted diced tomatoes
  7. Take 1 cup fresh chopped spinach
  8. Prepare 2 tbs Jalapeño Ranch
  9. Prepare 1 tbs garlic powder
  10. Make ready 1 tbs onion powder
  11. Take Salt to taste
  12. Make ready Pepper to taste
  13. Prepare 1/4 cup chopped green onion
Instructions to make Bacon Breakfast Taco:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil and place bacon onto baking sheet. Bake until meat is dark brown and crispy (about 30 minutes)
  2. While bacon is in the oven place eggs, milk, salt, pepper,onion powder, and garlic powder into a large mixing bowl. Beat eggs and other ingredients with a fork until smooth.
  3. Spray a large pan with non-stick cooking spray. Pour egg mixture, fire roasted diced tomatoes, and spinach into the pan. Cook over a medium high heat until eggs have a soft fluffy texture.
  4. Pour mozzarella cheese over top of the eggs and cover until cheese is melted. Remove from heat.
  5. Heat flour tortillas in microwave for 30 seconds or heat tortillas in a skillet for 1 to 2 minutes on each side until crispy.
  6. Serve eggs in a warm flour tortilla topped with three pieces of bacon, jalapeño ranch and green onion.
