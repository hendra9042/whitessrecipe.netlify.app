  1. fry hash browns and sausage together or separately
  2. beat eggs and add milk; whisk well
  3. pour hash browns and sausage into glass casserole dish
  4. add egg and milk mixture
  5. stir in cheese, salt, and pepper
  6. bake at 350 for 35 minutes
