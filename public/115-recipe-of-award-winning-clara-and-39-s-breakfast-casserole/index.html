The ingredients needed to make Clara's Breakfast casserole:
  1. Get Hot sausage (I suppose you could use mild, but why?)
  2. Prepare bread
  3. Take shredded cheddar cheese
  4. Get eggs
  5. Take milk
  6. Make ready salt
  7. Take black pepper
Steps to make Clara's Breakfast casserole:
  1. Brown sausage.
  2. Mix cheese, eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place bread slices in bottom of a 9 x 13 casserole dish. Cover with sausage. Then cover sausage with egg and cheese mixture. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
  4. Uncover and bake at 350 degrees for 40 mins.
