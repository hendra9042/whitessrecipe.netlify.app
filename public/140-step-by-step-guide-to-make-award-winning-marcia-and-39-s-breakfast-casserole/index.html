The ingredients needed to make Marcia's Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Make ready eggs
  2. Make ready milk
  3. Get salt
  4. Make ready mustard
  5. Get thick bread cubed
  6. Make ready shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  7. Take cooked pork sausage (jimmy dean)
Steps to make Marcia's Breakfast Casserole:
  1. butter 9"x13" baking dish
  2. in bowl beat eggs with mixer; add milk, salt, mustard
  3. stir in bread, cheese, and cooked sausage; mix thoroughly
  4. spread in greased pan- cover in foil and refrigerate over night (at least 12hrs)
  5. take pan out at least 1 hour before baking
  6. bake in oven at 350 for 45 minutes uncovered
