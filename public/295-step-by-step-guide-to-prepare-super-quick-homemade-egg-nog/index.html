The ingredients needed to make Egg nog:
  1. Get 6 each egg yokes
  2. Get 1 1/2 cup sugar
  3. Get 1 whiskey
  4. Prepare 1 gallon milk
  5. Take 1/2 gallon vanilla ice cream
  6. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
  7. Get 1 tsp nutmeg

Steps to make Egg nog:
  1. Separate egg yokes and whites. set whites aside for later
  2. In large mixing bowl add egg yolks, 1 and a half cups of sugar and jigger of whiskey. ,Mix together very well
  3. Add 1 gallon of milk stirring constantly
  4. Add scoops of vanilla ice cream by spooning in the entire half gallon
  5. Whip egg whites until stiff and fold into mixture
  6. Add nutmeg to mixture. Stir and serve
