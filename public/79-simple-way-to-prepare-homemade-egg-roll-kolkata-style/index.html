The ingredients needed to make EGG ROLL (kolkata style):
  1. Make ready 150 gms refined flour
  2. Get 3 tbsp white oil
  3. Take 1/2 tsp black pepper
  4. Make ready 2 eggs
  5. Take 1 onion chopped
  6. Take 1 capsicum chopped
  7. Get 1 tomato chopped
  8. Take 2 green chillies, chopped
  9. Take 1 tsp tomato sauce
  10. Make ready 1 tsp green chili sauce
  11. Get as required Salt

Instructions to make EGG ROLL (kolkata style):
  1. Add white oil and salt to the refiend oil n make a dough with the help of water. Chopped onion, tomato, green chilies, and capsicum and keep aside.
  2. Make the parathas in round shaped.
  3. Beat the egg add it with black pepper and salt. Add some oil to the pan n spread the beaten egg. Now put the paratha on the top of the egg spread.
  4. Saute the chopped onion, green chilies, tomato, and capsicum for 2 mins n sprinkle some black pepper and salt.Now add tomato n chilli sauce to middle of each parathas
  5. Now add saute vegetable on top of the sauce. Roll the whole parathas with the help of the tissue paper.Now the item is ready to serve.
