The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Sausage Casserole:
  1. Get can jumbo buttermilk biscuits
  2. Get hot sausage, cooked & crumbled
  3. Make ready Colby & Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
  4. Prepare large eggs
  5. Get milk
  6. Make ready salt & pepper
  7. Prepare packet country sausage gravy
Instructions to make Breakfast Sausage Casserole:
  1. Preheat oven 350° in a 9 x 13 dish, coated with butter or spray.
  2. Cut each biscuit into 8th. Line bottom of baking dish with the cut biscuits.
  3. Sprinkle cooked and crumbled sausage evenly over biscuits.
  4. Sprinkle shredded cheese over sausage. (Calls for Colby & Jack, I think I will use sharp cheddar next time for a little more cheese flavor.)
  5. In a medium bowl, whisk eggs with milk (for two-full minutes.) Season with salt & pepper. Pour over cheese layer.
  6. In a medium bowl, combine packet of gravy mix with 2 cups water. Whisk until combined. Pour gravy over egg mixture.
  7. Cover and bake 45 to 55 minutes.
  8. To make it a little more moist, I made another packet of gravy to spoon over casserole.
