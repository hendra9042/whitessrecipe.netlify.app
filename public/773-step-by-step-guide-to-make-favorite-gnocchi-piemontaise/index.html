The ingredients needed to make Gnocchi Piemontaise:
  1. Take 300 grams chat potato or Kipfler
  2. Take 80 grams flour
  3. Take 1 egg
  4. Make ready 1 eggyolk
  5. Take 15 grams butter
  6. Get 1 grams nutmeg
  7. Prepare 1 seasoning
Instructions to make Gnocchi Piemontaise:
  1. Bake the potatoes in their jackets or steam. Peel and mash and mix whilst hot with the remaining ingredients.
  2. Pipe or roll into a sausage shape. Cut into 3cm pieces and slightly flatten with a fork.
  3. Poach gently for 3-5 minutes and drain in a colander. Serve immediately or chill in iced water and reheat for service.
  4. Serve with tomato paste or pesto.
