The ingredients needed to make Sausage Biscuit Casserole:
  1. Take ingredients
  2. Get crescent rolls
  3. Make ready pork sausage
  4. Make ready grated cheddar cheese
  5. Make ready eggs, beaten
  6. Get milk
  7. Get salt & pepper to taste
Steps to make Sausage Biscuit Casserole:
  1. Brown sausage, crumble in skillet. Drain fat after browned.
  2. Preheat oven to 350. Spray 13x9 baking dish. Line bottom of dish with crescent rolls, closing perforations.
  3. Spread sausage & cheese over crescent rolls.
  4. Sprinkle cheese over top of sausage
  5. Mix eggs & milk together, then pour over top of cheese & sausage.
  6. Bake uncovered 15 mins.
