The ingredients needed to make Sausage Biscuit Casserole:
  1. Prepare Biscuits
  2. Take Sausage
  3. Prepare Eggs
  4. Make ready Salt
  5. Take Pepper
  6. Prepare Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  7. Make ready Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  8. Take Milk
  9. Prepare Yellow Onion
  10. Get Olive Oil
Instructions to make Sausage Biscuit Casserole:
  1. Preheat the oven to 375
  2. Brown the sausage
  3. Dice the onion and sauté it in olive oil
  4. Beat the eggs and add milk and salt and pepper to taste
  5. Grease a 9 inch pan and use the biscuits to make a crust
  6. Crumble the sausage and layer it and the onions over the biscuits
  7. Pour the egg and milk mixture over the sausage and biscuits
  8. Layer the cheese on top
  9. Bake for 35-37 minutes. Cover with foil for the first 25 minutes.
