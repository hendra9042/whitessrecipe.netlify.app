The ingredients needed to make Egg Salad for Sandwiches in Just One Minute:
  1. Prepare Egg (medium)
  2. Make ready Mayonnaise
  3. Get Salt and pepper
Instructions to make Egg Salad for Sandwiches in Just One Minute:
  1. Break the egg into a mug, and prick the yolk in several places with a fork. It's a good idea to make lots of holes, until the egg yolk nearly falls apart.
  2. Cover the mug lightly with plastic wrap (I use a cover), and microwave for one minute (at 500 W).
  3. I recommend microwaving it for 50 seconds first, and then microwaving it for an additional 10 seconds, rather than microwaving it for a full minute at once, so that it is less likely to explode.
  4. Mash the egg in the mug with a fork.
  5. Season with the mayonnaise, salt & pepper.
  6. Done! You can use it for tartar sauce as well as egg sandwiches.
  7. Large eggs may take longer to cook. Microwave these for an additional 10 seconds at a time, checking for doneness.
