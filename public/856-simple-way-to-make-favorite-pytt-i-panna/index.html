The ingredients needed to make Pytt i Panna:
  1. Prepare finely diced white onions
  2. Take cooked and diced potatoes
  3. Take leftover beef (pork/lamb/ham/steak)
  4. Prepare eggs
  5. Take cooked pork sausage or bacon
  6. Prepare olive oil to fry
  7. Take salt
  8. Get thyme
  9. Take black pepper
  10. Get sliced beet root
Steps to make Pytt i Panna:
  1. Finely dice sausage. Leave in pan and drain off all but 4 Tbsp. sausage grease.
  2. Add diced potatoes to pan and stir-fry in sausage fat.
  3. Reduce heat to low,add sausages and potatoes; mixing all ingredients together and heating until warm.
