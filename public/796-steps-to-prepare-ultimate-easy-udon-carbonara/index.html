The ingredients needed to make Easy Udon Carbonara:
  1. Get 1 portion Frozen udon noodles
  2. Prepare 2 slice Half-strip of bacon
  3. Prepare 1/4 Onion
  4. Make ready 5 grams Butter
  5. Take 1/2 tsp Coarsely ground garlic
  6. Make ready 100 ml Heavy cream (or milk)
  7. Take 1 slice Sliced cheese
  8. Prepare 1 Salt
  9. Get 1 Black pepper
  10. Make ready 1 Egg yolk
  11. Take 1 dash Parsley

Steps to make Easy Udon Carbonara:
  1. Microwave the frozen udon for 3 minutes at 600 W.
  2. While the udon is in the microwave, melt butter in a pan over medium heat. Add garlic. When fragrant, add onion and bacon to sauté.
  3. When that's cooked, add cream and roughly chopped sliced cheese. Bring to a boil.
  4. Add the microwaved udon noodles into the pan. Season with salt and black pepper.
  5. When the sauce and noodles are well-mixed, add beaten egg yolks. Stir briskly.
  6. Dish it up, then sprinkle with black pepper and parsley to finish. Top with soft-poached eggs if you'd like.
