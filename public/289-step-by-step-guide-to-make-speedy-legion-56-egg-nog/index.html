The ingredients needed to make Legion 56 Egg Nog:
  1. Make ready 9 Egg yolks
  2. Get 4 Egg whites
  3. Prepare 1 1/2 cup Super fine sugar
  4. Make ready 1 quart Whole milk
  5. Prepare 2 quart Heavy cream
  6. Prepare 16 oz Brandy
  7. Take 8 oz Cognac
  8. Prepare 2 oz Dark rum , optional
  9. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp Too 2 teaspoons of nutmeg
  10. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp Cinnamon
Steps to make Legion 56 Egg Nog:
  1. Beat yolks till thick pale yellow using hand mixer
  2. Add sugar and milk while stirring
  3. Add heavy cream while stirring then alcohol keep stirring
  4. In other container beat the egg whites to stiff peaks
  5. Add other quart of heavy cream to egg whites while stirring
  6. Fold egg white mixture into yolk mixture
  7. Add nutmeg and cinnamon stir most in leave some on top
