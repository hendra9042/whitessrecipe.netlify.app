The ingredients needed to make Custard Pie:
  1. Make ready Base
  2. Prepare 50 grams butter
  3. Take 1/2 cup sugar
  4. Make ready 1 medium egg
  5. Take 1 cup all-purpose flour
  6. Make ready Topping
  7. Prepare 1 Ready made custard
Instructions to make Custard Pie:
  1. Add butter, sugar, egg beat at medium speed until fluffy.
  2. Add flour in small parts and it should form a dough use hand when the beater is not usable or the dough maker.
  3. Prepare a sprayed pan. Preheat oven at 250. Bake at 180 for 20 minutes.
  4. Add ready made custard and wait untill it settles
