The ingredients needed to make Sig's Potato Cake Pizza:
  1. Get 3 cup water
  2. Take 3 cup best potato mash, (same size cup used as for water)
  3. Get 2 tbsp sunflower oil
  4. Make ready 6 medium tomatoes
  5. Prepare 1 onion finely sliced
  6. Make ready 1 large clove of garlic crushed or very finely chopped
  7. Prepare 1 good pinch each of salt or salt substitute, black pepper freshly cracked, thyme and oregano
  8. Prepare 10 olives black or green
  9. Make ready 200 grams sheep or goats cheese
  10. Make ready 5 tbsp grated parmesan cheese
  11. Take 1 large egg
  12. Prepare 1 tbsp cornstarch
  13. Get 4 basil leaves very finely torn. Or use a little dried basil
  14. Make ready 2 tbsp olive oil

Steps to make Sig's Potato Cake Pizza:
  1. I use a homemade potato mash and celeriac or garlic mash, that is not runny or sloppy, but if you are in a rush boil three cups of water, then put into a bowl add the powder mash, make sure it is of good quality, stir and leave to stand for a minute, The egg and cornstarch needs to be added whether you use homemade mash or powder mash, mix well.
  2. Grease a baking tray with the oil
  3. Add your potato mash to the tray making a pizza shaped base, about 30 cm big. Bake at 225C for about 20 minutes.
  4. Wash the tomatoes,slice thinly, lay onto the potato pizza
  5. Sauté one thinly sliced onion and one crushed or very finely chopped clove of garlic. Drain fat from it and the onion and garlic mix over the tomatoes on pizza
  6. Season well with thyme, oregano,salt or salt substitute and pepper.
  7. Cut the sheep's or goats cheese into small cubes sprinkle over the pizza
  8. Add the olives and sprinkle with parmesan
  9. Drizzle with olive oil
  10. Bake for about 25 minutes until slightly browned on 225C. Sprinkle with a little fresh basil.
  11. This makes 12 slices. You can of course use other toppings too.
