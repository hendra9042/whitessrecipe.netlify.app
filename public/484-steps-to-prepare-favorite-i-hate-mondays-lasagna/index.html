The ingredients needed to make I hate Mondays, Lasagna!:
  1. Get 2 lb ground beef (80/20)
  2. Prepare 8 oz fresh spinach (chopped)
  3. Take 8 oz mushrooms (chopped)
  4. Get 26 oz pasta sauce (I use mock marinara, will post the recipe seperately)
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp horseradish
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp lemon pepper
  7. Take 4 tbsp Italian seasoning
  8. Get 1 salt
  9. Prepare 1 pepper
  10. Get 1 tbsp oregano paste
  11. Prepare 1 tbsp basil paste
  12. Take 1 packages no boil lasagna noodles
  13. Prepare 32 oz mozarella
  14. Take 16 oz monterey cheese
  15. Take 16 oz Italian Blend cheese
  16. Take grated parmesan cheese
  17. Take 2 eggs
  18. Make ready 24 oz Ricotta (cottage cheese may be used as a substitute, if used you will not need the eggs)

Instructions to make I hate Mondays, Lasagna!:
  1. Brown ground beef with horseradish, salt, pepper, Italian seasoning.
  2. Once the meat is browned add in the spinach, mushrooms and remaining seasonings. Cook over medium until mushrooms and spinach have cooked down.
  3. Whip 2 eggs in a bowl, add in ricotta cheese and parmesan. Mix thoroughly.
  4. Coat bottom of lasagna pan with marinara. Layer as directed on package of no boil lasagna. I use lots of cheese on each layer.
