The ingredients needed to make Shepherd's Pie:
  1. Prepare Mash Potato Topping
  2. Get 4 large russet potatoes
  3. Take 1/4 cup cooking cream
  4. Prepare 175 g salted butter
  5. Make ready 1/4 tsp ground pepper
  6. Prepare 1 egg yolk
  7. Take Meat Fillings
  8. Get 2 Tbsp canola oil
  9. Prepare 1 cup diced onion
  10. Prepare 1 carrot, peeled and diced
  11. Get 3 cloves garlic
  12. Take 600 g ground beef or lamb
  13. Prepare 1 tsp each salt & black pepper
  14. Get 2 Tbsp all-purpose flour
  15. Prepare 3 1/2 Tbsp tomato paste
  16. Prepare 2 cups chicken broth
  17. Prepare 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  18. Prepare 2 tsp rosemary leaves (fresh or dried)
  19. Make ready 2 tsp thyme leaves (fresh or dried)

Steps to make Shepherd's Pie:
  1. Preheat oven to highest temperature
  2. Heat the pan with oil in medium high heat. Cook onion and carrots approximately 3 to 4 minutes. Add the garlic saute awhile, then add meat, salt, pepper and cook until browned, approximately 3 minutes.
  3. Sprinkle the meat with the flour and toss to coat, continuing to cook for another minute. Add the tomato paste, chicken broth, Worcestershire, rosemary, thyme, and stir to combine.
  4. Bring to a boil, reduce the heat to low, cover and simmer slowly 10 to 12 minutes until the sauce is thickened. Pour filings into a casserole.
  5. Remove skin and boil potatoes until soft and mash it with the ingredients.
  6. Place mashed potatoes on top of meat fillings. Bake until potatoes r brown at the peaks.
