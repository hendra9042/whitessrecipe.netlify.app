The ingredients needed to make Curried Scrambled Eggs:
  1. Get Ingredients
  2. Make ready onion
  3. Prepare butter
  4. Take Polish sausage (or equivalent)
  5. Get eggs
  6. Get milk
  7. Prepare curry powder
  8. Make ready salt
  9. Get pepper
  10. Make ready basil
  11. Make ready side dish
  12. Prepare ciabatta bread
  13. Make ready butter
Steps to make Curried Scrambled Eggs:
  1. Chop onions
  2. Chop sausage. I used Polish sausage but any other dried sausage would be ok.
  3. Heat butter in frying pan
  4. Add onions and fry until soft but not brown
  5. Add sausage and fry for a few more minutes, stirring constantly
  6. In bowl beat eggs, milk, curry powder and salt & pepper
  7. Pour mixture over the onions and sausages. Stir constantly.
  8. Spread butter onto one side of the bread and lightly grill
  9. Sprinkle basil over the eggs and bread and serve
