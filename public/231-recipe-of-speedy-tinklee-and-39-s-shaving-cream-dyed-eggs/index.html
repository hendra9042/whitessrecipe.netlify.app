The ingredients needed to make Tinklee's Shaving Cream Dyed Eggs:
  1. Prepare 1 cold hard boiled eggs
  2. Prepare 1 can cheap white shaving cream
  3. Make ready 1 liquid neon food coloring drops
  4. Take 1 cookie sheet
  5. Get 1 paper towels
  6. Take 1 toothpicks
  7. Prepare 1 paper plates
Instructions to make Tinklee's Shaving Cream Dyed Eggs:
  1. Spread the cheap white shaving cream all across the cookie sheet
  2. Apply drops of food coloring all over the surface of the shaving cream
  3. Take toothpick and swirl around, to swirl the colors together making a nice marble effect
  4. Roll the eggs in the shaving cream,( use gloves if you don't want to dye your hands also, it does wash away after a bit though), set on a paper plate to dry
  5. Allow to dry for 3-4 minutes, then wipe off with the paper towel, set aside
  6. When done with all of then, take and then rinse off under cold water, and now check out your great marbled eggs!
  7. Enjoy! Remember to keep refrigerated
