The ingredients needed to make Brunch Casserole:
  1. Take 10 Oz package hash browns
  2. Get chopped onion
  3. Make ready parsley
  4. Take butter
  5. Take flour
  6. Get milk
  7. Prepare salt
  8. Prepare pepper
  9. Get sour cream
  10. Prepare sausage
  11. Take bacon
  12. Take scrambled eggs
Steps to make Brunch Casserole:
  1. preheat oven to 350°
  2. cook hash browns according to package directions
  3. stir in onion and parsley
  4. place in greased 9x13 pan
  5. cook sausage
  6. fry bacon and chop
  7. melt butter in pan add flour salt and pepper
  8. add milk and cook until it thickens
  9. stir in sour cream and remove from heat
  10. cover potatoes with sausage and bacon pour half the sauce over the sausage
  11. cover with scrambled eggs and pour remaining sauce over them
  12. bake for 30 mins
