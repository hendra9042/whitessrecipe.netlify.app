The ingredients needed to make R-Bars:
  1. Prepare honey
  2. Prepare brown sugar
  3. Make ready egg whites, lightly beaten
  4. Prepare canola oil
  5. Make ready non-fat milk
  6. Get non-fat plain yogurt
  7. Make ready wheat flour
  8. Prepare ground flax seed
  9. Take baking soda
  10. Take cinnamon
  11. Prepare salt
  12. Prepare oats
  13. Prepare dried cranberries
  14. Make ready chopped walnuts
  15. Make ready raisins
Steps to make R-Bars:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Combine the honey, brown sugar, egg whites, oil, milk, and yogurt in a large bowl. Set aside.
  2. Mix together the wheat flour, flax seed, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt together in a medium size bowl. Then add to the wet ingredients.
  3. Then add to the combined ingredients the oats, dried cranberries, raisins, and chopped walnuts. Mix until just combined.
  4. Spread in a 9x13x2-inch greased pan.
  5. Bake for 25 minutes or until cooked through.
