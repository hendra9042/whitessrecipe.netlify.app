The ingredients needed to make Sauted Veggie Fritters:
  1. Make ready King oyster mushrooms already sauteed and flavored
  2. Prepare sauteed zucchini
  3. Prepare a sauteed onions (with garlic) chopped
  4. Make ready small red potato shredded
  5. Make ready small sweet potatoes peeled and shredded
  6. Take fresh herb of your choice I used parsley chopped
  7. Make ready flax eggs
  8. Make ready cup almond flour
  9. Make ready oat milk
  10. Prepare onion powder
  11. Take garlic powder
Instructions to make Sauted Veggie Fritters:
  1. Prepare the flax eggs (1 Tablespoon ground flax to 2.5 water)
  2. Cut the mushrooms and zucchini into into cubed size pieces.
  3. Shred both potatoes.
  4. Place the shredded potato's in large mixing bowl. Add herb, onion, zucchini and mushrooms. Mix well.
  5. Add the flax egg, nut milk and flour. Mix well.
