The ingredients needed to make Sierra's Perfect Scrambled Eggs for 2:
  1. Get Large Eggs
  2. Make ready Seasoning Salt
  3. Get Fresh Ground Black Pepper
  4. Get Mild Cheddar Cheese
  5. Get Fresh Parsley to taste as garnish
  6. Make ready Butter
  7. Prepare Milk
Steps to make Sierra's Perfect Scrambled Eggs for 2:
  1. Place medium size frying pan on med-high heat and add the butter. While pan is heating whisk together eggs, salt, pepper, parsley if you choose, cheese and milk
  2. Whisk until mixture is thoroughly blended and add to hot frying pan.
  3. Continue to whisk while mixture is in frying pan. Whisk quickly and continuously. Eggs are done when they are soft scrambled with almost no liquid. Your eggs should be fluffy with small curds. Plate and sprinkle with parsley as a garnish.
