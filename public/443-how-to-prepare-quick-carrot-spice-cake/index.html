The ingredients needed to make Carrot Spice Cake:
  1. Get 1 1/2 cups firmly packed brown sugar
  2. Make ready 3/4 cup vegetable oil
  3. Take 4 eggs or equivalent amount of egg substitute
  4. Prepare 1 1/2 cups gluten-free all purpose flour or flour of your choice
  5. Get 3/4 tsp xanthum gum (if using gluten-free flour)
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp baking powder
  7. Get 1 tsp salt
  8. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp apple pie spice
  9. Take 3 cups shredded carrots (about 5-6 medium)
  10. Get 1 cup oats
  11. Get 6 oz cream cheese
  12. Get 3 cups powdered sugar
  13. Make ready 6-8 tsp soy milk or milk of your choice

Steps to make Carrot Spice Cake:
  1. Heat oven to 325 degrees
  2. Spray a long cake pan with cooking spray.
  3. In large bowl, beat together brown sugar & oil. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. If using egg substitute, add gradually.
  4. Combine flour, xanthum gum, baking powder, salt, & apple pie spice. Stir into egg mixture.
  5. Fold in carrots & oats. Mix well.
  6. Pour into prepared pan.
  7. Bake 55-60 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
  8. Let cool completely.
  9. For frosting, combine cream cheese & powdered sugar, mix well. Add milk 1 tsp at a time, until desired consistency is reached. Spread over completely cooled cake.
