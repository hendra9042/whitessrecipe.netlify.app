The ingredients needed to make Keto Egg Roll in a Bowl:
  1. Make ready 1 lb ground pork, mild flavor
  2. Take 1 head cabbage, thinly sliced
  3. Take 1/2 medium onion, thinly sliced
  4. Take 1 T sesame oil
  5. Get 1/4 c soy sauce
  6. Prepare 1 garlic clove, minced
  7. Get 1 tsp ground ginger
  8. Make ready 2 T chicken broth
  9. Get to taste Salt and pepper
  10. Take 2 stalks green onion, chopped
Instructions to make Keto Egg Roll in a Bowl:
  1. Brown ground pork in a large pan or wok over medium heat.
  2. Add sesame oil and onion to pan with browned ground pork. Mix together and continue cooking over medium heat.
  3. Mix soy sauce, garlic, and ground ginger together in a small bowl. Once onions have browned, add the sauce mixture to the pan.
  4. Immediately add the cabbage to the pan and toss to coat evenly. Add chicken broth to the pan and mix. Continue cooking over medium heat for three minutes, stirring frequently.
  5. Garnish with salt, pepper, and green onion.
