Instructions to make Mini fruit custard tart:
  1. For the dough. Stir the flour in a large bowl.Then add the butter & rub in with your fingertips until the mixture resembles breadcrumb.
  2. Stir in the sugar,egg & vanila until it become soft dough.Let it chill in the fridge for 30 minute
  3. Take small portion of the dough & press it into the tart mould.use a folk to make a hole onto bottom of dough.(it's help the dough bake completely)
  4. Bake the tart into the oven around 20-25minute (175°c)
  5. Remove the tart from the oven once it baked & let it cold
  6. Meanwhile, for the custard. Put the milk,sugar,custard flour in a medium saucepan.
  7. Stir using low heat until the mixture fluffy.
  8. Remove the saucepan & add the butter, vanila & continues stirring until thickened
  9. The custard filing ready to pipe in the crust.pipe the custard & decorate with slice fruit & chocolate ganache
