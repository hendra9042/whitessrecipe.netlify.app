The ingredients needed to make Scrambled Eggs Beef and Cheese:
  1. Take dried beef
  2. Take tomatoes
  3. Prepare grated cheese
  4. Prepare butter
  5. Make ready Paprika
  6. Prepare eggs
  7. Prepare Black pepper
  8. Prepare Few grains red pepper
Steps to make Scrambled Eggs Beef and Cheese:
  1. Chop beef heat over hot water
  2. Heat tomatoes add cheese butter seasoning well beaten eggs and later beef
  3. Cook until eggs are creamy but not dry
  4. Serve on hot buttered toast
