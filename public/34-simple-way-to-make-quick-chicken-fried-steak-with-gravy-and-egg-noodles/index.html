The ingredients needed to make chicken fried steak with gravy and egg noodles:
  1. Prepare 2 eggs
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  3. Make ready 1 cup flour for breading
  4. Make ready 1 johnnys, garlic powder, pepper to taste
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp oil
  6. Make ready 1/3 packages wide egg noodles
  7. Take 1 tsp salt for water
  8. Prepare 2 stick carrot
  9. Get 3 tbsp butter
  10. Make ready 2/3 cup corn and pea mix
  11. Prepare 1 salt and pepper to taste
  12. Take 1 1/2 cup half and half
  13. Make ready 1 2/3 lb cube steak
  14. Make ready 1/4 cup flour for gravy
Steps to make chicken fried steak with gravy and egg noodles:
  1. for steak;
  2. cut cube steak in 3 sections
  3. beat egg in glass bowl
  4. mix flour and bread crumbs
  5. season the flour mixture to taste. i use about 1/4 cup johnnys, 2 tbsp garlic 2 tbsp pepper
  6. bread cube steak, fry until done in 1 tbsp oil on med. heat
  7. for noodles;
  8. boil 5 cups water. add salt, noodles and sliced carrots.
  9. after tender, add peas and corn.
  10. drain almost all the water, leaving behind about 2 tbsp. season to taste
  11. for gravy;
  12. melt butter in sauce pan
  13. add flour, stirring constantly. texture will be gooey
  14. after about 2 mins cooking slowly add half and half, stirring constantly.
  15. remove from heat. add seasonings to taste
  16. set up noodles and steak on plate, smother with gravy, eat! :)
