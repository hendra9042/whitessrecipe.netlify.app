The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Make ready Sausage
  2. Prepare shredded hash browns
  3. Take eggs
  4. Take milk
  5. Get cheese
Instructions to make Breakfast Casserole:
  1. grease large casserole dish.
  2. Cook the sausage and add to bottom of casserole dish.
  3. Spread a layer of shredded hash browns.
  4. Spread a layer of cheese.
  5. Mix dozen eggs and 4 cups of milk in large mixing bowl. DO NOT COOK.
  6. Pour egg and milk mixture over the cheese, hash browns and sausage.
  7. Set casserole dish in refrigerator over night.
  8. Preheat oven to 350°F Fahrenheit.
  9. Bake casserole for 30 minutes.
