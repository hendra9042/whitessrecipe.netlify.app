Instructions to make Jordan's Nicely Spiced Scrambled Eggs:
  1. First put the 4 eggs inside the bowl and only put 2 yolks inside the bowl.
  2. Second put 1 1/4 of Cayenne pepper and 1 1/4 of Salt in and mix until well.
  3. Turn the stove on medium-low and wait 1 minutes to put the pan on
  4. After when the 1 minute is up you put the eggs on the pan.
  5. Once the eggs are on the pan add 1 1/8 of Salt.
  6. Once the eggs are on the pan use the spatula to mash it up and continue to mash until the 6 minutes are up.
  7. Now you have a nice plate full eggs
