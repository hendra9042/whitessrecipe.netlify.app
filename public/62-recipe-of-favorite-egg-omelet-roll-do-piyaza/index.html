The ingredients needed to make Egg omelet Roll Do piyaza:
  1. Make ready 2 Eggs
  2. Prepare 2 onions Julienne
  3. Make ready 1 green chilli
  4. Make ready As per taste salt
  5. Take As per taste red chilli powder
  6. Take As required green fresh corinder leaves
  7. Prepare 2 spoons milk
  8. Prepare 1 tsp oil ya butter
Instructions to make Egg omelet Roll Do piyaza:
  1. Very easy to make this recipe with 5 minutes of preparation and five minutes to cook.
  2. First to oil in pan and heat to gas and onion to saute them other side take the bowl and egg wheat with hand whisker and add salt and other spices and add milk-egg make fully with milk.
  3. Then put all material on the pan and saut them and after 2 minutes to change other side and saute them and ready to eat them
