The ingredients needed to make Egg Sandwich:
  1. Prepare 4 eggs
  2. Make ready 1 pinch of salt
  3. Take 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  4. Get 3 drops of ketchup (optional)
  5. Make ready 6 pieces of bread
  6. Take 2 drops of mayonese (optional)
Steps to make Egg Sandwich:
  1. Boil the eggs in deep water in a saucepan for 15 minutes
  2. After boiled peel off the shell and place in a bowl and mash with masher or spoon
  3. Add salt, ketchup and mayonese and again start to mash
  4. Once it turns sticky use spoon or knife to spread it in bread. Spread large quantity of mixture
  5. You can cut off the brown sides of bread. Your choice
  6. Serve with chai or tea
