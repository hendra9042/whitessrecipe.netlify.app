  1. Fried garlic + oil with low heat until crispy as shown • boiling soup with coriander root + garlic + pork bone + rock sugar +salt

  3. After 3 hrs. Boiling pork by use enough water to cover the pork until fully cooked and roasted pork in oven for color
  4. Preparation egg noodle + choy sum + mince pork + wonton (mince pork + sugar + sesame oil + soy sauce)
