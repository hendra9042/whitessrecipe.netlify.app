The ingredients needed to make Flan de queso (cheese custard):
  1. Take large eggs
  2. Take evaporated milk
  3. Get condensed milk
  4. Get cream cheese
  5. Prepare vanilla
  6. Prepare sugar cup
Instructions to make Flan de queso (cheese custard):
  1. Mix together the evaporated milk, condensed milk, eggs, table spoon of vanilla and the cream cheese.
  2. (Caramel)Add the cup of sugar in medium heat and moving until get light brown color.
  3. Add the caramel on your mold and cover completely. Add the mix on top.
  4. Cook at 350 degrees about 40 minutes.
