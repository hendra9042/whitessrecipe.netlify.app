The ingredients needed to make Scrambled Eggs:
  1. Get Large Eggs
  2. Make ready Butter
  3. Take Salt
  4. Make ready Pepper
  5. Make ready Water
  6. Take Corn Starch
Instructions to make Scrambled Eggs:
  1. Put 2 tbsp water in a skillet over medium heat
  2. Combine 1 tbsp water and about 1 tbsp corn starch in mixing bowl
  3. Crack both eggs into mixing bowl. Add desired level of salt
  4. Cut 1.5 tbsp butter into small cubes, and add to mixing bowl
  5. Whisk egg mixture until combined
  6. Immediately after the water in the pan is boiled completely off, add 1/2 tbsp to pan and spread around pan. Add egg mixture. Over the course of the next ~15-25 seconds, periodically mix/move the egg mixture around, turning it into scrambled eggs.
  7. Place scrambled eggs onto plate and pepper to taste
