The ingredients needed to make Egg roll Bowl:

Steps to make Egg roll Bowl:
  1. Chop the red onion, garlic, green onions (separating white and green parts)
  2. Cook the 1/2 cup red onions, 5 garlic cloves, and chopped white parts of the green onion in sesame oil for about 5 mins
  3. Brown the 1 lbs pork, stirring in 1 tsp ginger and 1 Tbsp Sriracha
  4. Add the coleslaw, 3 Tbsp soy sauce (or coconut aminos), 1 Tbsp rice wine vinegar, and 1 tsp pepper with salt. Cook for about 5 mins
  5. Whisk the 1/4 cup mayo and 1-2 Tbsp Sriracha.
  6. Garnish with the green, green onions
