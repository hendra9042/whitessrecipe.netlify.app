The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Egg Casserole:
  1. Prepare 8 large eggs
  2. Prepare 1 cup milk (I used skim)
  3. Get 5 piece bacon
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  5. Make ready 3 tbsp margarine (melted)
  6. Make ready 1/4 onion, chopped (optional)
  7. Take 1/2 can sliced mushrooms or several fresh mushrooms sliced (optional)
  8. Make ready salsa (optional)
  9. Make ready 1/2 green pepper (optional)

Steps to make Breakfast Egg Casserole:
  1. Preheat oven to 325. For shorter cook time, you can increase the temperature- you just need to watch it more carefully.
  2. Cook bacon and cut into small pieces.
  3. Crack eggs into medium sized bowl.
  4. Add margarine and milk. Whip them.
  5. Pour mixture into a glass baking dish. I used a rectangular one approximately 6"x 8".
  6. Add the sliced bacon and the shredded cheese. If desired, add the chopped onion, green pepper, and mushrooms at this time. (I put the cheese on last.)
  7. Bake at 325 for approximately 1 hour or until golden brown. Larger baking dishes decrease baking times. Remove from oven and cool for several minutes before serving. Serve with salsa if desired.
