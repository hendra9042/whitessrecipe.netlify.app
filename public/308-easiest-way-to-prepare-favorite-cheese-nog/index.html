Steps to make Cheese nog:
  1. Heat oil in a frying pan.Break the bottom of the egg and gently let loose the white portion of the egg on the oil.
  2. Thereafter allow the egg yolk to be placed on the white part of egg spread over the pan.
  3. As the egg remain tender place the cheese slice on the top of the egg yolk.
  4. Lower the flame.Allow the materials to get cooked stably.
  5. Now gently put the cheese nog on a plate and top up with sweetchilli sauce and pepper powder.
  6. Serve hot.
