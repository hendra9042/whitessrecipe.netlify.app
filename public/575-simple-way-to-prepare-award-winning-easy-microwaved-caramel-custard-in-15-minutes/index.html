The ingredients needed to make Easy Microwaved Caramel Custard in 15 Minutes:
  1. Get 2 tbsp ★ Sugar
  2. Make ready 1 tsp plus ★ Water
  3. Get 1 ☆ Egg
  4. Take 1 tbsp ☆ Sugar
  5. Take 120 ml ☆ Milk
Instructions to make Easy Microwaved Caramel Custard in 15 Minutes:
  1. [For the caramel] Put the ingredients marked ★ in a heatproof bowl and microwave at 600 W for 1 and 1/2 minutes. Mix and let it cool.
  2. [For the pudding] Mix the ingredients marked ☆ well and then strain. Don't let it get frothy!
  3. Pour the pudding mixture into the bowl containing the caramel. Loosely wrap, and microwave at 600 W for 2 minutes.
  4. Remove it from the microwave and pierce with a toothpick. If the toothpick comes out clean, it's done. (It's fine if it's a little runny.)
  5. Leave the plastic wrap on, and wrap it with a towel. Let it sit for 10-15 minutes.
  6. Let it chill in the fridge to finish.
