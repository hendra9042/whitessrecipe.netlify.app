Instructions to make Breakfast lasagna:
  1. Butter a 9×13 inch baking dish
  2. Layer French toast and then add maple syrup
  3. Add eggs on top of French toast
  4. And diced ham and cheddar cheese
  5. And then add another layer of French toast and scrambled eggs and cheddar cheese and diced ham and maple syrup
  6. And then layer with Hash browns on top
  7. And then bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes
