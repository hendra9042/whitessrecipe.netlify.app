The ingredients needed to make Breakfast crescent rolls:
  1. Get bacon
  2. Get Brown n serve sausage
  3. Get eggs
  4. Prepare Crescent rolls
  5. Take Pre heat oven to 375
Instructions to make Breakfast crescent rolls:
  1. Cook sausage, bacon and scramble eggs
  2. Place ingredients on Cresent rolls
  3. Roll em up and cook 10 -12 minutes or until golden brown at 375
  4. Enjoy
