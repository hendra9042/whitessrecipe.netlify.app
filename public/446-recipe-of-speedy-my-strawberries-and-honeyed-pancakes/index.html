The ingredients needed to make My Strawberries and Honeyed Pancakes ðŸ’Ÿ:
  1. Take Plain Flour
  2. Take Full Cream Milk
  3. Make ready Large Egg beaten
  4. Make ready Runny Honey
  5. Take Strawberries cut into thin slices
  6. Prepare butter

Steps to make My Strawberries and Honeyed Pancakes ðŸ’Ÿ:
  1. Slice the strawberries. In a large Bowl add the flour and Milk also the beaten egg. Stir it in until its thick but runny too.
  2. When whisking the mix its ok to drop in a little more milk if its too thick. then transfer to a jug for pouring it on the frying pan.
  3. Heat up the Butter then pour carefully the pancake mix into the middle of the pan let it cook underneath for 2-3 minutes then turn the pancake over.
  4. Turn over 1 more time then add to a servig plate and add another. Add a few strawberries on the first pancake on the serving plate. Then do the same until all pancakes are done. I got 6 stacked pancakes and Strawberries out of that amount. On the last layer add a little sieved icing sugar then add the last pancake
  5. Put the last pancake on top and sieve some more icing sugar just dust. Then add a sliced Strawberry to finish off also pour a little runny honey on top.
