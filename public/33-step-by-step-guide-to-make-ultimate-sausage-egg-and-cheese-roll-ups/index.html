The ingredients needed to make Sausage Egg And Cheese Roll Ups:
  1. Get 3 Crescent Roll Triangles
  2. Prepare 3 Sausage Links
  3. Get 1 1/2 slice Cheese (I used American)
  4. Take 1 cup Egg Whites
  5. Get Salt and Pepper
Steps to make Sausage Egg And Cheese Roll Ups:
  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Unroll crescent roll triangles onto a greased baking sheet.
  3. Top with cheese slices.
  4. Scramble egg whites over medium low heat.
  5. Add eggs to triangles.
  6. Top with sausage links (I always have some precooked turkey sausage links on hand for stuff like this.)
  7. Roll them up.
  8. Brush with egg whites.
  9. I topped them with salt and pepper.
  10. Bake in oven for 20 minutes.
  11. Serve and enjoy :)
