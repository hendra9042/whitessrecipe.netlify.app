The ingredients needed to make Cajun Egg Roll:
  1. Prepare 1 pack 8"-8" egg roll wrappers ( called rice paper or spring pastry )
  2. Get 2 cup fresh shrimp chopped ( about a pound)
  3. Get 2 strips bacon cut in small pieces
  4. Make ready 2 cup shredded cabbage
  5. Get 1 large parboiled mirleton ( you can leave this out and add one more cup of cabbage or bean sprouts
  6. Get 1 cup bean sprouts
  7. Make ready 1/2 medium chopped onion
  8. Take 1 stick celery chopped fine
  9. Prepare 5 chopped green onion leaves
  10. Get 1 handful or fresh chopped parsley ( 1tbs. dried )
  11. Get 3 tsp minced garlic
  12. Get 1 A few drops of liquid crab boil
  13. Take 1 salt, pepper, creole seasoning, hot sauce and whateverelse
  14. Take 2 tsp soy sauce
  15. Get 1 egg beaten ( pastry glue )
Steps to make Cajun Egg Roll:
  1. Get the rice paper to use
  2. Cajun egg rolls render the bacon on a medium fire in a large skillet.
  3. Add onion and celery and cook until wilted.
  4. Tire fire up to Med - high and add shrimp and green onions and sprinkle some creole seasoning on them.
  5. Stir - fry until shrimp turn pink, remove from fire and put this in a large bowl.
  6. Cajun egg rolls in the same skillet add a tabs. of cooking oil and on a medium fire stir - fry the cabbage , mirleton and bean sprouts until the cabbage starts to wilt.
  7. sprinkle a little Creole seasoning and add parsley.
  8. stir a time or two and add this to the shrimp mixture .
  9. Add soy sauce and mix everything up well.
  10. Let this sit for about 15 minutes stirring a few times, taste and seasoning to your liking.
  11. Let this cool for a little while;hot mixture on rice paper is tough to deal with.
  12. Paint the wrapper with a little egg on the parts shown by the white line
  13. Fold the bottom edge up then gently tighten it up by sliding the top pastry flap toward you.
  14. That's why you put the egg mixture in the center, so it will slide easy.
  15. Fold the left and right corners over and roll forward.
  16. Fry them in 360°F oil until golden brown.
