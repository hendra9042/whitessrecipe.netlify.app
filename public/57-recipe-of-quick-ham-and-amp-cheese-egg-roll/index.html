The ingredients needed to make Ham & Cheese Egg Roll 🥚🧀:
  1. Make ready 2 large eggs
  2. Take 60 ml fresh milk
  3. Get 1 Tbs chives
  4. Take 1 pinch salt
  5. Make ready Black pepper to taste
  6. Prepare 1/2 Tbs butter
  7. Get 1 Tbs shredded cheddar cheese
  8. Make ready 2 slices ham

Instructions to make Ham & Cheese Egg Roll 🥚🧀:
  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk the eggs, milk, and chives. Season with salt and pepper. Mix well.
  2. In a pan over medium heat, melt butter. Pour half of the egg mixture into the pan, moving to create a thin layer that covers the entire pan
  3. When the egg is almost cooked, add the shredded cheddar cheese in the centre of the pan, followed by the slices of ham
  4. Lower the heat and use 2 spatula to roll up the egg into an egg roll. Serve.
