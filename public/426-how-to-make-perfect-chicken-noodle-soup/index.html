Instructions to make Chicken Noodle Soup:
  1. Fill stock pot half way. Roughly 2-3 quarts. Bring to a boil add chicken diced, bay leaves, and butter. Boil for 45 minutes.
  2. Add sliced carrots, diced onion, sliced celery, and all spices and garlic. Boil for 30 minutes.
  3. Add frozen egg noodles or noodles and boild for another 20 minutes. Reduce to medium heat and let simmer until noodles are cooked.
