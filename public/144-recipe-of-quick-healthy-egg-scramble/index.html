The ingredients needed to make Healthy Egg Scramble:
  1. Make ready baby spinach
  2. Make ready sweet peppers
  3. Take chopped green onion (as much as you like)
  4. Prepare sprinkle of salt and pepper
  5. Prepare sprinkle of mrs dash onion and herb seasoning
  6. Prepare sprinkle of shredded pepper jack cheese
  7. Prepare chopped up chicken lunch meat (i used rotisserie flavor from Oscar Meyer)
  8. Get smidge :) of butter
  9. Prepare large eggs
Instructions to make Healthy Egg Scramble:
  1. Heat nonstick skillet at a setting of 5 on the stove.
  2. Add your smidge of butter and evenly coat pan with it
  3. Add chopped sweet peppers and green onions and let simmer in the pan for 2 minutes or so, stirring occasionally
  4. Add chicken to the skillet and stir
  5. Add spinach and Mrs Dash seasoning and stir. Let the spinach cook down till it's all wilted (approx. 2 minutes while stirring occasionally)
  6. Crack your eggs in a bowl and add your salt and pepper to them. Beat them until they are fully incorporated
  7. Pour eggs over veggie mix in skillet
  8. Scramble them up!
  9. Turn burner off and toss cheese in.
  10. Put on a plate and enjoy! Great with honey wheat toast
