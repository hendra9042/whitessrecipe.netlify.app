The ingredients needed to make Spinach and Feta Egg White Frittata:
  1. Prepare 16 ounces egg white
  2. Take 1/4 cup Feta Cheese
  3. Prepare Salt and Pepper to taste
  4. Take 1 Cup Spinach (Chopped)
  5. Get Calories
  6. Take Spinach 10 Calories
  7. Make ready Egg Whites 250 Calories
  8. Take Feta Cheese 70 Calories
  9. Make ready 330 Total Calories
  10. Make ready Servings 5, Calories per serving 66
Steps to make Spinach and Feta Egg White Frittata:
  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees and grease a cake dish with olive oil
  2. Pour egg whites, spinach, salt, and pepper into the pan and stir lightly with a fork
  3. Put egg mixture on a sheet pan (lined with parchment paper) and cook 30 to 35 minutes until egg whites are fluffy and firm.
