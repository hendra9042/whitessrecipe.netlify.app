The ingredients needed to make Vickys Cornbread, GF DF EF SF NF:
  1. Take cornmeal (1 cup)
  2. Get rice flour (1/2 cup)
  3. Prepare cornstarch / cornflour (1/2 cup)
  4. Get baking powder
  5. Make ready xanthan gum
  6. Get salt
  7. Make ready sugar (optional but I think it makes a smoother cornbread)
  8. Take light coconut milk or milk of choice
  9. Take vegetable oil
  10. Make ready ground chia or flax seeds mixed with 6 tablespoons hot water (or 2 eggs)
Instructions to make Vickys Cornbread, GF DF EF SF NF:
  1. Let your chia seed/water mixture stand to become gelatinous while you put the oven on
  2. Preheat the oven to gas 6 / 200C / 400Â°F and grease an 8" square baking dish or cake tin. Place the tin in the oven as it heats while you mix the batter
  3. Meanwhile whisk the dry ingredients together in a mixing bowl then stir in the milk, oil and chia mixture (or 2 eggs) until the batter is completely smooth. Let stand for 10 minutes
  4. Remove the hot tin from the oven and pour the batter into it
  5. Put the tin back in the oven and bake for 20 - 25 minutes or until golden brown on top
  6. Cut into squares and serve hot from the oven either on its own or with a drizzle of honey or maple syrup. Goes great with a Southern stew or barbeque!
