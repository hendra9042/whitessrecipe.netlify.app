Instructions to make Deviled eggs and broccoli:
  1. Boil the eggs 10 minutes, and peel let cool
  2. Steam the broccoli add the salt
  3. Slice the eggs in half
  4. Carefully remove the yokes to a bowl
  5. Add mayonnaise, relish, chopped olives, and paprika
  6. Add salt and paprika to the boiled egg whites.
  7. Add the yoke mixture to the egg whites. Add whole olive to top of deviled eggs. Arrange the eggs atop the broccoli
  8. Serve I hope you enjoy!
