The ingredients needed to make Banana Cake:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup butter
  2. Get 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  3. Make ready 2 cups sugar or equivalent sugar substitute, such as splenda
  4. Prepare 4 eggs or equivalent of egg substitute
  5. Make ready 3 cups all purpose flour or gluten free flour mixture
  6. Get 2 1/2 tsp baking soda
  7. Get 1 1/2 tsp ground cloves
  8. Prepare 2 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  9. Get 1 cup chopped walnuts
  10. Get 6 ripe bananas
  11. Get 1 canister cream cheese frosting
Steps to make Banana Cake:
  1. Cream butter & oil together. Add sugar & eggs. Mix well. Add flour, baking soda, cloves, & cinnamon. Mix well. Add walnuts. Mix well. Mash bananas well & add to mixture. Mix well.
  2. Pour into greased 13x9 baking pan. Bake at 375 degrees for 1 hour 15 minutes.
  3. Spread cooled cake with Cream cheese frosting. Serve.
