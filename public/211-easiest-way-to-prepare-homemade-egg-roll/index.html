Instructions to make Egg roll:
  1. Boil your egg and potatoes
  2. Mash your potatoes and chop your garlic, onions and rodo
  3. Mix your mashed potato, chopped veggies and spices mix well
  4. Roll your potatoes to cover the egg
  5. Dip in egg and cover it with bread crumbs
  6. Fry to golden brown and enjoy
