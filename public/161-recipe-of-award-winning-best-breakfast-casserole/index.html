The ingredients needed to make Best Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Get sour cream
  2. Make ready butter, melted
  3. Prepare eggs
  4. Get sausage, browned
  5. Prepare cheese
  6. Take self rising flour
Steps to make Best Breakfast Casserole:
  1. preheat oven to 350.
  2. mix melted butter, sour cream & flour together in a bowl.
  3. grease cast iron skillet and pour in batter.
  4. beat eggs and pour on top of the dough. crumble browned sausage over eggs and sprinkle half the cheese.
  5. bake for 20 minutes. add remaining cheese and cook for 5 to 10 more minutes.
  6. you can also add other ingredients such as bacon, mushrooms, peppers, etc.
