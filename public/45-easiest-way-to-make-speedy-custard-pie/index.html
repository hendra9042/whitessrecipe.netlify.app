The ingredients needed to make custard pie:
  1. Make ready 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
  2. Prepare 3 eggs
  3. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla
  4. Get 1 cup sugar
  5. Prepare 1 can fat free evaporated milk
  6. Take 2 tbsp melted butter
  7. Get 3 tbsp self rising flour
