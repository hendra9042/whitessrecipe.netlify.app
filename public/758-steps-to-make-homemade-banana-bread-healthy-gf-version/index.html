The ingredients needed to make Banana bread (Healthy GF version):
  1. Prepare gluten free plain flour
  2. Make ready buckwheat flour
  3. Get flaxseed/linseed ground mix
  4. Take butter or coconut oil (melted)
  5. Prepare coconut sugar
  6. Take (optional) cocoa powder
  7. Prepare vanilla essence
  8. Make ready cinnamon powder
  9. Prepare honey
  10. Take large or 4 small ripe bananas
  11. Take egg
  12. Get baking powder
  13. Get bicarbonate soda
  14. Prepare milk (or almond milk)

I like to make both ahead of time for quick breakfasts and snacks for my family. How to Make this Healthy Banana Bread Recipe. This whole wheat banana bread is made in one bowl, except for mashing the banana. This is not your average banana bread (which is usually full of sugar and butter).

Instructions to make Banana bread (Healthy GF version):
  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees and prepare a loaf or cake tin (grease with butter or coconut oil)
  2. Mix all the ingredients in a bowl until fully combined. I use a blender/food processor. Pour into the tin. I drizzle a small amount of honey on the top of the cake before putting into the oven.
  3. Bake for 50mins.
  4. Remove from oven and let cool. Eat while still warm. Enjoy!
