The ingredients needed to make Trio Eggs w/Spinach in Soup:
  1. Make ready century egg
  2. Make ready salted egg
  3. Prepare egg
  4. Take Goji berries
  5. Get Garlic and ginger,
  6. Make ready spinach
  7. Make ready Salt
  8. Prepare White pepper
Steps to make Trio Eggs w/Spinach in Soup:
  1. Wash spinach and soak in salt water for 30 mins. Then wash again, separate the stalk and leaves. Next is boil the century and salted egg in 10 mins. When it is already cool down, slice them.
  2. Add water in a pot. Let it boil add ginger and garlic boil a while. Then add salted egg and century egg then the spinach stalk and Goji berries. Let it cook a while then add spinach leaves. When halfway cook beat the egg and slowly drop in. Season with salt and pepper.
