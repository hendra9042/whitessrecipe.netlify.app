The ingredients needed to make Peanut butter Banana Breakfast Cookies:
  1. Prepare peanut butter
  2. Make ready apple sauce
  3. Get Brown sugar
  4. Prepare mashed ripe bananas
  5. Prepare egg
  6. Make ready vanilla
  7. Take baking soda
  8. Get salt
  9. Make ready flour
  10. Prepare ground flax seed
  11. Make ready wheat bran
  12. Get wheat germ
  13. Take quick oats
  14. Prepare dried currants
  15. Get raisins
  16. Make ready chocolate chips
Steps to make Peanut butter Banana Breakfast Cookies:
  1. preheat oven to 350°F
  2. mix peanut butter & applesauce
  3. add brown sugar
  4. add egg & vanilla
  5. beat until smooth
  6. stir in bananas, baking soda & salt
  7. add flour, bran, flax, & wheat germ
  8. mix well then add oatmeal, currants, raisins, & chocolate chocolate chips
  9. Drop spoonfuls onto an ungreased cookie sheet
  10. bake 15 minutes or until edges have browned.
  11. cool on wire racks
