The ingredients needed to make Casserole omelet:
  1. Prepare eggs
  2. Take paprika
  3. Make ready salt
  4. Take heavy cream
  5. Prepare sliced red onions
  6. Prepare sliced red bell peppers
  7. Take sliced sausage
  8. Prepare olive oil
Instructions to make Casserole omelet:
  1. Set onions,Peppers,Sausages and oil in your casserole bowl
  2. In another small bowl mix eggs,heavy cream,salt,paprika
  3. Pour the egg mixture in casserole bowl and put into the over at °350
  4. Watch as it rises… Take out when u see that nice golden color … Or top of the eggs is cooked
