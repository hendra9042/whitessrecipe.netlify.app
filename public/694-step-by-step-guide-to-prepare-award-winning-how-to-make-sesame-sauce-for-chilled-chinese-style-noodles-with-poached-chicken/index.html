The ingredients needed to make How to Make Sesame Sauce for Chilled Chinese-style Noodles with Poached Chicken:
  1. Make ready For chilled Chinese-style noodles (reference)
  2. Prepare 2 portions Chinese noodles
  3. Make ready 1 Poached chicken (use any cuts)
  4. Take 1/2 Cucumber
  5. Make ready 1 Tomato
  6. Make ready 1 Egg
  7. Take 1 as required Doubanjiang or Ra-yu
  8. Take For the sesame sauce
  9. Prepare 3 tbsp Vinegar
  10. Prepare 3 tbsp Soy sauce
  11. Take 3 tbsp Sugar
  12. Get 1/2 tbsp Chinese chicken stock granules
  13. Get 1/2 tsp Juice from grated ginger (optional)
  14. Make ready 1/2 tbsp Miso
  15. Prepare 2 tbsp Zhi ma jiang (sesame paste or peanut butter)
  16. Take 2 tbsp Ground sesame seeds
  17. Make ready 3 tbsp Boiling water
  18. Make ready 120 ml Mineral water (tap water)
  19. Prepare 1/2 tbsp Sesame oil
Steps to make How to Make Sesame Sauce for Chilled Chinese-style Noodles with Poached Chicken:
  1. [For the sesame sauce] Dissolve the Chinese chicken stock granules in boiling water. Add the zhi ma jiang (sesame paste) to the stock mixture while it is still hot and mix well.
  2. Add all the remaining seasonings and mix well. Adjust the amount of the sugar and vinegar to suit your taste. Adjust the consistency of the sauce with water. Chill in the fridge until required.
  3. [For the chilled Chinese-style noodles] Poach the chicken until just cooked thoroughly and still very moist and chill. - - https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/153769-how-to-cook-moist-chicken-breast-for-boiled-chicken
  4. The chicken is difficult to slice when warm, so chilled it completely, and then slice or shred.
  5. Bring the eggs to room temperature. Boil enough water to completely submerge the eggs. Boil the eggs for 7 minutes for very soft egg yolks, 8 minutes for soft egg yolks and 12 minutes for hard boiled eggs.
  6. Julienne the cucumber. Cut the tomato in half and then slice thinly. Slice the boiled eggs.
  7. Cook the Chinese-style noodles to your desired firmness. After cooking, rinse in cold water and drain. Transfer the noodles and vegetables onto serving dishes and pour over plenty of sesame sauce.
