The ingredients needed to make Egg roll:
  1. Make ready 1 bowl Flour
  2. Get as needed Oil
  3. Prepare to taste Salt
  4. Make ready 2 Egg
  5. Prepare 1 Carrot
  6. Make ready 1 Cucumber
  7. Prepare 1 Onion
  8. Prepare 2 pinch Black peper
  9. Prepare As needed Tomato ketchup
  10. Prepare to taste Red chilli Powder
Steps to make Egg roll:
  1. Atfirst flour knead into dough. Than flour make a shape with rolling pin &bord
  2. Than fry it with egg & keep it. Into a plate & grated carrot,onion, cucumber,black peper,tomato ketchup pour on it. Fold it with paper
  3. Ready to eat.
