The ingredients needed to make Apple Blueberry Custard Pie Crumble:
  1. Get Dough
  2. Take 2 cup flour
  3. Make ready 1 1/2 stick margarine
  4. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  5. Take 3 tbsp ice water
  6. Take Filling
  7. Prepare 1/2 3/4 can Blueberry Pie Filling
  8. Make ready 3 Cortland apples (peeled, cored and thinly sliced)
  9. Make ready 3/4 cup sugar
  10. Make ready 1 tbsp cinnamon
  11. Prepare 1 Custard (store bought or homemade)
  12. Get Crumbs
  13. Get 3 cup flour
  14. Take 1/2 tbsp baking powder
  15. Take 1 cup sugar
  16. Make ready 3/4 cup oil
  17. Get 1 egg
  18. Take 1 tbsp vanilla sugar
Steps to make Apple Blueberry Custard Pie Crumble:
  1. For the dough: Knead ingredients quickly to form a soft dough. (Do not overwork dough) Press into bottom of a 9x13 pan.
  2. For the filling: Spread a thin layer of custard ontop of dough.
  3. Mix sugar and cinnamon and sprinkle over sliced apples.
  4. Layer apples over custard. Spoon dollops of blueberry pie filling ontop of apples, spreading it as evenly as possible.
  5. For the Crumbs: Combine all ingredients by hand until crumbs form. Sprinkle ontop of blueberry pie filling.
  6. Bake pie for 45 minutes to an hour or until golden.
  7. You will have extra crumbs- use it in a 9" round or muffin tins to spread the bottom of the pan. Then fill with remaining sliced apples and blueberry filling and top with additional crumbs. Bake 30-35 minutes.
