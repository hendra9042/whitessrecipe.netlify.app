Steps to make Spaghetti egg stew:
  1. Prepare all the ingredients you will use
  2. In a sufuria cook onions until golden brown
  3. Then add crushed garlic and ginger stir let the mixture cook
  4. Add tomatoes,capsicum,carrots
  5. Add tomato paste
  6. Add green bananas and let them cook for a while
  7. Add spices
  8. Then add the cooked sausages, boiled and sweet potatoes
  9. Taste to ensure all spices are well incorporated
  10. Add some water or vegetable stock
  11. Let it boil in low heat for ten minutes
  12. Then serve with cheese spaghetti
