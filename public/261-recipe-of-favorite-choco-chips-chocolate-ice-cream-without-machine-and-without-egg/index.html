Instructions to make Choco-chips chocolate ice cream without machine and without egg:
  1. Microwave the chocolate for 1 minute to melt the chocolate.
  2. To the molten chocolate add, condensed milk and cocoa powder and mix properly.
  3. Take a bowl which will be used to whip the cream and the whisk/ electric beater blades and keep them in freezer for 15 minutes. This helps in whipping the cream.
  4. Take the whipping cream in a bowl and whip till it forms stiff peaks.
  5. Fold the whipped cream with the chocolate mix and whip again to just mix properly. Do not over whip.
  6. Add the chocolate chips and mix.
