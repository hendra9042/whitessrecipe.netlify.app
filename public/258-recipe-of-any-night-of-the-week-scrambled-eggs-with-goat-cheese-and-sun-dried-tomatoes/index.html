Steps to make Scrambled eggs with goat cheese and sun dried tomatoes:
  1. Heat butter in pan over medium heat until foamy.
  2. Whisk 3 eggs and add them to the pan on low heat.
  3. Add 2 tablespoons of goat cheese when eggs are 50% cooked.
  4. Add 1 tablespoon of chopped chives and mix.
  5. Add sun dried tomatoes and mix.
  6. Add ground black pepper and salt (if needed) to taste.
