The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Prepare eggs
  2. Get large potato, grated
  3. Take fav. cheese
  4. Take handful spinach
  5. Take italian sausage
  6. Take onion, minced
  7. Prepare milk or heavy cream
Steps to make Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Preheat oven to 375
  2. Brown sausage. Once almost done add onion and cook until done
  3. While sausage is cooking mix eggs,spinach,half cheese and milk until incorporated
  4. Add sausage to eggs and mix. Then press excess water out of potatoes and add to eggs and mix
  5. Put in greased baking dish add remainder of cheese to the top and bake for 40-45 min
  6. Enjoy
