The ingredients needed to make Custard Rice Pudding:
  1. Make ready eggs
  2. Make ready sugar
  3. Prepare vanillia
  4. Prepare salt
  5. Prepare milk
  6. Take cooked minute rice (2 cups dry)
  7. Get water (for rice)
  8. Make ready raisins
Instructions to make Custard Rice Pudding:
  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees. Place a 9x13 pan that is half full of water under the rack you will cook the rice. Let the pan heat with the oven.
  2. Follow the minute rice directions and prepare rice in the microwave.
  3. In a dish, add eggs, sugar and salt. Beat slightly to mix.
  4. Stir in milk, and add vanillia.
  5. Pour the mixture into a 3 qt baking dish, and add the cooked rice and 1 cup of raisins and stir gently.
  6. Gently sprinkle nutmeg on top to taste.
  7. Place in the oven over the pan of water. Cook at 350 degrees for 50-60 minutes. When finished it should look gelatinous.
