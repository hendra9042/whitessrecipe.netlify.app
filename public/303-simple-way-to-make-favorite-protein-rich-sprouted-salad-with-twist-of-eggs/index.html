The ingredients needed to make Protein Rich Sprouted Salad with TWIST of Eggs:
  1. Prepare whole moong dal
  2. Prepare chick pea(chana)
  3. Take Rajma
  4. Get Lobhiya
  5. Make ready chhole
  6. Prepare capsicum
  7. Make ready mushroom
  8. Prepare Tomatoes
  9. Get hard boiled eggs
  10. Take Beetroot
  11. Make ready Rock Salt (sendha namak)
  12. Get Blackpepper
  13. Get lemon
Instructions to make Protein Rich Sprouted Salad with TWIST of Eggs:
  1. First, soaked all the legumes/beans overnight. Next day drain off the water and tie up all the legumes in wet muslin cloth for sprouting.
  2. Now give 2-3 whistles to all the legumes/beans by adding 1 cup water with 1/2 tsp salt.
  3. During boiling process cut all the veggies according to you and also you can add more veggies what would you like (it's totally optional).
  4. On other side also boil the 2 eggs properly(hard boiled eggs).
  5. Now sprinkle salt, Blackpepper and lemon according to your taste.
  6. A healthy protein rich sprouted salad with twist of eggs is ready to eat.
