The ingredients needed to make Egg Cream:
  1. Prepare 3 tbsp chocolate syrup (Fox's U-Bet chocolate flavor syrup is recommended if you can find it, but I couldn't so I used Hershey)
  2. Make ready 1/4 cup milk or half and half
  3. Make ready 1 Ice cold club soda or seltzer water
Instructions to make Egg Cream:
  1. Pour chocolate syrup and milk or half and half into a 16 ounce glass.
  2. While beating vigorously with a fork, slowly add club soda or seltzer water until the glass is almost full.
  3. Add a straw and serve very cold.
