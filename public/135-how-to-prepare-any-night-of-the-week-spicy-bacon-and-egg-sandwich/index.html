How to Prepare Any-night-of-the-week Spicy bacon and egg sandwich :-)

Nellie Cobb

Spicy bacon and egg sandwich :-) is one of the most popular of current trending foods in the world. It’s enjoyed by millions daily. It’s easy, it’s fast, it tastes delicious. Spicy bacon and egg sandwich :-) is something that I have loved my whole life. They’re fine and they look wonderful.

To begin with this particular recipe, we must prepare a few ingredients. You can cook spicy bacon and egg sandwich :-) using 5 ingredients and 6 steps. Here is how you cook that.

The ingredients needed to make Spicy bacon and egg sandwich :-):
  1. Make ready Thick cut bacon
  2. Get Egg
  3. Prepare Pickled Jalapeno
  4. Make ready American Cheese
  5. Prepare Toast
Steps to make Spicy bacon and egg sandwich :-):
  1. Cook bacon, I like to bake on foil covered cookie sheet at 375 until crisp.
  2. Toast bread in toaster oven (roasting prevents mushy bread from yolk)
  3. Cook egg, for this sandwich over easy is the most requested in our house.
  4. Take the seeds out of jalapeno while egg is cooking and cut into strips. Skip this if you don't like spicy.
  5. On one side of toast place cheese. On the other side place the egg, breaking the yolk and soaking bread. Top with bacon, jalapeno slices, and other piece of toast.
  6. I pack this sandwich for the hubby to eat on the way to work. He asks for it nearly every morning, sometimes sub sausage for bacon, and hoagie bread for toast. Variations here are only limited to your imagination! Enjoy!!
