Instructions to make Plum cake glutten free:
  1. Add butter @ room temperature, sugar, eggs @ room temperature, vanilla, yogurt in bowl and mix together..
  2. Add almond meal, gf flour, baking powder, flax seeds, and cinammon and add to wet ingredients.
  3. Butter pie bowl, put a layer of plums on bottom, close together.
  4. preheat oven to 375°F
  5. spread half of pie mixture over plums, add another layer of plums and cover with remaining mixture.
  6. place in oven for about 35 minutes, until knife comes out clean.
  7. Let cool.
  8. Serve with yogurt
