The ingredients needed to make Egg roll:
  1. Get 1 cup wheat flour
  2. Get 1/2 cup maida/All-purpose flour
  3. Get 6 eggs
  4. Take 1 big size onion
  5. Get 1 big size capsicum
  6. Take to taste Salt
  7. Take 1 bowl Grated Cheese
  8. Get 1 cup refined oil
  9. Take 1 tsp Red chilli powder
  10. Make ready 1 tbsp chopped ginger
  11. Get 1 tbsp chopped garlic
  12. Prepare 1 small size cabbage
  13. Make ready As needed Water
  14. Get 5 spoon tomato ketchup
  15. Take 3 spoon green chilli sauce

Steps to make Egg roll:
  1. In a bowl mix wheat flour maida salt and oil using water make a soft dough keep it aside
  2. Clean and chopped the vegetables saute it in a pan on gas on medium flame in hot oil adding greenchilli ginger garlic and let it cool
  3. Make 6 round balls from the dough give it round shape with rolling pin cook it on a hot twa from both side using oil on it on medium flame make all the 6 parathas one by one
  4. In a bowl crack one egg and mix salt and 1pinch red chilli powder Mix it well pour in a pan 1/2spoon oil on medium flame make omelette in hot oil flip it and cook it from both side one by one make 6 omelettes
  5. In one bowl mix ketchup and sauce spread it on the parathas with a spoon then put the 1omelette on one paratha then in the middle put the saute vegetable and spread the grated cheese and make a roll one by one make all the 6 rolls
  6. Your hot egg roll is ready to eat enjoy
