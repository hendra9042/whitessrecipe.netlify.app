Steps to make Vegetarian Meatloaf Sandwich:
  1. Preheat oven to 375F.
  2. In a food processor combine beef, onions, barley, eggs, panko, salt mustard, barbeque sauce, garlic, pepper, and seasoning rub together.
  3. Transfer to oiled baking dish and bake for approximately 35 mins. Brush top with barbeque sauce and return to oven. Bake until sauce is caramelized and meatloaf is cooked. Slice into desired shape.
  4. Assemble flatbread with lettuce, meatloaf, cheese, and onions.
