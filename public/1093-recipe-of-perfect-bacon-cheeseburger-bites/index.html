The ingredients needed to make Bacon Cheeseburger Bites:
  1. Take lbs. hamburger
  2. Prepare egg whites
  3. Take shredded cheddar cheese
  4. Take grated parmesan cheese
  5. Make ready bread crumbs
  6. Take ketchup
  7. Make ready mustard
  8. Prepare relish
  9. Get onion, diced
  10. Get Worcestershire sauce
  11. Make ready Seasonings to taste: garlic powder, pepper & parsley
  12. Get For topping: 1/2 pound bacon, diced & 1 cup cheddar cheese
Steps to make Bacon Cheeseburger Bites:
  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Combine all ingredients (except toppings) and mix well.
  2. Pat down equally divided among 36 muffin cups. Make sure to spray muffin cups with cooking spray before placing meat mixture in. Bake in oven for 25 minutes.
  3. While bites are cooking, cook your diced bacon and drain on paper towel lined plate.
  4. Top cooked bites equally with cheddar cheese. Place back in oven for 5 minutes to melt cheese.
  5. Enjoy topped with bacon and ketchup and mustard!
