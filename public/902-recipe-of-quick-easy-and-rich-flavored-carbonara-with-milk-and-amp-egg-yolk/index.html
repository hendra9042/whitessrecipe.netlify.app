Instructions to make Easy and Rich-Flavored Carbonara with Milk & Egg Yolk:
  1. Cook the pasta with salt according to the instructions on the package. Use plenty of salt!
  2. Cut the bacon into bite-size strips, and stir-fry in a pan.
  3. After stir-frying the bacon, pour in the milk, and cook for a little while over medium heat.
  4. Tear the sliced cheese into thin strips, and add to the pan. Add the rest of the 〇 ingredients.
  5. When the cheese starts to melt, add the freshly cooked pasta, and mix.
  6. When mixed well, turn off the heat. Pour in the egg yolks, and mix quickly!
  7. Sprinkle with black pepper, and serve onto a plate, then done.
