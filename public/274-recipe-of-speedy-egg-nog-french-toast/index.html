The ingredients needed to make Egg Nog French Toast:
  1. Prepare 1 Egg whisked
  2. Get 1/4 cup Eggnog
  3. Make ready 2 tsp Ground cinnamon
  4. Prepare 1 loaf Bread (any kind)
Steps to make Egg Nog French Toast:
  1. Whisk one egg and some eggnog into a small bowl
  2. Heat a skillet and put a small piece of butter so the bread won't stick
  3. Soak the bread in the mixture of eggnog and one egg
  4. Add cinnamon to each side of bread before flipping
  5. Add syrup if you'd like, and enjoy!
