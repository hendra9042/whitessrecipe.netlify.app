The ingredients needed to make One Mean Egg Sandwich:
  1. Get olive oil
  2. Take Egg
  3. Prepare roma tomato
  4. Make ready jalepeño
  5. Prepare onion powder
  6. Make ready parsley flakes
  7. Get sea salt
  8. Get ground white pepper
  9. Make ready all natural provalone cheese
  10. Make ready all natural,nitrate free, smoked deli ham
  11. Prepare whole wheat bread,toasted
Steps to make One Mean Egg Sandwich:
  1. Slice tomato and jalepeño to desired sizes.
  2. Heat olive oil to a sizzle in a skillet.
  3. Add egg.
  4. Add sliced tomato and jalepeño on top of egg.
  5. Add all spices and seasonings to the egg while the top layer is still runny. (Up the ante with crushed red pepper,or more jalepeño for more heat)
  6. While top layer of egg is still runny,add slice of provolone cheese and slice of ham on top of that.
  7. Let the cheese melt and hold it all together,flipping occasionally until egg is cooked to desired level. (I like a slightly runny yoke,but this is optional.)
  8. Slide the whole thing between two slices of whole wheat toast.
