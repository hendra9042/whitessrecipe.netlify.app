The ingredients needed to make Tasty Boneless chicken wings:
  1. Make ready boneless and skinless chicken breast
  2. Prepare flour
  3. Prepare eggs scrambled
  4. Prepare buttermilk
  5. Get vegetable oil
  6. Get Your favorite wing sauce (Buffalo, honey mustard, teriyaki, bbq, etc)
  7. Make ready salt and pepper each
Steps to make Tasty Boneless chicken wings:
  1. Cut chicken into 1 inch pieces. Add about a teaspoon of salt and pepper to flavor meat.add buttermilk and let it sit in the fridge for about an hour.
  2. Get your dredging station ready… flour in a bowl and egg/water mix in another. Lightly salt the eggs. You want flavor throughout all the layers, not just on the outside with the sauce.
  3. Dip chicken on at a time into egg mix, then into flour. Add back into egg mix and back into flour (flour being the final dip)
  4. Place coated pieces on a plate or baking sheet while you work on the rest.
  5. In a deep skillet, add just enough oil to cover a quarter inch of the pan. Heat to just above medium heat. Let the oil get thoroughly heated before dropping in chicken to ensure a crispy exterior.
  6. Drop chicken in one at a time. Let cook on 1 side for about 3 to 4 min until golden brown. Flip for another 2 to 3 min. Don't overcrowd the chicken, to ensure a crispy exterior
  7. Place on plate with paper towel or on a cooling rack while cooking in batches.
  8. When finished toss with about a cup of your favorite wing sauce to gently coat. You can serve more sauce on the side if desired. Also, heat up your sauce if you'd like so cold sauce doesn't cool down the temp of the chicken.
