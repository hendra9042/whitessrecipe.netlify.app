The ingredients needed to make Chicken pot pie (for two pies):
  1. Take 3-4 cups cooked chicken chopped
  2. Take 3 stalks celery chopped
  3. Make ready 1 onion chopped
  4. Take 2 small potatoes chopped small
  5. Prepare 3 tablespoons butter
  6. Get 2 tbls flour
  7. Take 2 cups chicken broth
  8. Get 1 frozen bag mixed veggies- steamed and drain water off!
  9. Take 1 can healthy request cream of chicken
  10. Make ready Salt, pepper, parsley, adobo
  11. Take 2 boxes pie crusts (2 per box)
  12. Make ready 1 egg scrambled for egg wash on crust

Instructions to make Chicken pot pie (for two pies):
  1. In butter sauté celery, onions, and potatoes on low (add some olive oil if it sticks too much)
  2. Add flour once translucent, and mix. Add seasoning and broth. Bring to simmer to thicken.
  3. Add drained veggies, chicken, and cream of chicken. Mix well and place in pie crusts and then top with pie crust. Press and seal edges and slit on top to steam.
  4. Optional- add egg wash on top of crust before baking. Bake at 375 for 50 min or until top is golden brown
