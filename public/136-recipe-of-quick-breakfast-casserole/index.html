The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Make ready sausage
  2. Prepare eggs
  3. Prepare milk
  4. Take bread
  5. Get cheddar cheese
Steps to make Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Preheat oven at 375
  2. Beat eggs, add milk and bead
  3. Pour into casserole dish
  4. Cook sausage and spread over egg/milk mixture
  5. Sprinkle with cheese
  6. Refrigerate overnight
  7. Bake for 30 - 35 minutes
