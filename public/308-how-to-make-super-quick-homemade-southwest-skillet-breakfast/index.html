The ingredients needed to make Southwest skillet breakfast üç≥:
  1. Get 1 cup frozen hash browns
  2. Take 1/4 cup diced purple onion
  3. Get 2 diced portobello mushrooms
  4. Get 1/4 cup diced green pepper
  5. Make ready 1/2 of a jalepeno FINELY diced
  6. Get 1 cup diced cheddar smoked sausage or you could use ground Chorizo
  7. Take 2 eggs
  8. Make ready 1/4 cup shredded Mexican cheese
  9. Make ready 1 corn tortilla cut into thin strips
  10. Make ready To taste Cajun seasoning, garlic powder, pepper, sazon, adobo
Steps to make Southwest skillet breakfast üç≥:
  1. Heat oil to 375 either in a deep fryer or put a half inch of oil in a shallow fry pan and heat to 375.
  2. Fry tortilla strips until crispy and place them on a paper towel. Fry both eggs to desired doneness in the same oil.
  3. In a pan mix the potatoes, onion, peppers and spices and cook them for 5 minutes on medium heat.
  4. Remove and place in a bowl and top with eggs and tortillas strips and cheese.
  5. You can serve with salsa and tortillas if you like.
  6. Mix in mushrooms and your smoked sausage. Cook another 5-7 minutes until hash brown potatoes are crispy.
