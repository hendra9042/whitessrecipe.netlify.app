Steps to make English muffin & sausage casserole:
  1. Toast all of the english muffins then cut into bite size pieces and set aside
  2. Brown and crumble ground sausage in a skillet till done
  3. Beat eggs with salt, pepper and milk then mix in pimentos
  4. Mix 1/2 cup softened butter with maple syrup then melt in the microwave till fully melted
  5. In a 13 × 9 inch baking dish spray with cooking oil
  6. Add half of the muffin pieces, then use a spoon too drizzle on about 1/4 cup of butter mixture on the english muffins pieces, add half of the sausage and pour on half of the egg mixture, then sprinkle on 1 cup of cheese. Repeat layer.
  7. Cover and refrigerate for a few hours or overnight
  8. Remove from refrigerator while oven is pre heating to 375 degrees
  9. Bake for about 35 minutes
  10. Let cool for a few minutes then enjoy
