The ingredients needed to make Simple Egg salad:
  1. Get Hard boiled eggs,
  2. Make ready Mct Mayonnaise,
  3. Take Vinegar,
  4. Get Mustard,
  5. Take Salt
  6. Make ready Black pepper
  7. Make ready Garlic powder
Steps to make Simple Egg salad:
  1. Mash eggs with fork.
  2. Add all other ingredients and keep mashing the eggs until a chunky paste is formed.
  3. Place in sealed container in fridge. Lasts for around 5 days or more
