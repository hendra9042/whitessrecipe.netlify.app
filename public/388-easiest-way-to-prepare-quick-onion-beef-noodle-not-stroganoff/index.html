The ingredients needed to make Onion Beef Noodle (Not Stroganoff):
  1. Get Cooking Oil
  2. Prepare onion chopped
  3. Make ready chopped garlic
  4. Get Approx 1 lb sliced beef
  5. Get Egg noodles
  6. Prepare Onion Dry Soup/Mix
  7. Make ready can of beef broth
  8. Make ready sour cream
  9. Make ready Salt, Pepper and/or garlic/onion powder (optional)
  10. Prepare Parmesan cheese topper (optional)
Instructions to make Onion Beef Noodle (Not Stroganoff):
  1. Boil water for noodles (continue recipe) and cook noodles according to noodle bag
  2. Sauté onion and garlic
  3. Add beef and sauté on both sides; adding salt, pepper, garlic/onion powder to taste
  4. Add onion soup/dip powder packet and sauté for a minute; then add beef broth; let cook for approx 10 minutes on high
  5. Add sour cream; stir in
  6. Add cooked noodles and stir
