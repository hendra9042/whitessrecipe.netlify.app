The ingredients needed to make Pancakes:
  1. Prepare self-raising flour
  2. Prepare baking powder (baking soda)
  3. Prepare sugar (you can also use brown sugar!)
  4. Take large eggs
  5. Prepare melted butter, cooled (plus a little later for cooking
  6. Prepare milk
  7. Make ready Vegetable oil for cooking
  8. Prepare Optional
  9. Prepare Chocolate chips
  10. Get Raisins
Steps to make Pancakes:
  1. Mix the flour, baking powder, caster sugar and a pinch of salt together in a large bowl.
  2. Add the eggs, melted butter and milk.
  3. Whisk together, then pour into jug.
  4. Heat a small amount of butter and 1 tsp of oil in a large, non-stick frying pan over a medium heat.
  5. When the butter looks frothy pour in rounds of the batter.
  6. Cook the pancakes on their first side until lots of tiny bubbles start to appear and pop on the surface. Flip the pancakes over and cook for a further minute on the other side.
