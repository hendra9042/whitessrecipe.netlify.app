The ingredients needed to make Scrambled Eggs:
  1. Get Eggs
  2. Prepare Milk
  3. Prepare salt
  4. Prepare pepper
  5. Get Paprika
  6. Get Vanilla
  7. Make ready Butter
Instructions to make Scrambled Eggs:
  1. Turn stove on to medium heat. In a non-stick pan add the butter.
  2. While the butter is melting, crack your eggs into a bowl.
  3. Add the milk to the bowl and the seasonings.
  4. With a fork mix your ingredients.
  5. If your butter has melted completely you can now add your egg mixture.
  6. After about half a minute to a minute start mixing the eggs around in the pan.
  7. when the eggs look cooked serve and enjoy!
