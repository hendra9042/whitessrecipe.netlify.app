The ingredients needed to make honey poached egg:
  1. Take 1 egg

  3. Make ready 2 peices of bread
  4. Get 1 oz honey

Instructions to make honey poached egg:

  2. once preheated crack your egg And gently slide the egg into the pan
  3. let the egg lay for three minutes, as you occasionally agitating the egg
  4. toast your bread and slightly warm the honey
  5. lay for presentation then drizzle the honey over your egg and selected items
