The ingredients needed to make Whosayna’s Shakkar Pareys:
  1. Prepare Maida (All Purpose Flour)
  2. Make ready Custard powder
  3. Make ready Egg
  4. Take Baking powder
  5. Prepare Salt
  6. Prepare Elaichi (Cardamon powder)
  7. Make ready Margarine or Butter
  8. Get Castor sugar
  9. Prepare oil
Steps to make Whosayna’s Shakkar Pareys:
  1. Mix all dry ingredients then add egg and butter and mix well.
  2. Bind into soft dough using warm milk. Roll into roti then cut in diamonds, when all done deep fry on medium or low flame to golden brown colour.
  3. Can sprinkle castor sugar on it or just leave as it is. Store in airtight when it cold.
