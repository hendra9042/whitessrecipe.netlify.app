The ingredients needed to make Oil Free Chocolate Brownie:
  1. Prepare curd
  2. Take rolled oats
  3. Take cocoa powder
  4. Get coconut sugar
  5. Make ready vanilla
  6. Make ready skimmed milk
  7. Get himalaya salt
  8. Take baking powder
  9. Take coffee
  10. Take whole egg
  11. Prepare Flax seed
  12. Prepare Cinnamon powder
  13. Take Sesame
  14. Prepare Cashews/Pistachios
Instructions to make Oil Free Chocolate Brownie:
  1. Preheat oven at 180℃. Lightly grease the tin/mould with coconut oil and place the butter paper over it so that brownie do not stick to the base.
  2. Into food processor, add curd, oats, cocoa powder, coconut sugar, vanilla, skim milk, salt, baking powder, egg, flax seeds, cinnamon and coffee, blend to smooth paste.
  3. Pour brownie mixture into prepared cake tin/mould and garnish it with sesame seeds, pistachios and cashews. Now bake it for 15 mins at 180 ℃.
  4. Once baked, let it cool down for 5-10 mins and then demould and cut into desired shape.
