The ingredients needed to make Elisa's Supreme Scrambled Eggs:
  1. Make ready Eggs
  2. Get Virginia Ham, in small pieces
  3. Make ready Mortadela, in small pieces
  4. Get Bacon, uncooked, chopped
  5. Make ready Onion, chopped
  6. Take Sofrito (Recipe on profile)
  7. Prepare garlic, minced
  8. Make ready butter
  9. Get Badia Complete Seasoning
  10. Prepare fresh cilantrillo, chopped
  11. Make ready Sazón Goya
  12. Make ready Salt & Pepper,
Instructions to make Elisa's Supreme Scrambled Eggs:
  1. In a pan, add the bacon and cook.
  2. In the same pan, add the ham, mortadela, cilantrillo and onion.
  3. In a bowl, add all the other ingredients except the butter and beat.
  4. Add the butter to the pan with the ham and other ingredients and mix.
  5. Add the egg batter to the pan and keep moving until it's done.
  6. Top with some cheese (optional)
  7. Serve and enjoy!
