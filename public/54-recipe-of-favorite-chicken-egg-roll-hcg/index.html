The ingredients needed to make Chicken Egg Roll-HCG:
  1. Prepare 3 Cabbage Leaves
  2. Get 1 cup Shredded cabbage
  3. Make ready 1 dash Onion salt, garlic powder, Chinese 5 spice,
  4. Get 1/2 packages Stevia
  5. Make ready 2 Melba toast rounds
  6. Get 3 1/2 oz Boneless, skinless chicken breast, cooked and chopped
Instructions to make Chicken Egg Roll-HCG:
  1. Steam big cabbage leaves for 5 minutes.
  2. Move over to side of steamer to make room for shredded cabbage.
  3. Steam shredded cabbage for 5 min.
  4. Remove shredded cabbage to mixing bowl with chicken and spices.
  5. Wrap in big cabbage leaves and crunch Melba toast on top to give crunch.
