The ingredients needed to make Varza Calta with Maltese Sausage(cabbage with tomato sauce):
  1. Prepare 3 tablespoons canola oil
  2. Make ready 1 small cabbage, finely diced
  3. Make ready 1 medium onion, finely diced
  4. Get 4 Maltese sausages, beaked for 20 minutes and sliced in half. Or you use any sausage of your preference
  5. Prepare 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  6. Prepare 1/2 tablespoon dill
  7. Prepare 1/2 tablespoons thyme
  8. Prepare 1 tablespoon chilli flecks
  9. Prepare 4 poached eggs
  10. Get Natural plain yogurt
  11. Prepare Slat and black pepper
  12. Make ready 2 cups polenta
Instructions to make Varza Calta with Maltese Sausage(cabbage with tomato sauce):
  1. Season the sausage with a pinch of salt and black pepper. Places on a sheet tray and bake for 20 minutes.
  2. In a large skillek heat a thin layer of canola oil medium high heat until shimmering. Once shimmerd put the onions pinch salt and black pepper, cook until golden brown. Then add the cabbage, 1/2 tablespoon dill, 1/2 tablespoon thyme, 1 tablespoon chilli flakes and 2 tablespoon tomato past. Stir together and let it cook for 4 to 5 minutes. In the meantime cook the polenta and the eggs.
  3. For plating. 2 good scoop of polenta, 2 good scoops of the cabbage mixture, 2 tablespoon of natural plain yogurt, poached egg on top and sausage.
