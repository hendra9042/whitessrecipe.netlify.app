The ingredients needed to make Ham and Mushrooms Scramble Eggs:
  1. Make ready butter
  2. Take onion, minced
  3. Make ready red bell pepper, diced
  4. Get smoked ham, diced
  5. Get champignons, drained, thinly sliced
  6. Prepare large eggs
  7. Take whole milk
  8. Prepare cheddar cheese or to taste
  9. Make ready salt and pepper
  10. Prepare Additional side dish:
  11. Take avocado, sliced
  12. Prepare fresh strawberries or your favorite fruits
Steps to make Ham and Mushrooms Scramble Eggs:
  1. Heat the butter on a pan over medium high heat. Saut√© onion until fragrant.
  2. Add champignons, smoked ham, and red bell pepper. Cook for about 1 minute.
  3. Whisk eggs and milk. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Add the egg batter into the pan. Stir slowly with a spatula.
  5. Sprinkle the cheese on top of the eggs. Cover the pan with the lid for another minute. Remove from the heat. Put it aside for 2-3 minutes until the cheese melts.
