The ingredients needed to make Egg and Hotdog Sandwich:
  1. Make ready 1/4 pound beef hotdogs 2
  2. Prepare 1 large egg
  3. Make ready 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  4. Take 1/4 cup banana pepper rings
  5. Get 1/4 cup dill pickle chips
  6. Take 2 slices bread
Instructions to make Egg and Hotdog Sandwich:
  1. Fry the egg with whites done but yoke runny
  2. Split the hotdogs down the middle length wise and fry
  3. Mayonnaise one slice of bread add pickles and pepper rings
  4. Add egg
  5. Add hotdogs
  6. Cut in half and serve I hope you enjoy.
