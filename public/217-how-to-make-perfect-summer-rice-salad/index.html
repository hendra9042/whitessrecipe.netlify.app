The ingredients needed to make Summer Rice Salad:
  1. Make ready white rice
  2. Make ready canned tuna
  3. Take corn
  4. Take cucumber
  5. Take tomatoes
  6. Make ready boiled eggs
  7. Make ready oil
  8. Prepare hot water
Instructions to make Summer Rice Salad:
  1. Heat the oil+rice in a pressure cooker for 2 minutes then add the hot water + a pinch of salt. Close the pressure cooker & let cook for 20 minutes on low heat
  2. Cut the tomatoes, the cucumber & 1 boiled egg into small cubes
  3. Drain the cooked rice mix it with the tuna, corn and the rest of ingredients. Put in the fridge & serve fresh. Might add some mayonnaise for a better taste. Enjoy!
