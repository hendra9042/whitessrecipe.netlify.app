The ingredients needed to make For Sandwiches An Easy To Make Egg Salad Using A Microwave:
  1. Get cube Egg
  2. Get Vegetable oil
  3. Prepare Salt and pepper
  4. Make ready Mayonnaise
  5. Get Chopped fresh parsley ( dried parsley )
Steps to make For Sandwiches An Easy To Make Egg Salad Using A Microwave:
  1. Put 1/2 teaspoon of vegetable oil in a heat-resistant bowl
  2. Break an egg on top of the bowl and stick a bamboo skewer or tooth pick through the whole egg's yolk and egg white many times.
  3. Cover it with a cellophane wrap and heat it up in a 500 W microwave for about 1 minute to 1 minute and 10 seconds.
  4. If the egg has set it's done. Check how hard it is and adjust the cooking time accordingly.
  5. Mash it with a fork likely to mince it.
  6. Add about 1 tablespoon of mayonnaise, salt and pepper and mix it briefly.
  7. Add chopped fresh parsley or dried parsley for finishing and mix the whole thing, then it is ready You can add black pepper instead too!
  8. Here is an egg salad sauce which matches very well with pork and chicken Adding a little bit of vinegar and sugar will change it into a chicken nanban sauce !
