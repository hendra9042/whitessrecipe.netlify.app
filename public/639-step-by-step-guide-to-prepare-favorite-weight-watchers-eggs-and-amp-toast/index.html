The ingredients needed to make Weight Watchers Eggs & Toast:
  1. Make ready Joseph's Flax Pita
  2. Prepare Egg/Poached
  3. Make ready Tomato
  4. Get Baby Spinach
  5. Take FatFree Cheddar Cheese/Shredded
Steps to make Weight Watchers Eggs & Toast:
  1. Preheat Oven to 400°F. Once preheated place your 1/2 pita on the rack to toast for about 8min. But watch it, every oven is different.
  2. Begin to sautée your baby spinach with a cooking spray of your choice. You can leave it as is, or season it with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  3. Slice your tomato, almost an 1/8in thick.
  4. Bring a shallow pan to a boil, and once boiling, lower the heat down to medium and SLOWLY begin to drop your egg. I let mine poached for about 4min, but you can go up to 7-8min if you don't want a runny yolk
  5. Lay the spinach down on your pita toast, then your tomato, and follow with your egg. Top off with sprinkling about 3tbsp of your Fatfree cheddar
