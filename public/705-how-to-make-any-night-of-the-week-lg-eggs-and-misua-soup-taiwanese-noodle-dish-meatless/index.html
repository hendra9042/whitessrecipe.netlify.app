The ingredients needed to make LG EGGS AND MISUA SOUP

( TAIWANESE NOODLE DISH ) MEATLESS:

  1. Get 1 liter stock of your choice
  2. Prepare 3 cup bundle of misua noodle
  3. Take 1 carrot
  4. Make ready 2 leeks
  5. Prepare 2 eggs
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup spring onion or cilantro
  7. Get seasoning
  8. Make ready 1 dash salt and pepper

Steps to make LG EGGS AND MISUA SOUP

( TAIWANESE NOODLE DISH ) MEATLESS:

  1. bring stock to a boil then add carrot and leeks simmer for 3 minute
  2. add misua noodle and break egg in and dont stir to poach the eggs just let it simmer another 2 minute
  3. season and top with spring onion and off heat and serve immediately
