The ingredients needed to make Scrambled Eggs:
  1. Make ready 6 eggs
  2. Take to taste salt
  3. Get to taste pepper
  4. Make ready to taste butter
Instructions to make Scrambled Eggs:
  1. Put the butter on the skillet and wait till it melts all over the skillet between 4 and 5 minutes.
  2. Put 6 eggs in, mix it and cook. When it's almost ready add a little salt and pepper then mix it all together.
  3. Serve to 6 people.
