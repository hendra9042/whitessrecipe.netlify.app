The ingredients needed to make Au Gratin redneck style (cheese & heat):
  1. Prepare golden potatoes
  2. Get milk
  3. Take butter
  4. Make ready minced onion
  5. Get mushroom
  6. Get some pepper kernals
  7. Take touch of chicken stock or boulion
  8. Get egg yolks
  9. Prepare dollops of sour cream
  10. Prepare sharp cheddar
  11. Get provalone cheese
  12. Prepare parmesean
  13. Prepare paprika
  14. Prepare italian seasoning
  15. Make ready garlic salt
  16. Take sour cream
  17. Take red pepper flakes
  18. Make ready sliced peppercinni
Steps to make Au Gratin redneck style (cheese & heat):
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Mince onions & slice the potatoes and mushrooms
  3. Heat butter on high
  4. Saute onions & add garlic salt, peppers, boulion/stock.
  5. Mix in mushrooms after onions are browned
  6. Turn off heat and mix in egg yolks & milk
  7. Stir sour cream & parmesean cheese into sauce
  8. In greased casarole pan layer onion sauce & potato slices
  9. Top with other cheeses then dollop more sour cream on top
  10. Sprinkle the seasoning, peppercinni & paprika on top
  11. Bake covered for 45 minutes and 15 uncovered
  12. Let stand 10-15 minutes before serving
