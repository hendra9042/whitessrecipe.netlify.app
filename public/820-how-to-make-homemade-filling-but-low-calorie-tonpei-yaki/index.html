The ingredients needed to make Filling But Low Calorie ~ Tonpei Yaki ~:
  1. Get 50 grams Pork
  2. Get 1 bag Bean sprouts
  3. Take 2 Eggs
  4. Take Seasonings
  5. Make ready 1 Salt and pepper
  6. Make ready B seasonings (to season the eggs)
  7. Prepare 1 dash Salt
  8. Get 1 tsp Sugar
  9. Take 1 tsp Shiro-dashi
  10. Prepare Toppings
  11. Prepare 1 Japanese Worcestershire-style sauce, Mayonnaise, green onions, natto (to taste)
Steps to make Filling But Low Calorie ~ Tonpei Yaki ~:
  1. Parboil the pork.
  2. Beat the eggs in a bowl, add the B seasonings and mix.
  3. Put the pork from Step 1 and the bean sprouts in a non-stick pan, season with salt and pepper, and sauté.
  4. Remove Step 3 from the pan, then put pour all of Step 2 into the pan. Add Step 3 to the side of the egg mixture and wrap it up with the eggs.
  5. Once rolled up, transfer to a plate and top with the toppings. Enjoy.
  6. Adjust the fillings to however you like. Try with various fillings.
  7. You can try filling it with stir-fried squid or other seafood and cabbage or other vegetables. I recommend serving as a seafood okonomiyaki.
