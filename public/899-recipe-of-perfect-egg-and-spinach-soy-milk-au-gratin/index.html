Recipe of Perfect Egg and Spinach Soy Milk au Gratin

The ingredients needed to make Egg and Spinach Soy Milk au Gratin:
  1. Take Spinach
  2. Get Potatoes
  3. Get Eggs
  4. Make ready ●Soy milk
  5. Make ready ●Soup stock cube granules
  6. Take ●Salt and pepper
  7. Get Pizza cheese
  8. Make ready Parmesan cheese
  9. Make ready Butter
  10. Take Salt and pepper
Steps to make Egg and Spinach Soy Milk au Gratin:
  1. Boil the eggs and slice into rounds. Boil the spinach and slice into 4-5 cm pieces. Cut the potatoes into 5 mm thick slices.
  2. Heat the sliced potatoes and ingredients marked ● in a saucepan. Bring to the boil, but make sure the soy milk doesn't boil over. Then turn the heat to medium low, and simmer until the potatoes soften.
  3. Melt the butter in a frying pan and sauté the spinach. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Put the potato and soy milk from Step 2 in a heatproof dish, then top with the spinach and boiled egg slices.
  5. Sprinkle on some pizza cheese and Parmesan cheese. Bake in the toaster oven for 7-10 minutes until golden.
