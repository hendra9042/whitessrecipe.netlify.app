The ingredients needed to make Vegetarian Pad Thai:
  1. Make ready 1-2 lime wedges, juiced
  2. Take 1 tbsp fish sauce or substitute
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp soy sauce
  4. Take Dash sugar
  5. Make ready Rice vinegar to taste
  6. Get Sriracha to taste (optional)
  7. Make ready Cooking oil
  8. Make ready 1/2 package cooked rice noodles, drained
  9. Take 1/2 cup carrots, juilenned
  10. Take 2 eggs, cooked and sliced
  11. Prepare 5 oz cooked vegetarian chicken, sliced
  12. Prepare 3-5 scallions, chopped
  13. Take 1/3 cup fried tofu strips
  14. Make ready 1 cup fresh bean sprouts
  15. Take Sesame seeds (optional)
Instructions to make Vegetarian Pad Thai:
  1. In a bowl, combine lime juice, fish sauce, soy sauce, sugar, rice vinegar, and *sriracha. Adjust to taste. Set aside.
  2. Heat oiled pan on medium high setting. Add rice noodles to pan. Gradually pour 3/4 of sauce mixture and coat noodles evenly. Add remaining ingredients along with remaining 1/4 sauce. Stir and coat evenly.
