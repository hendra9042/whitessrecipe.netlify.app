The ingredients needed to make Coffee Toffee Cheesecake:
  1. Get Crust
  2. Take 2 1/2 cup Fiber One 80 Calories Chocolate Cereal
  3. Take 1/2 cup fat free choc syrup
  4. Get Filling
  5. Make ready 1 cup semi sweet choc chips
  6. Get 1/4 cup light cool whip
  7. Take 4 tsp instant coffee granules
  8. Take 3 packages 8 oz. fat free cream cheese
  9. Prepare 1 1/3 cup sugar
  10. Make ready 1 1/2 cup plain greek yogurt
  11. Get 1 tsp vanilla extract
  12. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt
  13. Get 3 eggs, beaten lightly
  14. Get Topping
  15. Prepare 4 heath candy bars, crushed
  16. Get 1 dark choc. candy bar
Instructions to make Coffee Toffee Cheesecake:
  1. Preheat oven at 350
  2. Crush cereal into pieces and combine with chocolate syrup. Press on to the bottom and 1 inch up the sides of the prepared 9-inch springform pan.
  3. In a microwave-safe bowl melt the chocolate chips and stir in with the whip cream until smooth. Stir in coffee granules until dissolved. Set aside.
  4. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Beat n the yogurt, vanilla and salt; gradually beat in the prepared chocolate mixture.
  5. Add eggs; beat on low speed until combined.
  6. Pour into crust. Place springform pan and a large baking pan; add 1 in. hot water to larger pan.
  7. Bake at 350°F for 55 - 65 minutes or until center is just set and top appears dull.
  8. Remove springform pan from water bath. Cool on a wire rack for 10 minutes. Sprinkle with Heath bars
  9. Carefully run a knife around edge of pan to loosen; cool one hour longer. Refrigerate overnight.
  10. Before serving chop the dark chocolate candy bar and melt in the microwave; stir until smooth. Drizzle over top of cheesecake.
