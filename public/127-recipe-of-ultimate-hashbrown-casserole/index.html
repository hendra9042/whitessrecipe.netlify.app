The ingredients needed to make Hashbrown Casserole:
  1. Get chopped onion
  2. Get Shredded Cheddar
  3. Prepare sour cream
  4. Prepare Sausage links, cut
  5. Get eggs
  6. Take Package of frozen Hasbrown
  7. Get Melted Butter
Instructions to make Hashbrown Casserole:
  1. Preheat oven to 400°
  2. In large casserole dish mix in, hashbrown, sour cream, onion, sausage, eggs, butter, and 1/2 package of cheese until all evenly combined
  3. Top with remaining cheese
  4. Cover with tinfoil and bake for 1 hour
  5. Once done let it cool for 10 minutes. Serve with hotsauce if desire
