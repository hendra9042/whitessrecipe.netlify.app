The ingredients needed to make Baked Scotch Eggs:
  1. Take hard-boiled eggs
  2. Make ready MEAT MIXTURE m
  3. Prepare ham, minced
  4. Get ground pork sausage
  5. Prepare paprika
  6. Prepare dried sage
  7. Make ready dried marjoram
  8. Get ground cloves
  9. Get chili powder
  10. Take salt and pepper
  11. Make ready BREADING b
  12. Make ready ground pork rinds
  13. Get ground flax seed
  14. Take salt and pepper
  15. Take grated Parmesan cheese, optional
Instructions to make Baked Scotch Eggs:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, combine meat mixture ingredients. Divide into four portions and flatten into patties. Wrap a patty around each egg and seal closed.
  3. In another bowl, combine breading ingredients. Roll meat-wrapped eggs in breading mixture until thoroughly coated. Transfer to baking sheet.
  4. Bake about 25 min. Serve hot with mayo, mustard, Ketchup or BBQ sauce.
