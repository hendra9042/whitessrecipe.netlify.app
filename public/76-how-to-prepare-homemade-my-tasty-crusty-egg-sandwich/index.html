The ingredients needed to make My Tasty Crusty Egg Sandwich ðŸ˜˜:
  1. Make ready 6 Eggs
  2. Take 1 cup sweetcorn
  3. Take 2 tbsp Mayonnaise or Sour Cream
  4. Get to taste salt and white pepper
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup Grated cheese
  6. Take 1 tsp Lemon juice
  7. Take 1 pinch fresh Parsley
Instructions to make My Tasty Crusty Egg Sandwich ðŸ˜˜:
  1. Boil eggs until hard boiled, take all the shell off and add to a large bowl.
  2. Mash the eggs, then add the mayo or sour cream and mash together
  3. Next add the salt & pepper mix with spoon and add lemon and stir in.
  4. Then add grated cheese.
  5. Stir carefully into the mix the sweetcorn, so as not to squash them.
  6. And lastly add the fresh parsley and mix. Serve on crusty bread.
