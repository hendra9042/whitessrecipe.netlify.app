The ingredients needed to make Fancy and simple eggs in a basket:
  1. Make ready butter or bacon grease as needed to fry
  2. Take 2 eggs
  3. Make ready 1 bagel -split
  4. Prepare 1 slice cheese of your choice or shredded cheese
  5. Prepare 1 slice ham ( I use sweet ham)
  6. Prepare 1 packages strawberry, cherry, or raspberry yogurt ( I use greek yogurt)

Steps to make Fancy and simple eggs in a basket:
  1. Use bacon grease or butter to grease your fry pan.
  2. Crack one egg in pan careful not to break the yolk
  3. Place bagel crust side up on top of egg squeezing the yolk through the center hole
  4. Cook until white is solid then flip and cook about one minute more
  5. While crust is cooking add cheese and ham then flip and heat slightly about 30 seconds
  6. Remove from pan and top with yogurt
  7. Serve quickly it cools fast
