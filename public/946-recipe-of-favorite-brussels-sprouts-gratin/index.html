The ingredients needed to make Brussels Sprouts Gratin:
  1. Make ready brussels sprouts, halved
  2. Get bacon, cooked and crumbled
  3. Prepare cheddar cheese, grated
  4. Get 18% table cream
  5. Make ready olive oil
  6. Take egg
  7. Get rosemary, minced
  8. Take garlic powder
  9. Make ready onion powder
  10. Prepare red pepper flakes, optional
  11. Make ready salt & pepper
Instructions to make Brussels Sprouts Gratin:
  1. Preheat oven to 400° F.
  2. Toss brussels sprouts in olive oil and season generously with salt and pepper. Place them in a large baking dish and roast for 15-20 minutes, or until semi-softened.
  3. In a large bowl whisk together egg, cream, rosemary, garlic powder, onion powder and red pepper flakes. Then stir in cheese.
  4. Remove brussels sprouts from oven and add 3/4 bacon to the baking dish. Pour cheese mixture over the top and stir everything together, then top with remaining bacon.
  5. Reduce oven temperature to 350° F, return baking dish to oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until cheese is bubbly.
  6. Remove from oven and let cool 5 minutes before serving.
