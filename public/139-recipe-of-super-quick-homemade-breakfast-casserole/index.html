The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Get frozen hashbrowns
  2. Make ready eggs
  3. Get milk
  4. Take ground mustard
  5. Take roll sausage
  6. Take salt
  7. Get pepper
  8. Make ready shredded cheddar cheese
Instructions to make Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Spray crock pot and evenly spread hash browns at the bottom
  2. Crack 12 eggs in a large bowl
  3. Mix well (and slowly) using a whisk
  4. Add the milk
  5. Sprinkle in the ground mustard
  6. Add salt
  7. Cook the sausage on high heat, drain, and set aside
  8. Add sausage on top of hash browns
  9. Add shredded cheese
  10. Mix all ingredients
  11. Pour the egg mixture over everything, spread evenly
  12. Turn the crock pot on low for 6-8 hours
