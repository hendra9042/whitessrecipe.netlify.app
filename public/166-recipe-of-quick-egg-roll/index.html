The ingredients needed to make Egg roll:
  1. Make ready 2 boiled eggs
  2. Make ready 1 cup flour
  3. Get 1/2 tsp baking powder
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp margarine
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp sugar
  7. Prepare Oil for frying

Instructions to make Egg roll:
  1. Deshell your boiled egg. Mix the flour, baking powder,salt and sugar
  2. Then mix in the margarine.
  3. Whisk egg in a separate bowl, add to the flour mixture. Mix everything together thoroughly
  4. Coat the dough with your eggs
  5. Fry until golden brown. Enjoy
