The ingredients needed to make Southwestern BLT Egg Salad:
  1. Get For Salad
  2. Make ready 4 large Boston lettuce leaves
  3. Prepare 4 cups chopped blend of iceburg and romaine lettece, purple cabbage and carrots
  4. Get 1 cucumber, sliced
  5. Get 1 radish, sliced
  6. Get 1 red onion, sliced
  7. Make ready 1-2 tomatoes, cut in wedges
  8. Prepare 6 slices crispy cooked bacon
  9. Make ready 4 hard boiled eggs, cut in halve, sprinkled with southwestern seasoning
  10. Prepare 1/4 cup finely shredded Monterey jack cheese
  11. Get 1/4 cup crushed blue and white tortilla chips
  12. Take For Dressing
  13. Make ready 1 recipe my spicy Ranch Dip/z Dressing with 1 tablespoon southwestern seasoning blended in. My dressing can be found in my profile and in search
  14. Take 1 lime, wedged for adding as desired
Instructions to make Southwestern BLT Egg Salad:
  1. Lay Boston lettece on serving plate. Put chopped salad in the center
  2. Add cucumbers and radishes around edges
  3. Add tomatos and red onion
  4. Add eggs
  5. Add cheese and tortillas
  6. Finally add bacon and lime wedges
  7. Drizzle a small amount of dressing over and pass more to add to each serving as needed, adding a bit of fresh lime
