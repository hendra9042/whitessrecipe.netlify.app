The ingredients needed to make George Washington's Egg Nog:
  1. Make ready 1 pints brandy
  2. Make ready 1 1/2 pints rye whiskey
  3. Prepare 1/4 pints rum
  4. Make ready 1/4 pints sherry
  5. Get 12 tbsp sugar
  6. Make ready 1 quart cream
  7. Prepare 1 quart milk
  8. Make ready 12 eggs
Instructions to make George Washington's Egg Nog:
  1. Mix liquors first.
  2. Separate eggs. Beat yolks in a large bowl.
  3. Add sugar to the yolks and, beating slowly, add liquor mixture drop by drop.
  4. Stir in cream and milk.
  5. Beat egg whites until stiff and slowly fold into the mixture.
  6. Set in a cool place for several days.
