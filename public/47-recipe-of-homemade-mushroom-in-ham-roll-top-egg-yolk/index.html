The ingredients needed to make Mushroom In Ham Roll Top Egg Yolk:
  1. Make ready 2 boiled egg yolks only
  2. Prepare 1 bunch enoki mushroom
  3. Prepare 4 slice cooked thinly cured ham
  4. Get 1 dash olive oil
  5. Take season egg yolk
  6. Take 1 pinch sugar and salt
  7. Take dressing
  8. Get 1 dash balsamic vinegar glaze
Instructions to make Mushroom In Ham Roll Top Egg Yolk:
  1. bring to a boil water and egg then simmer for 8 minute and off heat
  2. roll enoki with ham and drizzle olive oil then bake 180 for 10 minute till lightly brown
  3. break egg yolk gently like crumbs and season it mix well like crumbs like
  4. serve enoki mushroom with egg yolk crumbs top dressing
