Instructions to make Easy stir fry purple cabbage:
  1. Heat up sesame oil in a pan and put in garlic. Saute until fragrant.
  2. Throw in baby corn. Stir fry until you can see a slight change of colour
  3. Throw in sausage.
  4. Throw in purple cabbage
  5. Sprinkle pepper and mushroom powder as you like.
  6. Saute until cabbage is a bit soft but still crunchy.
  7. Top up japanese sesame and flax seed if you like!
  8. I made scrambled egg after finished to cook this using same pan. It will be a blue scrambled egg.
