The ingredients needed to make Custard Cream in the Microwave:
  1. Make ready Cake flour
  2. Make ready Sugar
  3. Prepare Milk
  4. Make ready Egg yolks
  5. Get Butter (unsalted)
Steps to make Custard Cream in the Microwave:
  1. Put the sifted flour, sugar, and a bit of milk in a heat-resistant dish, and whisk well.
  2. Add the egg yolks and the rest of the milk, and mix well.
  3. Without covering, microwave at 600 W for 2 minutes, then mix.
  4. Microwave again for another 2 minutes, then mix.
  5. Microwave for 2 and 1/2 minutes, making a total of 6 minutes and 30 seconds. Add the butter, mix well, and, if available, add a few drops of vanilla essence and mix.
  6. While hot, wrap the surface in plastic, then chill.
