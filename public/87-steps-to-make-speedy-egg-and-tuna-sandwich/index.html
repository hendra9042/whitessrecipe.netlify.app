The ingredients needed to make Egg and tuna sandwich:
  1. Prepare Egg and tuna sandwich
  2. Make ready 2 boiled eggs
  3. Get 1 drained tin tuna
  4. Take 1/2 chopped onion
  5. Get 1/2 chopped bell pepper
  6. Get piece sliced cheese
  7. Prepare 1 mayonnaise
  8. Take 1 black pepper
  9. Take 1 reddish
Steps to make Egg and tuna sandwich:
  1. Boil eggs
  2. Drain water from tuna and place in dish.
  3. Cut up onion and bell pepper place in dish with tuna and grated cheese.
  4. After egg is boiled take out of hot water n put to cool in cold water before peeling off shell.
  5. Add egg to mix along with mayonnaise, black pepper and resddish.
  6. Mix and prepare to serve.
