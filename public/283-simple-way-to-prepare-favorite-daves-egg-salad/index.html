The ingredients needed to make Dave’s Egg Salad:
  1. Make ready eggs
  2. Prepare mayo
  3. Make ready yellow mustard
  4. Take sweet relish
  5. Make ready Fresh dill weed to taste
  6. Take Ground black pepper to taste
Instructions to make Dave’s Egg Salad:
  1. Boil eggs: place eggs in pot. Fill with cold water until eggs are covered. Bring water to boil. Once water is boiling, remove from heat, cover and let sit for 12 min.
  2. De shell eggs and chop eggs up in smaller pieces.
  3. Mix eggs and ingredients in a bowl.
  4. Let chill and serve.
