The ingredients needed to make Egg Custard Pie:
  1. Get 1 1/2 cup Sugar
  2. Take 3/4 cup Butter
  3. Take 5 Eggs
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup Flour (All Purpose)
  5. Make ready 2 12 oz cans Evaporated Milk
  6. Prepare 1 tsp Vanilla Flavor
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp Lemon Flavor
  8. Make ready 1/3 tsp Nutmeg
  9. Prepare 2 9 in Deep Dish Pie Crust
Instructions to make Egg Custard Pie:
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Blend together butter and sugar, until incorporated into sugar.
  3. Add eggs one at a time until well blended.
  4. Add flour until blended. Then add 2 cans of evaporated milk.
  5. Add vanilla, lemon and nutmeg, beat until well blended.
  6. Prep pie crust and evenly pour mixture into both pie crust, scraping bowl of all remaining butter from bowl.
  7. Place both pies in oven and bake for 45 mins to 1hour. If pies are brown and firm they are done. Let cool for 15 mins.
  8. Serve either warm or cold…..Enjoy!
