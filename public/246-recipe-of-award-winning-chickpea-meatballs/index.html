Instructions to make Chickpea meatballs:
  1. Sauté onion and garlic in saucepan with olive oil until slightly browned.
  2. When onion is finished being sautéed, add all ingredients to a mixing bowl or food processor. Process until entire mix is thick and fully chopped together.
  3. Add olive oil to saucepan and heat up on medium heat. Roll mixture into 1 - 1.5 inch balls. Place in heated oil and turn until browned on all sides. (Be Careful not to break them apart when you turn them as they will be a little fragile until cooking is complete)
  4. Serve over spaghetti or spaghetti squash with some marinara. Enjoy.
