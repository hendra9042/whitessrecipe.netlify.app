The ingredients needed to make Tuna Noodle Casserole:
  1. Prepare 1 package egg noodles
  2. Prepare 1 small yellow onion, french cut
  3. Take 1 can sweet peas
  4. Get 1-2 cans tuna
  5. Prepare 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  6. Make ready 1 can cream of chicken soup
  7. Take 1/2 cup milk
  8. Make ready 1 cup sour cream
  9. Make ready 2 cups cheddar cheese, shredded
  10. Get Salt
  11. Get Pepper
  12. Prepare Garlic powder
  13. Prepare Parsley
  14. Prepare 1 cup cornflakes, crushed
Instructions to make Tuna Noodle Casserole:
  1. Preheat oven to 425¬∞. Place all ingredients (saving aside cornflakes & 1 cup of shredded cheddar) in a large casserole dish and mix well. Seasoning in desired amounts.
  2. Level out and top with an even layer of shredded cheddar. Cover and place into oven for 45mins.
  3. Remove covering and add an even layer of crushed corn flakes across the top. Return to oven for 15mins or until cornflakes begin to get browned and crispy.
