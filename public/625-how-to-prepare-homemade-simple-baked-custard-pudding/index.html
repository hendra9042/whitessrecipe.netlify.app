The ingredients needed to make Simple Baked Custard Pudding:
  1. Prepare Milk
  2. Take Heavy cream
  3. Make ready Sugar
  4. Make ready approx. 6 drops Vanilla Oil (or Vanilla Extract)
  5. Prepare Eggs
Instructions to make Simple Baked Custard Pudding:
  1. In a heatproof container with a spout, add the cream, milk, and sugar and microwave at 500W for approx. 3 minutes. Mix well.
  2. Add vanilla oil (or vanilla extract) and mix lightly. Let cool.
  3. In a separate container, beat the eggs together and add them little by little to the mixture from Step 2, stirring well after each addition.
  4. Preheat the oven to 140℃. Begin to boil the water.
  5. Strain Step 3 into ramekins, mugs or small heatproof containers.
  6. Place the ramekins on a baking tray and fill halfway up with boiling water. Steam bake the custard puddings for 50 minutes at 140℃.
  7. When out of the oven, check to see if they're done by inserting a skewer into the center. If it comes out clean, they're done. Add whipped cream or caramel sauce and chill.
