The ingredients needed to make Old Fashioned Tri-color Rice Bowl with Chicken Soboro:
  1. Get 200 to 250 grams Ground chicken
  2. Get 3 tbsp ■ Soy sauce (or dashi soy sauce)
  3. Take 1 1/2 tbsp ■ Sugar
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp ■ Mirin
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp ■ Sake
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp ■ Grated ginger
  7. Make ready 1 bunch Spinach
  8. Take 1 tbsp ▲ Soy sauce
  9. Prepare 1/2 tbsp ▲ Sesame oil
  10. Make ready 1 dash ▲ Doubanjian
  11. Make ready 2 Eggs
  12. Get 1 tbsp ● Sugar
  13. Get 1 Red pickled ginger
Steps to make Old Fashioned Tri-color Rice Bowl with Chicken Soboro:
  1. Mix the ■ ingredients together, and the ▲ ingredients together respectively.
  2. Blanch the spinach, refresh in cold running water and squeeze out well. Marinate in the combined ▲ ingredients.
  3. Stir fry the ground chicken and flavor with the ■ ingredients. Keep cooking over medium heat until there's no liquid left in the pan.
  4. Make the iri tamago scrambled egg in a frying pan.
  5. Top neatly on a bowl of rice, and garnish with red pickled ginger.
