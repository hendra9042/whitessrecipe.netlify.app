The ingredients needed to make Christmas Morning Casserole:
  1. Make ready hash browns
  2. Make ready sausage
  3. Prepare shredded cheddar cheese
  4. Make ready shredded mozzarella cheese
  5. Prepare eggs
  6. Get milk
  7. Get salt and pepper to taste
Instructions to make Christmas Morning Casserole:
  1. Fry hash browns and sausage until done (can be done together or separately)
  2. Drain well.
  3. Beat eggs, add milk and whisk well.
  4. Pour has browns/sausage into glass casserole dish.
  5. Add egg and milk mixture.
  6. Stir in cheese, salt and pepper.
  7. Bake at 350°F for 35 minutes
