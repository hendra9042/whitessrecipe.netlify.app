The ingredients needed to make Applesauce Cake:
  1. Get 3 1/2 cup Self rising flour
  2. Take 1/2 cup raisins
  3. Take 1 tsp Cinnamon
  4. Get 1 tsp Allspice
  5. Make ready 1 cup Melted butter
  6. Prepare 1 tsp Baking soda
  7. Take 1 cup chopped pecans
  8. Get 1 oz Chopped cherries mix
  9. Prepare 1 tsp Ground cloves
  10. Prepare 1 tsp Nutmeg
  11. Make ready 2 cup Granulated sugar or substitute
  12. Get 3 medium eggs
  13. Prepare 1 1/2 cup applesauce
Steps to make Applesauce Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F
  2. In small bowl, mix applesauce with 1 teaspoon baking soda. In seperate bowl, mix nuts, raisins and cherries. In seperate bowl, mix flour, 1 teaspoon cinnamon, 1 teaspoon nutmeg, and 1 teaspoon allspice. In large bowl, cream butter, sugar and 3 eggs. Add flour mixture, mixwell; add applesauce mixture, add fruit mix mix well.
