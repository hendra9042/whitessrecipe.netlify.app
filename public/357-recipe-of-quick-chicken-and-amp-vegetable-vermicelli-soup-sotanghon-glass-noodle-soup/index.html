The ingredients needed to make Chicken & Vegetable Vermicelli Soup - Sotanghon / Glass Noodle Soup:
  1. Get Chicken - chopped or shredded or your favorite parts
  2. Get vermicelli / bihon / thin glass noodles / cellophane noodles - already softened
  3. Get water or chicken broth
  4. Get chicken broth cube (if not using liquid broth)
  5. Get medium onion, chopped
  6. Make ready garlic cloves, chopped
  7. Make ready Carrots, julienned

  12. Get Salt
  13. Make ready Cooking oil
  14. Take Top with Garnish: (optional)

Instructions to make Chicken & Vegetable Vermicelli Soup - Sotanghon / Glass Noodle Soup:
  1. Saute the garlic and onions in oil.
  2. Add the chicken, frozen is okay. Run it through the tap first before adding to the pot.
  3. Add a few tablespoons water, a good amount of salt and cover. Turn chicken, to cook on all sides, until it changes color.
  4. Add the chicken broth or water+broth cube. You can already add the hard vegetables. I added the drumstick seeds and green beans. Bring to a boil.
  5. Add the fish sauce or patis. Add the rest of the vegetables and the noodles. The noodles should be pre-softened before adding here. - - Bring to a boil, taste and adjust seasoning as desired.
  6. Turn off heat and serve immediately. Garnish with toppings. - - Note: the noodles may/will absorb most of the soup if not consumed right away. ☺️
