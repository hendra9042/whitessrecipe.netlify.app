The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Chip bunt cake:
  1. Get fat free sour cream
  2. Take and 1 tablespoon water warm
  3. Take vegetable oil
  4. Prepare instant coffee
  5. Prepare 8 ounce egg beaters
  6. Make ready devils food cake mix no pudding in it
  7. Take small instant chocolate pudding mix
  8. Make ready semisweet chocolate chips
  9. Make ready powdered sugar
Instructions to make Chocolate Chip bunt cake:
  1. preheat oven to 350°F
  2. spray bunt pan with cooking spray
  3. combine first7 ingredients
  4. best three minutes
  5. add chocolate chips
  6. pour in pan and bake for one hour
  7. cool for ten minutes and invert on plate and sprinkle too with powdered sugar
