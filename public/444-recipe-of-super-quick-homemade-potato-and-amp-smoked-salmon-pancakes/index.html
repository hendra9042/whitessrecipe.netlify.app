The ingredients needed to make Potato & Smoked Salmon Pancakes:
  1. Make ready medium size Potatoes *about 300g, peeled and cut into small pieces
  2. Take Egg
  3. Make ready Flour of your choice *I use Plain Flour
  4. Make ready Baking Powder
  5. Make ready Salt *reduce the amount if Smoked Salmon is very salty
  6. Get Black/White Pepper as required
  7. Get Spring Onion *finely chopped
  8. Prepare Smoked Salmon *cut into small pieces
  9. Get Edamame *optional
  10. Make ready Oil for cooking

Instructions to make Potato & Smoked Salmon Pancakes:
  1. Place Potato pieces and Egg in a blender. Blend until fairly smooth.
  2. Place Spring Onion in a large bowl, add the potato mixture, Flour, Baking Powder, Salt & Pepper, and mix to combine. This should be quite thin batter. Thin but not runny when cooked.
  3. Add Smoked Salmon and Edamame, and combine.
  4. Heat a small amount of Oil in a frying pan over medium heat, pour the mixture to make 10cm round, and cook both sides until golden brown. Add more oil if needed.
