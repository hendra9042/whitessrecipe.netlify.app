The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Salad/ A Hot Mess:
  1. Make ready large eggs
  2. Take smoked sausage
  3. Take grated parmesan cheese
  4. Prepare shredded cheddar cheese
  5. Prepare your favorite croutons
  6. Take salad mix
  7. Prepare chopped ham
  8. Prepare salt and pepper
  9. Get your favorite salad dressing
Instructions to make Breakfast Salad/ A Hot Mess:
  1. Fry the eggs wit the yoke runny
  2. Mix the salad mix with croutons, ham and cheese and put on the plate add to eggs on top
  3. Fry the sausage
  4. Add parmesan cheese, salt and pepper, on top
  5. Add salad dressing I used deluxe French Salad Dressing
  6. Cut the eggs and stir around a bit. Serve I hope you enjoy!!
