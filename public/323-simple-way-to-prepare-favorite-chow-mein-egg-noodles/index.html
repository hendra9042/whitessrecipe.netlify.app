The ingredients needed to make Chow Mein (egg noodles):
  1. Get Egg noodles
  2. Get Cha siu
  3. Take sesame oil
  4. Make ready Big cabbage
  5. Get Green Onion
  6. Prepare Garlic
  7. Take Ginger
  8. Make ready Veggie Oil
  9. Prepare Dark soy sauce
  10. Take Oyster sauce
Instructions to make Chow Mein (egg noodles):
  1. Cook the egg noodles. Rinse with cold noodles so they wont stick.
  2. Cok the veggies & cha siu in a pan.
  3. Stir in noodles. Add the soy sauce and oil.
  4. Add oyster sauce for taste.
