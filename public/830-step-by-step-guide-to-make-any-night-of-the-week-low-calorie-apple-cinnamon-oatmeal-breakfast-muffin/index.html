The ingredients needed to make low calorie apple cinnamon oatmeal breakfast muffin:
  1. Get 1 cup rolled oats
  2. Make ready 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  3. Prepare 1 cup whole wheat flour
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup Brown sugar
  5. Get 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
  6. Get 2 egg whites
  7. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking soda
  9. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  10. Prepare 1 tsp cinnamon
  11. Get 1 tsp sweetener that measures like sugar

Steps to make low calorie apple cinnamon oatmeal breakfast muffin:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Soak oats in milk for one hour.
  3. Mix all ingredients. Don't over mix or muffin will be tough
  4. Coat muffin pan with nonstick cooking spray. Spoon mixture into pan.
  5. Bake 20 minutes
  6. You can heat these In the microwave in the morning:)
