The ingredients needed to make Crispy Bacon and Spinach Salad:
  1. Prepare unsmoked bacon
  2. Prepare bread, crusts removed, cut into cubes
  3. Get spinach
  4. Take Salt
  5. Make ready olive oil or use fry light as a healthier alternative
  6. Prepare eggs, fried in fry light spray
Instructions to make Crispy Bacon and Spinach Salad:
  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil over medium-high heat add the bacon and cook until crispy which should take about 5-8 minutes. Remove onto a plate lined with kitchen paper to absorb some of the grease.
  2. Add one more tablespoon of oil to the same pan. Add the bread and fry for 5-6 minutes or until crispy on the outside. Transfer to a plate lined with kitchen paper aswell.
  3. Using the same pan, heat the remaining 2 tablespoons of oil and add the spinach. Stir fry for 4-6 minutes or until wilted. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Crush the bacon into bits and set aside.
  5. Transfer to a serving plate. Top with bacon and the bread croutons. Serve with a fried egg on the side.
