The ingredients needed to make low calorie chicken patty:
  1. Get 4 cup shredded cooked chicken breast
  2. Get 1 cup reduced fat chedder cheese
  3. Get 2 eggs
  4. Take 3 tbsp poultry seasoning
  5. Get 1/2 onion chopped
  6. Take 2 garlic cloves minced
  7. Get 1/4 cup bread crumbs
  8. Prepare 1 season to taste
