The ingredients needed to make Open Bacon & Egg Sandwich 4-1:
  1. Make ready 2 Sliced thick crusty bread
  2. Make ready 1 Rasher bacon, sliced
  3. Prepare 1 Egg
  4. Get 1 Baby cos lettuce
  5. Make ready 1 Fresh basil, chopped (some extra leaves to serve)
  6. Get 4 Cherry tomatoes, halved
  7. Prepare 1 Finely sliced red onion
  8. Prepare 3 or 4 little button mushrooms, halved
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp Honey
  10. Make ready 2 tbsp Whole egg mayonnaise
  11. Make ready 1/2 tsp Dijon mustard
  12. Make ready 1/2 tsp Whole grain mustard
  13. Get 1 Splash of red balsamic vinegar
  14. Prepare 1 Olive oil
  15. Take 1 Salt and pepper
Steps to make Open Bacon & Egg Sandwich 4-1:
  1. Grill your bacon and when almost cooked, remove- mix with honey and return to finish. Set aside.
  2. Mix tomatoes, olive oil, balsamic vinegar and basil in a small bowl and set aside.
  3. Mix mayo and mustards and set aside.
  4. Fry mushrooms in butter until golden and set aside.
  5. Drizzle some olive oil on one side of both slices of bread and place on a heated, lined grill. Grill both sides until you've got some good coloured lined on both sides.
  6. Fry egg in pan on low heat.
