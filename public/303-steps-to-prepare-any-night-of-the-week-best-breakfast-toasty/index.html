The ingredients needed to make Best breakfast toasty:
  1. Take toast
  2. Get eggs
  3. Prepare salt
  4. Make ready cayenne pepper
  5. Take Ham
  6. Take Salami
  7. Get Lemon juice
  8. Get butter
Instructions to make Best breakfast toasty:
  1. Separate three egg whites mixing bowl till frothy
  2. Add butter to sauce pan on medium heat
  3. Add lemon juice cayenne pepper and salt to butter Mix turn down to low heat takes about 6 minutes
  4. Cut ham and salami into fryable chunks you can use whatever size you like frying until golden brown in pan
  5. Add the two remaining eggs along with the yolks of the other eggs Cook as long as you like again I like it a little crispy a little texture
  6. Toast bread add butter add toppings finish off with hollandaise
  7. You can add more salt and pepper to taste as well as cheese
