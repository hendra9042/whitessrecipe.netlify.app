The ingredients needed to make Egg kathi roll:
  1. Take For rolls covering:
  2. Get 2 cups maida
  3. Make ready 4 tbsp oil
  4. Get 1 tsp oil
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking powder
  6. Prepare 4 eggs
  7. Make ready For stuffing:
  8. Take 2 cups thinly sliced onions
  9. Make ready 1/2 cup slices of cucumber
  10. Make ready 1 tsp lemon juice
  11. Take 1/2 tsp Rock salt
  12. Take 1/2 tsp chaat masala
  13. Prepare 4 chopped green chillies
  14. Take 1/2 cup tomato ketchup
  15. Take 1/2 cup oil for frying

Steps to make Egg kathi roll:
  1. Make a soft dough of the covering ingredients. Cover and leave for 10 minutes.
  2. Take onion slices in a bowl add green chillies and lemon juice.
  3. Divide dough into 4 equall parts and roll each part into a paratha.
  4. Cook paratha on tawa,apply 2 tbsp oil.Beat one egg in a bowl,add a pinch of salt and spread on paratha. Fry paratha till golden.Take out.
  5. Prepare all parathas in same way.
  6. Sprinkle chaat masala and rock salt on parathas.
  7. Put 4 tbsp onion stuffing on a paratha.Apply some tomato ketchup and slices of cucumber over onion. Roll into a roll.
  8. Egg kathi roll is ready to serve.
