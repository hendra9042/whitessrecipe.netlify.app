The ingredients needed to make Beef and Egg over Rice:
  1. Take 1 Shirataki Beef Rice Bowl
  2. Make ready 4 medium Egg
  3. Prepare 1 Red pickled ginger
  4. Get 1 Shichimi seasoning
Steps to make Beef and Egg over Rice:
  1. Make the shirataki beef rice bowl in the frying pan.
  2. Pour the eggs into Step 1 and cover with a lid. Simmer on high heat for 30 seconds. Arrange over rice and season with shichimi spice.
