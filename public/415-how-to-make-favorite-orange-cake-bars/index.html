The ingredients needed to make Orange Cake Bars:

  2. Make ready 6 oz pkg orange jello, divided
  3. Take 4 eggs or equivalent of egg substitute
  4. Get 1/4 cup water
  5. Get 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  6. Get 1 container fluffy white frosting

Steps to make Orange Cake Bars:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease 13x9 pan.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine cake mix, 3 oz jello, water, oil, & eggs.
  3. Once mixed, pour into baking dish.
  4. Bake for 30-40 until done. Let cool completely.
  5. For frosting, mix frosting & remaining jello. Spread over cooled cake.
