Steps to make Hashbrown breakfast casserole:
  1. In a skillet, cook bacon & set aside. In same skillet cook onions & pepper in the bacon grease & also set aside.
  2. Next cook sausage & set aside.
  3. Preheat oven @ 400 °. In a 13x9in pan, mix all ingredients together, except for eggs & 1/2 of cheese. Next pour eggs over & top with rest of cheese.
  4. Cover with foil & bake for 30 - 40 minutes. Let rest for 15 minutes then serve.
