Recipe of Award-winning Breakfast casserole

To get started with this recipe, we must prepare a few ingredients. You can have breakfast casserole using 4 ingredients and 6 steps. Here is how you can achieve that.

The ingredients needed to make Breakfast casserole:
  1. Prepare Sausage
  2. Make ready eggs
  3. Make ready Shredded Cheese (I use cheddar-mozzarella)
  4. Make ready Frozen Tater Tots
Steps to make Breakfast casserole:
  1. Fry sausage into crumbles..drain fat.
  2. In large bowl crack eggs & mix well
  3. Place cooked sausage in bottom in 9x13 casserole dish. Pour raw eggs over the top of sausage. Scatter 1 cup of shredded cheese over the top of the eggs. Place tater tots on top of above ingredients. Finally place remaining cheese on top of tater tots.
  5. Bake in 350º oven for 40 minutes.
  6. Can be served with Salsa and or Sour Cream.
