The ingredients needed to make Cream Cheese Pancakes:
  1. Take fat free cream cheese
  2. Take cinnamon
  3. Make ready baking powder
  4. Prepare splenda
  5. Prepare oat bran
  6. Take vanilla or almond extract
  7. Get eggs or egg beaters
Steps to make Cream Cheese Pancakes:
  1. warm cream cheese in microwave about 30 seconds
  2. add remaining ingredients ( can add oat bran if you like ) and mix until smooth
  3. cook like a normal pancake over medium heat
