The ingredients needed to make Creamed Eggs:
  1. Get 1/4 cup Butter
  2. Take 4 tbsp Flour
  3. Make ready 1 tsp Salt
  4. Prepare 1 pinch Pepper
  5. Take 2 cup Milk
  6. Make ready 6 each Hard Boiled Eggs
Steps to make Creamed Eggs:
  1. Melt butter
  2. Add flour & seasonings
  3. Cook until bubbly & 1 min.
  4. Add milk, cook until thickened.
  5. Add chopped eggs.
