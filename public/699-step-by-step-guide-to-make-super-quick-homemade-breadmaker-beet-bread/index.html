The ingredients needed to make Breadmaker Beet Bread:
  1. Take better bread flour
  2. Get yeast
  3. Prepare multigrain granola cereal
  4. Make ready rolled oats
  5. Get ground flax seed
  6. Prepare ground cinnamon
  7. Get cloves
  8. Prepare egg
  9. Make ready honey
  10. Get beets (reserve juice)
  11. Take reserved beet juice
Steps to make Breadmaker Beet Bread:
  1. Crush/grind granola cereal into powder or small chunks.
  2. Drain beets (reserve juice).
  3. Chop beets into small pieces.
  4. Whisk egg.
  5. Put beet juice into small saucepan. Add water if needed to bring volume to 3/4 cup. Heat on low to 75-85°F.
  6. Add ingredients into Breadmaker pan in the order listed in the recipe.
  7. Add beet juice last and pour around the inner perimeter of the pan.
  8. Select French/Oat bread and light crust options.
  9. Start. Takes a little over 3 hours with my Breadmaker. Test - it may be necessary to remove about 10 minutes before end of cycle.
