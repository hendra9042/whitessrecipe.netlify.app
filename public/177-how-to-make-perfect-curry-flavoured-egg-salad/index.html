The ingredients needed to make Curry Flavoured Egg Salad:
  1. Make ready Boiled Eggs *finely or coarsely chopped
  2. Make ready finely chopped Onion
  3. Get finely chopped Parsley
  4. Make ready Japanese Mayonnaise
  5. Get Curry Powder
  6. Prepare finely chopped Pickled Cucumber *optional
  7. Take Salt & Pepper
Steps to make Curry Flavoured Egg Salad:
  1. Cut Boiled Eggs finely, coarsely, or even into chunkier pieces depending on how you eat this salad.
  2. Add all other ingredients and mix to combine. Season with Salt & Pepper to taste.
