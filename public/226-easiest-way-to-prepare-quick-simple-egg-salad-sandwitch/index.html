The ingredients needed to make Simple Egg Salad Sandwitch:
  1. Make ready butter
  2. Prepare Eggs
  3. Take Mayonaise
  4. Take Mustard
  5. Take Relish or Jalapeno (to taste)
  6. Get Bread
Steps to make Simple Egg Salad Sandwitch:
  1. Set stove top to Medium-High and melt Butter in a Small to Medium frying pan.
  2. Scramble the eggs until cooked and put the eggs in a small mixing bowl.
  3. Add Mayonnaise, Mustard, and Relish or Jalapeno's to taste. Stir ingredients until good and mixed.
  4. Scoop a heaping spoon full of the mix on some bread and enjoy.
