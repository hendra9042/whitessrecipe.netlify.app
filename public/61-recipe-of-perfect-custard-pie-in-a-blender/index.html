The ingredients needed to make Custard Pie in a Blender:
  1. Make ready 1/4 Cup Butter (room temp)
  2. Take 4 Eggs
  3. Get 3/4 Cup Sugar
  4. Make ready 1/2 Cup Flour
  5. Get 1 Teaspoon Vanilla
  6. Take 2 Cups Milk
  7. Get Pinch Salt
  8. Prepare Nutmeg
Instructions to make Custard Pie in a Blender:
  1. Preheat oven to 350F, generously butter a pie dish
  2. Add all ingredients EXCEPT nutmeg to a blender, pulse for a minute or so, until the ingredients are well combined.
  3. Pour it all into the prepared pie dish, sprinkle with nutmeg, and bake for 45 minutes. Allow it to cool in the refrigerator completely before slicing and serving.
