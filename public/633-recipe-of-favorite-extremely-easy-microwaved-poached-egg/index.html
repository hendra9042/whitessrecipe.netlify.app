The ingredients needed to make Extremely Easy!! Microwaved Poached Egg:
  1. Make ready 1 Egg
  2. Prepare 1 Ramekin
  3. Prepare 1 Water
Steps to make Extremely Easy!! Microwaved Poached Egg:
  1. Break the egg into the ramekin.
  2. Fill the ramekin with water. Pour the water gently around the edges!
  3. Microwave! It takes about 1 minute 20 seconds at 600 W in my microwave!
  4. Discard the water! It's hot, so be careful!
