The ingredients needed to make Egg Gramflour pancake:
  1. Prepare eggs
  2. Make ready gramflour
  3. Get samolina (sooji)
  4. Get medium onion (chopped)
  5. Prepare green chilli chopping
  6. Prepare coriander powder
  7. Make ready garam masala
  8. Get turmeric powder
  9. Prepare Salt
  10. Prepare baking soda
  11. Take Butter or desi ghee
  12. Get water
Instructions to make Egg Gramflour pancake:
  1. Take mixing bowl add gram flour, semolina and egg, mix well with help of water,and make semi-liquid batter like dosa.
  2. Add all spices, onions,chilli and mix well and the last add 1/4 tsp baking soda and mix well then rest for 5 minutes
  3. Take nonstick pan grease with ghee or butter, put batter spread evenly and cover it for 1minutes then turn it over,
  4. Same process repeat on other pancakes, take out on plate, serve hot with your favourite sauce. Enjoy healthy breakfast.
