The ingredients needed to make Cold Ramen Noodle Salad:
  1. Take servings Ramen Noodles OR Chinese Egg Noodles
  2. Make ready Cucumber
  3. Prepare Lettuce
  4. Take Eggs *cooked into thin omelet (see recipe)
  5. Get Tomato
  6. Prepare Roast Pork OR Ham
  7. Get Hot Mustard *Japanese Mustard recommended
  8. Make ready Sauce
  9. Make ready Soy Sauce
  10. Take Rice Vinegar
  11. Get Sugar
  12. Prepare Cold Water
  13. Prepare Sesame Oil
Instructions to make Cold Ramen Noodle Salad:
  1. Toppings are better to be sliced or prepared into thin strips so that easy to eat with noodles. To make sauce, combine all ingredients and mix well.
  2. Cook Ramen Noodles OR Chinese Egg Noodles as instructed. Basically cook noodles in rapidly boiling water until cooked ‘al dente’. Then drain, rinse in cold water, and drain well.
  3. Place the drained cold noodles in a deep plate or bowl, arrange toppings, pour the sauce over and enjoy with Hot Mustard if you like it.
  4. Other Topping Suggestions: Thinly sliced Carrot, Capsicum, cooked Seafood, Chicken, Wakame, etc.
