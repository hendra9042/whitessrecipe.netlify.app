The ingredients needed to make Chocolate chip oat cookies:
  1. Take 1 Cup applesauce
  2. Get 1/2 cup sugar
  3. Take 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  4. Prepare 2 eggs (substitute 1/4 cup applesauce for each egg)
  5. Make ready 1 teaspoon vanilla
  6. Prepare 1 cup whole wheat flour
  7. Prepare 1/4 cup vanilla protein powder
  8. Prepare 2 1/4 cup quick oats
  9. Make ready 1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions to make Chocolate chip oat cookies:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees and grease cookie sheets
  2. In large bowl, mix together the applesauce, sugar, brown sugar and vanilla until blended.
  3. Gradually stir in flour, protein powder and oats. Add chocolate chips.
  4. Drop a spoon size amount on cookie sheet
  5. Bake 12 to 15 minutes
