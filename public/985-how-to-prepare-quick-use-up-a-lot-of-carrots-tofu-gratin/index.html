The ingredients needed to make Use Up a Lot of Carrots! Tofu Gratin:
  1. Get Carrot
  2. Prepare Corn kernels
  3. Get Butter
  4. Prepare Silken tofu
  5. Take Egg
  6. Prepare Consomme soup stock granules
  7. Get Salt and pepper
  8. Prepare Pizza cheese
Instructions to make Use Up a Lot of Carrots! Tofu Gratin:
  1. Be sore to thoroughly drain the tofu before you proceed.
  2. Slice the carrot into thin sticks and cook in butter until tender (on medium-low heat). Add the corn and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Use a spoon to mash the tofu until smooth. Combine with the egg and consomme.
  4. Place the cooked carrot into a heat-resistant dish, pour the tofu sauce over it, and top with pizza cheese.
  5. Place in the toasteroven for 5 minutes until the cheese is nicely browned, and then it's done. Garnish with parsley for some nice color.
  6. You can make it in small ramekins as well Since the tofu sauce is full of flavor, you don't need to worry about the smell of the tofu. Try it out!
