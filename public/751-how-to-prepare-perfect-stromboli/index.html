Steps to make Stromboli:
  1. In a bowl, add Flour. Make 3 wells in flour, add salt, sugar and yeast. mix
  2. Add oil and water. knead smooth dough
  3. Transfer dough to greased bowl. cover and rest for 2 hours in warm place
  4. In a bowl, mix paneer and veggies
  5. Add pizza sauce and mix well
  6. Gently deflate dough and fold
  7. Roll the dough in rectangular shape
  8. Place mozzarella cheese towards one side leaving gap towards edges
  9. Add paneer mixture on top of cheese
  10. Add cheddar cheese on top of veggies
  11. Gently roll the dough to form a log
  12. Seal edges from all sides
  13. Place on baking tray and make equal slits halfway through dough
  14. Give egg or oil wash
  15. Sprinkle sesame and flax seeds - optional step
  16. Bake for 20-22 minutes in preheated oven at 180 deg until golden brown
  17. Sprinkle parsley on top
  18. Cut and serve warm
