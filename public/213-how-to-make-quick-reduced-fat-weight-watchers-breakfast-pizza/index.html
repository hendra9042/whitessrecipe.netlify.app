The ingredients needed to make Reduced Fat (weight watchers) Breakfast Pizza:
  1. Get crescent rolls (8)
  2. Take Simply Potatoes©
  3. Take Turkey Bacon
  4. Make ready reduced fat shredded cheddar cheese
  5. Get Egg Beaters
  6. Make ready Parmesan cheese
  7. Get salt
  8. Make ready Pepper
  9. Take Optional
  10. Make ready assorted chopped vegetables (peppers, tomatoes, green onions)
Steps to make Reduced Fat (weight watchers) Breakfast Pizza:
  1. Preheat oven to 375
  2. Layout crescent triangles in a circle/pizza shape
  3. Place potatoes on dough
  4. Cover potatoes with 1/2 cheese
  5. Sprinkle with Turkey bacon
  6. Cover with remaining cheese
  7. In a a mixing bowl combine egg beaters, salt and pepper
  8. Sprinkle with veggies *optional
  9. Sprinkle with parmesan cheese
  10. Cook for 25-30 min
  11. note if egg mixture spills over dough wipe off excess to avoid spillage in oven
