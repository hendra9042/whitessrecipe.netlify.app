The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Sheet Cake (so MOIST) and Icing:
  1. Get 2 cup Granulated sugar-Cake
  2. Get 4 tbsp Heaping choc Cocoa-Cake
  3. Take 2 Eggs- beaten-Cake
  4. Take 2 cup All purpose flour-Cake
  5. Make ready 1 tsp Vanilla extract-Cake
  6. Get 1 tsp Baking soda-Cake
  7. Take 1/4 tsp Salt-Cake
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup Vegetable oil-Cake
  9. Prepare Eggs
  10. Prepare 1 stick UNSALTED Butter (do not substitute)-Cake
  11. Prepare 1/2 cup Buttermilk-Cake
  12. Prepare 1 cup Boiling water-Cake
  13. Prepare Frosting
  14. Get 4 tbsp Heaping choc Cocoa-Icing
  15. Prepare 1 1/2 stick Butter (do not sub)-Icing
  16. Take 6 tbsp Whole milk or 3 tbsp each of whole and buttermilk-Icing
  17. Make ready 1/2 cup Chopped pecans/walnuts or candy bar (I didn't add them them this time)-Icing
  18. Take 3 cup Powder confectioners sugar-Icing
  19. Prepare 1 tsp Vanilla extract-Icing
Instructions to make Chocolate Sheet Cake (so MOIST) and Icing:
  1. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, granulated sugar, and salt.
  2. In a saucepan on stove, melt together the butter, oil and cocoa. Add boiling water, stir then allow mixture to boil for a minute, then turn off heat. Pour it over your bowl of flour mixture, and stir lightly to cool.
  3. In a measuring cup, pour the buttermilk and add beaten eggs, baking soda, and vanilla.
  4. Pour buttermilk mixture into the chocolate mixture, and stir.
  5. Pour into sheet cake pan and bake at 350°F for 20 minutes.
  6. Directions For the ICING: When cake is about 5 min from being done, start making the icing/frosting: It is important that your cake is still hot/warm when you pour the icing on it.
  7. Melt butter in another saucepan. Add cocoa, stir to combine, then turn off heat. Add the milk, vanilla, and powdered sugar. Stir together until smooth.
  8. Add the pecans/nuts, stir together, and pour and spread over warm cake. Let cool for a little bit on counter.
