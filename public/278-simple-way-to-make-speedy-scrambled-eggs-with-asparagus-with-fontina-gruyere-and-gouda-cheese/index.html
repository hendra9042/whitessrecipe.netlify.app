  4. Make ready 30 gr gruyere cheese, shredded ☆
  5. Prepare 30 gr gouda cheese, shredded ☆
  6. Get 140 g asparagus, sliced
  7. Take 1/2 onion, thinly sliced
  8. Take 1 clove garlic, minced
  9. Take Salt
  10. Get Pepper
