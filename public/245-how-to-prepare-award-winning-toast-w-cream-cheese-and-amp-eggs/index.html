The ingredients needed to make Toast w/ Cream Cheese & Eggs:
  1. Get 2 slice Bread
  2. Get 1 dash Milk
  3. Get 1 Egg
  4. Prepare 1 Spreadable cream cheese
  5. Get 1/2 tbsp Butter
  6. Get 1 dash White Pepper
  7. Prepare 1 pinch Salt
Steps to make Toast w/ Cream Cheese & Eggs:
  1. Put both slices of bread into the microwave for 4 minutes
  2. At the same time, heat your pan to medium heat and spread your butter around the pan
  3. Crack you egg into a small bowl and add your salt, pepper and milk and whisk to combine
  4. Scramble the eggs on the pan
  5. When done, place eggs and toasted bread on plate, and spread cream cheese on toast
