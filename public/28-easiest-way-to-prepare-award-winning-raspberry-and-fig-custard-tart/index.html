The ingredients needed to make Raspberry and fig custard tart:
  1. Prepare 1 each pie crust baked
  2. Make ready Custard
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup cane sugar (or more to taste if not using honey drizzle))
  4. Get 3 tbsp cornstarch
  5. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  6. Make ready 4 egg yolks
  7. Take 3 cup whole milk
  8. Make ready 2 tbsp butter
  9. Get 2 tsp vanilla extract
  10. Make ready 1/4 tsp cinnamon (optional)
  11. Make ready 8 each black mission figs
  12. Make ready 1 cup fresh raspberries
  13. Prepare 1 tbsp warm honey (optional)
Steps to make Raspberry and fig custard tart:
  1. Whisk together sugar, cornstarch, salt and cinnamon.
  2. Add milk and whisk.
  3. Add egg yolks and whisk.
  4. Put all in pan on medium heat and continue whisking until thickens. (Up to 20 minutes)
  5. Remove from heat and add butter and vanilla extract.
  6. Allow to cool.
  7. Pour custard into baked pie shell, cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for two hours.
  8. Slice figs into fours.
  9. Place figs and raspberries on tart.
  10. Drizzle with warm honey and serve.
