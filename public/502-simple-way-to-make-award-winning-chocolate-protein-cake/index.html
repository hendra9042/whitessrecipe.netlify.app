The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Protein Cake:
  1. Prepare 1 cup unsweetened chocolate almond milk
  2. Make ready 2 cup liquid egg whites
  3. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking powder
  4. Get 4 tsp sucralose based sweetener (sugar substitute)
  5. Make ready 4 tbsp cocoa powder (unsweetened)
  6. Prepare 4 scoops chocolate protein powder
  7. Prepare 1 oz 1.4 ounce dark chocolate
Steps to make Chocolate Protein Cake:
  1. Combine ingredients. Stir. Put in cake pan. Bake at 375°F for 25 minutes.
