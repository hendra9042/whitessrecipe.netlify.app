The ingredients needed to make Asian Veggie Noodles:
  1. Take white onions, diced
  2. Prepare big tomato, grated
  3. Get garlic clove, minced
  4. Get tomato sauce
  5. Prepare egg noodles, cooked
  6. Get heavy cream
  7. Take Salt,
  8. Get Black pepper spice
  9. Prepare dried basil
  10. Get dried thyme
  11. Prepare Vodka
  12. Take Vegetables
  13. Prepare red cabbage
  14. Make ready medium carrots, grated
  15. Make ready green, yellow and red bell peppers
  16. Take peas
  17. Get olives
Steps to make Asian Veggie Noodles:
  1. Cook onions and garlic in 1 tablespoon oil in a large skillet until onions are translucent.
  2. Add the grated tomato and leave until simmered.
  3. Add tomato sauce, vodka, black pepper, dried basil and thyme. Let simmer for 2 minutes.
  4. Add all vegetables except olives. Put the cover on and leave for 15 mins until they are well cooked. Don't forget to stir.
  5. Stir well and add heavy cream. Add salt to taste. Turn off the heat.
  6. Put in the noodles and olives and stir well.
  7. Add parmesan to taste. Enjoy!
