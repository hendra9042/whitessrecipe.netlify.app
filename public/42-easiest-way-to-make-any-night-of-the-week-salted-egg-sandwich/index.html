The ingredients needed to make Salted Egg Sandwich:
  1. Take Slices french bread/ baguette
  2. Prepare 1 salted egg
  3. Make ready 1 large tomatoe
  4. Make ready leaves Cooked spinach
  5. Prepare Mozarella
  6. Get Ground pepper
  7. Make ready Paprika
Instructions to make Salted Egg Sandwich:
  1. Lay or arrange sliced bread on a baking pan. Drizzle with olive oil or spread mayonaise (optional)
  2. Cook spinach on a non stick frying pan. Add the spinach on the bread.
  3. Next, put some mozzarella.
  4. Slice the tomato as a circle. Top it on the mozarella.
  5. Slice the salted egg and top it on the tomato.
  6. Sprinkle pepper and paprika.
  7. Toast for 5 min. Or until mozarella melted.
  8. Enjoy!
