Steps to make Egg salad sandwich:
  1. Roughly mash eggs with fork
  2. Add salt and sugar
  3. Add mayonnaise
  4. Add soy sauce (optional)
  5. Squeeze lemon juice and mix everything
  6. Lay a lettuce leaf on a slice of bread, add the egg salad. Add another lettuce and cover with another bread slice.
