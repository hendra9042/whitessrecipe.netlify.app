The ingredients needed to make Tartar Sauce for Fried Food:
  1. Take 2 Boiled eggs
  2. Get 1/4 Onion
  3. Get 20 grams Pickled cucumber
  4. Get 3 tbsp Plain yogurt
  5. Prepare 6 tbsp Mayonnaise
  6. Get 1 tsp Ketchup
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp Parmesan cheese
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp Dried parsley
  9. Take 1 dash Salt and pepper
Steps to make Tartar Sauce for Fried Food:
  1. Finely chop the pickles, onions, and eggs.
  2. Sprinkle 1 teaspoon of salt (amount separate from listed ingredients) onto the onions and let sit. Then, rinse and squeeze out the water.
  3. Mix all the ingredients together and adjust the taste with salt and pepper to finish.
  4. It's great on fried oysters, fried shrimp, and fried squid rings!
  5. It's also delicious on a baguette.
