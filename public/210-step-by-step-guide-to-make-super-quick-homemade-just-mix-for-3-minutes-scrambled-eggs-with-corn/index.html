The ingredients needed to make Just Mix for 3 Minutes Scrambled Eggs with Corn:
  1. Take Eggs
  2. Make ready Milk (or soy milk)
  3. Prepare Corn kernels
  4. Take Sliced cheese (melting or non-melting type)
  5. Take Salt and pepper
  6. Make ready Vegetable oil
Instructions to make Just Mix for 3 Minutes Scrambled Eggs with Corn:
  1. Beat eggs in a bowl, add milk and season with salt and pepper. Tear the cheese into small pieces.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the eggs and corn and mix round and round…
  3. When the eggs start to settle and you can see the bottom of the frying pan…
  4. …turn down the heat to low, add the cheese on top of the eggs so they don't touch the pan, and mix rapidly. When the surface of the egg is no longer wet and it's fluffy, it's done.
