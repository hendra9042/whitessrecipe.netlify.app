The ingredients needed to make quick and easy Breakfast casserole:
  1. Take 1 cup bisquick
  2. Get 2 cup milk
  3. Make ready 6 eggs
  4. Get 1 tsp season all
  5. Prepare 1 packages real bacon pieces
  6. Take 1 lb turkey or pork sausage
  7. Take 1 packages shredded cheese of your choice

Steps to make quick and easy Breakfast casserole:
  1. pre heat oven@ 350
  2. fry sausage
  3. mix eggs and milk,add bisquick and season all
  4. drain sausage and put as first later in cassarole dish
  5. pour egg mixture over sausage
  6. sprinkle cheese until covered
  7. sprinkle bacon pieces over cheese
  8. bake for 40 mins
  9. can be prepared the night before and put in fridge overnight for the morning
