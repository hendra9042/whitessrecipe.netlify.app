The ingredients needed to make BC Skinny Benny:
  1. Prepare 2 poached eggs
  2. Prepare 6 oz Smoked Sockeye Salmon Lox
  3. Make ready Chili Sauce
  4. Make ready 1/4 cup Guacamole
  5. Prepare 2 Organic Quinoa Crisp Bread
Instructions to make BC Skinny Benny:
  1. Poach two eggs to desired ft doneness
  2. Spread guacamole onto each flatbread crisp, layer lox on top
  3. Add chili sauce to taste, poached egg and top with remaining lox. Garnish with cilantro and herbs.
  4. Enjoy
