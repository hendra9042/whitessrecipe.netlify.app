The ingredients needed to make Egg Buns/roll:
  1. Make ready 2 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  2. Take 1 1/2 tbsp sugar
  3. Prepare 1 1/2 tbsp butter
  4. Take 1 tsp milk flavor (optional)
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking powder
  6. Get 1/4 tsp salt
  7. Prepare 1/3 cup water
  8. Take 5 boiled eggs
  9. Make ready Veg oil for deep frying
  10. Take I tbsp powdered milk (optional)
Instructions to make Egg Buns/roll:
  1. Add all dry ingredients to sifted flour in a bowl and mix
  2. Add butter and incorporate into the flour
  3. Add water gradually and mix till a soft dough is formed
  4. Allow dough to rest for 10min
  5. Transfer dough to work surface and knead for 1-2min. Divide the dough into 4 or 5. Flatten each ball
  6. Place the boiled egg in the center
  7. Wrap the egg and pinch the edges to combine. Gently roll again in between your palms to form a smooth round ball
  8. Do the same with the remaining egg and deep fry in hot oil over medium heat until golden brown
  9. Enjoy!
