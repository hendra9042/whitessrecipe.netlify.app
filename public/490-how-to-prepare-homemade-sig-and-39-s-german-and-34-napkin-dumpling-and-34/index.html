The ingredients needed to make Sig's German "Napkin Dumpling":
  1. Take 400 grams soft white bread
  2. Take 500 ml full cream milk
  3. Make ready 4 eggs
  4. Make ready 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  5. Get 1 pinch salt or salt substitute
  6. Get 1 table napkin (cloth) or other cotton cloth of same thickness.
  7. Get 1/2 tsp salt or salt substitute for boiling dumpling
  8. Prepare 1 pinch sugar and cinnamon mixed for the sweet version only
Steps to make Sig's German "Napkin Dumpling":
  1. Chop the bread slices into equal small chunks 1. Mix all other ingredients and pour over the bread that you have placed in a bowl.
  2. Fill two pots one large one medium with water one big enough to hold your dumpling, the other to boil and sterilise your cloth .Bring to simmer both pots. Sterilise your cloth in the smaller pot.
  3. When the egg/milk liquid is all soaked up into the bread, (on a tray to avoid to much mess ), remove cloth and roll the bread mass into the sterilised ( make sure cloth is cool enough to touch) napkin, sausage shape . Securing tightly with string each end. Do not roll the dumpling itself in to tight as it needs to expand.
  4. In the larger pot boil add the salt for simmering the dumpling.
  5. Hang the knotted ends of the dumpling over a wooden spoon and gently place the dumpling into the simmering salted water for 45 minutes
  6. Remove and leave to drain for a while . Remove carefully from cloth and leave to cool slightly. Slice into thin portions either to serve as a sweet or to add to a savoury meal.
