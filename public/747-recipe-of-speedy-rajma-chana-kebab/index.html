The ingredients needed to make Rajma Chana Kebab:
  1. Take boiled black chana (chickpeas), mashed (makes 10 kebabs)
  2. Take boiled rajma (kidney beans), mashed
  3. Prepare egg
  4. Take salt
  5. Take lime juice
  6. Take coriander leaves, chopped
  7. Get flax seed
  8. Get red chilli flakes
  9. Take roasted cumin powder
  10. Get garam masala powder
  11. Get oil for shallow frying
Steps to make Rajma Chana Kebab:
  1. In a bowl, combine all the above mentioned ingredients (except oil) and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  2. Divide into equal portions and form into kebabs. Refrigerate agan for some time.
  3. Heat oil and shallow fry them few at a time till golden brown on both sides. Drain on a kitchen towel.
  4. Serve with tomato sauce or green chutney.
