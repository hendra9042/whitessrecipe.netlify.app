The ingredients needed to make Ham and Cheese Toasted Roll Ups:
  1. Make ready soft sandwich bread, crusts removed
  2. Take thin slices deli ham, I used hot spicy deli ham
  3. Get extra sharp cheddar cheese about 1/4 inch thick and the length of the bread, see direction step #3
  4. Prepare butter
  5. Prepare large egg
  6. Take milk
  7. Get my creamy mustard sauce, found in my profile and in search
Instructions to make Ham and Cheese Toasted Roll Ups:
  1. Flatten bread with a rolling pinn
  2. Place 2 thin ham slices on each rolled bread slice
  3. Add the cheddar cheese on the end of bread
  4. Roll bread upin a tight roll
  5. Combine egg and milk in a dish that can fit rolls, dip bread rolls in egg/milk mixture to coat
  6. Heat butter in skillet. Cook rolls starting seam side down until golden an cheese is melting
  8. Serve with creamy mustard sauce
