The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Pancakes:
  1. Get plain flour
  2. Get baking powder
  3. Prepare milk
  4. Prepare salt
  5. Prepare ground almonds
  6. Prepare egg
  7. Get butter, melted plus some extra for cooking
  8. Prepare Topping of your choice
Steps to make Breakfast Pancakes:
  1. Add all ingredients to a bowl and beat together to create a batter.
  2. Heat a frying pan over a medium heat with a knob of the extra butter.
  3. Once hot, ladle in the batter and cook on the first side until lots of little bubbles appear on the surface, then flip and cook for a minute or two on the other side, until golden brown.
  4. Serve with the topping of your choice.
