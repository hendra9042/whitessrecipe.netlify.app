The ingredients needed to make Scrambled Eggs with Spinach and Cherry Tomatoes:
  1. Prepare 6 eggs
  2. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  3. Get 1/2 tsp pepper
  4. Get 2 cup chopped spinach
  5. Prepare 6 cherry tomatoes, halved
  6. Get 1 tbsp oil (they used fermented garlic oil)
  7. Take 1 onion, diced

Steps to make Scrambled Eggs with Spinach and Cherry Tomatoes:

  3. Pour the eggs to the pan and use a wooden spatula to scramble the eggs.
