The ingredients needed to make Scrambled eggs with basil and butter:
  1. Get 2 large eggs
  2. Make ready 2 Tbsp. butter
  3. Get 2 Tbsp heavy cream
  4. Get 2 oz. shredded cheese
  5. Take 2 Tbsp fresh basil or 2 tsp. dried basil

Scrambled Eggs and butter toast are ready to serve! Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Pour in egg mixture; cook, stirring constantly, until eggs reach the desired consistency. I never added sour cream to scrambled eggs but Hubs and I found we enjoyed it.

Instructions to make Scrambled eggs with basil and butter:
  1. Melt butter in a small pan over medium heat
  2. Whisk gently eggs, heavy cream, shredded cheese and seasonings in a bowl.
  3. Pour egg mixture into the pan of melted butter. Stir with a spatula from the edge towards the center until eggs are scrambled.
  4. If you prefer it soft and creamy, stir on a lower temperature until desired consistency.
  5. Top with fresh basil.
