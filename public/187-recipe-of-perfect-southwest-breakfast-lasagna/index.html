Steps to make Southwest breakfast lasagna:
  1. Spray a large baking dish we'll place 2 tortillas on bottom
  2. Combine egg beaters with 1 cup of low fat half and half
  3. Combine onions, peppers, mushrooms, ham and sausage in a large mixing bowl
  4. Place half of mix for 1st layer
  5. Pour half of egg mixture distribute evenly
  6. Cover with half of cheese
  7. Repeat process for 2nd layer
  8. Cover bake for 1 hr at 375
  9. Divide up into servings mine was 8. Add low fat sour cream or plain Greek yogurt with salsa or pico de gallo
