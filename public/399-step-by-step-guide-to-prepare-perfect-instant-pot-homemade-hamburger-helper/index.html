The ingredients needed to make Instant Pot Homemade Hamburger Helper:
  1. Get ground beef
  2. Take large can cream of chicken
  3. Get chopped garlic
  4. Make ready beef broth
  5. Get Salt, pepper
  6. Get velveeta cheese
  7. Get shredded cheddar cheese
  8. Make ready sour cream
  9. Prepare egg noodles
Instructions to make Instant Pot Homemade Hamburger Helper:
  1. Set your IP to saute. Saute ground beef, garlic, s&p until done. Add egg cream of chicken and mix. Add egg noodles. DO NOT MIX. Add beef broth until noodles are covered. Set IP to manual for 5 minutes. Do a quick release unless you have time then do 5 minute natural release then quick release. Add 4 slices of velveeta and shredded cheese. Mix well. Add sour cream and mix. Serve with your favorite side!! This is so good and kid approved!!
