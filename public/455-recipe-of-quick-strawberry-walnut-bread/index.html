The ingredients needed to make Strawberry Walnut Bread:
  1. Make ready 3 cup Rice flour
  2. Make ready 1/2 tsp xanthum gum
  3. Prepare 2 cup sugar substitute, such as splenda
  4. Get 1 tbsp ground cinnamon
  5. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  6. Take 1 tsp salt
  7. Take 4 eggs (or equivalent in egg substitute)
  8. Take 1 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  9. Prepare 10 oz frozen, sliced strawberries, thawed (do not drain)
  10. Take 1 1/4 cup chopped walnuts

Steps to make Strawberry Walnut Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease 1 loaf pan.
  2. Combine flour, xanthum gum, sugar, cinnamon, baking soda, & salt.
  3. Whisk eggs in small bowl, then add eggs & oil to mixture. Stir just until blended.
  4. Fold in undrained strawberries & walnuts.
  5. Spoon into pan.
  6. Bake for 70 minutes. Let cool.
