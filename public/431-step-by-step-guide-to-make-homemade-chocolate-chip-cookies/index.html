The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. Take 2 1/4 cups gluten-free flour (or reg flour)
  2. Make ready 1/4 tsp xanthum gum (if using reg flour, don't need this ingredient)(if your gluten-free flour mixture already includes xanthum gum, you don't need to add more)
  3. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Take 1 cup butter, softened
  6. Get 3/4 cup sugar substitute, such as splenda (or granulated sugar)
  7. Take 3/4 cup coconut sugar or brown sugar
  8. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla
  9. Get 2 eggs or equivalent of egg substitute
  10. Make ready 12 oz semi-sweet chocolate chips
Instructions to make Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. In med bowl, stir together flour, xanthum gum, baking soda, & salt. Set aside.
  3. In large bowl, beat butter, sugar substitute, coconut sugar, & vanilla until creamy. Add eggs, beat well. Gradually add flour mixture, beating well. Stir in chocolate chips.
  4. Drop by rounded spoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  5. Bake for 8-10 minutes or until lightly browned.
  6. Cool completely before storing in airtight container.
