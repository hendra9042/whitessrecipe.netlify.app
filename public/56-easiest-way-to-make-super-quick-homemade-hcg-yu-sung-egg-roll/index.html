The ingredients needed to make HCG Yu Sung Egg Roll:
  1. Take 4 oz chicken
  2. Prepare 3 each cabbage leaves
  3. Take 1 cup shredded cabbage
  4. Take 1/2 cup green onion
  5. Make ready 1/2 tbsp garlic salt
  6. Take 1/2 tbsp onion powder
  7. Get 1 ginger root
  8. Make ready 1 soy sauce
Instructions to make HCG Yu Sung Egg Roll:
  1. steam cabbage leaves for 7 minutes.
  2. remove and steam shredded cabbage for 7minutes
  3. cook chicken until done. chop up into little pieces
  4. combine all ingredients.
  5. wrap in leaves
