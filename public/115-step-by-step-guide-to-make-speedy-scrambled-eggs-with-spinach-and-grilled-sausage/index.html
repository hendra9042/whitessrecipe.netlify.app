Instructions to make Scrambled Eggs with Spinach and Grilled Sausage:
  1. Grill / saute beef sausages until cooked through.
  2. In a food processor, combine eggs, milk, spinach, onion, garlic, red pepper, black pepper, and salt. Pulse until mixed well and spinach is chopped. ghee in large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Melt ghee in large skillet over medium heat.
  4. Add sausages and egg mixture to skillet.
  5. Stirring constantly, cook until all liquid is absorbed and eggs are cooked through. Serve.
