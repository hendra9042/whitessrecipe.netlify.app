The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Take Tubes of Jimmy Dean sausage
  2. Get chorizo or andouille sausage
  3. Make ready Lg individually wrapped ham steaks chopped into cubes
  4. Get Lg bag frozen southern hash browns thawed
  5. Get shallots chopped
  6. Make ready minced garlic
  7. Get mini sweet peppers chopped
  8. Take rotel drained
  9. Take Eggs
  10. Take shredded cheese of your choice
  11. Make ready Chives
  12. Make ready Old bay seasoning
  13. Take Salt and pepper
Steps to make Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Cook sausages, shallots, garlic and sweet peppers together. Reserve 1 cup of mixture and set aside.
  2. Cook southern hash browns in the oven until they are slightly brown. Salt and pepper to season.
  3. Mix eggs, salt and pepper, old bay, and chives to season.
  4. In a large baking dish layer sausage mixture, southern hash browns, ham, rotel and 1 pkg of cheese. Repeat once more ending in cheese and chives.
  5. Pour egg mixture over the top of the ingrdients.
  6. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 min or until eggs are set. Add remaining bag of cheese and reserved sausage mixture and spread evenly over the top of casserole. Bake for 15 min more.
  7. Let cool for 15 min. Serve and enjoy.
