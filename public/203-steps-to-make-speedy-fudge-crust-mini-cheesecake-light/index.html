The ingredients needed to make Fudge Crust Mini Cheesecake (light):
  1. Take Fudge vanilla wafers
  2. Prepare Stevia in the raw
  3. Get Smart balance butter (light)
  4. Get Cream cheese (fat free)
  5. Take Stevia in the raw
  6. Prepare Vanilla extract
  7. Prepare Egg beaters
Instructions to make Fudge Crust Mini Cheesecake (light):
  1. PREHEAT oven to 300°F - Mix wafer crumbs, 3/4 tbs stevia and butter. - Press mixture onto 4-1/2" springform pan - BAKE 7 min
  2. BEAT cream cheese, 1/4 cup stevia and vanilla with electric mixer till well blended. - Slowly add egg on low - Pour batter into pan
  3. OPTIONAL: Add some stir-in ideas, pour over crusts
  4. Place pans on baking sheet - BAKE 40 to 45 min or until centers are almost set. - Cool before removing rims - Refrigerate for at least 4 hrs
