The ingredients needed to make Century egg and minced Pork Congee:
  1. Prepare Ground pork
  2. Get Century eggs
  3. Make ready Uncooked rice
  4. Prepare Water
  5. Get Salt
  6. Get some Black pepper
  7. Make ready some Pepper salt
Instructions to make Century egg and minced Pork Congee:
  1. Rinse the rice (roughly 3 times). Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes.
  2. Peel century eggs, chop into small pieces.
  3. Cook and mash ground pork in the pot.
  4. Add water, uncooked rice into the pot. Put the lid on but don’t close it completely. Boil with medium heat then turn down to low heat, simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Put only 1/3 of the century eggs, salt and pepper into the pot, mix, continue to simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Finally, add the rest of the century eggs, pepper salt, mix gently. Turn off the heat.
