The ingredients needed to make Truffle purple sweet potato salad:
  1. Take 2 pcs purple sweet potato
  2. Get 1/4 cup corn
  3. Make ready 1 large egg
  4. Get 1 medium cucumber
  5. Get 4 slices breakfast ham
  6. Take 1/3 cup Japanese mayonnaise
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp mustard (optional)
  8. Make ready 1 tsp sugar (adjust on preference)
  9. Get Pinch truffle salt
  10. Make ready 1-2 tsp truffle oil (desired flavour and smell)
  11. Get Pinch white ground pepper

Steps to make Truffle purple sweet potato salad:
  1. Gather all ingredients, to start peel the sweet potatoes and cut them into 1/2 inches and bring them to boil until fork tender.
  2. Drain the sweet pototoes and and put back on the stove without the water for the moisture to evaporate. Alternatively put on kitchen paper towels to absorb the excess water.
  3. Cut the cucumber into thin slices then soak in salt water for about 10-15 minutes until slightly softened. Wash and drain thoroughly to remove exess salt water. You may squeeze it gently with kitchen paper.
  4. Cut the breakfast ham into small size.
  5. Mix the mayonnaise, mustard, pepper, sugar, pinch of truffle salt (don't put too much since cucumber has the slightly saltiness in it), truffle oil and boil egg (make sure to mash it well and mix with all the rest of ingredients.
  6. Combine all the ingredients in the sauce mix it gently to avoid breaking the potatoes into small pieces. Note: you can use a pea size portion of wasabi paste instead for more flavorful taste.
