The ingredients needed to make Low-Carb Breakfast, Hearty:
  1. Take 6 large eggs
  2. Make ready 2/3 cup ham or spam
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese shredded
  4. Make ready To taste your favorite hot sauce
  5. Get 2 tablespoons diced pickled jalapeno peppers optional
Instructions to make Low-Carb Breakfast, Hearty:
  1. Cube the meat, then dry dry the ham till just crispy.
  2. Beat 6 eggs and pour over the meat. Cook till done, add jalapenos if using, turning as needed. Add hot sauce and cheese and serve. I hope you enjoy!
