The ingredients needed to make Dinner Rolls:
  1. Take Milk
  2. Get Sugar
  3. Take Yeast
  4. Prepare Egg
  5. Take Butter
  6. Get Salt
  7. Make ready All-purpose flour
  8. Get Oil
  9. Prepare Egg
  10. Prepare Milk
Steps to make Dinner Rolls:
  1. Add the first 6 ingredients in a bowl and mix well.
  2. Now add All-purpose flour 350g and make a dough. (you may feel that your dough is a bit sticky but in the next step, you will get rid of the stickiness in your dough.
  3. Add 1 tsp of oil and knead again to get rid of the stickiness.
  4. Now make balls out of the dough and press gently. Arrange all the rolls on a greased baking pan and keep aside for an hour or two.
  5. After the dough has rise mix 1 tbsp milk with 1 egg.
  6. Now apply the egg and milk mixture on the top portion of the rolls and bake in a preheated oven at 180¬∞C for 18 - 20 mins
  7. Remove from oven, Serve and Enjoy.
