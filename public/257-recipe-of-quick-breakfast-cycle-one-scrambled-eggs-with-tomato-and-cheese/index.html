The ingredients needed to make Breakfast: Cycle One: Scrambled eggs with tomato and cheese:
  1. Prepare butter
  2. Take white potato
  3. Make ready cherry tomatoes halved
  4. Make ready green onions chopped
  5. Get eggs beaten
  6. Prepare feta cheese
  7. Prepare spinach
Steps to make Breakfast: Cycle One: Scrambled eggs with tomato and cheese:
  1. Dice potatoes and microwave 3 to 4 minutes until soft
  2. In medium frying pan melt butter over medium heat. Cook potato and green onions for 5 to 6 minutes.
  3. Mix eggs and scramble. Add tomatoes and greens and cook. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Top with feta and serve.
