The ingredients needed to make Egg Roll:
  1. Make ready 2 eggs
  2. Take 1 chapati
  3. Make ready 1 sliced onion
  4. Take 1 Sliced capsicum
  5. Make ready 1 tsp tomato sauce
  6. Get 1 tsp mayonnaise
  7. Get As needed Grated cheese
  8. Make ready To taste Salt
Instructions to make Egg Roll:
  1. In a bowl break the eggs add salt and whisk well.
  2. Heat oil in a skillet add the eggs mixture and make on omelette
  3. Put the chapati over it, top it with the rest of the egg mixture and flip and cook both sides.
  4. Now place the egg chapati on a clean surface,spread tomato sauce, mayonnaise, also place onion, capsicum and top with cheese and roll
  5. Heat the skillet over medium heat, place the egg roll over skillet, sprinkle light brush the oil all sides of roll and cook lightly brown, nice and crisp.
