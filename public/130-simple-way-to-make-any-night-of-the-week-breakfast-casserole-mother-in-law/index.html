The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Casserole (Mother In Law):
  1. Take Everything
  2. Get Condensed Cream of Chicken (10.75 oz)
  3. Get sour cream
  4. Make ready Frozen Hash Browns
  5. Make ready ham
  6. Prepare onion, chopped
  7. Get green pepper
  8. Make ready red pepper
  9. Take shredded cheddar cheese
  10. Get butter, melted
  11. Make ready eggs
  12. Take pepper
  13. Take sausage (browned)
Steps to make Breakfast Casserole (Mother In Law):
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Lightly grease 9x13 baking dish
  3. Season beaten eggs with pepper, pour into baking dish
  4. In a large bowl, combine soup, sour cream, hash browns, ham, cheese, mix and pour over eggs
  5. Bake uncovered for 30 mins, remove, and put butter on top (more cheese is better). Return to oven and bake (up to another 30)
