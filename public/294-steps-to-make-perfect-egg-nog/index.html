The ingredients needed to make Egg Nog:
  1. Prepare 4 each egg yolks
  2. Take 1/3 cup sugar
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp sugar
  4. Prepare 1 cup heavy cream
  5. Take 1 tsp nutmeg
  6. Take 4 each egg whites
Steps to make Egg Nog:
  1. Beat egg yolks until they lighten in color.
  2. Add sugar slowly.
  3. Add milk, cream, and nutmeg.
  4. Beat egg whites until sift peaks for add tbsp sugar until peaks stiffen.
  5. Whisk together, chill, and serve.
