The ingredients needed to make Egg, Natto and Onion with Shirataki Noodles:
  1. Get 1 bag Shirataki noodles
  2. Make ready 1/4 Onion
  3. Make ready 1 ● Egg
  4. Make ready 1 bag ● Natto (fermented soy beans)
  5. Make ready 2 tsp ● Mentsuyu
  6. Take 1 sheet Nori seaweed
Instructions to make Egg, Natto and Onion with Shirataki Noodles:
  1. Finely chop the onion.
  2. Put the shirataki noodles in a pan with water. Bring to a boil, and boil for 3 minutes. Drain well, and cut up in to easy to eat lengths.
  3. Mix the chopped onion, drained shirataki noodles, egg, natto and mentsuyu together very well. Top with shredded nori seaweed and it's done!
