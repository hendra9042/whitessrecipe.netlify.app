The ingredients needed to make Rise and Shine Casserole:
  1. Make ready ground sausage
  2. Take 32oz frozen diced hashbrowns
  3. Make ready onion, chopped
  4. Take shredded cheese
  5. Make ready eggs
Instructions to make Rise and Shine Casserole:
  1. Cook sausage and drain
  2. Spray 9x13 pan with nonstick cooking spray. Preheat oven to 425°
  3. Pour package of frozen cubed hashbrowns in the pan
  4. Add onions or peppers to taste
  5. Pour sausage evenly over the top of the hashbrowns and top with as little or as much shredded cheese as you like.
  6. Beat 8 eggs in a bowl and pour evenly over the shredded cheese
  7. Bake for 50-60 minutes. If the cheese is getting too brown, place aluminum foil over the top for the remaining cooking time.
