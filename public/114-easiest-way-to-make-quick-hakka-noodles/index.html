The ingredients needed to make Hakka Noodles:
  1. Take chicken boiled
  2. Prepare noodles
  3. Take carrot & capsicum chopped
  4. Get cabbage
  5. Take garlic minced
  6. Prepare ginger grated
  7. Get eggs
  8. Make ready spring onion
  9. Prepare oil
  10. Prepare vinegar
  11. Take soya sauce
  12. Prepare pepper powder
  13. Get Salt
Steps to make Hakka Noodles:
  1. Boil noodles with salt & water keep aside. Boil chicken & shred. Make an omelette & cut into strips.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add ginger garlic & saute. - Add white onion, capsicum & carrot & saute. - Add chicken, cabbage & egg strips & cook for a minute.
  3. Add pepper, soya sauce, salt, vinegar & mix. - Add noodles & toss. - Cook for few minutes. Serve hot.
