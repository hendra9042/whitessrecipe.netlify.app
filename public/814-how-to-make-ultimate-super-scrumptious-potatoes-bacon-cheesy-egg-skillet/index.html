Instructions to make Super scrumptious potatoes,bacon cheesy egg skillet:
  1. Chop up bacon into small chunks and place into unheated skillet
  2. Next cut up potatoes into chunks and set aside in a bowl
  3. Cut up onion into small chunks and add to bowl with potatoes
  4. Dice up tomatoe and add to bowl
  5. Melt butter in macrowave and mix into bowl. Add dash of salt,pepper and garlic. Mix together well and set aside
  6. Cut up green onion and set aside.
  7. Scrambled two eggs with dash of salt and pepper into a bowl and set aside
  8. Turn on skillet medium high and cook bacon for about 8 mintues to lightly browned and crispy
  9. Very carefully add bowl of potatoes, onion,tomatoe mixture into bacon and grease in skillet. Cook 10 to 14 mintues untill potatoes are soft with crisp edges. Stirring often
  10. Add egg mixture into pan and cook egg for about 1 and 1/2 mintues untill cooked through . Stirring often
  11. Turn heat to simmer. Add cheese and cover with lid for 3 minutes
  12. Add green onion on top and eat up!!
