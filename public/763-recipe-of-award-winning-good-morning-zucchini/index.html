The ingredients needed to make Good morning, Zucchini!:
  1. Prepare 1/2 Zucchini
  2. Get 1/4 onion, chopped
  3. Get 1 eggs
  4. Prepare 1/4 green onion
  5. Prepare 1/2 tbsp butter
  6. Take 1 tbsp vinegar
  7. Take 500 ml boiling water
  8. Take 1/4 tbsp salt
  9. Prepare 1/3 grano padano cheese
