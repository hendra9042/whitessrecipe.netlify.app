Instructions to make Custard pudding:
  1. Preheat oven at 170C.
  2. Caramel : Put the sugar in a pan. Add 1 table spoon of water and melt sugar. Simmer it. When the edge becomes yellow, move it from time to time. - Add one table spoon of water and mix it.
  3. Pour the caramel into the pudding cup.
  4. Pour milk in a pan. Add sugar and boil it at low heat. Put eggs in a bowl and mix it well. Add the milk to the egg mixture.
  5. Strain egg mixture with a strainer.
  6. Pour it in each pudding cup.
  7. Put the pudding cups on the baking plate.. Pour hot water in the baking plate.
  8. Bake it in a 170C oven for 40 minutes.
