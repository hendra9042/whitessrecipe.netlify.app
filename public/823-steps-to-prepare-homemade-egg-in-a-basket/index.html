The ingredients needed to make Egg In A Basket:
  1. Get Large Egg
  2. Prepare Piece of bread (I used multi grain)
  3. Make ready Cracked Pepper
  4. Make ready Thyme Seasoning
  5. Make ready Garlic Seasoning
  6. Make ready Tub Butter
  7. Make ready Shot Glass
  8. Prepare Spatula
  9. Get Medium to Large Skillet
Steps to make Egg In A Basket:
  1. Using a large shot glass cut out a circle from the center of bread.
  2. Melt about 3 large spoonfulls of butter in medium sized skillet over medium low heat.
  3. Soak butter into one side of toast then flip and crack an egg in the middle.
  4. Add seasonings to taste (I used thyme, garlic seasonings, and cracked pepper. It also is good made with italian seasoning and seasoned salt instead).
  5. Cover with lid and let cook slowly until top of egg looks less runny and the breads underside looks toasted. Then flip and continue cooking uncovered for another minute or so.
  6. Your finished product should look something like this. If you don't prefer over easy eggs then be sure to cook it covered for a little while longer before serving.
