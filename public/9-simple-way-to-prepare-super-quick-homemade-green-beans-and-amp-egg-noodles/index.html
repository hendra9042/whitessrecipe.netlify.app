The ingredients needed to make Green Beans & Egg Noodles:
  1. Prepare 1 package egg noodles (16 ounce)
  2. Make ready 1 (1 lb) package ground beef
  3. Take 1 onion, chopped
  4. Prepare 1 green pepper, chopped
  5. Make ready Fresh minced garlic (6 cloves)
  6. Get 2 tablespoons margarine
  7. Prepare 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  8. Take 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  9. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  10. Make ready 1 (16 ounce) package frozen cut green beans, thawed
  11. Make ready 2 tablespoons margarine, added at end of recipe
  12. Make ready Parmesan cheese (sprinkle)
Steps to make Green Beans & Egg Noodles:
  1. In a large pot brown your ground beef, drain off any fat…
  2. Place your chopped onion, green pepper and minced garlic into pot with ground beef, sautÃ© until veggies are tender…..
  3. Now add : margarine, black pepper, oregano, and Italian seasoning, sautÃ© all well…..
  4. Next add your bag of thawed green beans, stir well….Heat on very low heat…..
  5. Meanwhile boil your egg noodles until they are done, drain well…
  6. Add cooked egg noodles to pot of ground beef and veggies, cook on very low heat until everything is mixed well…..I also added 2 more tablespoons of margarine to the ground beef / egg noodle mixture….
