The ingredients needed to make Egg Roll:
  1. Get 3 egg
  2. Make ready crushed cheese
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp onions chopped
  4. Get 1 tbsp tomato chopped
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp coriander leaves
  6. Get 1 tbsp capsicum chopped
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp broccoli chopped
  8. Prepare as needed Some olive chopped
  9. Make ready to taste Salt
  10. Get As needed Mixed herbs
  11. Make ready 1 pinch chilli flakes
  12. Make ready As needed butter
Instructions to make Egg Roll:
  1. Firstly, take a bowl Heat crack eggs and beat it.
  2. Take a nonstick pan, add some butter, add egg spread it, then it cook after 1 min spread onions, tomato, capsicum, chilli herbs, olive, cheese and other ingredients.
  3. Roll it slowly, transfer in the tissue,Garnish It with your favorite sauce, Sprinkle some black pepper.
  4. Now serve it quickly.
