Steps to make Oreo baked donuts:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C or 365°F.
  2. Mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl whisk the milk, egg, vanilla extract, and applesauce.
  4. Combine the wet ingredients and the dry ingredients together.
  5. Add in the finely chopped Oreos.
  6. Pour the batter into a piping bag. And fill the molds.
  7. Bake for 10 mins or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean or with moist crumbs. And let it slightly cool.
  8. Melt the white chocolate in the microwave. In a separate bowl crush the rest of the Oreos.
  9. Dip the donuts in the white chocolate, then in the crushed Oreos. And place on a cooling rack.
  10. Enjoy while it's still slightly warm.
