Steps to make overnight sausage egg casserole (crock pot):
  1. Spray crock pot with nonstick cooking spray.
  2. Press potatoes into bottom of pot and about 3" up the sides.
  3. Whisk together eggs, milk, cheese, and seasonings.
  4. Crumble sausage into egg mixture and stir.
  5. Cover and cook on low 8 hours… or until you wake up.
