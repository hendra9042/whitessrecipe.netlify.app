The ingredients needed to make Chicken & Vegetable Vermicelli Soup - Sotanghon / Glass Noodle Soup:
  1. Get Chicken - chopped or shredded or your favorite parts
  2. Prepare 2 handfuls vermicelli / bihon / thin glass noodles / cellophane noodles - already softened
  3. Prepare 3-4 cups water or chicken broth
  4. Make ready 1 chicken broth cube (if not using liquid broth)
  5. Make ready 1 medium onion, chopped
  6. Take 3-4 garlic cloves, chopped
  7. Make ready Handful Carrots, julienned
  8. Make ready Few leaves of Cabbage, julienne then chop
  9. Get Moringa drumsticks, peeled (optional)
  10. Get Handful green beans, chopped
  11. Prepare 1-2 Tbsp fish sauce (patis)
  12. Prepare Salt
  13. Prepare Cooking oil
  14. Prepare Top with Garnish: (optional)
  15. Get Boiled egg, halved or quartered
  16. Prepare Fried chopped garlic
  17. Prepare Chopped green onions

Steps to make Chicken & Vegetable Vermicelli Soup - Sotanghon / Glass Noodle Soup:
  1. Saute the garlic and onions in oil.
  2. Add the chicken, frozen is okay. Run it through the tap first before adding to the pot.
  3. Add a few tablespoons water, a good amount of salt and cover. Turn chicken, to cook on all sides, until it changes color.
  4. Add the chicken broth or water+broth cube. You can already add the hard vegetables. I added the drumstick seeds and green beans. Bring to a boil.
  5. Add the fish sauce or patis. Add the rest of the vegetables and the noodles. The noodles should be pre-softened before adding here. - - Bring to a boil, taste and adjust seasoning as desired.
  6. Turn off heat and serve immediately. Garnish with toppings. - - Note: the noodles may/will absorb most of the soup if not consumed right away. ‚ò∫Ô∏è
