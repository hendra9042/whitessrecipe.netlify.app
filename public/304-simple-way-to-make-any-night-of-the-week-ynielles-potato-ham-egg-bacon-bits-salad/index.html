The ingredients needed to make Ynielle’s Potato Ham Egg Bacon Bits Salad:
  1. Get Medium boiled potatoes
  2. Take mayo
  3. Get all-purpose cream
  4. Take mustard dressing
  5. Make ready ground black pepper
  6. Make ready Sliced Cooked Ham
  7. Prepare Bacon bits
  8. Prepare boiled eggs mashed
  9. Get Basil (up to you how much basil you want to add)
  10. Prepare Parmesan cheese
Steps to make Ynielle’s Potato Ham Egg Bacon Bits Salad:
  1. Boil potatoes for about 3-5 minutes. Let it cool down and then cut into cubes. Slice the ham and toast together with bacon bits. Mash the eggs.
  2. Mix all ingredients with mayo and cream. Then let it chill for about an hour and it’s ready to serve!
