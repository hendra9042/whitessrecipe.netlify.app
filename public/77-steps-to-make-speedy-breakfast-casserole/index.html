Instructions to make breakfast casserole:
  1. Cube bread and put in bottom of greased pan
  2. Brown and drain sausage
  3. mix hot sauce, half n half, dry mustard, salt, pepper, and eggs
  4. layer sausage over bread
  5. pour mix over top of sausage and bread
  6. cover with sliced potatoes and then cheese
  7. cover and refrigerate over night
  8. bake @ 350 for about 30 min or center is firm
