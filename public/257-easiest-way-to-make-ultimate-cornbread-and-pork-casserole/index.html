The ingredients needed to make Cornbread and Pork Casserole:
  1. Get ground pork
  2. Prepare Chili———
  3. Take small onion diced
  4. Get ground cumin
  5. Take granulated garlic powder
  6. Prepare ground paprika
  7. Get celery salt
  8. Make ready salt
  9. Get can of field peas
  10. Take can of tomato soup
  11. Take milk
  12. Take Cornbread————–
  13. Get self-rising cornbread mix
  14. Take milk
  15. Get salt
  16. Take granulated garlic powder
  17. Get ground paprika
  18. Take large organic duck eggs
  19. Take sugar
  20. Prepare vegetable oil

  22. Take shredded cheddar cheese

  25. Prepare pickled jalapeño peppers
  26. Make ready sour cream
Instructions to make Cornbread and Pork Casserole:
  1. Preheat oven 400 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Mix the pork and onions with the chili spices listed and brown.
  3. Add the field peas sauté for 7 minutes
  4. Add the tomato soup and milk
  5. Cook 10 minutes
  6. Mix the cornbread ingredients and make a dough. It will be just a bit sticky
  7. Add a third of the dough to the bottom of a casserole dish I used a 10 inch deep dish pan
  8. Add all the chili
  9. Add half the cheese
  10. Add the vegetarian beans
  11. Add the rest of the dough on top
  12. Add the remaining paprika and cheese
  13. Bake for 45 minutes to an hour
  14. Serve with toppings I hope you enjoy!!!!
