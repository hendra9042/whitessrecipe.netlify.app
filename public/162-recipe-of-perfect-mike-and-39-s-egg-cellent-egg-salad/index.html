The ingredients needed to make Mike's Egg-cellent Egg Salad:
  1. Take Egg Salad Sandwich
  2. Prepare 10 large Eggs [use older eggs - use 8 - 2 meant for garnish]
  3. Take 2 tbsp Chives [chopped]
  4. Get 1 large 1/2 Claussens Pickle Spear [or 2 medium - diced]
  5. Prepare 1/8 tsp Garlic Powder
  6. Get 1 tsp Yellow Mustard
  7. Take 1 tsp Dried Parsley
  8. Get 1/4 cup Celery [minced]
  9. Make ready 1/3 cup Mayonnaise
  10. Take Sides & Garnishments
  11. Make ready Bread
  12. Make ready 2 large Sliced Eggs
  13. Take Paprika
  14. Take Crispy Lettuce
  15. Make ready Claussens Chilled Pickles
Instructions to make Mike's Egg-cellent Egg Salad:
  1. Bring eggs to boil. Turn off heat. Leave eggs to sit in pot for 18 minutes covered. Rinse in cold water immediately to stop cooking process.
  2. Chop vegetables.
  3. Peel eggs and dry them. Then, mash.
  4. Add veggies, mayonnaise, mustard, garlic powder and salt and pepper to taste. Mix well. Chill in fridge for an hour covered.
  5. Spread on fresh bread or bagels of your choosing. Garnish with lettuce, sliced eggs and paprika if desired.
