  2. Take egg
  3. Prepare turkey ham
  4. Get butter
  5. Make ready sweet white bread
  6. Make ready salt and pepper
  7. Make ready garnish
  8. Prepare Italian herbs
  9. Make ready thousand islands sauce
  10. Prepare Cheddar cheese
