The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Prepare pico de gallo
  2. Make ready 1% low fat cottage cheese
  3. Get low fat shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  4. Make ready cooked Turkey sausage (precooked)
  5. Make ready Egg Beaters (use Southwest Style)
  6. Get simply shredded hash browns (use southwest)
Steps to make Breakfast Casserole:
  1. preheat oven to 350°
  2. Spray a 13x9 pan with Pam.
  3. mix hash browns with 1 tablespoon of butter and press into bottom of pan.
  4. Place pan into a 375° oven for 20 to 30 minutes until potatoes begin to brown.
  5. While waiting for potatoes, brown sausage and shred.
  6. Mix pico de gallo, Cheddar cheese and cottage cheese. Once blended add the Egg Beaters.
  7. Combine the browned sausage with the mixture.
  8. Once potatoes are slightly brown pour mixture into pan and return to oven for 30 to 35 minutes. Cook thoroughly.
