The ingredients needed to make Egg Nog Creme Brulee:
  1. Prepare 2 cup Egg Nog
  2. Take 1/2 cup Heavy Cream
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup Sugar (white)
  4. Make ready 1 tsp Fresh ground Nutmeg
  5. Get 5 medium Eggs yolks
Instructions to make Egg Nog Creme Brulee:
  1. Preheat oven to 325. Then in a small sauce pan combine egg nog, heavy cream,sugar, and nutmeg.
  2. Next take 5 egg yolks and whisk till combined
  3. Heat liquid mixture until bubbles just appear on the sides of the pan. Remove these to ensure a smooth texture.
  4. Temper the hot liquid into egg mixture (do this by slowly pouring the hot liquid into the bowl of eggs while continually whisking)
  5. Take combined mixture and pour into ramekins
  6. Place the ramekins in a larger vessel and pour booking water around the ramekins. Enough water to reach at least 3/4 of the way up the ramekins.
  7. Bake at 325 for 40minutes, or until the edges are set and the middle is still slightly jiggly
