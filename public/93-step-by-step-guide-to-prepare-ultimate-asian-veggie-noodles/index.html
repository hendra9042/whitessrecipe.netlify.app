The ingredients needed to make Asian Veggie Noodles:
  1. Make ready 2 white onions, diced
  2. Prepare 1 big tomato, grated
  3. Prepare 1 garlic clove, minced
  4. Make ready 3 Tbsp tomato sauce
  5. Make ready 1 bag egg noodles, cooked
  6. Prepare 300 ml heavy cream
  7. Get to taste Salt,
  8. Prepare 1 teaspoon Black pepper spice
  9. Prepare 1 teaspoon dried basil
  10. Take 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  11. Take 3/4 cup Vodka
  12. Take Vegetables
  13. Make ready 2 cups red cabbage
  14. Get 3 medium carrots, grated
  15. Prepare 2 cups green, yellow and red bell peppers
  16. Make ready 1/2 cup peas
  17. Take 1/2 cup olives
Instructions to make Asian Veggie Noodles:
  1. Cook onions and garlic in 1 tablespoon oil in a large skillet until onions are translucent.
  2. Add the grated tomato and leave until simmered.
  3. Add tomato sauce, vodka, black pepper, dried basil and thyme. Let simmer for 2 minutes.
  4. Add all vegetables except olives. Put the cover on and leave for 15 mins until they are well cooked. Don't forget to stir.
  5. Stir well and add heavy cream. Add salt to taste. Turn off the heat.
  6. Put in the noodles and olives and stir well.
  7. Add parmesan to taste. Enjoy!
