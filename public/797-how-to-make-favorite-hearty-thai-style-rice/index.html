The ingredients needed to make Hearty Thai-style Rice:
  1. Prepare 300 grams Ground pork (or pork/beef mix, ground chicken)
  2. Take 1/2 Minced onions
  3. Get 5 cm Minced carrots
  4. Take 2 rashers Minced bacon
  5. Make ready 2 clove Minced garlic
  6. Get 1 tsp Thinly sliced Japanese red chili pepper
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp Sesame oil
  8. Prepare 4 tbsp Soy sauce
  9. Prepare 2 1/2 tbsp Sugar
  10. Make ready 1 tbsp each Mirin and sake
  11. Get 1 Lettuce
  12. Take 1 one per serving Onsen tamago (poached egg in its shell) a fried egg with a runny yolk
Steps to make Hearty Thai-style Rice:
  1. Heat sesame oil in a frying pan, and fry the red peppers and garlic. Once it's fragrant, add the onions and carrots, and continue frying.
  2. Once the onions have wilted, add the ground meat, and fry some more. Add the bacon once the meat browns, and cook together.
  3. Once everything is well fried, add the soy sauce, sugar, sake, and mirin, and boil down the liquid until a little juice remains.
  4. Scatter lettuce on a plate, arrange the rice, and add the ingredients. Add the onsen tamago or half-cooked egg on top.
  5. Mix it all up and enjoy!
