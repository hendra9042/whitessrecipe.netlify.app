The ingredients needed to make Coffee Creamer Pasta Carbonara:
  1. Take 1.5 mm pasta (boiled for 5 minutes)
  2. Take Bacon (cut in half)
  3. Get Egg (medium)
  4. Make ready Grated cheese
  5. Take Coffee creamer (5 g type)
  6. Prepare Salt
  7. Make ready Black pepper
  8. Take Mizuna greens
Instructions to make Coffee Creamer Pasta Carbonara:
  1. Cook the pasta. I used in a microwavable container for 9:30 minutes (30 seconds less than you usually would).
  2. While cooking the pasta, cut the bacon into 1 cm pieces and cook in a frying pan with oil until crispy. Turn off the heat and let cool.
  3. Combine the egg, grated cheese, and coffee creamer in a bowl and mix well.
  4. Add the cooled bacon to Step 3, then add the salt and pepper and mix together.
  5. Once the pasta is done cooking, add to Step 4 while still hot and quickly toss.
  6. Transfer to a plate, then sprinkle black pepper on top. Garnish with chopped mizuna and serve.
