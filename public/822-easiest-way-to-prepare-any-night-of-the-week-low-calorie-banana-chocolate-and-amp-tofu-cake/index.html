The ingredients needed to make Low-Calorie Banana, Chocolate & Tofu Cake:
  1. Make ready 150 grams ☆Silken tofu
  2. Take 1 ☆Egg
  3. Prepare 70 grams ☆Sugar
  4. Get 7 drops ☆Vanilla oil (if you have it)
  5. Get 100 grams ＊Cake flour
  6. Make ready 20 grams ＊Cocoa powder
  7. Get 3 grams ＊Baking powder
  8. Get 2 Ripe bananas
Instructions to make Low-Calorie Banana, Chocolate & Tofu Cake:
  1. Mix together the ☆ ingredients (in order) till smooth.
  2. With cutting motions, mix together the ＊ ingredients with a spatula. Then mix in the mashed bananas with the backside of a fork.
  3. Coat a mold with oil and then bake for 30 to 40 minutes at 180℃. Cover with a moistened and tightly wrung out towel, and then with some plastic wrap and let it cool. Store it in the refrigerator.
