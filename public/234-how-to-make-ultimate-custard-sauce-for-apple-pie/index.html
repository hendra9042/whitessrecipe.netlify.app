The ingredients needed to make Custard Sauce for Apple Pie:
  1. Make ready Egg yolk
  2. Make ready Sugar
  3. Make ready Milk
  4. Take less than 1 teaspoon Liqueur (Calvados)
Instructions to make Custard Sauce for Apple Pie:
  1. Mix together the egg yolk and sugar in a bowl until it becomes white.
  2. Put the milk in a pot and heat until it is just about to boil. Pour half into the bowl from Step 1 and mix well.
  3. Pour in the remaining milk and mix well. Return the entire mixture to the pot.
  4. Heat on medium heat while stirring until it is thick enough to draw a figure 8.
  5. When it has thickened, remove from the heat and cool in an ice water bath.
  6. Stir while cooling in the ice water bath. When it has cooled, add the liqueur.
  7. Top an apple pie with the sauce.
