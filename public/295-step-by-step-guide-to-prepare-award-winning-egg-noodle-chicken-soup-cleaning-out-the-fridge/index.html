The ingredients needed to make Egg noodle/Chicken Soup (Cleaning out the fridge):
  1. Take leek
  2. Get carrots
  3. Make ready potatoes
  4. Make ready chicken breast
  5. Get egg noodles
  6. Prepare salt
  7. Get black pepper
  8. Get chicken
Instructions to make Egg noodle/Chicken Soup (Cleaning out the fridge):
  1. Prepare ingredients. Use any chicken without bones.
  2. Peel potatoes and carrots, and cut into small pieces. Cut chicken into bite size.
  3. Boil water in a large pot. Add salt, black pepper, carrots, potatoes, and chicken. Boil. Then add egg noodles. Stir to avoid egg noodles from sticking.
  4. Skim the foam from the surface. Add chicken cube. Stir.
  5. Add leek. Stir until leek gets soft.
