The ingredients needed to make Meatball Noodle Soup:
  1. Prepare tomato paste
  2. Get chili garlic paste
  3. Take beef broth
  4. Take five spice
  5. Make ready bay leaves
  6. Get garlic powder
  7. Make ready ginger powder
  8. Prepare onions, juilenned
  9. Prepare white peppercorn, crushed
  10. Make ready Water
  11. Get Cilantro, chopped (optional)
  12. Take Pickled cabbage (optional)
  13. Get spiced tofu, cubed (optional)
  14. Take Noodles, cooked + drained
  15. Take Meatballs
  16. Take firm tofu, cooked + crumbled
  17. Prepare shiitaki mushrooms, finely chopped
  18. Take wood ear mushrooms, finely chopped
  19. Get fish sauce
  20. Take sesame oil
  21. Take soy sauce
  22. Take eggs
  23. Prepare barley
  24. Make ready Beyond plant-based ground meat or substitute
  25. Make ready panko
  26. Make ready cornstarch
  27. Prepare Salt to taste
Instructions to make Meatball Noodle Soup:
  1. Preheat oven to 375F. In a large mixing bowl, combine all meatball ingredients. Shape mixture into balls and place on to greased baking sheet. Bake for approximately 25 mins until slightly brown. Set aside when done.
  2. In a large pot heat oil, tomato paste, and chili garlic paste. Add broth, onions, bay leaves, five spice, garlic powder, ginger powder, and peppercorn. Boil until onions soften. Add water as needed.
  3. Place cooked noodles into bowls. Top with meatballs, cilantro, cabbage, and tofu. Pour in boiling stock.
