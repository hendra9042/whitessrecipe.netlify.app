The ingredients needed to make Nana's brunch casserole:
  1. Make ready white bread cubed
  2. Make ready homo milk
  3. Prepare 18% cream
  4. Make ready shredded cheddar cheese
  5. Take eggs beaten
  6. Take breakfast sausage
  7. Prepare cream of mushroom soup

Steps to make Nana's brunch casserole:
  1. Butter the inside of a baking dish
  2. Slice the bread into cubes, add to baking dish
  3. Brown the sausages in frying pan, remove, cut into 3rds. Add on top of bread
  4. Wisk eggs, cream, and milk. Pour into casserole dish over cubed bread.
  5. Shred the cheese, add to top of casserole.
  6. Pour can of mushroom soup over casserole. Spread so it covers entire surface.
  7. Keep in fridge overnight.
  8. Preheat oven to 350. Bake for 1 hour. Enjoy!
