The ingredients needed to make Mike's Egg-cellent Egg Salad:
  1. Take Egg Salad Sandwich
  2. Make ready large Eggs [use older eggs - use 8 - 2 meant for garnish]
  3. Prepare Chives [chopped]
  4. Prepare large 1/2 Claussens Pickle Spear [or 2 medium - diced]
  5. Make ready Garlic Powder
  6. Take Yellow Mustard
  7. Take Dried Parsley
  8. Make ready Celery [minced]
  9. Get Mayonnaise
  10. Make ready Sides & Garnishments
  11. Prepare Bread
  12. Take large Sliced Eggs
  13. Make ready Paprika
  14. Prepare Crispy Lettuce
  15. Get Claussens Chilled Pickles
Steps to make Mike's Egg-cellent Egg Salad:
  1. Bring eggs to boil. Turn off heat. Leave eggs to sit in pot for 18 minutes covered. Rinse in cold water immediately to stop cooking process.
  2. Chop vegetables.
  3. Peel eggs and dry them. Then, mash.
  4. Add veggies, mayonnaise, mustard, garlic powder and salt and pepper to taste. Mix well. Chill in fridge for an hour covered.
  5. Spread on fresh bread or bagels of your choosing. Garnish with lettuce, sliced eggs and paprika if desired.
