The ingredients needed to make Tomatoes Fried with Egg and Cheese Noodles:
  1. Prepare 1/2 tsp oil
  2. Prepare 2 tomatoes
  3. Take 1 egg
  4. Take 2 pieces low fat cheese
  5. Get 1 1/3 bowls noodles
  6. Take 1 chicken meat
  7. Get 1 little black pepper
Steps to make Tomatoes Fried with Egg and Cheese Noodles:
  1. Prepare all the ingredients and mixed the egg with a little water
  2. Cut the tomatoes into pieces first and cut the tomatoes into a small pieces that looked like a square shape. After that, cut the chicken meat into a small pieces too
  3. Add the tomatoes and chicken into the egg and add the black pepper
  4. Add the mixture to the frying pan, add 1 piece of cheese to the pan and let it melt
  5. Cooked the noodles with water until it becomes soft and add 1 piece of cheese and melt it with the noodles. Finally, the dish is ready to serve now! Enjoy :)
