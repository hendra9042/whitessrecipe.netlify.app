The ingredients needed to make Egg Noodles with Meatballs in Alfredo Sauce:
  1. Prepare Meatballs———
  2. Take ground sirloin
  3. Take panko breadcrumbs
  4. Get milk
  5. Make ready salt
  6. Get chopped parsley flakes
  7. Get egg
  8. Make ready ground black pepper
  9. Take Rest of needed ingredients———-
  10. Make ready Alfredo sauce see my recipe
  11. Prepare egg Noodles
  12. Get water to boil noodles
  13. Prepare salt for water to boil noodles
  14. Take extra Virgin olive oil
  15. Take oil to fry the meatballs
  16. Make ready panko breadcrumbs for topping
  17. Make ready melted butter for breadcrumbs
  18. Get chopped parsley flakes
Steps to make Egg Noodles with Meatballs in Alfredo Sauce:
  1. Preheat oven 400 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Soak the milk and half cup breadcrumbs
  3. Mix the soaked breadcrumbs with the rest of the meatball ingredients.
  4. Form into meatballs and fry
  5. Make Alfredo sauce see one of my recipes or buy a jar pre-made sauce.
  6. Boil the noodles
  7. Mix the sauce and noodles
  8. Pour over the top of the meatballs.
  9. Mix the melted butter and half cup of breadcrumbs with the parsley flakes.
  10. Sprinkle over the top of the noodle mixture.
  11. Bake in oven for 20-25 minutes. Let rest 7 minutes
  12. Serve, I hope you enjoy!
