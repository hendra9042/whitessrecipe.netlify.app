The ingredients needed to make Easter Breakfast Casserole üê£:
  1. Make ready 1 pack bacon
  2. Get 1 yellow onion, chopped
  3. Prepare 6 eggs, beaten
  4. Get 1 bag frozen hash brown potatoes (I used a 30 ounce bag)
  5. Take 1 (8 ounce) bag shredded cheddar cheese
  6. Take 1 (16 ounce) container cottage cheese
  7. Take 1 (8 ounce) block Swiss cheese(chopped into cubes)
  8. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon parsley flakes
  9. Take Sprinkle Black Pepper
  10. Get Sprinkle salt
  11. Make ready Butter cooking spray
Steps to make Easter Breakfast Casserole üê£:
  1. In a large pan cook bacon and onion together until bacon is crisp and onions are wilted…..Drain grease……Let bacon and onion cool before adding to the rest of the ingredients…….

  3. Add wet ingredients to hash brown potatoes and gently mix together, then bake, uncovered in a 350 degree oven for 1 hour or until casserole is lightly browned on top……. I added an extra sprinkle of parsley flakes to the top of my casserole……
