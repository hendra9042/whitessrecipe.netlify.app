The ingredients needed to make Egg Salad For Sandwiches:
  1. Prepare 12 eggs
  2. Get 2/3 cup miracle whip
  3. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  4. Get 1/4 tsp season salt
  5. Make ready 1/4 tsp celery salt
  6. Get 1/4 tsp garlic salt or powder
  7. Make ready 1/4 tsp onion powder or 1 tsp. minced onion
  8. Take 1/4 tsp dill weed
  9. Make ready 2 tbsp pickle relish, drained

Instructions to make Egg Salad For Sandwiches:
  1. Peel and dice eggs with egg slicer, pastry blender or knife and fork.
  2. Add remaining ingredients and stir until well blended.
  3. Refrigerate.
  4. Put egg salad mixture on bread on in buns.
  5. Top with lettuce.
