The ingredients needed to make Flat Bread with Poach Egg:
  1. Prepare For flat bread :
  2. Prepare 150 gr plain flour
  3. Prepare 6 tbsp plain yoghurt
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  5. Take Pinch salt
  6. Make ready 2 eggs
  7. Take For salad :
  8. Make ready 50 gr pickle melon
  9. Prepare 50 gr pickle tomato
  10. Get 50 gr tin sweet corn
  11. Take 1 tbsp mayonnaise/ dressing salad
  12. Make ready Pinch salt and black pepper
  13. Make ready 1 red chilli cayenne for garnish
  14. Take 1 pcs avocado for garnish asside
Steps to make Flat Bread with Poach Egg:
  1. Put flour in the bowl and add pinch of salt mixing with spoon.. adding the wet ingredient until going to combine and dough with bread consistency.. set asside
  2. Cut as you like the salad and combine together season well..
  3. Heat the non stick pan out grill pan.. Cut the dough to be 2 and roll over until thin enough to be a circle.. and put in the hot pan for 3/4 minute each side until cook..
  4. For pouch eggs boil the water and add 1 tsp of vinegar.. stir the boil water for ready pouch the egg.. pouch about a minute or two..
  5. Slice Avocado and chilli cayenne for complete the dish.. enjoy your meal!!
