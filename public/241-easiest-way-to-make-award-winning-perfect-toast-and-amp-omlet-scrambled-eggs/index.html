Steps to make Perfect toast & omlet/scrambled eggs:
  1. To make the toast, heat 1tspn of oil in the pan n spread it well
  2. now put some chatmasala all over the pan and place the bread on it
  3. let it stay for about 30 seconds and then flip it
  4. To make the omlet, take 2 eggs, add some onions, origano, coriander, stem garlic, baking soda and redchilli powder(as per needed) n wisk it all togather
  5. heat 2tbspn oil on the pan and simply pour the mixture to make an omlet
  6. For scrambled egg keep mixing the mixture in the pan till cooked properly
