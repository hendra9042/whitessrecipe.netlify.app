The ingredients needed to make Tilapia Dinner:
  1. Make ready talpia fillet (uncooked)
  2. Make ready crushed korn flakes
  3. Prepare flour
  4. Prepare corn startch
  5. Make ready table spoons egg whites (1egg)
  6. Prepare teaspoon. Knorr chicken bullion
  7. Get seafood seasoning
  8. Take parsley flakes
  9. Take onion powder
  10. Prepare cup. Frying oil (i use canola)
  11. Take Veggie
  12. Take string green beans
  13. Get salted butter
  14. Prepare garlic
  15. Get cooked white rice
  16. Prepare fresh salsa
  17. Take sliced mini cucumbers
Instructions to make Tilapia Dinner:
  1. Put oil to heat in a shallow skillet over med heat
  2. In a separate Bowl mix crushed Corn Flakes chicken bullion, seafood seasoning and
  3. Onion powder and parsley flakes.
  4. On a plate add your flour and corn startch can add 1 tsp salt
  5. In a bowl mix egg white
  6. Take your fish and coat with flour mixture evenly both sides.
  7. Dip in egg evenly coat fish
  8. Remove from egg and place on top of the corn flakes mixture and evenly coat.
  9. Carefully place fish in hot oil
  10. Fry for 2-3 min on each side till golden. Place on a wire rack to cool
  11. Saute green Beans in butter on skillet with garlic
  12. Plate fish topped with salsa with a side white rice, green beans and sliced cucumber with lemon wedge
