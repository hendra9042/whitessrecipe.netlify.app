The ingredients needed to make Roll out egg nog cookies:

  2. Prepare 1/4 tsp baking powder

  5. Take 16 tbs butter (2sticks)
  6. Get 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  7. Make ready 1/4 cup brown sugar
  8. Get 1 egg
  9. Take 1/2 cup egg nog

Instructions to make Roll out egg nog cookies:
  1. In a mixer bowl, beat softened butter, sugar, bone sugar, until fluffy. Add egg.
  2. In separate bowl, using a whisk or fork combine the flour, baking powder, nutmeg and salt.
  3. Add dry mix into butter mix. Mix on low.
  4. Add eggnog, mix until combined well, don't over mix.
  5. Chill dough for at least an hr.
  6. Roll out, cut shapes, bake on 375F for 8-10 minutes.
