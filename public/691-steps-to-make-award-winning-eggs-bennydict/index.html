The ingredients needed to make Eggs Bennydict:
  1. Take 2 each Crumpets
  2. Make ready 2 slice Bacon
  3. Take 2 each eggs
  4. Prepare 1 egg yolks
  5. Take 1 tbsp lemon juice
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp butter
  7. Take cayenne pepper
  8. Prepare dash vinegar
  9. Prepare water
Steps to make Eggs Bennydict:
