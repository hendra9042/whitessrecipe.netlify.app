Steps to make Healthy breakfast omelette:
  1. Wash the green beans and tomato
  2. Chop them up
  3. Slice the turkey
  4. In a bowl, add the beans, tomato and turkey
  5. Crack the egg in the bowl
  6. Mix them all up
  7. Pour them in a frying pan
  8. Cook until the omelette is done
  9. Add the grill spices on top for more taste
  10. Enjoy!
