The ingredients needed to make Breakfast French Toast:
  1. Prepare eggs
  2. Get milk
  3. Prepare orange juice
  4. Prepare vanilla extract
  5. Take cinnamon powder
  6. Make ready Bread for French toast
  7. Make ready Butter cooking spray
  8. Prepare Maple syrup
  9. Take Confectioners sugar (sprinkled over top)
Steps to make Breakfast French Toast:
  1. In a large bowl combine : eggs, milk, orange juice,vanilla extract and cinnamon powder, mix all with a whisk…..
  2. Get your skillet ready by spraying it with the butter cooking spray and leave it on low heat…..
  3. Next slice your bread (Iâ€™m using a Brioche loaf) into 2 inch thick slices….
  4. Add one slice of bread at a time to your egg mixture, making sure to coat both sides of bread then immediately place that coated bread slice into your hot skillet…..
  5. Cook on medium heat until both sides of your bread turns golden brown…..
