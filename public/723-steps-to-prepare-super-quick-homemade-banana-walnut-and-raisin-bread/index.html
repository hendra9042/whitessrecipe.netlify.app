The ingredients needed to make Banana walnut and raisin bread:
  1. Get 125 grams butter softened
  2. Get 2 large eggs
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup caster sugar
  4. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Prepare 3 tbsp maple syrup
  6. Make ready 2/3 tsp ground cinnamon
  7. Make ready 5 lady finger bananas
  8. Get 1/3 cup walnuts
  9. Take 2 tbsp poppy seeds
  10. Prepare 1/4 cup LSA(ground linseed soy and almond)
  11. Take 1/2 cup plain flour
  12. Prepare 3/4 cup selfraising flour
  13. Take 1 pinch sea salt
  14. Take 1/2 tsp bicarbonate soda
  15. Prepare 1/3 cup raisins

Steps to make Banana walnut and raisin bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 180°c
  2. in an electric mixer with beater attachment cream butter, sugar, maple and vanilla
  3. add eggs one at a time beating well after each addition
  4. with mixer on slow add mashed banana till incorporated
  5. while still slowly mixing add remainder of ingrediants till just combined
  6. pour into greased and lined large loaf tin
  7. cook for approx 40 min or till starting to brown on top
