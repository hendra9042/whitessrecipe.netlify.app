The ingredients needed to make Soft Potato Donuts:
  1. Prepare mashed potato, I boiled the potato and mashed them
  2. Prepare flour high pro
  3. Make ready egg yolk
  4. Take teaspoon yeast
  5. Prepare sugar
  6. Take salt butter
  7. Prepare fresh milk
  8. Get To taste salt (optional)
  9. Prepare Cooking oil
  10. Prepare Topping
  11. Prepare powdered sugar
  12. Make ready butter
  13. Prepare white sweetened condensed milk
  14. Get To taste Vanilla powder (optional)
  15. Prepare Chocolate
  16. Get Grated cheese
Instructions to make Soft Potato Donuts:
  1. Mix flour, sugar, and yeast. Add egg yolk mashed potato, fresh milk, butter and salt.
  2. Knead the dough until it doesn't stick to the bowl. Then cover the dough with a clean fabric or plastic food wrap (I use clean fabric) and let the dough rest for 10 minutes so that the dough rises.
  3. After 10 minutes, knead the dough for a while and then cut the dough into the same size. I cut them into 13 pieces.
  4. Make the dough that has been cut into rounds, cover the dough use clean fabric and let the dough rest for 45 minutes until the dough is rising.
  5. After 45 minutes, press the rounds dough slowly, so that the air in the dough comes out, and make a small hole in the midle. Or to make it easier use donut mold.
  6. In the pan, heat cooking oil and deep fry rounds dough on medium heat until brown, and turn over until another side brown also. Don't turn it over often because it will absorb the oil 1. For topping : Make whipped cream from powdered sugar, butter, Vanilla powder, white sweetened condensed milk.
  7. When the donuts are cold, spread the whipped cream and then dip them into the cheese or chocolate. Topping is optional.
