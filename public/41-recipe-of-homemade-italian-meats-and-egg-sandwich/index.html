The ingredients needed to make Italian Meats and Egg Sandwich:
  1. Get 4 slices cotto salami luncheon meat
  2. Make ready 1/3 pound pepperoni
  3. Make ready 2 large eggs
  4. Prepare 1 teaspoon chopped parsley flakes
  5. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon basil
  6. Prepare As needed tomato slices
  7. Take To taste salt
  8. Make ready To taste ground black pepper
  9. Take 3 slices bread
  10. Prepare 1/4 pound Bacon
  11. Make ready 1/3 pound hot Italian sausage
  12. Make ready 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  13. Prepare 1 medium sized kosher pickle sliced lengthwise
Instructions to make Italian Meats and Egg Sandwich:
  1. Toast the bread and fry the bacon crispy.
  2. .
  3. .
  4. Slice the pepperoni thin. Form a big sausage pattie and fry
  5. Heat the pepperoni. Set aside.
  6. .
  7. Beat the eggs and add cheese, parsley, salt and pepper. Steam the eggs till done. Set aside.
  8. To steam pour the egg mixture out flat and spread it out let cook 2 minutes with a lid. Add water and cover this will cause it to steam the top then after 3 minutes flip it to brown the top.
  9. Slice the tomatoes thinly season with salt, pepper, and basil.
  10. Build the sandwich starting with salami, pickles, and half the eggs.
  11. Add a slice of toasted bread. Then add the rest of the eggs, sausage pattie, tomatoes, pepperoni, and bacon.
  12. Add last slice of bread.
  13. Serve I hope you enjoy!
