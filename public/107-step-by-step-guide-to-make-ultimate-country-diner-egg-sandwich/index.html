The ingredients needed to make Country Diner Egg Sandwich:
  1. Take eggs
  2. Make ready of white bread
  3. Get of American cheese
  4. Prepare mayonnaise
  5. Get butter
  6. Prepare Salt and pepper
Instructions to make Country Diner Egg Sandwich:
  1. Fry 2 eggs over easy or well done according to preference. Add a dash of salt and pepper to the age when they are in the pan
  2. Spread mayonnaise on one piece of bread and butter on the other
  3. When eggs are done place them on the bread with a slice of cheese on top
  4. Place sandwich back on skillet and toast until golden brown
