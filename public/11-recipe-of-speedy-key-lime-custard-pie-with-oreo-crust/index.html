The ingredients needed to make Key Lime Custard Pie with Oreo Crust:
  1. Make ready 5 Egg yolks
  2. Get 1/3 cup Key lime juice
  3. Make ready 9 oz Sweet condensed milk
  4. Make ready 1 Oreo crust ( shop bought )
Instructions to make Key Lime Custard Pie with Oreo Crust:
  1. Preheat oven to 180 c
  2. Squeeze some lime juice .
  3. Beat egg yolks.
  4. Add lime juice and condensed milk.
  5. Mix all together.
  6. Pour the mixture into the crust and bake for 20-25 mins
  7. Cool down and enjoy!!!!
