The ingredients needed to make Steak egg and cheese bagel:
  1. Make ready 1 pkg breakfast steaks
  2. Take 1 pkg everything bagels
  3. Make ready 1/2 onion
  4. Make ready 1 Tbsp garlic podwer
  5. Prepare 1 Tbsp season salt
  6. Take 1 Tbs Chicago steak seasoning by grill mate
  7. Make ready 1 Tbsp butter
  8. Make ready 1 tsp olive oil
  9. Take 6 slices cheese of your choice
  10. Take 1 tsp chipotle aoli
  11. Get 1 tsp steak sauce of your choice
  12. Make ready 6 eggs
Steps to make Steak egg and cheese bagel:
  1. Slice your onion.
  2. Using a cast iron skillet on med heat. Melt butter
  3. Once butter is melted add onion and half the seasonings.
  4. Once the onions are sautéed in same pan add the steak and rest of the seasonings. Cook about 3-5 min on each side adding onion to top of steak
  5. Toast the bagels while meat is cooking
  6. Once toasted spread on the chipotle aoli on both sides
  7. Steak should be done by this time. So in a small frying pan add oil.
  8. Once heated fry an egg like you like it. I prefer overeasy
  9. Now time to assemble while egg cooks place cheese steak and onion then once egg is done egg and top with steak sauce and other part of bagel
  10. Enjoy
  11. Side note.: you can leave it open face without other part of bagel and eat it with a fork and knife. It is messy from the egg but soooo worth the extra napkins
