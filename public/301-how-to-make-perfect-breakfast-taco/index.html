The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Taco:
  1. Get Corn tortila
  2. Make ready Over easy eggs
  3. Make ready Shredded cheese (any kind you like)
  4. Prepare Tomatoes, diced
  5. Get Sour cream
  6. Take Garlic, minced
  7. Make ready Onion, chopped
  8. Make ready Ground beef
  9. Take Taco seasoning (see recipe)
  10. Take Tomato paste
  11. Take Water
  12. Make ready Salt and pepper
  13. Prepare Lime juice
Instructions to make Breakfast Taco:
  1. Saute the garlic and onion until the onion looks wilted.
  2. Add the ground beef, taco seasoning and salt. Stir until the beef cooked.
  3. Add tomato paste and water. Stir until the water evaporated.
  4. Drizzle the lime juice. Stir then taste. Add more seasoning if needed.
  5. Turn the heat off and set aside.
  6. Toast the tortilla (both side) on a pan.
  7. Move to a serving plate. Add the beef and egg. Top with cheese, tomato and sour cream. Enjoy.
  8. You can add shredded lettuce or sliced avocado if you like.
