The ingredients needed to make Lee's Breakfast Cheesy Hashbrown-Sausage Casserole:
  1. Make ready ' Preheated Oven
  2. Get Greased 8×8 Baking Dish
  3. Make ready Frozen Hashbrown Patty's (Precooked)
  4. Prepare Precooked Breakfast Sausage (crumbled)
  5. Get Olive Oil
  6. Prepare Butter
  7. Prepare Small Diced Onion
  8. Make ready Small Diced Green Bell Pepper
  9. Get Eggs
  10. Get Milk
  11. Take Garlic Minced
  12. Make ready Seasoned Salt
  13. Take Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese
  14. Make ready Black Pepper To Taste
Steps to make Lee's Breakfast Cheesy Hashbrown-Sausage Casserole:
  1. Precook the frozen hashbrown patty's to your desired crispness.
  2. Cook breakfast sausage and crumble up. Set aside.
  3. Saute diced onion and green bell pepper in olive oil and butter, until soft. Add minced garlic during the last minute or so. Set aside to cool.
  4. Whisk together eggs and milk until well incorporated. Whisk in cooled onion, bell pepper, and garlic.
  5. Line the bottom of greased baking dish, with the precooked hashbrown patty's. Cut hashbrown patty's to fit your dish if needed.
  6. Pour egg mixture all over the top of hashbrowns. Sprinkle crumbled cooked sausage over the top. Sprinkle with the shredded cheddar cheese.
  7. Bake at 350 degrees for 30-35 minutes. Let cool and slice. Will feed four people. Easily double recipe to serve more. Serve with your favorite hot sauce, sour cream, etc. Enjoy
