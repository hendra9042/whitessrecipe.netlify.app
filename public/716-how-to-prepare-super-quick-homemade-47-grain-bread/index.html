Steps to make 47 Grain Bread:
  1. Activate yeast in warm water
  2. Mix wet ingredients well
  3. Add grains
  4. Add water
  5. Add salt
  6. Add wheat flour, mix well
  7. Add white wheat flour, mixing well, until dough can be handled easily
  8. Knead 15 minutes until stretchy and silky
  9. Cover and let rise until doubled
  10. Punch down, divide into 3 loaf pans
  11. Preheat oven to 275
  12. Let rise 40 minutes
  13. Bake 2 hours or until hollow sounding when tapped
