The ingredients needed to make Chocolate biscuit cake(steamed)No maida,No cream,no eggs:
  1. Take 100 g chocolate flavoured biscuit(I have used happy happy)
  2. Make ready 4-5 plain biscuit(I have used Marie light)
  3. Make ready 1 tsp sugar
  4. Prepare 1 tsp coffee powder/ coco powder(optional)
  5. Get 60 ml milk
  6. Get 2 tsp chocolate syrup
  7. Prepare 1 tsp baking soda
  8. Get 1/2 tsp baking powder
  9. Make ready 1 tsp butter/oil

Steps to make Chocolate biscuit cake(steamed)No maida,No cream,no eggs:
  1. In a mixer add crushed biscuits,coffee powder,sugar and make it fine powder.
  2. Now add milk and grind it for 1 mintue.
  3. Add baking powder,soda and mix everything well.
  4. Take a cake tin/vessel that's base is equal in shape and grease it with oil/butter.
  5. Pour the batter into vessel and tap twice.
  6. Take a pressure cooker add 2 glasses of water and pre heat it for 10 mintues.
  7. Place the cake vessel/tin.(Put a stand under it so that it doesn't fall off in the water).
  8. Cover it with plate/aluminium foil and steam it for 30-35 minutes in medium flame.
  9. Check the cake if it's cooked well by using a stick/spoon. Let it become cool
  10. Transfer the cake upside down to a plate
  11. Now add chocolate syrup (I have used Hershey's) and spread well into the cake.
  12. Keep it in fridge for 5-10 mintues. Your easy chocolate biscuit cake is ready to eat!!!

  14. Note: covering the cake is must if u are steaming the cake:) or else it will become soggy.
