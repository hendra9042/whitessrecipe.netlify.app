Steps to make Delicious Egg Sandwich w/Bacon:

  2. Put butter in the pan. Fry the egg over easy.
  3. Cook the bacon and scrapple until crispy.
  4. Butter the English Muffin.
  5. Place egg on the bottom half. Meat Products on the top half, season with salt and pepper to taste.
