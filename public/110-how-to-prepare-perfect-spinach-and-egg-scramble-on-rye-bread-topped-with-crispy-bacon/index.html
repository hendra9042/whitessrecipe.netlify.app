The ingredients needed to make Spinach and egg scramble on rye bread, topped with crispy bacon:
  1. Take Rye bread
  2. Get 2 eggs
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp softened cream cheese/ cream/full cream milk
  4. Get 2 tbsp fresh/frozen spinach
  5. Get to taste Garlic powder,salt n pepper
  6. Take 2 or more bacons strips
  7. Make ready Tsp olive oil and butter
Instructions to make Spinach and egg scramble on rye bread, topped with crispy bacon:
  1. Whisk eggs with the cream cheese and spinach. Add seasoning.
  2. Scramble eggs in olive oil and butter.
  3. Toast rye bread and fry bacon.Top bread with scramble eggs and crispy bacon.
