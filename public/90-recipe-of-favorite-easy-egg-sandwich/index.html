The ingredients needed to make Easy Egg Sandwich:
  1. Take 2 slice bread
  2. Take 2 eggs
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp mayonnaise
  4. Make ready 1 salt and pepper to taste
  5. Prepare 1 shredded cheese or a slice of cheese ( your preference )
Steps to make Easy Egg Sandwich:
  1. Spray skillet with non stick spray and heat skillet to med-high heat
  2. Throw bread in toaster
  3. Fry two eggs til over easy or whatever doneness you prefer. I like over easy so the yolk oozes when I take a bite.
  4. Add salt and pepper
  5. When bread is done toasting spread mayo on both slices.
  6. Sprinkle cheese on eggs and when melted place eggs on toast and grab a napkin ..it gets messy lol
