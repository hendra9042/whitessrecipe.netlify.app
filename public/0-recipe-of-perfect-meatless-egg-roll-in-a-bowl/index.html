The ingredients needed to make Meatless Egg roll in a Bowl:
  1. Get 1/2 sweet onion, sliced
  2. Prepare 1 package Trader Joe's beefless beef
  3. Get 1 tbsp olive oil
  4. Prepare 3 tbsp tamari or coconut aminos
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 tbsp rice vinegar
  6. Get 2-3 tbsp mirin
  7. Prepare 1/2-1 tbsp Sriracha
  8. Take 1/2 tbsp chopped garlic
  9. Make ready 2 tbsp grated ginger
  10. Get 2 tsp coconut sugar
  11. Prepare 1-12 oz bag of broccoli slaw
  12. Take 1/2-1 baby bok choy sliced or bean sprouts
  13. Get Sesame seeds
  14. Prepare 1 tbsp toasted sesame oil

Steps to make Meatless Egg roll in a Bowl:
  1. Heat olive oil in a frying pan. Saute onions for 4-5 minutes on medium heat
  2. Mix tamari, vinegar, mirin, Sriracha, garlic, ginger and sugar in a small bowl.
  3. Add beefless beef and saute. Add a tbsp of water and cover if needed to prevent burning.
  4. Mix sauce into beefless beef and onion mixture. Add broccoli slaw and bok choy. Add a couple tbsp of water and cook covered for 3-4 minutes.
  5. Remove lid and saute until vegetables are tender crisp or to your preference.
  6. Dress with sesame oil. Mix and serve topped with sesame seeds.
