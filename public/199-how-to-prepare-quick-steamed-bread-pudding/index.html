The ingredients needed to make Steamed Bread Pudding:
  1. Take granulated sugar
  2. Make ready low fat milk
  3. Prepare eggs
  4. Get vanilla essence
  5. Get breads
  6. Make ready raisins
  7. Take butter
  8. Get For the sauce :-
  9. Prepare Custard Flour
  10. Take Evaporated Milk
  11. Make ready Sugar
  12. Make ready Vanilla Essence
  13. Get water
Steps to make Steamed Bread Pudding:
  1. Mix together granulated sugar, eggs, and milk in a bowl; add vanilla. Pour over teared bread and let sit for 10 minutes.
  2. In another bowl, pour melted butter then pour prepared breads into it. Sprinkle with raisins. Steam for 45 minutes to 1 hour.
  3. For the sauce: - - Mix together the granulated sugar, custard flour, evaporated milk, water and vanilla essence in a saucepan. Mix it well before stir it over medium heat. Stir together until the sugar is melted and the sauce is thicken.
  4. Pour the sauce over the bread pudding. Serve warm or cold.
