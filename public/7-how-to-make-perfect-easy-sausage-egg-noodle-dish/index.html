The ingredients needed to make Easy Sausage Egg Noodle Dish:
  1. Make ready 2 cups egg noodles, cooked
  2. Get 4 links Italian sausage, (hot or sweet, either one is just fine !)
  3. Prepare 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  4. Get 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  5. Prepare 5 cloves garlic, minced
  6. Get 1 can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  7. Take 1/2 soup can milk
  8. Get Sprinkle of parsley flakes
