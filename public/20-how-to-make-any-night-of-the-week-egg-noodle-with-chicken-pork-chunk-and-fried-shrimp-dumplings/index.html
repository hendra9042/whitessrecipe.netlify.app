The ingredients needed to make Egg Noodle With Chicken Pork Chunk And Fried Shrimp Dumplings:
  1. Take 20 g total of chicken breast and pork loin (cut diced)
  2. Prepare 1 egg noodle
  3. Make ready 2 bokchoy
  4. Get 2 shrimp dumplings (fried)
  5. Prepare 2 garlic (chopped)
  6. Make ready 1 spring onion (chopped)
  7. Get 1 ginger (crushed)
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp soy sauce
  9. Get 1 tbsp fish sauce
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp mushroom sauce
  11. Take 1 tsp sesame oil
  12. Get 1 tbsp sweet soy sauce
  13. Prepare Salt, pepper, and sugar
  14. Take Vegetable soup
Instructions to make Egg Noodle With Chicken Pork Chunk And Fried Shrimp Dumplings:
  1. Marinade the chicken and pork loin with garlic, sweet soy sauce, salt, pepper, sugar for about 10 minutes, then sauteed with ginger and last, add sesame oil.
  2. Fry the shrimp dumplings
  3. In a serving bowl, add soy sauce, mushroom sauce, fish sauce, salt and pepper, then on top of it is the boiled noodle, boiled bokchoy, fried dumplings, and the chicken pork chunk.
  4. After boiling bokchoy, add some salt and pepper in the water and sprinkle with spring onion to become the soup.
