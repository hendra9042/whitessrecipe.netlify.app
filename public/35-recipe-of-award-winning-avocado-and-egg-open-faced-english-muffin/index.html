The ingredients needed to make Avocado and egg open faced English muffin:
  1. Prepare 2 eggs
  2. Make ready Half an avocado
  3. Take 1 English muffin
  4. Take 2 slices tomato, thin
  5. Prepare Salt free seasoning
  6. Take 1 pinch basil

Instructions to make Avocado and egg open faced English muffin:
  1. Boil eggs. For this recipe I like them medium boiled. Place eggs in boiling water for about 6-7 minutes. Remove and immediately put in ice bath.
  2. While boiling eggs, toast English muffin.
  3. Slice avocado into medium pieces.
  4. Slice 2 slices of tomato, thin.
  5. When eggs cool, peel and slice.
  6. Place tomato and avocado on English muffin. Top with egg slices.
  7. Season with salt free seasoning and basil. Enjoy!
