The ingredients needed to make Egg roll:
  1. Get 2 cups flour
  2. Make ready 3 tbls sugar
  3. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt
  4. Get 2 tbls butter
  5. Prepare 1 tsp b. Powder
  6. Prepare Pinch nutmeg
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup warm water
  8. Make ready 1 egg
  9. Prepare 6 boiled eggs
  10. Prepare Oil for deep frying

Instructions to make Egg roll:
  1. Combine flour, sugar, salt, b. Powder, butter, nutmeg and mix thoroughly
  2. Add 1 egg and water and mix well until u have a soft dough
  3. Peel off the shell of the egg and set aside. Divide the dough into 6 portion. Take 1 portion of the dough and make a hole in the middle, then place your egg and cover it well. Repeat the process with the remaining dough and egg. Deep fry and serve hot
