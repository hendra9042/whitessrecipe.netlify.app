The ingredients needed to make Panakuchen German Apple Pancake:
  1. Get 3 large eggs
  2. Take 3/4 cup flour all purpose
  3. Take 3/4 Milk or any alternative milk substitute
  4. Get 1 tsp Cinnamon sprinkled over the top or to taste
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp Sugar or honey
  6. Take 2 tbsp Butter or coconut oil
  7. Take 1 One Apple sliced
  8. Prepare 1 Salt to taste about a half teaspoon added to the batter
Instructions to make Panakuchen German Apple Pancake:
  1. Grease s cast iron skillet with the butter or oil. Add sliced Apple spread evenly. Top with batter. Sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar or honey drizzled. Bake 400 for 25 minutes
