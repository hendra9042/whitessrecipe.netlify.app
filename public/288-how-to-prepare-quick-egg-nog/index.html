The ingredients needed to make Egg Nog:
  1. Make ready 4 cup milk
  2. Take 5 cloves
  3. Get 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  4. Prepare 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  5. Prepare 12 egg yolks
  6. Get 1 1/2 cup sugar
  7. Make ready 2 1/2 cup light rum ?
  8. Make ready 4 cup light cream
  9. Make ready 2 tsp vanilla extract
  10. Prepare 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
Instructions to make Egg Nog:
  1. Combine milk, cloves, 1/2 teaspoon vanilla, and cinnamon in a saucepan. Heat over lowest setting for 5 min. Slowly bring to a boil.
  2. In a large bowl combine egg yolks and sugar.
  3. Whisk until fluffy.
  4. Whisk hot milk mixture into the eggs slowly.
  5. Pour mixture into saucepan and cook over medium heat stirring constantly for 3 min or until thick.
  6. DO NOT ALLOW MIXTURE TO BOIL
  7. Strain to remove cloves and let cool for about 1 hour.
  8. Stir in rum, cream, 2 teaspoon vanilla, and nutmeg.
  9. Refrigerate overnight before serving
