The ingredients needed to make White chocolate mud cake:
  1. Take butter or marg
  2. Make ready white chocolate melts
  3. Get sugar
  4. Prepare coffee
  5. Take milk
  6. Prepare self raising flour
  7. Take custard powder
  8. Make ready eggs
Instructions to make White chocolate mud cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 150°C
  2. Line a large cake tin with baking paper
  3. Melt chocolate & butter in bowl.
  4. Add all ingredients &mix with electric mixer till all combined
  5. Add to prepared tin
  6. Bake for 1 hr or till skewer comes out clean
  7. Leave to cool
