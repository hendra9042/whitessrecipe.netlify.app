The ingredients needed to make Apple & Cinnamon Scotch Pancakes:
  1. Get SR flour
  2. Take Egg
  3. Prepare milk of choice
  4. Get bicarbonate of Soda
  5. Take Red Apples (I used Gala)
  6. Make ready Cinnamon
  7. Prepare Zest of 1 orange
  8. Prepare Butter
  9. Take Natural yoghurt
  10. Prepare Honey
Instructions to make Apple & Cinnamon Scotch Pancakes:
  1. Mix all ingredients except apple. Leave mix to rest for 20mins
  2. Grate apples & add to pancake mixture
  3. Fry in butter for a few minutes on each side over a medium heat
  4. Serve
