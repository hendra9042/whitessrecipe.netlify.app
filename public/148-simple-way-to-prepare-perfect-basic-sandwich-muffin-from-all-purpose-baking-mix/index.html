The ingredients needed to make Basic Sandwich Muffin (from All-purpose Baking Mix):
  1. Make ready All-purpose Baking Mix
  2. Prepare baking powder
  3. Prepare sea salt
  4. Make ready EVOO
  5. Take egg
  6. Make ready water + additional water if needed
Steps to make Basic Sandwich Muffin (from All-purpose Baking Mix):
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease 4 cups of a muffin pan.
  2. In a bowl, combine baking mix, baking powder and salt. Stir in oil thoroughly. Add egg and stir until mixed. If dough too stiff, add water 1 T at a time.
  3. Divide dough into 4 muffin cups. Using spoon, flatten mounds until 1/2" thick, leaving shallow well in center.
  4. Bake 12 min. or until edges begin to brown. Allow to cool 3 min. before carefully removing from pan.
