The ingredients needed to make Sam's Kickin' Breakfast Burritos! ðŸ”¥ðŸŒ¯:
  1. Prepare 8 large eggs
  2. Take 1/2 pack sausage links
  3. Get 1/2 pack bacon

Steps to make Sam's Kickin' Breakfast Burritos! ðŸ”¥ðŸŒ¯:
  1. Crack & beat 8 eggs and pour into large sauce pan. Put on low heat & add some cheese, red pepper flakes, black pepper, & garlic powder.
  2. Meanwhile, roast peppers in oven on olive oil coated pan @ 400 for 15 minutes. Flip over & roast for 10 more minutes.
  3. Saute onions in olive oil in a separate small sauce pan. Fry bacon and sausage in a medium sauce pan. Chop all ingredients up while continuing to add cheese to the egg mix. Add all ingredients to egg mix with even more cheese.
