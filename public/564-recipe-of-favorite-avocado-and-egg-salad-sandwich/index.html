The ingredients needed to make Avocado and egg salad sandwich:
  1. Get 2 Eggs
  2. Make ready 1 Avocado
  3. Prepare 1 Green onion, chopped
  4. Take 1 Black pepper
  5. Make ready 1 Currey powder
  6. Get 1 tbsp Mayonnaise
  7. Make ready 1 Whole wheat wraps or rye bread

Steps to make Avocado and egg salad sandwich:
  1. Hard boil two eggs
  2. Cut up half a ripe avocado into small pieces
  3. Mash them in a bowl with a fork and add in one chopped green onion
  4. Add in freshly ground black pepper to taste
  5. Add in 1/8tsp of medium curry powder
  6. Add in a tbsp or two of mayonnaise
  7. Mix well and spread on whole wheat wraps, bread or rye bread
  8. Top with romaine lettuce or sugar snap peas for some crunchiness
