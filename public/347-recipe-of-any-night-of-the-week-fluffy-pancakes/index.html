The ingredients needed to make Fluffy Pancakes:
  1. Make ready plain flour
  2. Make ready sugar or sweetener
  3. Get melted almost cooled butter
  4. Make ready baking powder
  5. Make ready table salt
  6. Get baking soda
  7. Get milk
  8. Take vanilla extract
  9. Take egg
Steps to make Fluffy Pancakes:
  1. Mix the dry ingredients together in a large bowl.
  2. Make a well in the middle then whisk the wet ingredients together with the dry ingredients until smooth. There may be a few lumps which is fine.
  3. Gently whisk in a couple of tablespoons of milk if the batter is slightly too thick to ladle or scoop into the pan.
  4. Set aside the batter then heat a non-stick frying pan over low-medium heat.
  5. After 5 minutes of heating the pan dry, grease with a little butter or fry lite spray.
  6. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter into the pan at a time. When the top starts to form bubbles flip the pancake. The pancake should be golden brown after flipping. Cook until second side is golden in colour again. Should take about 1-2 minutes each side depending on how hot the pan is and how thick the pancake is.
  7. Repeat steps 5 & 6 until batter is used up.
  8. Serve as soon as possible.
