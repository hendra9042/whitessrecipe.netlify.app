The ingredients needed to make Maggi Egg Devil:
  1. Take 5 packets (70 g) each) Maggi masala noodles
  2. Get 6 eggs, hard boiled
  3. Take 3 cups breadcrumbs
  4. Take as required Oil for deep frying
Steps to make Maggi Egg Devil:
  1. Cook the noodles as per package instructions and then keep aside. Allow it to cool down.
  2. Cover the boiled egg with Maggi and roll it into breadcrumbs.
  3. Heat oil on medium-high flame. When the oil is hot, add the egg and cook until golden-brown in colour.
  4. Serve with carrot-cucumber salad and tomato ketchup.
