The ingredients needed to make Chorizo sausage casserole:
  1. Prepare spicy sausage or regular
  2. Make ready package of chorizo
  3. Get eggs
  4. Take shredded cheddar or pepper jack divided
  5. Prepare milk
  6. Get frozen potatoes (not hashbrowns.too mushy
  7. Get minced garlic
  8. Prepare Salt and pepper to season

Chorizo is a type of pork sausage. In Europe, chorizo is a fermented, cured, smoked sausage, which may be sliced and eaten without cooking, or added as an ingredient to add flavor to other dishes. Elsewhere, some sausages sold as chorizo may not be fermented and cured. You will know when the Egg.

Steps to make Chorizo sausage casserole:
  1. Cook sausage and chorizo together. Add the minced garlic toward the end when meat is almost cooked.
  2. While that is cooking beat your 12 eggs with one cup of milk and salt and pepper.
  3. Add in 1 cup of shredded cheese and your potatoes and mix all together.
  4. Drain your sausage/ chorizo mix. I used paper towels and did it twice. I did not want alot of grease.
  5. Add the meat to the the egg mix and pour in 9x13 greased casserole dish. Add the remaining cup of cheese on top.
  6. Bake at 350 for 40 to 55 minutes by checking the top to see if it starts to brown a little and you don't see runny egg on top. It took mine 45 minutes. I set it on stove and let sit for at least 10 minutes before serving.
  7. You can add onions and peppers to yours.my boys will not eat so I did not add. Also can top with green onions but once again mine will not eat those! I love to top mine with sour cream.
