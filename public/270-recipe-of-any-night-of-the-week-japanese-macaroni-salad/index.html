The ingredients needed to make Japanese Macaroni Salad:
  1. Make ready Macaroni (Maccheroni) *about 200g
  2. Get Continental Cucumber OR 2 smaller ones *thinly sliced
  3. Take Sweet Corn Kernels
  4. Make ready Onion *thinly sliced
  5. Take Ham *cut into about 2cm squares
  6. Get Boiled Eggs *coarsely chopped
  7. Take Japanese Mayonnaise
  8. Make ready Salt & Pepper
Steps to make Japanese Macaroni Salad:
  1. Thinly slice Cucumber into a bowl. Add 1/4 teaspoon Salt and massage, then rest for a while.
  2. Cook Macaroni in a large saucepan of salted boiling water as instructed. Drain, rinse with cold water and drain very well.
  3. Squeeze the Cucumber to remove excess water.
  4. Place the cooked Macaroni, Cucumber, Corn, Onion, Ham and Boiled Eggs in a large bowl, add Japanese Mayonnaise and combine well. Season with Salt and Pepper as required.
