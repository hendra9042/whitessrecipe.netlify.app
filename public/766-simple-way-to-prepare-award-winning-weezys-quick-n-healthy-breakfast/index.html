Instructions to make Weezys quick n healthy breakfast!:
  1. On a greased baking tray, place portobello mushrooms, stalk facing up.
  2. In a bowl combine tomato, avocado, onion and oil. Add preferred seasoning if desired
  3. Top mushrooms with mixture
  4. Cook in oven at approx 200°c for 10 min sprinkle cheese ontop and cook a further 2min.
  5. Remove mushrooms from oven and top each with a poached egg
