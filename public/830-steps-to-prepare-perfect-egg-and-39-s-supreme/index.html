Instructions to make Egg's supreme:
  1. On medium heat Add butter to skillet and let it melt
  2. Add onions and hot dogs along with all the seasons and cook until the onions soften
  3. Once the onions are soft add in the chopped tomato's along with hot sauce and continue to cook until tomato's start to mush
  4. Add the water once the tomato's start to mush and stir cooking for about 2 more minutes
  5. Turn heat down to medium low and crack the eggs over the mixture and cover …cook until eggs are cooked to your liking ..and serve with toast and sour cream on the side
