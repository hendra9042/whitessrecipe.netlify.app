Instructions to make Yam egg roll:
  1. Add oil in a pan,put sliced onion,blended pepper n seasonings. Fry for some minutes.
  2. Mash/pound cooked yam. Add fried pepper n mix. Add maggi if the taste is not ok and mix again until it's well combine.
  3. Grease some oil on hand. Scoop the yam mixture and flatten it,put boiled egg and seal.
  4. Dip in flour, then in whisked egg
  5. Then coat with cornflakes. Fry in hot oil,remove when golden
