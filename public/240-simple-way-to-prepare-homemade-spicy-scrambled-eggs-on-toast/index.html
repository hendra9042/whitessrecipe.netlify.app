Steps to make spicy scrambled eggs on toast:
  1. add oil to frying pan and heat over medium heat.
  2. add onion and chilli to pan, and saute until softened. Add the garlic after 20 seconds to prevent it burning. Try not to brown the onion.
  3. mix eggs and milk in a bowl or jug.
  4. Add the butter and once melted, add the eggs. mix well in the pan with a wooden spoon and allow to 'scramble'.
  5. Add the coriander and salt & pepper to taste.
  6. once cooked to your desired consistency, serve on two slices of toast.
