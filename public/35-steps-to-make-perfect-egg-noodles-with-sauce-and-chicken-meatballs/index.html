The ingredients needed to make egg noodles with sauce and chicken meatballs:
  1. Take 1 packages of egg noodles
  2. Get 1 jar of prego spagetti sauce
  3. Take 1 lb ground chicken
  4. Make ready 1 egg
  5. Take 1 bread crumbs
  6. Get 1 molasses bacon spice from McCormick
Steps to make egg noodles with sauce and chicken meatballs:
  1. put noodles in a pot of water to cook with a drop of oil to prevent sticking together
  2. open sauce and put in another pot and heat on medium until hot
  3. mix together the chicken the egg and breadcrumbs and the spice and mix together
  4. make the mixture into balls and put on baking sheet and once done bake in oven at 350 for 20-25 minutes all depending on the oven
  5. once everything is done mix together in pot with spoon and eat
