The ingredients needed to make Egg roll:
  1. Make ready 3 eggs
  2. Make ready 1 onion
  3. Get 1 Capsicum
  4. Prepare 1 tsp Oregano
  5. Make ready 1 tsp Chilli flakes
  6. Get 1/2 tsp Black pepper powder
  7. Make ready 2 tsp Tomato Sauce
  8. Take 1 tsp Tandoori Mayonnaise
  9. Make ready 2 Green chilli
  10. Prepare as needed wheat Flour (aata)

Instructions to make Egg roll:
  1. Make a dough of flour as you do everyday for making roti
  2. Take a small sized ball from dough and roll it and dust it with flour
  3. Make a round shape chappati
  4. Put this on hot tawa and fry it with ghee until gets cooked
  5. Chop onions and capsicum and saute it in a oil for 2 mins
  6. Crack 3 eggs in a bowl and beat it well
  7. Now add green chilli, salt and black pepper in it and mix it well
  8. Add 1tbsp of ghee in a pan and add half of the egg mixture in it and put chappati on it.now after 2 mins flip the chappati and cook it for 1 min
  9. Now spread sauce and tandoori mayonise on it
  10. Put onions and capsicum mixture in the centre and add a little bit oregano and chilli flakes on it
  11. Now fold it from both sides and serve hotly.
