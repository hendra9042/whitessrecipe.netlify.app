The ingredients needed to make Boiled Custard:
  1. Make ready Evaporated milk
  2. Prepare Water
  3. Prepare Eggs
  4. Prepare Sugar
  5. Get Cornstarch
  6. Prepare Salt
  7. Take Nutmeg
  8. Get Vanilla extract
Steps to make Boiled Custard:
  1. Add water and milk to large pan.
  2. Mix sugar and cornstarch well. Add to milk a little at a time.
  3. Beat eggs well. Add to milk mixture.
  4. Add salt.
  5. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture just begins to boil and coats spoon.
  6. Remove from heat.
  7. Add vanilla and nutmeg.
  8. Cool in refrigerator about an hour before serving.
