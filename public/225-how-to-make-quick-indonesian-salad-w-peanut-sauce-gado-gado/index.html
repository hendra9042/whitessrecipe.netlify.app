The ingredients needed to make Indonesian Salad w/ Peanut Sauce (Gado-Gado):
  1. Make ready Bean sprouts. Remove the roots
  2. Make ready Green beans. Discard both ends, break in half
  3. Make ready Peeled carots. Cut into pieces like a matchstick
  4. Get Potatos. Cut into cubes
  5. Take Tofu. Chop into pieces
  6. Make ready Eggs
  7. Make ready Peeled peanuts. Roasted
  8. Get Salt
  9. Prepare Brown sugar
  10. Take Coconut milk
Steps to make Indonesian Salad w/ Peanut Sauce (Gado-Gado):
  1. Crush the roasted peanuts with a food processor until it become peanut powder.
  2. Boil the peanut powder with coconut milk, brown sugar and salt. Cook until boiling, lift and set aside.
  3. Boil the potatos, eggs, and all veges until cooked. Fry the tofu.
  4. Put all veges, tofu, and eggs on plates. Pour it with the peanut sauce on top. Enjoy it with sweet soy sauce and your favourit crackers.
