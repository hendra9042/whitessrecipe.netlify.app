The ingredients needed to make Hakka Noodles:
  1. Get noodles
  2. Get sliced cabbage
  3. Prepare sliced carrot
  4. Take sliced capsicum
  5. Prepare soya sos
  6. Make ready ginger garlic paste
  7. Get green chilli sos
  8. Prepare black pepper powder
  9. Get Oil
  10. Take Salt
  11. Take Shredded chicken
  12. Make ready egg
Instructions to make Hakka Noodles:
  1. Heat water(3 glass) in a deep bottomed kadhai put oil(1 tsp) noodle packet + salt and boil noodle (90%)
  2. Remove in a strainer and pour cold water and keep aside.
  3. Heat oil (2 tsp) put whisked egg and make omelette remove in a plate and cut roughly into small pieces.
  4. Heat kadhai or pan put oil 1 tbsp and put ginger garlic paste cook 1 mins.then add cabbage + carrot + capsicum + salt(little) and saute on a slow flame for 3 to 4 mins.
  5. Add noodles + soya sauce + green chilli sauce + black pepper powder + omellete pieces + shredded chicken and mix well on a high flame and cook 2 mins on high flame.
  6. And your noodles is ready.
