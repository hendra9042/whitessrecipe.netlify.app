The ingredients needed to make 番茄炒蛋 (Scramble Egg with Tomatoes):
  1. Take Egg Mixture
  2. Prepare Unit x Eggs (or 3 to have enough portion)
  3. Make ready Some Milk
  4. Prepare Some Salt
  5. Make ready Others
  6. Get Unit x Tomatoes
  7. Make ready Enough Sugar
  8. Take Enough Water
  9. Take Optional Ingredients
  10. Make ready x Scallops
Steps to make 番茄炒蛋 (Scramble Egg with Tomatoes):
  1. Cook Egg: #1 Add enough Oil to pan to stir fry Egg Mixture to Medium Cooked (50%) #2 Put egg temporarily aside on dish for later use. #3 Add some Oil for cooking the Optional Ingredients, then place it aside together with the egg.
  2. Cook Tomato Sauce: #1 Cook Tomatoes with Medium Heat until it gets soft, #2 Gently crush tomatoes to push its juice out. #3 Add Water and Sugar to make the the desired Tomato sauce for mixing together with thr Egg.
  3. Final Mix: #1 Put back the egg to pan and mix it quickly and well with the Tomato Sauce.
