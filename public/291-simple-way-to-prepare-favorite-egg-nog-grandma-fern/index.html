The ingredients needed to make Egg Nog (Grandma Fern):
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup sugar
  2. Get 3 egg yokes
  3. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
  4. Get 4 cup whole milk scalded
  5. Get 1/8 tsp salt
  6. Prepare 3 egg whites
  7. Make ready 1/4 cup sugar
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp vanilla
Instructions to make Egg Nog (Grandma Fern):
  1. Beat 1/2 c sugar into egg yokes;add 1/4 tea. salt and stir in milk slowly.
  2. Cook in double boiler until mixture coats spoon, stirring constantly
  3. Cool
  4. In another bowl add 1/8 tea. salt to egg whites and beat to stiff peaks, add remaining 1/4 cup sugar. Beat well .
  5. Add to custard with vanilla. Mix thoroughly ad chill 4 hours
  6. Pile lightly in punch cups sprinkle with nutmeg
  7. ****one tbs of cooking sherry may be substituted for vanilla.
  8. ********I use a heavy sauce pan instead of double boiler. be sure stir constantly and keep fire low. To get custard to thicken I sometimes use a little cornstarch (2 Tab) mixed with the cold milk, Like white sauce stir in.
