The ingredients needed to make Chicken and Dumplings /Egg Noodles:
  1. Make ready Chicken—————-
  2. Take chicken thighs bone in and skin on
  3. Take water
  4. Prepare carrots
  5. Get chopped celery
  6. Make ready kosher salt 1/2 teaspoon ground white pepper
  7. Get Dumplings——————-
  8. Get homemade dumplings/ egg noodles see my recipe
  9. Prepare Thickening paste——————
  10. Make ready self-rising flour
  11. Prepare melted butter
Instructions to make Chicken and Dumplings /Egg Noodles:
  1. Add the chicken in the water and boil for 10 minutes.
  2. Wash the celery and carrots. Peel and chop the carrots, and chop the celery. Put in the pot of boiling chicken.
  3. Cut the dumplings.
  4. Move to some wax paper.
  5. Add the dumplings to the chicken.
  6. They need to boil for 15 minutes
  7. They should be close to being done.
  8. Mix the melted butter with the 1/2 cup of self-rising flour.
  9. Pour the butter flour mixture into the boiling broth.
  10. Stir well this will thicken into a creamy thickened like sauce.
  11. Cover and let rest 15 minutes. Serve, I hope you enjoy!!
