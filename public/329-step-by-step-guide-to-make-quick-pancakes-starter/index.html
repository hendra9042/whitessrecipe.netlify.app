The ingredients needed to make Pancakes Starter:
  1. Make ready flour T45
  2. Make ready tsp salt
  3. Prepare tbsp sugar
  4. Prepare tsp baking soda
  5. Get melted butter
  6. Get egg
  7. Get healthy ready starter
  8. Take A splash of milk if needed
Steps to make Pancakes Starter:
  1. Mix the dry ingredients
  2. Mix the butter and the egg
  3. Add wet ingredients to the dry ingredients
  4. Add your starter. You should NOT mix it, just let it drip into the bowl. - Also, don't mix everything violently. Follow a scrap and fold method to keep the bubbles in the starter.
  5. Let it rest for 30 mins (very important)
  6. Heat a pan to medium, slightly oil it.
  7. Pour in the batter let it cook for max 1 minute per side. It cooks quite quickly. - Flip it CAREFULLY and do the same. - Don't over cook it, just aim for a light brown color on the surface.
  8. Voilà
