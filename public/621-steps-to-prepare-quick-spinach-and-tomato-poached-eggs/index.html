The ingredients needed to make Spinach and Tomato Poached Eggs:
  1. Make ready 1 tsp olive oil, extra virgin
  2. Take 1/2 cup canned diced tomatoes(dont bother draining,the juice is used for poaching)
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp water
  4. Make ready 3 sausage links (brown and serve style) thawed
  5. Make ready 1 cup fresh spinach
  6. Get 2 eggs
  7. Take 1/4 cup shredded cheese of choice(I prefer swiss)
  8. Make ready 1 dried parsley
  9. Get 1 salt and pepper to taste
Steps to make Spinach and Tomato Poached Eggs:
  1. Turn on heat.In a medium sautee pan that has a fitted lid add olive oil,water,sausages and spinach.
  2. Crack both eggs on top and toss on the cheese. Sprinkle with parsley.
  3. Add the lid to pan on on a low to medium heat let poach for 4-7 minutes. If you prefer a softer center 4-5 min…hardboiled yolk would be 6-7 minutes. Plate and enjoy!!
