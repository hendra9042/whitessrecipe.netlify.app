The ingredients needed to make Meatball Noodle Soup:
  1. Get 3 oz tomato paste
  2. Take 1.5 tbsp chili garlic paste
  3. Prepare 7-8 cups beef broth
  4. Take 2 tsp five spice
  5. Get 2 bay leaves
  6. Make ready Dash garlic powder
  7. Prepare Dash ginger powder
  8. Take 1.5 onions, juilenned
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp white peppercorn, crushed
  10. Get as needed Water
  11. Prepare Cilantro, chopped (optional)
  12. Prepare Pickled cabbage (optional)
  13. Get 3-4 oz spiced tofu, cubed (optional)
  14. Get Noodles, cooked + drained
  15. Make ready Meatballs
  16. Prepare 1 block firm tofu, cooked + crumbled
  17. Take 3 shiitaki mushrooms, finely chopped
  18. Take 2 wood ear mushrooms, finely chopped
  19. Make ready Dash fish sauce
  20. Take Dash sesame oil
  21. Get Dash soy sauce
  22. Get 3 eggs
  23. Make ready 3 tbsp barley
  24. Prepare 3 oz Beyond plant-based ground meat or substitute
  25. Take 1/2 cup panko
  26. Make ready 1 tbsp cornstarch
  27. Get Salt to taste
Steps to make Meatball Noodle Soup:
  1. Preheat oven to 375F. In a large mixing bowl, combine all meatball ingredients. Shape mixture into balls and place on to greased baking sheet. Bake for approximately 25 mins until slightly brown. Set aside when done.
  2. In a large pot heat oil, tomato paste, and chili garlic paste. Add broth, onions, bay leaves, five spice, garlic powder, ginger powder, and peppercorn. Boil until onions soften. Add water as needed.
  3. Place cooked noodles into bowls. Top with meatballs, cilantro, cabbage, and tofu. Pour in boiling stock.
