Instructions to make Low calorie courgette mini quiche with cheese straws:
  1. Make pastry - - https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/339718-low-calorie-short-crust-pastry-easy-quick
  2. Pre heat oven to 180°
  3. Whisk egg
  4. Add all ingredients to egg and give good mix
  5. Divide equally into 4 cases & set aside on baking tray
  6. Divide left over pastry and add 2 gram mozzarella to each and give good mix then using palm of hands roll into about 5 mm thick then pop onto tray & bake 8-10 minutes with quiches.
