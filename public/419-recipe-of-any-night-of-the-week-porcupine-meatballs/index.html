The ingredients needed to make Porcupine Meatballs:
  1. Make ready 1 lb ground beef
  2. Get 1 lb italian sausage, taken out of casing
  3. Get 2/3 cup basmati rice
  4. Get 1/2 cup soy milk (or milk of your choice)
  5. Make ready 1 egg, beaten (or equivalent of egg substitute)
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp salt, divided
  7. Prepare 2 tsp chili powder, divided
  8. Get 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  9. Get 28 oz canned crushed tomatoes
  10. Get 2 1/2 cups water
  11. Make ready 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  12. Make ready 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  13. Prepare hot cooked rice, optional
Instructions to make Porcupine Meatballs:
  1. In a large bowl, combine ground beef & ground pork, mix well. Add rice, milk, egg, 2 tsp salt, 1 tsp chili powder, & mix well. Shape into 1 1/2 inch balls.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat until hot. Cook the meatballs in batches in the hot oil until browned on all sides. Drain by putting them on paper towels on a plate.
  3. In a large saucepan, combine tomatoes, 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp chili powder, water, garlic powder, & cayenne pepper. Mix well. Bring to a boil over high heat, stirring occasionally. Reduce heat to low.
  4. Add meatballs to tomato mixture & mix well. Cover. Cook over low heat, stirring occasionally, for 1 hour.
  5. Serve over hot cooked rice, if desired.
