The ingredients needed to make Sausage Biscuits & Gravy Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Prepare Refrigerated Buttermilk Biscuit Dough
  2. Take Eggs
  3. Get Gravy Mix
  4. Take Sausage
  5. Take Shredded Cheese
  6. Prepare Milk
Instructions to make Sausage Biscuits & Gravy Breakfast Casserole:
  1. Brown and Drain Sausage
  2. Cut biscuit dough into one inch pieces and line in the bottom of 9x13 greased pan.
  3. Layer cooked sausage and shredded cheese over dough.
  4. Wisk together eggs and milk, add salt and pepper and pour over biscuits.
  5. Make gravy according to package instructions and pour on top.
  6. Bake for 30-45 minutes at 350°F or until eggs and biscuits are cooked through
